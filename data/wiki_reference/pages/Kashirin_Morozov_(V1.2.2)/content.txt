Kashirin Morozov
(January 29, 1964 - May 17, 2024) was a Russian astronaut for Roscosmos. Prior to becoming an astronaut, he previously worked as a firefighter, police officer, computer programmer, and even served in the Soviet Air Forces as a distinguished fighter pilot. He deserted the military in 1992 when he was denied a discharge in response to injuries he sustained from his first and only deployment.

Early life

Kashirin was born in Kazan, Russia to stay-at-home mother Akinfeeva Yurievich (born 1945) and firefighter Ustinov Morosov (born 1946). He had a stepfather named Boris Putin (born 1946), a receptionist whom Akinfeeva married from 1966 to 1969. He also had a half-brother named Igor Fedorov (born 1970), a cameraman for Maestro TV.
