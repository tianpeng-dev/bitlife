Kashirin Morozov (January 29, 1964 - May 17, 2024) was a Russian astronaut for Roscosmos. Prior to becoming an astronaut, he previously worked as a firefighter, police officer, computer programmer, and even served in the Soviet Air Forces as a distinguished fighter pilot. He deserted the military in 1992 when he was denied a discharge in response to injuries he sustained from his first and only deployment.

Early life[]

Kashirin was born in Kazan, Russia to stay-at-home mother Akinfeeva Yurievich (born 1945) and firefighter Ustinov Morosov (born 1946). He had a stepfather named Boris Putin (born 1946), a receptionist whom Akinfeeva married from 1966 to 1969. He also had a half-brother named Igor Fedorov (born 1970), a cameraman for Maestro TV.

Personal life[]

In his fourth year of middle school, Morozov met Veronika Gorbachev, an eventual police officer. After dating for eight years, Gorbachev proposed in 1986, to which Morozov accepted, and they married that same year. Their son, Ilya, was born in 1994.
