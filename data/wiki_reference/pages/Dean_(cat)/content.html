Dean (later named Zeppelin; 2009 - 2025) was an American Maine Coon who lived in Portland, Maine. Starting his life as a stray, he would experience several shelter stints and embrace the hospitality of several domestic homes. Dean was also a father to thirteen offspring throughout his life.

Biography[]

Early life on the streets[]

Dean was born in Portland as the result of hedonism and debauchery in a back alley. He supposedly tried to interact with his birth parents as much as possible before they were taken from him. After the alley business, Dean started his life as a stray on Melrose Place. There, he became well-acquainted with four other cats, named Harpy, Sabrina, Jagger, and Siren, a mutt named Levi, and a rat named Stan, the latter of whom he would end up eating when he turned a year old.

First shelter stint and escape[]

In 2014, Dean was found in a grocery store parking lot by a young girl named Tove Mathisen. He smiled for the girl, hoping she would take him to her family. Much to his disappointment, she took him to the local animal shelter, Portland Humane Society (PHS). He would only stay there for six months, until the middle of the year when he escaped and went back to Melrose, but not before discovering a new street, Star Court.
