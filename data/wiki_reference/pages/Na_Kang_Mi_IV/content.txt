Early life (2805-2823)
[
]
Na was born in Gangnam, Seoul. She is the youngest of three children born to
Na Sip
and the only one born to his second girlfriend, Shin Somun Chun.
In 2811, Na enrolled at Seo-hyeon Cha Primary School.
In 2817, Na enrolled at Min Lower Secondary School.
In 2820, Na enrolled at Lee Upper Secondary School.
In 2823, Na graduated from upper secondary school.
Career (2823-2877)
[
]
In 2823, Na signed with Celestial Records as a K-pop singer. She released her debut anime album,
The Imagination Chapter
, which went gold in 2826.
In 2824, Na released her second album,
The Dream Introduction
, which went gold in 2826.
In 2825, Na released her third album,
The Birth of a Dream.
In 2826, Na released her fourth album,
Blueprint of the Soul.
In 2833, Na released her fifth album,
Hope Planet
, which went gold in 2837.
In 2836, Na released her sixth album,
IndiGO
, which went gold in 2838.
In 2839, Na released her seventh album,
False Devotion
, which went double platinum in less than a year.
In 2842, Na released her eighth album,
Feel Unique
, which went double platinum in less than a year.
In 2845, Na released her ninth album,
Enjoy Yourself
, which went platinum in less than a year and double platinum in 2847.
In 2847, Na released her 10th album,
Dynamite Heart
, which went gold in less than a year.
In 2854, Na released her 11th album,
The Fantasy Chapter
, which went platinum in less than a year and double platinum in 2855.
In 2867, Na released her 12th album,
The Daydream Chapter
, which went double platinum in less than a year.
In 2872, Na released her 13th album,
Map of the Spirit
, which went double platinum in less than a year.
In 2877, Na retired.
