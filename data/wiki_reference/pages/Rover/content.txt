Rover
(2013 - 2022) was an American mutt.
Life
[
]
Rover was born in Philadelphia, in the closet of his mother's owner. His owner was Dylan Goldfarb (born 1968), a restaurant worker. Rover also spent time with Dylan's daughter, Skylar (born 2000) for a year before she moved out of Dylan's house after finishing college.
