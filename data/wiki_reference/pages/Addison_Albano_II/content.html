Early life (3955-3973)[]

Albano was born in Malmö, Sweden. She was the youngest of nine children born to Rocco Albano II and the only born to his second girlfriend Ines Lindbergh. She was named for her older sister, Addison Albano I, who died as a child.

Albano graduated high school in 3973.

Modeling career (3973-3990)[]

In 3973, Albano signed with Dusky Rose Talent Agency as a body part model.

In 3982, Albano was promoted to stock photo model.

In 3990, Albano resigned from Dusky Rose Talent Agency.

Mail carrier career (3990-4021)[]

In 3990, Albano became a junior mail carrier for PostNord Sweden.

In 3994, Albano was promoted to mail carrier.

In 3997, Albano was promoted to senior mail carrier.

In 4021, Albano retired.

Albano was alive in 4036, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 3973 to 3974, Albano dated a photographer named Oliver Petterson (3948-4024). They had a son, Lucas Petterson.
