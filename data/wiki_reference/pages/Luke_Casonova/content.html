Lucas Casonova (born 1953) is an American dancer from Brooklyn. Aside from dancing, he is widely known as the husband (now widower) of actress Penelope Hunter.

Dancing career[]

Luke began dancing professionally in the early 1970s.

Personal life[]

Casonova met Hunter in 1976 when he was 23 years old (Hunter was 18 as rookie voice actress), working as a dancer for Granite Films. After three years of dating, they got engaged in 1979 and wed in 1981. They went to San Juan, Puerto Rico for their honeymoon. They remained married until Hunter's death in 2021. They had six children together, their first, daughter Shaniqua, being born before their engagement, and their last, son Hunter, being born on their 25th anniversary.
