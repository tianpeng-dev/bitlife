Notifications

Last Updated on iOS:

May 2020


Notifications are an extra to BitLife. Sometimes the BitLife developers will give your character a free car if they turn on notifications. Special things can happen when they are turned on, but the player must go onto BitLife within an hour of the notification. The notification sound effect is based on the sound the player makes when making a choice. Notifications can also tell you about Heirlooms.

Notifications can be a new pet scratching at your door and the character can make it theirs before they run away. They may be free money on the ground that a character must pick up within the hour or someone else will pick it up before them. It could also be something bad, like someone trying to steal the character's car and the character must reclaim the car within the hour before it is gone. If your character is currently serving jail time, they could get a parole and be released from prison if they react to the notification in time. As of May 2020, one can win the Sweepstakes by notifications and get the achievement that goes with it. Notifications may also give hints on the best time to win the Lottery and the best horse to bet on in the Horse Races but these may not be 100% accurate.
