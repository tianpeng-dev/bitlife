Joseph Statham (7015-after 7046) was an Aruban cook.

Early life (7015-7034)[]

Statham was born in Oranjestad, Aruba. He was the fifth of seven children born to Leo Statham LXIII (actually XLIII) and the only born to his sixth fling Ingrid Driver.

In 7021, Statham enrolled at St. Irena’s Primary School. He called his principal, Anna Kelly, a lint licker. He was suspended for four days.

In 7028, Statham enrolled at Oranjestad Secondary School. He skipped school to go skiing.

In 7032, Statham graduated from secondary school. He enrolled at Oranjestad Community College.

Career (7034-7046)[]

In 7034, Statham graduated from community college. He became a line cook for United Restaurant.

Statham was alive in 7046, but his death date is unknown.

Relationships and child[]

From 7025 to 7031, Statham dated a student named Elizabeth Colina (7015-after 7032).

In 7031, Statham started dating an Indian-born student named Kavya Lakshmi (7015-after 7046). They had a son, Leo Statham VL.
