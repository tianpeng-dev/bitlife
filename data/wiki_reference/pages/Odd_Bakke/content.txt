Odd Bakke
(2166-2220) was a Norwegian-American restaurateur.
Early life (2166-2186)
[
]
Bakke was born in Trondheim, Norway. He was the youngest of four children born to
Tarjei Bakke III
and the youngest of three born to his second girlfriend Eva Andresen.
In 2185, Bakke graduated from upper secondary school.
In 2186, Bakke emigrated to Indianapolis, Indiana.
Career (2204-2220)
[
]
In 2204, Bakke became a dishwasher for Emily’s Cafe.
In 2206, Bakke was promoted to busser.
In 2208, Bakke was promoted to host.
In 2210, Bakke was promoted to food runner.
In 2212, Bakke was promoted to server.
In 2214, Bakke was promoted to head waiter.
In 2216, Bakke was promoted to expeditor.
In 2220, Bakke was murdered by a hitman hired by his son.

[
