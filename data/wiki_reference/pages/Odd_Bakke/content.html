Odd Bakke (2166-2220) was a Norwegian-American restaurateur.

Early life (2166-2186)[]

Bakke was born in Trondheim, Norway. He was the youngest of four children born to Tarjei Bakke III and the youngest of three born to his second girlfriend Eva Andresen.

In 2185, Bakke graduated from upper secondary school.

In 2186, Bakke emigrated to Indianapolis, Indiana.

Career (2204-2220)[]

In 2204, Bakke became a dishwasher for Emily’s Cafe.

In 2206, Bakke was promoted to busser.

In 2208, Bakke was promoted to host.

In 2210, Bakke was promoted to food runner.

In 2212, Bakke was promoted to server.

In 2214, Bakke was promoted to head waiter.

In 2216, Bakke was promoted to expeditor.

In 2220, Bakke was murdered by a hitman hired by his son.

Relationship and children[]

In 2186, Bakke started dating a hairdresser named Hannah Stewart (2188-after 2238). They had six children:

  1. Elin Bakke II (2187-after 2238), Magistrate Judge for the Southern District of Indiana
  2. Hilde Bakke II (2188-after 2238), interior designer
  3. Ingrid Bakke II (2189-after 2238), US Army enlistee
  4. Asefi Bakke (2190-after 2238), crime scene analyst
  5. Mikayla Bakke (2191-after 2238), professor
  6. Tarjei Bakke V
