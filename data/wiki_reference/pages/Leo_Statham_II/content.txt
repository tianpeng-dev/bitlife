Early life (5277-5302)
[
]
Statham was born in Blackpool, England. He was the only child of
Jane Statham II
and her ex-boyfriend Terry Hayes, Baron of Buckley.
In 5295, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Lancashire County Council, but lost to Phil Ward. He enrolled at Lancaster University.
In 5299, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at Lancaster University Law School.
Career (5302-5317)
[
]
In 5302, Statham completed the Legal Practice Course. He ran for election to the Lancashire County Council. He beat Lewis Bowie-Ashurst and was inaugurated.
In 5307, Statham ran for reelection and beat Logan Edwards.
In 5312, Statham ran for Mayor of Blackpool with the Labour Party and beat Green Party candidate Jake Cambridge.
In 5317, Statham did not run for reelection.
