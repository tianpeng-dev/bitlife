Yuto Statham (b. 6718) is a British-Austrian retired army officer.

Early life (6718-6740)[]

Statham was born in Birmingham, England. He is the younger of two brothers born to Leo Statham XXXVIII and his ex-girlfriend Akemi Kimura.

In 6729, Statham enrolled at Hourglass Secondary School.

In 6736, Statham graduated from secondary school and enrolled at the University of Birmingham.

Career (6740-6767)[]

In 6740, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He emigrated to Kobe, Japan. He joined the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force as a santō rikui, or second lieutenant. He bought a sailing catamaran, which he named Favorite Mistake. He was shipwrecked after hitting a sea mine.

In 6741, Statham was promoted to nitō rikui, or first lieutenant.

In 6744, Statham was promoted to ittō rikui, or captain.

In 6747, Statham bought a pirate ship, which he named Asami’s Bounty.

In 6750, Statham was promoted to santō rikusa, or major.

In 6756, Statham was promoted to nitō rikusa, or lieutenant colonel.

In 6766, Statham was promoted to ittō rikusa, or colonel.

In 6767, Statham was promoted to brigadier general. He retired and emigrated to Salzburg, Austria.

Relationships and children[]

From 6728 to 6735, Statham dated a student named Ella Laws (6718-after 6774). They had a son, Leo Statham XL (6735-6808).

From 6767 to 6768, Statham dated a student named Johanna Wimmer (6748-6834). They had a son, Marcus Statham.
