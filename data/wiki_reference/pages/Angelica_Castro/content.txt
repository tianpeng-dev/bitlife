Angelica Castro
(May 20, 2005-March 12, 2025) was an American student.
Life (2005-2025)
[
]
Castro was born in Wichita, Kansas. She was the older of two children born to Kim Castro (1973-after 2025). Her father is unknown. She had a younger half brother named Dwight Morrison (2009-after 2025).
In 2011, Castro enrolled at Blair Goff Elementary School.
In 2017, Castro enrolled at Thoroughbred Middle School.
In 2020, Castro enrolled at St. Precious’s High School.
In 2024, Castro enrolled at Wichita State University. She majored in physics and was a member of Alpha Chi Omega.
Death
[
]
On March 12, 2025, Castro started taking flight classes to get a pilot’s license. She died in an airplane crash during a test flight.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 2016 to 2023, Castro dated a Costa Rican-born student named Ary Garcia (b. 2006). They had a daughter,
