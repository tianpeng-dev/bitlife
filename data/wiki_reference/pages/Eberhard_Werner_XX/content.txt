Eberhard Werner XX
(4628-after 4684) was a Japanese policeman.
Early life (4628-4666)
[
]
Werner was born in Tokyo. He was one of two twins born to
Eberhard Werner XIX
and his ex-girlfriend Aiya Taoka.
In 4646, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.
Career (4666-4684)
[
]
In 4666, Werner became a cadet for the Tokyo Metropolitan Police Department.
In 4669, Werner was promoted to patrolman.
In 4672, Werner was promoted to trooper.
In 4675, Werner was promoted to corporal.
In 4678, Werner was promoted to sergeant.
In 4681, Werner was promoted to inspector.
In 4684, Werner was promoted to lieutenant. His death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 4646 to 4647, Werner dated a trucker named Saki Masuda (4627-after 4684). They had a son,
Eberhard Werner XXI
.
