Leo Statham XXI (5760-after 5821) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5760-5778)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He is the younger of two children born to Leo Statham XX and his ex-girlfriend Erin Murphy.

Career (5778-5798)[]

In 5778, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Seamus Brown.

In 5783, Statham ran for reelection and beat Bono Graham.

In 5788, Statham ran for a third term and beat Brigid Taffe.

In 5793, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Brendan Sheehan.

In 5798, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5821, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 5778 to 5781, Statham dated a Costa Rican-born Naval Service enlistee named Luciana Munoz (5759-after 5821). They had three children, Kate XVIII (5779-5789), Karen (5780-after 5821), and Leo Statham XXII.
