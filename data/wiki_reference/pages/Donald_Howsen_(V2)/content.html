Donald Howsen (born 1952) is an American entrepreneur and retired restaurant worker. He is the older brother of football quarterback Derek Howsen.

Early life[]

Donald was born in Arlington County to magician Camille Howsen (1924 - 2012) and writer Dennis Howsen (1921 - 1994). His father was also a World War II veteran. Donald's brother Derek was born a year later, and his youngest brother, Antonio, a makeup artist, was born in 1960.

Career[]

Howsen originally intended to begin his restaurant career in 1970, straight out of high school, but had been drafted to serve in Vietnam. While stationed overseas, he would cook for his fellow soldiers in his free time, having accepted an apprenticeship by his platoon leader and commanding officers. After returning home a year later, at age 19, he secured an entry-level position as a dishwasher for Cafe Appaloosa.
