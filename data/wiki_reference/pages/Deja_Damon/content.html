Deja Damon (1978 - November 15, 2019) was a United States Navy veteran from Miami who was killed in action after being blown up by a land mine.

Early life[]

Damon was born in 1978 in Miami, Florida.

Navy career[]

In 1996, Damon enlisted in the Navy shortly after graduating high school.

Personal life and death[]

Damon's marital status is disputed. Although she claimed it, there were no public records of her ever being married or having children.

On November 15, 2019, Damon was deployed to Iran for a landmine excavation assignment. She accidently stepped on one of the landmines and was killed instantly. Her body was shipped back to the United States for her funeral, which was solely attended by her mother.
