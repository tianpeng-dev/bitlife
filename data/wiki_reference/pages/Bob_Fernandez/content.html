Bob Fernandez (2224-2307) was a British socialite, physical therapist, massage therapist, and crab fisherman.

Early life[]

Fernandez was born in Sheffield, England. His father was William Fernandez. He was on the Sheffield Secondary School table tennis team from 2237 to 2242. He then went to the University of Sheffield and graduated with a BS in Biology in 2246. He then went to Sheffield Medical School and graduated in 2253.

Jobs[]

In 2253, Fernandez briefly became a physical therapist for Sheffield Medical. However, he resigned less than a year later to become a massage therapist for Opus Salon.

In 2254, Fernandez went on vacation to Greece. He briefly became a crab fisherman, but resigned.

Later life[]

In 2256, Fernandez got a deep tissue massage.

In 2257, Fernandez and his boyfriend Pat McHolmes had a threesome with a real estate agent named Rupert Kingswood (b. 2233).

In 2269, Fernandez had an argument with his father.

Death[]

In 2307, Fernandez died of old age. His body was donated to science.

Relationships and child[]

From 2237 to 2242, Fernandez dated a student named Annabelle Beckinsale (2224-after 2307), who later became an operations analyst. They had a daughter, Luna Fernandez.

In 2242, Fernandez briefly dated four athletes: Austrian-born baseball player Andrew Breezy (2223-after 2278), football player Tom Shady (2223-after 2278), baseball player Seymar Junior (2223-after 2278), and James Hernandez (2224-after 2278). He then started dating baseball player Pat McHolmes (2224-after 2278), for which Fernandez became famous in 2247.
