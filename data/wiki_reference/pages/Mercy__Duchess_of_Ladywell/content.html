Dr. Mercy Bell, DClinPsy (2123-2243), formerly Duchess of Ladywell, was a British noblewoman.

Early life (2123-2147)[]

Bell was born in Southampton, England. She was the only child of Elijah, Earl of Warwick and his first wife Penelope Atkinson.

In 2134, Elijah married Mel, Countess of Warwick, making Bell the younger of her two daughters.

In 2142, Bell enrolled at the University of Southampton. She majored in psychology.

In 2146, Bell graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology. She enrolled at King’s College London Graduate School.

Duchess of Ladywell (2147-2235)[]

In 2147, Bell married Harry, Duke of Ladywell (2128-after 2235), becoming Duchess of Ladywell. In 2235, they divorced, making her no longer Duchess. They had a daughter:

  1. Chelsea, Marchioness of Brentford (2148-2235)

In 2148, Bell graduated with a Doctor of Clinical Psychology.

Death[]

In 2248, Bell died of old age.

Second marriage and stepchildren[]

In 2235, Bell married a professor named Luke Bell (né Hamilton, 2191-2280). She had three stepsons from Luke’s previous marriage to a woman named Natasha Thigpen:
