Early life (5377-5396)
[
]
Statham was born in Naples. He is the only child of
Clint Statham
and his ex-girlfriend Paola Alighieri.
Political career (5396-5421)
[
]
In 5396, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Omaima Choukri.
In 5401, Statham ran for reelection and beat Leonardo Saraceno.
In 5406, Statham ran for Metropolitan Mayor of Naples as a Democrat and beat Brothers of Italy candidate Nadia Pesce.
In 5411, Statham ran for reelection and beat FdI candidate Michelangelo Agnello.
In 5416, Statham ran for a third term, but lost to Italian Left candidate Russo Montalcini. He ran for a third term on the Provincial School Administration and beat Nadia Ricci.
In 5421, Statham did not run for a fourth term.
Real estate career (5422-5437)
[
]
In 5422, Statham became a real estate agent for Altera and Bruno Realty.
Statham was alive in 5437, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 5396 to 5398, Statham dated an unemployed woman named Angelica Gallo (5378-after 5437). They had a daughter,
Kate Statham VIII
.
