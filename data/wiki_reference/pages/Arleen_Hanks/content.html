Arleen Hanks (3364-after 3402) was an American-Taiwanese wedding planner.

Early life (3364-3382)[]

Hanks was born in New York City. She was the only child of Becky Snatch and her boyfriend Chauncey Snatch.

In 3369, Hanks enrolled at Fountainhead Elementary School.

In 3375, Hanks enrolled at Magnitude Middle School.

In 3378, Hanks enrolled at Devin Rogers High School.

In 3382, Hanks graduated from high school. She emigrated to Mazatlán, Mexico.

Career (3384-3402)[]

In 3384, Hanks became a junior wedding planner for Valencia Design.

In 3387, Hanks was promoted to wedding planner.

In 3388, Hanks emigrated to Taipei, Taiwan with her boyfriend and daughter.

In 3389, Hanks became a junior wedding planner for Hsiao and Sons.

In 3392, Hanks was promoted to wedding planner.

In 3395, Hanks was promoted to senior wedding planner.

Hanks was alive in 3402, but her death date is unknown.
