Li Peng
(16 february 1957 - 29 september 2022) - Chinese Gangster, founder of the mafia "Tiger 1949" The richest man in the world according to BitNews in 1983 and the most wanted man in the world according to NewsLife in 1985. Arrested in 1996. Sentenced to death for the murder of 31 people, which was commuted to life imprisonment. Died in Guangzhou Prison on September 29, 2022.
Early Childhood
Li Peng was born in Guangzhou on February 16, 1957. Li was not born into a wealthy family, as his family suffered greatly during the Civil War and the Mao Zedong regime. Upon entering school, he immediately became a social outcast who only had friends with his best friend Dong Hu.
After graduating from school in 1975, he joined the army, almost immediately before the end of the Vietnam War.
CAREER
Between July - November 1981, his father (1930 - July 8, 2001) founded the group "Tigers 1949". It included: His Father Shan Peng, Li, Mother Tao Yaling (January 1935 - 2018), His best friend Dong (1957 - present). They were engaged in extortion of money, theft and even murder. However, a year later, the father gave the leadership of the group to his son Li. During their career, they killed 31 people (According to unconfirmed information - 45). And they stole about 451,805 yuan ($63,154). During this time, they were famous. As his friend Dong said in an interview in 2004, "Those were the golden times. We felt richer than China's entire GDP (那真是黄金时代, or China's entire GDP)."
Raid on a house in Yongfu, arrest and death.
A raid was carried out on the house where the four lived. They were all arrested and taken to the police.
For everything he did while in the Mafia, Li Peng first received the death penalty, and then life Conclusion without parole. The father received 7 years in prison (But did not live to see it, in 2001 he died of old age). The mother and Dong - 5 years each. 
On September 27, 2022, he was found in a cell in a terrible state. He was taken to hospital but died two days later.
The farewell to Li took place on October 2 of the same year in Beijing. It was possible to attend the funeral, but it was forbidden to lay flowers on the grave (the same thing happened when his parents died).
