Ana Jones (2188-after 2237) was an American basketball player.

Early life[]

Jones was born in Detroit, Michigan. Her mother is Payton Jackson. She was on the St. Paula’s Middle School and Hourglass High women’s basketball teams.

Career[]

In 2206, Jones joined the Las Vegas Showgirls as a shooting guard.

In 2208, Jones was traded to the Dallas Blings.

In 2210, Jones was released by the Blings and joined the Los Angeles Vain.

In 2216, Jones was traded to the Indiana Sweats. She retired in 2222.

Relationship and child[]

From 2198 to 2220, Jones dated a student named Aurelio Fernandez (b. 2188), who became a restaurant worker in 2206. They had a son, William Fernandez.
