Zebediah Katz (April 15, 1914 - May 23, 2026) was an Israeli politican and lawyer who served as the second and fourth Prime Minister of Israel from 1949 - 1977 and again from 1981 - 1993. He was widely criticized during his reign for his strict policies limiting internal movement and starting mass deportations, as well as the mass mobilization of the entire IDF for the purposes of his War on Gangs that ran from 1969 until 1977. He was widely remembered as a dictator as he won most of his elections in a landslide, except the 1977 election where he lost, and the 1973 election where he won with 2% of the vote.
