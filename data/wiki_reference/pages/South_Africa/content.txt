South Africa
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the legislative capital, Cape Town, along with Durban, Johannesburg, and Soweto.
South Africa, alongside
Morocco
and
Nigeria
are the only countries where a capital is not a default city. South Africa has three capitals and only one of them is included. Cape Town (legislative) is included, but Pretoria (executive) and Bloemfontein (judicial) are not.
