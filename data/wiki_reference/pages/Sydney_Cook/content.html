Sydney Cook (4176-after 4235) was a Canadian-Norwegian politician and news reporter.

Early life (4176-4194)[]

Cook was born in Toronto, Ontario. She was the only child of Francois Cook and his second girlfriend Ariana Santiago.

In 4191, Cook enrolled at Aloha Secondary School.

Political career (4194-4198)[]

In 4194, Cook graduated from secondary school. She ran for election to the Toronto District School Board and beat Michelle Bergeron.

In 4198, Cook did not run for reelection. She emigrated to Bergen, Norway.

Reporting career (4200-4235)[]

In 4200, Cook became a junior reporter at Chateau TV.

In 4202, Cook was promoted to reporter.

In 4204, Cook was promoted to senior reporter.

Cook was alive in 4235, but her death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 4193 to 4198, Cook dated a student named Emile Valley (4176-after 4199). They have a daughter, Marie Valley (b. 4194).

In 4198, Cook started dating an enlistee in the Royal Norwegian Navy named Arne Grieg I (4170-after 4235). They had a son, Liam Grieg.
