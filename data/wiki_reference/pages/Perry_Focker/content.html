Perry Duke Focker (September 13, 1992 - December 1, 2022) was an American mobster-turned-informant as well as a convicted scam artist and fraudster from Chicago. Straight out of high school, Focker took up panhandling as an alternative method of business. He spent several years in prison for scamming several people out of money, though some say that said people had it coming for undisclosed reasons. Later, after obtaining a college degree, Focker joined the Flynn family of the Irish mob.

Early life[]

Focker was born in Chicago, Illinois to Duke (1946 - 2015) and Sophie Focker (born 1950). His father was a librarian, and his mother is a retired photographer; Duke and Sophie often collaborated, combining their professions for certain joint-operation assignments. Perry's parents divorced when he was twelve. He had three siblings, an older biological brother, Jaylen (born 1978), and a stepbrother and stepsister from his mother's marriage to his stepfather, Lonnie Armstrong (born 1960), a financial analyst; Kristen Armstrong (born c. 1987) and Chip Armstrong (born 1988/1989). Jaylen is a chief of the Chicago Fire Department who is also gay but was previously married to a woman named Penelope Moorehead (born 1984). Kristen is a police officer for the Chicago Police Department, and Chip is a Database Administrator for Eager Beaver Properties.

Hustling career[]

Focker's first act of deviancy occurred at the age of eight, when he and his best friend, Elektra Bell, slashed stranger's tires in a busy parking lot. He would sometimes get into trouble in middle school; one time, he skipped class to watch a spelling bee at a local elementary school. Another time, he farted in class and blamed it on a classmate named Nicole, who then proceeded to assault him, resulting in her facing repercussions. When Nicole's family tried to sue that of Focker's for the incident, Duke and Sophie would take Perry's side, despite the clear evidence of him being the instigator. During his freshman year of high school, Focker stole his first car, an eighth generation Honda Civic.

In 2010, within the first few days of graduating high school, Focker took to the streets to start hustling. After settling down at Lombard St., he became a fire eater. Focker would then proceed to scam several individuals by "accidently" bumping into them and stealing their personal items, which he would later sell. Eventually, Focker got the attention of the police, from which he tried to escape, but to no avail. After his arrest, he was charged with fraud and running from the police. Using the money he had collected from his scams, he hired Pickler & Associates to represent him in court and pled not guilty. Focker's trial lasted several weeks until he was found guilty and sentenced to two years in prison. He was sent to Beacon Correctional Center. A year into his sentence, he was insulted by a prisoner named Bear, provoking him to fight him, resulting in a year extension to his sentence. Focker was released in 2013 on his 21st birthday.

Irish mob[]

After finishing college in 2017, Focker started delivering packages for the Irish mob's Flynn crime family. After performing these tasks for two months, he was sworn in by the family as an associate.

Personal life[]

Focker met Alexa Morgan (born 1992) in seventh grade. Alexa was firefighter, who sometimes worked with Jaylen, which would annoy Perry. After fifteen years of dating, they married in 2019. They had two sons, Dennis (born 2020) and Nicholas (born 2022).
