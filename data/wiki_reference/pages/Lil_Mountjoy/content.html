Lil Mountjoy (1964 - 2021) was a United States Army veteran who was known as one of the owners of Joy the Cat. An abusive husband and owner, he pushed Joy over the edge, which resulted in his undoing at her paws.

Early life[]

Lil was born in Boston to a proud military family. His father was a Korean War veteran, while his mother worked in military medicine.

Military career[]

Mountjoy enlisted in the Army in 1982.

Personal life[]

During the early 1980s, Mountjoy met rookie trucker Veronica Ryder. After a few years of dating, they married and had a son named Elijah (born 1986).
