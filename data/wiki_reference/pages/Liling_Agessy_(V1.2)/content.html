Liling Agessy (née Zedong; born July 10, 1997) is a Canadian professional volleyball player. She has played for the Vancouver Vans since 2015. Agessy competed in the 2024 Paris Olympics, representing her team and country.

Early life[]

Liling was born in Saskatoon to housewife Jiao Chu (born 1968) and travel agent Chenglei Zedong (born 1958). Both her parents, who divorced the year after her birth, were from Guangzhou, China. They had immigrated during their early years of life to escape from the clutches of communism, especially under the rule of Mao Zedong (no blood relation to Chenglei). Liling has an older biological brother named Leo (born 1993), a real estate agent for Higgins & Hatch Realtors. In 1999, Chu married Wesley Mitchell (born 1968), a restaurant worker with a son named Tristan (born 1987), a library clerk.
