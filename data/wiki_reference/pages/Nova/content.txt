Background
[
]
Cardi was born in New York as the result of her father barking up the "right tree." She started her life as a pet for sale at Pet City, a popular pet store in Manhattan. Her starting price was $745 ($1,321.96 in 2022; $1,416.98 in 2025). During her nine months at the store, she practiced her dance skills. At month nine, she was purchased for $555 by a medical student named Isabella Sato (born 1971 or 1972), who would eventually graduate from New York Medical College to become a family physician at Vitality Medical Center. Sato gave Cardi a new name, Nova, though she would respond to both names. The two had a strong connection together. She also became well-acquainted with Isabella's cat, Li.
