Otto Nietzche
(November 20, 1910 - April 24, 2024) was a German politician, museum worker, soldier, and supercentenarian who served many terms as Mayor of Cologne and School Board Director.

Early life
[
]
Otto was born in Cologne, Germany to jeweler Lara Klum (1876 - 1964) and hairdresser Nicholas "Nick' Nietzche (1875 - 1957). He had an older brother named Friedrich (1905 - 1992), a policy analyst, and a younger sister named Hanne (1915 - 2006), a computer programmer. He had a mongrel named Marlon (1909 - 1920).

Personal life
[

Nietzche met Charolette Beethoven (1910 - 2020), a firefighter, in 1933 in college. They married in 1936 after graduation. They had three children and five grandchildren together:

Ansel (born 1950) - Trucker for Schlittenhund Lkw und Dienstleistungen (Husky Trucking & Services)
Death
[
]
Nietzche passed away peacefully on April 24, 2024, at the age of 113 years, 5 months, and 4 days. He is survived by his last surviving child and three grandsons.
