Eleanor (2009 - 2025), also known by her secondary adoptive name Giulia, was an Italian mutt.

Life[]

Eleanor was born in Naples as the result of her mother playing with her father's "special rope toy." She was first put up for sale at the pet store Essenza (Essence) for €235 ($243.60 USD; $356.18 in 2025), though her price would later be lowered to €175 ($181.41 USD; $265.25 in 2025). Eleanor made good friends with the other pets for sale, including three cats named Wilma, Lea, and Clive, Rocky the Australian shepherd, Oakley the Yorkshire terrier, Gabriel the turtle, and Foxy the rat. She also got along well with the two main store employees, Rosalba Lettiere (born 1960), the manager of Essenza at the time, and Maurizio Toscani (born 1988), one of Essenza's pet stylists and alleged relative of the famed photographer Oliviero Toscani.
