Early life and education[]

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (1987 - 2061) and Cooper Piner (1988 - 2073). Her mother was a school administrator and her father was a marine biologist. She had an older brother, Steven (2009 - 2085), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 2035).
