Victoria Lidbom (October 31, 1950 - June TBD, 1995) was an Australian wildlife expert, outdoorswoman, zookeeper and veterinarian. A curious explorer since childhood, she initially struggled to afford her passions due to various financial responsibilities. This changed when she received a big inheritance from her late father, which she used to open her zoo, Lidbom's Sanctuary.

Childhood[]

Lidbom was born in the City of Knox to firewoman Aurora Lidbom (1916 - TBD) and senior Army enlistee Gavin Lidbom (1895 - 1972). She had an older brother named Eric (born 1946), a teacher.
