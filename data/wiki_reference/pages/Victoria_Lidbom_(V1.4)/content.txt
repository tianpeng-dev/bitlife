Victoria Lidbom
(October 31, 1950 - June TBD, 1995) was an Australian wildlife expert, outdoorswoman, zookeeper and veterinarian. A curious explorer since childhood, she initially struggled to afford her passions due to various financial responsibilities. This changed when she received a big inheritance from her late father, which she used to open her zoo, Lidbom's Sanctuary.
