Eberhard Werner XXV (4835-after February 8, 4902) was a German-Greek accountant and mailman.

Early life (4835-4859)[]

Werner was born in Dortmund, Germany. He was the eldest of six children born to Otello Werner IV and the only one born to his ex-girlfriend Franziska Konig.

In 4853, Werner enrolled at the Technical University of Dortmund.

In 4857, Werner graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Accounting. He enrolled at the International School of Management.

Accounting career (4859-4867)[]

In 4859, Werner graduated with a Master of Business Administration. He became a junior accountant at Inertia International.

In 4862, Werner was promoted to accountant.

In 4865, Werner was promoted to senior accountant.

First mail carrying career (4867-4884)[]

In 4867, Werner became a junior mail carrier for Deutsche Post.

In 4868, Werner murdered his mother with a set of plastic vampire teeth.

In 4871, Werner was promoted to mail carrier.

In 4875, Werner was promoted to senior mail carrier.

In 4883, Werner went to an Enrique Iglesias concert with his friend, Anni Fuchs (4824-after 4885). He read Stellaluna by Janell Cannon. He earned a blue belt in jiu jitsu and learned how to perform a grapple. He adopted a goldfish named Clown (4882-4888).

In 4884, Werner prayed to the BitLife development team. He resigned and emigrated to Santorini, Greece.

Second mail carrying career (4886-4901)[]

In 4886, Werner became a junior mail carrier for Hellenic Post.

In 4889, Werner was promoted to mail carrier.

In 4892, Werner was promoted to senior mail carrier.

In 4901, Werner resigned.

Werner outlived his daughter, Jasmin XVII, but his death date is unknown.
