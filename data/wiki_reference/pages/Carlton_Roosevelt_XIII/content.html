Carlton Roosevelt XIII (2494-2545) was an American Army enlistee, businessman, baker, crab fisherman, and magician.

Early life (2494-2502)[]

Roosevelt was born in San Francisco, California. He was the 74th of 102 children born to Derek Roosevelt and the 31st of 41 born to his second girlfriend Parker Jefferson.

In 2502, Roosevelt graduated from high school.

Military career (2502-2526)[]

In 2502, Roosevelt joined the US Army as a private.

In 2526, Roosevelt retired.

Business, baking, and crab fishing careers (2526-2527)[]

In 2526, Roosevelt inherited Ash Shoe Trends, which he sold at a $55 million loss. He was briefly an apprentice baker for Mr. Mercury, after which he became a greenhorn for Cadet Fisheries.

Magic career (2527-2545)[]

In 2527, Roosevelt became a magician for Rabbit’s Foot World Circus.

In 2545, Roosevelt was murdered by his daughter.

Relationships and children[]

In 2526, Roosevelt seduced five of his co-workers: a fisherman named Marcus Rossi (2465-after 2528), a fisherman named Al Lukes (2478-after 2528), a baker named Heather Collins (2482-after 2528), a fisherman named Louis Stoner (2496-after 2528), and a baker named Jake Rios (2505-after 2528).

From 2526 to 2528, Roosevelt dated a mailwoman named Charlotte Thompson (2488-after 2572). They had two daughters, Venus Roosevelt VIII and Erica (2528-after 2572).
