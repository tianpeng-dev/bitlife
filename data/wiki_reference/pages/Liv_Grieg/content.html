Liv Grieg (4235-4314) was a German bluegrass singer.

Early life (4235-4253)[]

Grieg was born in Stuttgart, Germany. She is the youngest of 14 children born to Liam Grieg and his girlfriend Solveig Lunde.

In 4241, Grieg enrolled at Hartmann Primary School.

In 4243, Grieg emigrated to Brussels, Belgium. She transferred to Dorian Claes Primary School.

In 4247, Grieg enrolled at Alexandre Thys Secondary School.

In 4250, Grieg created her Facebook, Twitch, and YouTube accounts.

Career (4253-4314)[]

In 4253, Grieg graduated from secondary school. She emigrated back to Stuttgart. She signed with Emerald Beats as a country singer. She released her first bluegrass album, Blowgun Fanny.

In 4314, Grieg died of natural causes. Her funeral was held in Stuttgart, but her daughter could not attend as she was in prison.

Relationships and children[]

From 4245 to 4251, Grieg started dating a student named Lukas Petit (4235-after 4254). They had a son, Arne Grieg II (4251-4328).

In 4253, Grieg started dating a flight attendant named Toni Kraus (4234-4312). They have a daughter, Camilla Grieg II.
