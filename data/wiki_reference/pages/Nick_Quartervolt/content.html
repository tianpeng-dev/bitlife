Nicholas Tennyson Quartervolt (April 30, 1930 - May 28, 2022) was an electrician from Columbus, Ohio.

Early life and career[]

Nick was born a Taurus in Columbus to mail carrier Ana Quartervolt (1902 - 1989) and flight attendant Hugh Quartervolt (1874 - 1945). He had two younger brothers, Matt (1931 - 2013) and Alvaro (1932 - 2006). Matt was a jewler for Chuck Jewlery, and Alvaro was an writer for Harvey Press.
