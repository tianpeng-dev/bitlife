Iraq
is a country added to BitLife in June 2019. Its five default cities are the capital, Baghdad, along with Erbil, Fallujah, Mosul, and Tikrit.
Information
[
]
Being an Muslim-based country, female characters in BitLife will wear hijabs when they are adults. This is shared with females in
Iran
,
Afghanistan
,
UAE
, or
Saudi Arabia
. If a female does not originally have one before emigrating, her emoji will be replaced with one that does. If she emigrates to a non-Muslim based country, her hijab may be removed. Female lovers or hook-ups who originate from one of these countries will have her hijab.
