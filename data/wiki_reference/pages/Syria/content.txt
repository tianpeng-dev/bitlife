Information
[
]
Despite being an Islam-based country, female characters in BitLife will not wear hijabs when they are adults, unlike
Afghanistan
,
Iran
,
Iraq
,
UAE
, or
Saudi Arabia
. If a female does not originally have one before emigrating, her emoji will be replaced with one that does. If she emigrates to a non-Islam based country, her hijab may be removed. Female lovers or hook-ups who originate from one of these countries will have her hijab.
Syria, along with
Afghanistan
,
Angola
,
Belize
,
El Salvador
,
Guatemala
,
Guyana
,
Haiti
.
Iraq
,
Pakistan
,
Papua New Guinea
and
Venezuela
, are the 12 countries in the game where
deaths
are influenced by the country a character is from, in this case, characters can die from unexpected terrorist or battlefield attacks. Being killed by an attack under the age of 30 will give your character the
Unlucky
ribbon, making it easier to obtain (though there is a chance that they may end up getting a different ribbon besides
Unlucky
).
Family
members can also die from these as well and it is likely that a parent or parents will die while your character is still very young.
