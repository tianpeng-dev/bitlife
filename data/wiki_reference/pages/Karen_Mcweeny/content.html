Karen Mcweeny (April 6, 2000 - October 12, 2022) was an American woman known for her masquerade as a pickpocket.

Early life[]

Karen was born in Atlanta, Georgia to a single mother named Tiara (born 1962), who had her via an anonymous sperm donation. In 2001, Tiara married Karen's stepfather, Randall Moore (born 1960), who worked as a travel agent. Randall and Tiara also had a daughter of their own (Karen's half-sister) named Ginger, who was born when Karen was one year old. The family also had a Saint Bernard named Draco (died 2006), who was named after Draco Malfoy from the Harry Potter franchise.
