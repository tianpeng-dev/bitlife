Chuck Taylor
(December 8th, 1988 - April 17th, 2023) was a corrupt
American
-
Mexican
police officer of the Cabo San Lucas Police Department. He was most infamously known for orchestrating the murders of several co-workers and his supervisor, providing security and immunity for various drug cartels and other criminal organizations, running an international sex trafficking ring, and killing multiple security guards during a massive prison riot. His reign of terror ended when he was shot in the head and killed in a standoff with fellow officers.
