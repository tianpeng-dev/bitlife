Psychiatrist
Launched on iOS:
February 17, 2019
Launched on Android:
March 1, 2020
The
psychiatrist
is a type of
doctor
that can be found in the Doctor section of the
activities
, who is specialized in curing mental ailments. When selecting this doctor, the player can choose between attending a counselling session or getting a shock therapy. After getting a shock therapy, a bar inside of a pop-up will display on the screen showing how much pain it caused.
