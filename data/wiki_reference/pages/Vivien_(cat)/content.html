Vivien (2007-2025) was an Slovakian tabby cat who lived a fascinating life in Bratislava. She made an daring move and left her homeland to Denmark, settling in Aarhus

Biography[]

Vivien's story begins when she was born in 2007 in the kitchen sink in her mother's owner's home, her owner Evzen Cibula (b. 1981) an former jeweler.

In 2008. Evzen adopted an unique Persian cat named Dylan. Vivien loved Dylan and played with Dylan

In 3 months. She went into heat to release pheromones. She released many pheromones as she could. And mated with Dylan.

In middle of 2009. Evzen adopted a boy named Leos (b. 1999). She welcomed Leos to his residence.

In 2010, She went into heat again and mated with Dylan. 3 months later. Vivien gave birth to litter of 2 kittens named Alexej and Jacko fathered by Dylan. But an depressing news happened. In weeks. Vivien's kitten Alexej died shortly after birth. Vivien even mourned Alexej.

In middle of 2011, She gave birth again and Even took him in the car ride to the vet to get Vivien spayed! Vivien successfully escaped from Veterinary clinic and started an special life on Marcin Estate blvd.

In middle of 2012. She wandered to Cibula Residence where she was greeted by the owner Ezven. She nuzzled Ezvan and Ezvan took her home!

In 2023. No matter what Vivien was infected with rabies. Which is a untreatable.

In 2024. Vivien experienced abdominal thrist. Which she didn't drank water. So Ezven took her to vet where she was to euthanized. But she managed to escape and resumed her life in Marcin Estate Blvd. She took a swim in sewer and reached the port to the cargo ship, Jadro (The Nucleus) she found a hiding spot in the cargo ship so no one could get her caught. Vivien hid in the pack of pillows. In tommorow. Vivien woke up and saw that she ended up in Denmark, Aarhus. So she sneaked to find the exit and Started a new life on Lumpy Sack Lane.

In 2025. Her heart began to weaken and perished to rabies.
