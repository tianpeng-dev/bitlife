Willy Roosevelt (3026-after 3094) was an American-German soccer player and hairdresser.

Early life (3026-3048)[]

Roosevelt was born in Los Angeles, California. He was the youngest of six children born to Carlton Roosevelt XXIV and his ex-girlfriend Viviane Dancer.

In 3044, Roosevelt enrolled at UCLA. He majored in political science, was a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi, and played soccer for the Bruins.

In 3048, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science.

Sports career (3048-3057)[]

In 3048, Roosevelt emigrated to Dortmund, Germany. He joined Dortmund as a defender.

In 3057, Roosevelt was released by Dortmund.

Hairdressing career (3058-3094)[]

In 3058, Roosevelt became an apprentice hairdresser for Legacy Salon Dortmund.

In 3060, Roosevelt was promoted to hairdresser.

Roosevelt was alive in 3094, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 3044 to 3054, Roosevelt dated a student named Becky Nix (3026-after 3094), who became a waitress in 3049. They had eight children, Venus XII (3045-after 3094), Kiera (3047-after 3094), Kaelynn (3048-3060), Carlton XXVI (3049-after 3094), Elijah (3051-after 3094), Jacob (3052-after 3094), Javier (3053-after 3094), and Kiana Roosevelt.
