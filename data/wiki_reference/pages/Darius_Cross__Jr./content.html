Darius Cross, Jr. (2044-2112) was a Dominican-American soldier.

Early life (2044-2062)[]

Cross was born in Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic. He was the younger of two children born to Renesmee Rizzo and her husband Darius Cross, Sr.

In 2062, Cross graduated from secondary school.

Career (2063-2101)[]

In 2063, Cross enlisted in the Dominican Army as a private.

In 2064, Cross was promoted to private second class.

In 2066, Cross was promoted to private first class.

In 2068, Cross was promoted to corporal.

In 2070, Cross was promoted to sergeant.

In 2072, Cross was promoted to staff sergeant.

In 2074, Cross was promoted to sergeant first class.

In 2076, Cross was promoted to master sergeant.

In 2080, Cross was promoted to sergeant major.

In 2101, Cross retired.

In 2102, Cross emigrated to Dallas, Texas.

Death[]

In 2112, Cross died of a stroke.

Relationship and children[]

In 2102, Cross started dating a student named Miley Richardson (2083-2164), who became a pilot in 2106. They had two children:

  1. Renesmee Cross II (2103-2183), banker
  2. Darius Cross III
