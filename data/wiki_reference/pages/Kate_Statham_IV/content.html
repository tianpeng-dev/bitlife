Kate Statham IV (5296-after 5349) was a British politician.

Early life (5296-5314)[]

Statham was born in Blackpool, England. She was the only child of Leo Statham II and his ex-girlfriend Lilly Brackley.

Career (5314-5334)[]

In 5314, Statham graduated from secondary school. She ran for election to the Lancashire County Council. She beat Willow Johnson and was inaugurated.

In 5319, Statham ran for reelection and beat Souhaila Chahid.

In 5324, Statham ran for Mayor of Blackpool with the Labour Party and beat Conservative candidate Tom Parker.

In 5329, Statham ran for reelection and beat Conservative candidate Lewis Eastbourne.

In 5334, Statham did not run for a third term.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5349, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5314 to 5315, Statham dated an Army enlistee named Simon Henderson (5296-after 5349). They had a daughter, Kate Statham V.
