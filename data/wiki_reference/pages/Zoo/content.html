Animal Hats[]

Some animals may unlock an animal hat with the purchase of an animal. These animal hats are collectible accessories that your character and NPCs may equip under the Main Menu. There are a total of 13 animal caps and obtaining all caps unlocks the Cap-ybara achievement.

Habitats[]

Zoos can be purchased in 3 sizes: small, medium and large. A small zoo can accommodate up to 4 habitats, a medium zoo 6 habitats and a large zoo 8 habitats. Small and medium zoos can be upgraded to medium and large zoos via the Expand Zoo button, though the application may be denied if the zoo is on a government stipend. Each habitat can accommodate up to 20 animals and 1 feature, there are a total of 8 unique habitats.
