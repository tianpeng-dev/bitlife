Darius Cross VIII
(3090-3180) was a French Air Force enlistee, pornographer, and politician who became the Governor of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes.

Early life (3090-3108)
[
]
Cross was born in Lyon, France. He was the only child born to
Perry Cross II
and his girlfriend Aroha Martin. He was named for several relatives named Darius, including his 18x great-uncle.
Cross graduated secondary school in 3108.
Military career (3109-3119)
[
]
In 3109, Cross enlisted in the French Air and Space Force as an
aviateur seconde classe
, equivalent to an airman basic.
In 3110, Cross was promoted to
aviateur premiere classe
, or airman.
In 3112, Cross was promoted to
caporal
, or airman first class.
In 3114, Cross was promoted to
caporal-chef
, or senior airman.
In 3116, Cross was promoted to
sergent
, or staff sergeant.
In 3118, Cross was promoted to
sergent-chef
, or technical sergeant.
In 3119, Cross was honorably discharged.
Porn career (3119-3136)
[
]
In 3119, Cross became a porn actor for Atmosphere Pictures.
In 3136, Cross became famous. He resigned and enrolled at Lyon 3, from which he graduated with a Licence in Criminal Justice in 3140 and an LLM in 3143.
Political career (3143-3173)
[
]
In 3143, Cross ran for election to his arrondissement’s school board and beat Elsa Bertrand.
In 3148, Cross ran for reelection and beat Lorenzo Boyer.
In 3153, Cross ran for Mayor of Lyon as a member of La France Insoumise and beat Socialist candidate Esteban Lavigne.
In 3158, Cross ran for reelection and beat Ecologist candidate Maya Martinez.
In 3163, Cross ran for President of the Regional Council of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes as a Republican and beat LFI candidate Charles Bonaparte.
In 3168, Cross ran for reelection and beat Socialist candidate Valentin Blanc.
In 3173, Cross ran for President of France, but lost to LFI candidate Leo Fontaine.
Death
[
]
In 3180, Cross died of old age.
