Iran
is an original
country
that was in BitLife in September 2018. Its only default city is the capital, Tehran.
Being an Islam-based country, female characters in BitLife will wear hijabs when they are adults. This is shared with females in
Afghanistan
,
Iraq
,
UAE
, or
Saudi Arabia
. If a female does not originally have one before emigrating, her emoji will be replaced with one that does. If she emigrates to a non-Islam based country, her hijab may be removed. Female lovers or hook-ups who originate from one of these countries will have her hijab.
Iran is also one of several countries in which casinos are considered illegal and therefore cannot engage in gambling activities.
Iran is also one of several countries in which being gay or bisexual is considered a crime, and it can lead to an arrest too if same-sex marriages occurs.
