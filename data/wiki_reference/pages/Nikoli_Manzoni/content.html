Nikoli Shakara Manzoni (née Bowe) (born November 17, 1965) is a Bahamian renowned singer-songwriter known for her extraordinary vocal capabilities. Born in Nassau, Bahamas, Manzoni is known for wide vocal range, performances, and distinctive style.
