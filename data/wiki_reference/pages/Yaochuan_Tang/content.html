Life[]

Yaochan Tung was adopted as an Infant by Suyin Cheng (*1956) and Ming-hua Tung (*1953). He was an creative Child and his Parents were Proud of him. He went to Dou Primary School where he was a great and active Student. Sadly his Parents divorced when he was nine, with them sharing custody evenly. Yaocuan continud his Life acting like nothing happened on the outside. He continued being a great Student until the age of 16. In this year his Dog got run over by a car and died, causing Yaochuan to finally break. He was diagnosed with Depression and Anxiety and tried to take his own life. This failed and after that he became better. After finishing School he enlisted in the Navy.

Navy Career[]

Yaochuans navy career was very short. He enlisted in the Navy at age 18 and got employed on the Guangzhou corvette. His Ship was deployed to the waterways in Taiwan in early 2014

Death[]

On the 27th February 2014 the "Guangzhou" hit an active sea mine, The resulting explosionn lead to the sinking of the Ship and the Deazh of 17 chinese Soldiers. It is unclear how Yaochan Tung exactly died but he did in fact die on that day, He was survived by his Mother and Father

Example[]

After his Death the Chinese Government made Yaochan an Example for Patrionism and was decorated with the Meritorious Service Medal post mortuum.
