Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XIX (4609-after 4665) was a Japanese fireman.

Early life (4609-4647)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. He was the only child born to Eberhard Werner XVIII and his ex-girlfriend Essi Hirvonen.

In 4627, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4647-4665)[]

In 4647, Werner became a probationary firefighter for the Tokyo Fire Department.

In 4649, Werner was promoted to firefighter.

In 4651, Werner was promoted to fire equipment operator.

In 4653, Werner was promoted to trooper.

In 4655, Werner was promoted to captain.

In 4657, Werner was promoted to battalion chief.

In 4659, Werner was promoted to assistant chief.

In 4661, Werner was promoted to fire chief.

Werner was alive in 4665, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 4627 to 4628, Werner dated a student named Aiya Taoka (4609-after 4665). They had two children, Eberhard Werner XX and Otello Werner III (4628-after 4665).
