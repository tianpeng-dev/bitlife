Early life
[
]
Bennett was born in Newcastle, Australia. She attended public school at Quantum Primary School and Macquarie Secondary School, but dropped out in 2024. Her parents were afro-punk singer
Lewis Bennett
(May 31, 1990-2024) and banker Chelsea Wade (1990-after 2024). She had a younger sister named Holly (2017-after 2024).
Pet
[
]
In 2017, Bennett received a Burmese python named James (2000-2018) from her father. James died after being run over by a car.
Relationship and child
[
]
Bennett dated a Georgian-Australian student named Koba Grigalashvili (2007-after 2046) from 2018 until her death. They had a son named Gavin Grigalashvili, formerly named
Georgia Grigalashvili
(b. 2024).
Murder of Lewis Bennett
[
]
In 2024, Julia Bennett murdered her father with a set of plastic vampire teeth. She was sentenced to 19 years in prison, but escaped shortly thereafter. On July 18, she attempted to murder her younger sister Holly in the same way, but was killed in the attempt.
