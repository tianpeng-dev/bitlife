Age 0 (1898)[]

Blair Rogers (1898-1967) was born on 9 January, 1898, in Washington, United States.

His father was James Rogers, then 32 years old, (1862-?) and was factory owner.

His mother was Maria Rogers, then 20 years old, (1878-?) and was lead cashier at an unknown supermarket.
