Leo Statham VL (7032-April 6, 7046) was an Aruban student.

Early life (7032-present)[]

Statham was born in Oranjestad, Aruba. He is the only child of Joseph Statham and his second girlfriend Kavya Lakshmi.

In 7038, Statham enrolled at Little Boekhout Primary School. He called his principal, Luz Kock, a nimrod. He received a warning.

In 7045, Statham enrolled at Oranjestad Secondary School. He skipped school to go to a magic show.

On April 6, 7046, Statham tried to steal a Smart Fortwo. The owner of the car shot him with an assault rifle. He died as a result, ending the Barbosa Legacy.

Relationship[]

In 7042, Statham started dating a student named Linda Eman (7032-after 7046).
