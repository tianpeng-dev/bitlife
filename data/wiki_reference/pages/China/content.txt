China
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Due in Real Life China is one-party country, that means characters that live in China cannot work in Political Offices. Its default cities are the capital, Beijing, along with Guangzhou, Shanghai, Shenzen, and Wuhan. Social Media can not be used in the country.
