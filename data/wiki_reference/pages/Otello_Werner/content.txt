Otello Werner
(4653-after 4695) was a Japanese policy analyst.
Early life (4653-4677)
[
]
Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. He is the youngest of three children born to
Eberhard Werner VI
and the younger of two born to his third girlfriend, Yuzuki Nakanishi.
In 4659, Werner enrolled at St. Tsunade’s Elementary School.
In 4663, Werner befriended his enemy, Neji Fujita (4653-after 4676). They became best friends.
In 4665, Werner enrolled at Nobunaga Lower Secondary School. He joined the volleyball team. He went to the gym 10 times.
In 4668, Werner enrolled at Marathon Preparatory School. He joined the volleyball team.
In 4671, Werner enrolled at the University of Tokyo on an athletic scholarship. He joined the volleyball team. He went on vacation to São Paulo, Brazil.
In 4674, Werner became the captain of the volleyball team.
In 4675, Werner graduated from university with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science.
Career (4677-4695)
[
]
In 4677, Werner became a junior policy analyst for the City of Tokyo.
In 4680, Werner was promoted to policy analyst.
In 4683, Werner was promoted to senior policy analyst.
Werner was alive in 4695, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 4663 to 4670, Werner dated a student named Aika Tanaka (4653-after 4695). They had a son, Eberhard Werner VIII (4670-after 4695).
From 4675 to 4676, Werner and Tanaka dated again. They had a daughter,
Greta Werner V
.
