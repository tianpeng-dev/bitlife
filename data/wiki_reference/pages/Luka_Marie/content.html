Luka Marie (2863-after 2919) was a French-British rugby player and marriage counselor.

Early life (2863-2885)[]

Marie was born in Lyon, France. His parents were Esteban Marie and his ex-girlfriend Anna Bardot. He has a younger half sister named Chelsea.

In 2881, Marie enrolled at Les Chartreux. He was on the rugby team. He graduated in 2885 with a Licentiate in Psychology.

Sports career (2885-2900)[]

In 2885, Marie emigrated to Liverpool, England and joined the Wigan Brawlers as a full back.

In 2886, Marie was released by the Brawlers and joined the Castleford Tonys. He won the Most Improved Player Award while on the team.

In 2890, Marie was released from the Tonys and joined the Huddersfield Gnats. He won six championships, Most Improved Player, and two Man of Steel Awards while on the team.

In 2900, Marie retired.

Marriage counseling career (2901-2919)[]

In 2901, Marie became a marriage counselor for Back Door Health Services, the same company his father had worked for.

Marie was alive in 2919, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2881 to 2882, Marie dated a Kuwaiti-French student named Baligha al-Hajri (2863-after 2919), who later became a policy analyst. They had a son, Ali Marie.
