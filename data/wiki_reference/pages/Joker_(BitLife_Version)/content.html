Jack Napier (August 26; 1975 - October 12, 2022) known by his stage name Joker, was an American comedian, clown and humorous vigilante.

Background[]

Jack was born in Gotham to Megan (born 1950) and Jude Napier (born 1951). His mother was a housewife, while his father was a jeweler. He had two younger sisters, Genesis (1979 - 1989) and Tara (born 1980), and a younger brother named Elijah (born 1984). Genesis died at age 10 from measles. Tara is an accountant, and Elijah is a restaurateur at Chez Pristine.
