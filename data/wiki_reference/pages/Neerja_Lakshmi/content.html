Neerja Lakshmi (March 15, 1986 - May 15, 2024) was an Indian wedding planner.

Early life[]

Neerja was born in Srinagar, India to insurance agent Anushree Gayakvad (1948 - 2020) and jeweler Sachin Laksmi (born 1950). She had two brothers, Ansh (born 1984), a writer, and Rahul (born 1988), a restauranter. She also had a pet Great Pyrenees named Dixie (1985 - 1996).

Career[]

After graduating from secondary school, Laksmi found a job as a wedding planner for Rajavade Ideas in 2004. The timing of her employment was fitting, because she herself had recently gotten married, at the age of 18. Laksmi worked very hard, and was eventually promoted to the senior level of her job in 2010 at the age of 24.

Personal life[]

At fourteen years old, Lakshmi started going out with a childhood friend named Pranac Gayakvad, who would go on to become an engineer for Soprano Consulting. They married at age eighteen in 2004. They had a daughter named Lasya (born 2006) and a son named Madhavaditya (born 2008).

Death[]

Lakshmi suffered a massive PCP overdose on May 15, 2024, exactly two months after her 38th birthday. She was rushed to the hospital, but was pronounced dead shortly after. Laksmi is survived by her father, husband, two children, and two brothers.
