Greta Werner VIII (4965-after 5041) was a Canadian-Mexican forensic scientist.

Early life (4965-4987)[]

Werner was born in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. She is the sixth of 19 children born to Greta Werner VII and the fifth of 17 born to her ex-husband Eli Lefebvre.

In 4966, Werner emigrated to Guadalajara, Mexico.

In 4977, Werner enrolled at Angel Molina Middle School.

In 4980, Werner enrolled at Humberto Fernandez Secondary School.

In 4983, Werner enrolled at the University of Guadalajara. She majored in criminal justice.

Career (4987-5028)[]

In 4987, Werner graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice. She became a crime scene technician for the Guadalajara Police Department.

In 4993, Werner was promoted to forensic investigator.

In 5028, Werner retired.

Werner was alive in 5041, but her death date is unknown.

Marriage, relationships, and children[]

From 4976 to 4982, Werner dated a student named Esteban Ramos (4965-after 5028). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XXVIII (4982-4997).

In 4993, Werner had one night stands with five unemployed men named Joel Aguilar (4975-after 5028), Oscar Chavez (4975-5028), Pancho de la Cruz (4975-after 5028), Pepe Hernandez (4975-after 5028), and Andres Soto (4975-after 5028).
