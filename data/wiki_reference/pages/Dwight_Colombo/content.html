Dwight Badcock (born 1990), also known as Dwight Colombo is an American United States Air Force officer and accountant. He is the eldest child of volleyball player-turned businesswoman Aretha Colombo.

Early life and education[]

Dwight was born in Pheonix, Arizona to Aretha Colombo and her husband, Jasper Badcock. His father is a librarian. He has two younger brothers, Andrew (born 1992), a reporter for Dominion Broadcasting Service, and Ellie (born 1994), an exotic dancer at The Axis Club.

Military service and accounting[]

After graduating UoPX in 2013, Colombo joined the Air Force as an officer, starting as a second lieutenant.
