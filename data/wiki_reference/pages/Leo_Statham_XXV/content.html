Leo Statham XXV (5843-after 5900) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5843-5868)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the only child of Leo Statham XXIV and his ex-girlfriend Alison Dewey.

In 5861, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5865, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5868-5883)[]

In 5868, Statham graduated with a Doctorate in Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Charles McGuiness.

In 5873, Statham ran for reelection and beat Bridget Moore.

In 5878, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Abigail Owen.

In 5883, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5900, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 5861 to 5863, Statham dated a student named Amelia Thomas (5843-after 5900). They had two children, Kate XXI (5862-after 5900) and Leo Statham XXVI.
