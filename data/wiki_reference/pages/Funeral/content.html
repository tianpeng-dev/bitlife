Funeral

Last Updated on iOS:

November 29, 2019

A Funeral is a feature in BitLife if the player dies, whoever attended the funeral will be listed on their gravestone. If the relationships are low than it will not bother to attend, sometimes spending time elsewhere instead. Characters may choose whether to attend their family and loved ones' funerals and how to plan it and rest the remains if they were in charge. Sometimes the deceased will have a wish on how they would like to rest, your character may or may not choose to do so. They may bury it, cremate it, taxidermise it, or donate it to science. If cremated, the ashes may be kept in an urn, buried, sprinkled somewhere special, or thrown away. On iOS, characters from a different country may travel to the funeral but will be unable to on Android. If your character is in prison, they will be unable to attend but may still get inheritance.
