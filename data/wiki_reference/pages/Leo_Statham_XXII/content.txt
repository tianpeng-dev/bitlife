Early life (5781-5799)
[
]
Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He is the youngest of three children born to
Leo Statham XXI
and his ex-girlfriend Luciana Munoz.
In 5799, Statham graduated from secondary school.
Career (5800-5822)
[
]
In 5800, Statham ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Noah McCarty.
In 5805, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.
In 5809, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.
In 5812, Statham ran for a second term and beat Abbie Morgan.
In 5817, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Green Party candidate Kieran Yeats.
In 5822, Statham did not run for reelection.
Later life
[
]
Statham was alive in 5842, but his death date is unknown.
