Kaelynn Cross II (2332-after 2401) was an American-Swedish policewoman, bill collector and social media star.

Early life (2332-2350)[]

Cross was born in Chattanooga, Tennessee. She was the youngest of nine children born to Perry Cross I and his girlfriend Mariam Gogoladze.

In 2345, Cross signed up for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, X, and YouTube.

Cross attended Cash Money High School and was the president of the drama club before graduating in 2350.

Police and social media careers (2350-2386)[]

In 2350, Cross was briefly a patrolman for Chattanooga Police Department. She embezzled $432,000 from her work. She was tried for embezzlement, but pled not guilty and was acquitted. After the trial, she embezzled another $568,000 and resigned. She signed up for OnlyFans and started a finance podcast called Retiring Rich.

In 2386, Cross became a social media star. She emigrated to Malmö, Sweden.

Bill collecting career (2387-2397)[]

In 2387, Cross became a bill collector for Berggren Ideas.

In 2397, Cross retired.

Later life[]

Cross was alive in 2401, but her death date is unknown.
