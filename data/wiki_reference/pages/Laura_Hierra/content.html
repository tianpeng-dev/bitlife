Laura Hierra (3383-3418) was a Mexican-American trucker.

Early life (3383-3401)[]

Hierra was born in Mazatlán, Mexico. She was the only child of Arleen Hanks and her boyfriend, Carlos Hierra.

In 3388, Hierra emigrated to Taipei, Taiwan with her parents.

In 3389, Hierra enrolled at Nourish Elementary School.

In 3395, Hierra enrolled at Kayak Junior High School.

In 3398, Hierra enrolled at Yi-hsuan Hu Senior High School.

In 3401, Hierra graduated from high school. She emigrated to Los Angeles, California.

Career (3403-3418)[]

In 3403, Hierra became an apprentice trucker for Aperture Trucking Company.

In 3405, Hierra was promoted to trucker.

In 3418, Hierra was murdered by her son with a set of plastic vampire teeth.

Relationship and child[]

In 3401, Hierra started dating a Navy enlistee named Nathan McWeeny, Sr. (3379-3418). They had a son:
