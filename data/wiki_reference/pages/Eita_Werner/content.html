Eita Werner (né Yoshida, 4732-after 4788) was a Japanese grocer.

Early life (4732-4762)[]

Werner was born Eita Yoshida in Tokyo, Japan. In 4735, he was adopted by Eberhard Werner X, formerly known as Greta Werner VI.

In 4744, Werner enrolled at Nobunaga Lower Secondary School.

In 4747, Werner enrolled at St. Daisuke’s Upper Secondary School.

In 4750, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4762-4788)[]

In 4762, Werner became an apprentice grocer for Wanderlust Grocery.

In 4764, Werner was promoted to grocer.

Werner was alive in 4788, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 4743 to 4749, Werner dated a student named Aratani Onoda (4732-4760). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner VII (4749-after 4788).

From 4750 to 4751, Werner and Onoda dated again. They had a son, Eberhard Werner XI.
