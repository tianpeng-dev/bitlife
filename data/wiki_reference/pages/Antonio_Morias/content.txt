Antonio Morias
(born 1997) is an American enlisted sailor in the United States Coast Guard. He is the middle son of Coast Guard officer and flute player
Alexandra Morias
.
Early life
[
]
Antonio was born in 1997 to Alexandra Morias and her husband, Sonny Burton, a truck driver.  The middle child of the family, his older brother is Moe (born 1988), and his younger brother is Buddy (born 2004).
Coast Guard
[
]
Inspired by his mother's service, Morias enlisted in the Coast Guard between 2015 and 2016 after graduating from high school.
