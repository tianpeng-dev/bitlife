Jerry Marshall Skelton (born January 6, 1958) is a former BitLife Football League quarterback who primarily played for the San Francisco Prospectors. He is regarded as one of the best players of all time.
Early life
[
]
Skelton was born on January 6, 1958 in Boulder, Colorado to parents Tara and Jerry Sr. He also had a brother David and a poodle Mocha.
At age 2, his brother was diagnosed with epilepsy. He started elementary school aged 5 at Little Assvogel Elementary School, a private school. The same year he got diagnosed with tonsillitis. When he was 7 years old, his father Jerry Sr. passed away in his sleep. Skelton attended the funeral. A year later, he was diagnosed with depression. 4 years later, he was diagnosed with anxiety, too.
At age 14, his poodle Mocha died of old age. He also started high school. And he got with a girl named Aiesha Cyrus. After graduating high school, he received an athletic scholarship for football to the Liberty Flames. He was expelled in his sophomore year for disrespecting staff.
Career
[
]
Skelton signed a 2 year, $1.1M contract to play for the San Francisco Prospectors. In his rookie season, the team went 9-7 snd he won ROTY with 26 passing touchdowns and 9 rushing touchdowns. He also threw for 3,728 yards and ran for another 1,180. In season 2, they went 10-6. Skelton threw for 27 touchdowns, 3,828 passing yards, 12 rushing touchdowns and 1,258 rushing yards. He was a nominee for OPOY. The next season, the Prospectors finished 14-2 and finished 3rd. Skelton threw for 3,824 yards and 30 touchdowns, and rushed for 1,398 yards and 13 touchdowns. He won Most Improved Player for his performances. Season 4 saw his best yet. Skelton threw 37 touchdowns and 4,212 yards, and rushed for 1,304 yards and 13 touchdowns. He won Most Improved Player for the second time. In season 5, the team finished 12-4. Skelton threw for 37 touchdowns and 4,245 yards, and ran for 1,478 yards and 18 touchdowns. Yet again he won Most Improved Player.
Personal life
[
]
Skelton met his ex-wife Aiesha Marconi (born Cyrus) in 9th grade during chemistry class. In 1982, Marconi cheated on him with another man and got pregnant. She gave birth to a daughter named Valeria. They broke up 1 year later with Skelton getting custody.
