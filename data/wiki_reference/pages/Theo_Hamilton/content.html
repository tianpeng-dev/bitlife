Lord Theo Hamilton, Sr., Viscount of Crumpet (2231-14 June 2352), formerly Duke of Mercury, was a British nobleman.

Early life (2231-2249)[]

Theo was born in Southampton, England. He was the youngest of three children born to Luke Bell and his first wife Natasha Thigpen.

In 2235, Bell married Mercy, Duchess of Ladywell, making Theo the youngest of her four children.

In 2236, Theo enrolled at Little Wallace Primary School.

In 2242, Theo enrolled at Webb Secondary School.

In 2249, Theo graduated from secondary school.

Duke of Mercury (2249-2329)[]

In 2249, Theo married Her Grace Sienna, Duchess of Mercury (b. 2231), becoming Duke of Mercury jure uxoris. They divorced in 2329, making Theo no longer Duke. They had a daughter:

  1. Tilda Hamilton I, Marchioness of Guilford (2250-2329)

Viscount of Crumpet (2329-2252)[]

In 2329, Theo married Lady Bella Nottingham, Viscountess of Crumpet (2311-2394), becoming Viscount of Crumpet jure uxoris. They had a son:

  1. Theo Hamilton, Jr., Baron of Warwick

On 14 June 2252, Theo died of old age.
