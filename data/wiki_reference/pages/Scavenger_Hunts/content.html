Scavenger Hunts

Launched on iOS:

April, 2024

Launched on Android:

April, 2024

Scavenger Hunts are a series of challenges a player can complete with their characters. These tasks generally revolve around seasonal events and players will be required to unravel vague clues to successfully complete the hunt. Once these challenges are completed, players will be rewarded with their choice of Accessories. As of August 2025, there have been 10 scavenger hunts.

🐇 Easter Egg Hunt (April 1st - April 15th)[]

Name Description Solution Method
30-Day Egg Sober up Go to rehab Get addicted to something (preferably alcohol), then to Activities > Rehab > Rehab Centre, choose to go to a normal or luxorious rehab (luxorious has a better chance of recovery but is more expensive)

Black Widow Egg Matricide Murder a spouse Go to Activities > Crime > Murder, and choose your spouse as your victim. Killing via drive-by is the safest, but requires you to own/loan a car, as well as still having the risk of failing/being caught by the cops.
Blackbeard Egg Theft Pirate a porch Go to Activities > Crime > Porch Pirate, and choose a random house to steal a package out of. There is a chance for the homeowner/bystander to catch you and either call the cops or attack you (which could even result in your character's death).
Blowhard Egg Blow Take a didgeridoo lesson Go to Activities > Mind & Body > Instruments, and pay to take didgeridoo lessons. It is recommended to be under 18 so your parents can pay for your didgeridoo lesson, but your parents can refuse.
Bolt Egg Run away Run from the cops Commit some sort of crime, then choose the "Run for it" option when caught by police. Works best when the police has low professionalism and you have high health and smarts.
Bookworm Egg Read me Read a book Go to Activities > Mind & Body > Book, and choose a book. Tap through every single page to finish it. It is recommended to be under 12 as the books you'll read by then is much shorter, and so require less taps.
CatLife Egg Meow Adopt a cat Go to Activities > Pets > Animal Shelter, and adopt a cat. It is recommended to be under 18 so you don't have to pay the price, but your parents can refuse if they are poor or already have pets.
Deviled Egg Backstab someone Murder a friend Go to Activities > Crime > Murder, and choose a friend of yours as your victim. Killing via drive-by is the safest, but requires you to own/loan a car, as well as still having the risk of failing/being caught by the cops.
Family Jewel Egg Play with your mom's old stuff Play with an heirloom Get an heirloom first by clicking on the heirloom option (refreshes every 14 hours). Then, go to Assets > Said Heirloom, and choose to play with it.
Flaming Egg Better than Doritos Go on the Hot Cheeto Diet Go to Activities > Mind & Body > Diet, and choose the Hot Cheeto diet. If there isn't an option for it, close and reopen the Diet tab until the option shows up.
Freshly-Hatched Egg Fertile Myrtle Have a baby Make love with someone the opposite gender for you, or go to Activities > Fertility, and choose to get a surrogate, get IVF, get artificial insemination.
Golden Egg You're a star Become famous Best ways to be famous is to be an actor, musician, or professional athlete from the job packs. You can also be a writer, a DJ, etc. to become famous, but it is harder to do and takes a lot of time.
Hoedown Egg Cool ranch Purchase an equestrian ranch Have at least 5-10 million dollars in your bank account and purchase an equestrian ranch from the shopping tab. Better if you have no spouse that can potentially disagree with your purchase.
Lucky Egg 21 Win at the casino Go to Activities > Gamble, and then choose a random casino to gamble in. Obviously, winning at the casino is based on luck, so it make take a few tries to win.
Phantom Egg Spook begone Exorcise a ghost Work as an Exorcist (requires you to not have any love interests before applying). Max out your work performance by choosing to work harder every year. Purchase/own a haunted property (preferably with low hauntedness so you only need to exorcise one ghost) and try to exorcise the ghost(s) inside.
Rotten Egg Grow old Live to 100 To have a better chance of living to 100, go on the Mediterrainean diet in the Diet tab, as well as keeping your happiness high by spending time with friends. Try to get rid of any diseases and addictions you get throughout your life.
Shawshank Egg Straight outta solitary Escape from supermax prison Commit murder and go to prison. Then, try to escape prison by clicking the Escape tab and solve the minigame puzzle. You can use online tutorials for solutions, or have the rare chance to watch an ad to pose as a guard and escape scot-free.
Thicc Egg Like & Subcribe Collaborate with someone on OF Open an OnlyFans account. You will randomly get collaboration offers as you age up, but you are more likely to get collabs if you post often and have a lot of followers.
XXX Egg With legs wide open Become an adult film star Work as an Porn Actor. It is necessary to have at least 80% looks to be hired.
