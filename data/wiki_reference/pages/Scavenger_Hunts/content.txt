Launched on iOS:

Launched on Android:

Scavenger Hunts
are a series of challenges a player can complete with their characters. These tasks generally revolve around seasonal events and players will be required to unravel vague clues to successfully complete the hunt. Once these challenges are completed, players will be rewarded with their choice of
Accessories
. As of August 2025, there have been 10 scavenger hunts.
