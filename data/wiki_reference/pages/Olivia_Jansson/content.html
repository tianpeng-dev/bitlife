Olivia Jansson (4094-after 4132) was a Swedish politician and insurance agent.

Early life (4094-4112)[]

Jansson was born in Malmö, Sweden. She was the younger of two children born to Ingrid Ekdahl and her second boyfriend Casper Jansson.

In 4101, Jansson enrolled at Nutti Primary School.

In 4110, Jansson enrolled at Illumina Preparatory School. She graduated in 4112.

Political career (4112-4116)[]

In 4112, Jansson ran for election to the Malmö School Board. She beat Sefu Derresse and was inaugurated.

In 4116, Jansson did not run for reelection.

Insurance career (4117-4132)[]

In 4117, Jansson became a junior insurance agent for Wanderlust Insurance.

In 4120, Jansson was promoted to insurance agent.

In 4123, Jansson was promoted to senior insurance agent.

Jansson was alive in 4132, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

In 4104, Jansson started dating a student named Mohamed Larsson (4094-after 4132). They have two sons, Hugo (4112-4118) and Henrik Larsson.
