Early life (1999-2017)[]

Sophie Robinson was born in Adelaide, South Australia in 1999. Her father was Xavier “Cheeks” Robinson.

In 2017, she graduated high school.

Grocer (first position, 2018-2022)[]

In 2018, Robinson became an apprentice grocer at Robinson Foods.

In 2020, she was promoted to grocer.

Writer (2023-2024)[]

In 2023, Robinson resigned from her job as a grocer and became a writer at Bell Press.

She dated Noah Jones (2005-2042) from 2023 to 2024. In 2024, they had a son named Chase.

Acting (2025-2033)[]

In 2025, Robinson resigned from her job as a writer and started playing Linda Newell on Filthy John.

In 2026, she became famous.

In 2029, she hired Harrison Clyde as her talent agent.

In 2032, she started dating Noah Jones again.

Grocer (second position, 2034-2042)[]

In 2034, after Filthy John ended, she became an apprentice grocer at Lifeblood Grocery.

In 2036, she was promoted to grocer.

In 2042, she was murdered.
