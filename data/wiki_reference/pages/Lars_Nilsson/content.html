Count Lars Nilsson (b. October 1, 1860 — d. November 11, 1929) was a Swedish count, born into the family of the Marquises of Nynäs Nils and Greta. He also has an older sister, Alice (the Countess of Meatballs), and a calico cat, Isabella. He was a Libra.

The Count of Rosenqvist & Möllnlücke, inheritor of the Marquises of Nynäs.

Early Life[]

Lars was born in Gothenburg, Sweden, with a net worth of $473,303 in 1860. A year after birth, his father was diagnosed with bowel polyps. At the age of two, the young count came to the conclusion that the world was obsessed with him. That same year, his older sister started primary school.
Lars also had a special talent for modeling, which his mother hinted at when he was 4 years old, saying that she would be happy if Lars grew up to become a model.
At the age of 5, he became friends with Vassilis Kafalas, a native of Greece. Two years later, the count entered the private Frontal Primary School.

Childhood[]

At school, Lars had no problems with grades or popularity, but he also began studying harder.

When he was 10 years old, his cat Isabella died.
At the age of 11, the count, at the suggestion of his parents, began taking trombone lessons and received a silver trombone as a gift.
A year later, his older sister started secondary school at age 16.
Lars realized his bisexuality.
Then, at the age of 13, Lars flirted with his Swedish teacher, Mr. Magnusson, nodding his head positively at everything he said.
A year later, he confessed his love to Vassilis, but was refused.
When Lars was 15 years old, his father passed away at the age of 79.
Then he enrolled in Harmony Secondary School of private type, by the way suffering from depression.
He tried to pass driving test but failed it.
In school he joined Amnesty International and the brainy kids clique. At age 17 he was cured of depression and his life began to sparkle with new colors.
At age 18 Swedish count graduated from secondary school and settled in royal life.

Government Tendency[]

Lars Nilsson, being a count, does not have the right to support or deny any laws, but performs other activities that characterize his government. Nilsson exhibits a liberal form of politics by not judging people for their mistakes or punishing them.
