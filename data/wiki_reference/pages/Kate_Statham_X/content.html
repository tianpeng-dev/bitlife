Kate Statham X (5438-5516) was an Italian politician and trucker.

Early life (5438-5457)[]

Statham was born in Naples, Italy. She was the only child of Kate Statham IX and her ex-boyfriend Herberto Valsalva.

Political career (5457-5477)[]

In 5457, Statham graduated from secondary school. She ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Board and beat Nathan Toscano.

In 5462, Statham ran for reelection and beat Dino Polo.

In 5467, Statham ran for a third term and beat Luisa Palermo.

In 5472, Statham ran for Metropolitan Mayor of Naples as a Democrat and beat 5 Star candidate Giambattista Fanucci.

In 5477, Statham did not run for reelection.

Trucking career (5478-5504)[]

In 5478, Statham became an apprentice trucker for Oracle Trucking.

In 5480, Statham was promoted to trucker.

In 5504, Statham retired.

Death and funeral[]

In 5516, Statham died of natural causes. Her daughter planned her funeral and arranged for her to be cremated per her wishes.

Relationship and child[]

From 5457 to 5458, Statham dated a student named Claudio Cutolo (4539-after 5516). They had a daughter, Kate Statham XI.
