Jaqueline Anna Knower
(June 8, 1992 - Jun 6, 2026), later known as
Jackie O'Neil
, was an American fast-food worker who was notorious for her gluttonous lifestyle.

Early and personal life
[
]
Jackie was born in Columbus, Ohio to receptionist Anna Knower (1958 - 2026) and grocer Latrell Knower (born 1957). Her younger brother, Bill (born 1997), is a wedding planner.
Grocer career
[
]
After finishing high school, Knower began her job search in the food industry. She soon settled on an apprenticeship at Pratt foods so she could work alongside her father.
