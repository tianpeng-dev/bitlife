Maxwell Richard Affleck, Duke of Sawrey (March 18 1954 - December 23 2024) was an American Heavy Metal, Folk and Country Musician, British Nobleman and father of famous musician Alphonso Harrison-Bale. Known professionally as "Max Flicker", "Ol' Max", "Max Keys" and "Uncle Maxwell", Affleck was one of the most revolutionary keyboardists of the 20th century, genre switching in the middle of his career from heavy metal to folk/country music.
