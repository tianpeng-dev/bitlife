Isabelle Crook (3090-October 6, 3115) was a British soccer player.

Early life (3090-3112)[]

Crook was born in Cardiff, Wales. Her parents were Jenson Crook and his ex-girlfriend Amelia Banbury-King.

In 3108, Crook enrolled at Cardiff University. She was on the soccer team and graduated in 3112 with a BS in Chemistry.

Career (3112-3115)[]

In 3112, Crook joined Arson Hall FC as a forward.

Death[]

In 3115, a gambling tycoon from Reading offered Crook £35,000 to throw a game. After Crook reported the tycoon, he attacked her with a grenade. She died on October 6.

Relationship and child[]

From 3108 to 3110, Crook dated a Bangladeshi-born student named Debesh Mitu (3090-after 3172). They had a daughter named Jennifer Mitu.
