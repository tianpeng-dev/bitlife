Job

Last Updated on iOS:

November 18, 2019
Since the Part-Time jobs update, characters may have more to do at their jobs. Following the November 2019 Careers update, there are new things that characters can do while at work. There are 6 activities they can try. Some have existed before, while some activities are entirely new at the time of the update.

Jobs[]

Activity Description Possible actions Notes
Co-Workers Interact with your supervisor or other employees Dependent on the actions below. Your character has a "Popularity" bar depending on how well your co-workers like them.
Hours Adjust your hours Raise hours to as high as 70 hour or as low as 38 hours per week. Your character's supervisor could tell them to put in more hours at random times, adjusting the hours for you if you agree. While the hours are adjusted, you may not be able to adjust them yourself until your supervisor resets them.
Human Resources Report someone to HR They may ignore the report, warn or reprimand the co-worker, dock their wages, or fire them. If you make too many complaints, they will reprimand you for wasting their time and launch an investigation into you. Your character may not report without a reason to do so.
Resign Tender your resignation You will leave your job. This activity will become unavailable after you have worked for 20 years or more.
Retire Consider retirement If they are old enough and/or have worked at least 20 years, they may retire and take pension. If they are not old enough and/or have not worked at least 20 years, they will be ineligible to do so and will not retire. Starting a new job will not carry over the years worked. Retiring and starting a new job will void that pension, unless you leave that new job.
Work Harder Put in some extra effort They will work harder, improving performance by a short burst.

Military[]

Activity Description Possible actions Notes
Co-Workers Interact with your commanding officer or other employees Dependent on the actions below. Your character has a "Relationship" bar depending on how well your co-workers like them.
Desert Desert your post You may get busted or could get away with it Warning: This is a crime. Getting away with it in the moment does not guarantee getting away with it for life.
Discharge Request a discharge You may or may not be discharged from your duties. This is the only time a discharge is an honourable discharge.
Report Misconduct File a grievance against someone They may ignore the report, warn or reprimand the co-worker, give extra duties, or dishonourably discharge them. Your character may not report without a reason to do so.
Retire Consider retirement If they have worked at least 20 years, they may retire and take a pension. If they have not worked at least 20 years, they will be ineligible to do so and will not retire. At the age of 62, if they have not chosen to retire sooner, they will retire at this age as it is the mandatory retirement age.
Work Harder Put in some extra effort They will work harder, improving performance by a short burst. The journal may say what rank they need to get to next and that working harder will help them get there.

Co-worker actions[]

Action Description Possible results
Ask Out Ask them out on a date They may accept or reject, with relationship bar adjusting accordingly.
Befriend Befriend them They may accept or reject, with relationship bar adjusting accordingly.
Compliment Compliment them They may return one or not, or insult your character, depending on how well they like you.
Conversation Have a conversation with them. They will have the conversation with you, tell you to leave them alone, or tell you to shut up.
Gift Give them a gift Have them accept the gift, have them refuse the gift and/or be reported to HR
Hook-up/Seduce (Seduce is for bosses only) Have a one-night stand with them Get rejected, rejected and reported, have a hook-up with them
Insult Call them a bad name Be insulted back, attacked, or reported to HR, unless the worker does nothing
Prank Play a trick on them They will do nothing, insult you, attack you, or report you
Promotion (bosses only) Ask your boss for a promotion They will accept or decline
Rumor Start a rumor about them They will do nothing, attack you, start a rumor about you, or report you, depending on whether or not they know you started it.
Spend Time Spend time with the co-worker They will accept or refuse the time
