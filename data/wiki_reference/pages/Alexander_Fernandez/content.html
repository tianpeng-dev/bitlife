Alexander Fernandez (2282-after 2334) was a British Royal Navy veteran and teacher.

Early life[]

Fernandez was born in Sheffield, England. His mother was Luna Fernandez. He graduated from high school in 2300.

Military career[]

In 2300, Fernandez enlisted in the Navy. He reached the rank of petty officer third class before receiving an honorable discharge in 2307.

In 2307, Fernandez enrolled at the University of Sheffield. He graduated in 2311 with a BS in Education.

Teaching career[]

In 2311, Fernandez became a school teacher for the City of Sheffield. He retired in 2331.

Relationships and children[]
