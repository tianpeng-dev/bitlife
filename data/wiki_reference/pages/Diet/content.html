Diets are an option for bitlife, which you can access from mind and body. Some of the diets are beneficial to health, some Increase happiness, and some are harmful!

Note: Going on the harmful "Hot Cheetos" diet can earn you an Achievement.

Increase Health[]

  • Vegan: Raises Health, Lowers happiness, can cause malnutrition
  • Keto: Lowers happiness, raises health
  • Mediterranean: Raises health and happiness
  • Paleo: Raises Health, Lowers happiness
  • South Beach: Raises health
  • Vegetarian: Raises health, can cause malnutrition
  • Weight Watchers: Raises happiness and health
  • Jenny Craig: Raises happiness and health
  • Whole30: Raises health, Lowers happiness

Decrease Health[]

  • Atkins: Lowers health
  • High Calorie: Lowers health, Raises happiness
  • Hot Cheetos: Lowers health
  • Liquid: Lowers health and happiness, can cause malnutrition
  • Tapeworm: Lowers health
