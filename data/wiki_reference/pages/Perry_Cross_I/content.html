Perry Cross I (2307-after 2386) was an Argentinian-American soccer player and receptionist.

Early life (2307-2324)[]

Cross was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina. He was the 91st of 100 children born to Darius Cross V and the eldest of 10 born to his fourth partner, Pilar Ramos.

Cross went to Sparkle Lower Secondary School and was on the soccer team.

Cross went to Santino Herrera Upper Secondary School and was on the soccer team.

Sports career (2324-2326)[]

In 2324, Cross emigrated to Barcelona, Spain. He joined Barcelona as a forward.

In 2326, Cross was released by Barcelona. He emigrated to Chattanooga, Tennessee.

Front desk career (2333-2373)[]

In 2333, Cross became a receptionist for Acoustic Motors.

In 2373, Cross retired.

Later life[]

Cross was alive in 2386, but his death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 2317 to 2323, Cross dated a student named Carmencita Cabrera (2307-after 2332).

In 2323, Cross started dating a Georgian-Argentinian student named Mariam Gogoladze (2307-after 2386), who became a travel agent in 2325. They had nine children:
