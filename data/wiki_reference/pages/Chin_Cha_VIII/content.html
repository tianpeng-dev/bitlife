Chin Cha VIII (3097-3167) was an American grocer and convicted car thief.

Early life (3097-3119)[]

Cha was born in Houston, Texas. He was the youngest of four children born Chin Cha VII and the youngest of three born to his second girlfriend Genevieve Romo.

In 3115, Cha enrolled at the University of Houston.

In 3119, Cha graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Architecture.

First career (3120-3135)[]

In 3120, Cha became an apprentice grocer for Symmetry Foods.

In 3122, Cha was promoted to grocer.

In 3133, Cha went to the movies and went to a Jungkook concert with his friend Andree Murphy.

In 3134, Cha was fired.

Prison sentence (3134-3137)[]

In 3134, Cha was convicted of grand theft auto and running from the police and was sentenced to 3 years in prison. He served his sentence at Synergy Asylum.

In 3137, Cha was released. He burgled a football coach’s house and ran from the police.

Second career (3138-3163)[]

In 3138, Cha became an apprentice grocer for Symmetry Foods.

In 3163, Cha retired.

Death[]

In 3167, Cha died of old age.
