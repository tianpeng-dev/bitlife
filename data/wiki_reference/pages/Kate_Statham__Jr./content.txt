Early life (5099-5123)
[
]
Statham was born in Blackpool, England. She was the younger of two children born to
Kate Statham
and her first boyfriend, Rod Sheringham, Sr.
Statham went to Lancaster University and graduated in 5123 with a Bachelor of Accounting.
Accounting career (5123-5162)
[
]
In 5123, Statham became a junior accountant for Requiem Systems.
In 5162, Statham retired.
Political career (5162-5192)
[
]
In 5162, Statham ran for election to the Lancaster County Council. She beat Rory Wood and served alongside her mother for her first term.
In 5167, Statham ran for reelection and beat Isabelle Glasscock.
In 5172, Statham ran for a third term and beat Katrina Willis.
In 5177, Statham ran for a fourth term and beat Jude Woodbridge.
In 5182, Statham ran for a fifth term and beat Jacob Austen.
In 5187, Statham ran for a sixth term and beat Hugo Hunt-Battle.
In 5192, Statham did not run for a seventh term.
Death
[
]
In 5208, Statham died of a stroke. Her son planned her funeral and donated her body to science.
