The name is Rocky Schlitz. He was born in Chicago, US, on October 5. His father, Grayson Schlitz, is a 33-year-old trucker and his mother, Dixie Khan, is also a 33-year-old, a mail carrier.

Early Life[]

Rocky's early life was plain, and somewhat boring. At the age of six, he got a dog, who consistently acted up but Rocky continued to reward his dog. At 13, he was being bullied at school, but he chose to ignore it. Three years later, he tried to get a car, but failed his only shot at a driving test. At 17, a year later, dog died. The dalmatian died of natural causes.

Work Life (Trooper and Patrolman)[]

At 18, he immediately got a job as a Trooper, fulfilling his long-life dream of becoming a Police Officer. At 22 years old, he reported his one and only friend to the authorities for being a kleptomaniac. It turns out that also his half brother worked with Rocky! It took him 15 years (and one termination later) to be promoted: he is now a Patrolman, and retires at the age of 65.

Personal Life[]

Rocky's father, Grayson, passed away at the age of 70. The cause of death was esophegal cancer. Not too long later, the mother, Dixie, dies while sleeping peacefully. She died at the age of 89. It doesn't get much better, either. His half brother, Luis, died from meningitis. After sixty-six years of being single, Rocky Schlitz now has a girlfriend! He is dating a 59-year-old named Harper. The relationship doesn't last very long though - he dies at the age of 67 by sleeping peacefully. At thetime, he was worth £670,097. His funeral was attended by his girlfriend, Harper. His nephew, Steve, decided to go snowboarding instead.

Overall Life[]

Rocky Schlitz earned the ribbon: 'Mediocre'.
