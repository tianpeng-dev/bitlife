Early life (3173-3195)
[
]
Mitu was born in Cardiff, Wales. Her parents were
Zachary Mitu
and his ex-girlfriend Eva Lawley.
In 3191, Mitu enrolled at Cardiff University. She was on the soccer team before graduating in 3195 with a BS in Psychology.
Sports career (3195-3210)
[
]
In 3195, Mitu joined Brighton, one of the teams
her grandmother
had been on, as a forward.
Psychology career (3211-3248)
[
]
In 3211, Mitu became a school psychologist for the City and County of Cardiff.
Mitu was alive in 3248, but her death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 3191 to 3192, Mitu dated a student named Olly Anderson (3173-after 3248). They had a daughter,
Aurora Anderson
.
