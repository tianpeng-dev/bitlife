Not to be confused with Greta Werner or her other namesakes, or Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner X, formerly known as Greta Werner VI (4714-after 4761) was a Japanese travel agent.

Early life (4714-4744)[]

Werner was born Greta Werner VI in Tokyo, Japan. He was the younger of two children born to Otello Werner II and his ex-girlfriend Fuka Yamada.

In 4718, Werner came out of the closet as transgender and changed his name to Eberhard Werner X.

In 4720, Werner enrolled at Kajuki Kubo Elementary School.

In 4726, Werner enrolled at Sugiyama Lower Secondary School.

Career (4744-4761)[]

In 4744, Werner became a travel associate for Accent Travel Agency.

In 4748, Werner was promoted to tour operator.

In 4752, Werner was promoted to general manager.

In 4756, Werner was promoted to regional manager.

In 4760, Werner was promoted to agency operator.

Werner was alive in 4761, but his death date is unknown.

Child[]

Werner had an adopted son, Eita Werner.
