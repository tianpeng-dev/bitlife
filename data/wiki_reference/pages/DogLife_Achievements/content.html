Achievements are special rewards for completing tasks in DogLife. Achievements can only be unlocked once. They do not have to be completed in one lifetime, unless the achievement says so. Some achievements take hard work while some are easy and other are random chance.

As of December 3, 2021, there are 61 achievements to unlock, divided into 7 different categories.
