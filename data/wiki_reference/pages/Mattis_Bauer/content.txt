Early life (4357-4381)
[
]
Bauer was born in Stuttgart, Germany. He is the second of three children born to
Luna Konig
and her first husband Jannik Bauer.
In 4375, Bauer enrolled at the University of Hohenheim. He graduated in 4379 with a Bachelor of Science in Accounting.
In 4379, Bauer enrolled at Stuttgart Institute of Management and Technology. He graduated in 4381 with a Master of Business Administration.
Career (4381-4422)
[
]
In 4381, Bauer became a marriage counselor for Aristotle Health and Addiction.
In 4422, Bauer retired.
Death
[
]
In 4439, Bauer died of a stroke.
Relationship and child
[
]
On December 31, 4396, Bauer started dating a marriage counselor named Franziska Schwarz (4371-after January 1, 4445). They have a daughter,
Anna Bauer
.
