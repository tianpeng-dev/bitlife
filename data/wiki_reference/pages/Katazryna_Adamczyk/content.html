Early Life[]

Katarzyna Adamczyk was born May 18th, 1991 in Krakow. Her biological mother gave her to adoption. Kornelia Adamczyk adopted Katarzyna, Kornelia is 24-year old Navy enlistee. In 1995 her mother gave birth to a baby boy named Dominik Adamczyk. In 1998 Katarzyna was enrolled in primary school. In 1999 her mother married 18-year old kind man Kamil Sokolowski who became Katarzyna's stepfather. Katarzyna started taking voice lessons in childhood because she wanter to become a singer. In 2004 she started dating very handsome classmate named Leon Zawadzki. She graduated from school in 2009.

Personal Life[]

Katarzyna has been dating Leon Zawadzki since 2004. He was the most popular boy in her class but she was not very popular but she doesn't matter. After graduating from school Katarzyna Adamczyk used Medical School golden diploma in 2009. Also in 2009 she started working in Polish hospital and got a lot of new friends here. But in 2014, when she was 23, she resigned from hospital because she wanted to become a singer. Divorced in 2020 because Leon cheated on her

Career[]

Katarzyna Adamczyk's musical career started in 2014 after she joined The Winks band. They recorded a single named "Fade To Black", but that year Katarzyna was kicked out from a band. In the same year 2014 Katarzyna joined Foo Writers, they recorded a single named "Here We Go Again", but she still was kicked out from band! After that in the same year of 2014 she recorded a single "Pooopy poooop" with her band Steely Can but... she was kicked out from another band! In 2015 she joined The What and recorded a single "Year Ago", but was kicked from another band! In 2016 she joined The Grated Knuckles and recorded singles "Green Fog", and she was kicked out another time! She tried to become solo singer but labels didn't want to sing her a contract. She became a medic again
