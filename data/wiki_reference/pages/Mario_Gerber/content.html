Mario Gerber (October 24, 1898 - November 12, 1979) was the Swiss CEO of Lugano Gems.

Early life[]

Born in October 24 of 1898, he spent most of his childhood in Lugano, Switzerland. At 18, he enrolled at the University of Lugano in 1916, studying economics and later enrolling in business school.

Career[]

Mario Gerber worked as a Database Administrator at Stoney Properties in 1922, leaving the job at 39 years old and starting to work at Mahogany Properties as their AVP (Assistant Vice President) and then their EVP (Executive Vice President) in 1960. He founded Lugano Gems in 1962, where he sold jewelry.

Personal Life[]

Mario Gerber went into a life of lust at 26 years old, having a one night stand with Saskia Arnold. In 1939, he married Karawek Romsee, who gave birth to his son Luca Gerber in the same year.

Death[]

In November 12 1979, Mario suffered a massive heart attack at the age of 81, believed to be caused by stress. He was later buried in the Sanctuary Sacro Cuore di Gesù.
