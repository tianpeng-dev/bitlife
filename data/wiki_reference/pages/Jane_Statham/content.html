Jane Statham (5184-5274) was a British magician and politician.

Early life (5184-5206)[]

Statham was born in Blackpool, England. She was the only child of Charlie Statham and his wife Felicity Darling.

In 5202, Statham enrolled at Lancaster University and majored in psychology.

Magic career (5206-5246)[]

In 5206, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology. She became a magician for Prosperity Brothers Circus.

In 5246, Statham retired.

Political career (5246-5251)[]

In 5246, Statham ran for election to the Lancashire County Council. She beat Graham Woodgate and was inaugurated.

In 5251, Statham did not run for reelection.

Death[]

In 5274, Statham died of old age. Her son attended her funeral and had her cremated per her wishes. Her ashes were sprinkled out of an airplane at 12 kilometers.

Marriage and child[]

In 5209, Statham married an unemployed man named Rufus Woodgate (5180-5251). They had an adopted son named Leo Statham.
