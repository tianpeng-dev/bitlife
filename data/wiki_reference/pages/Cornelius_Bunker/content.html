Cornelius Bunker (1973 - 2004) was a USMC veteran best known for his service in post-9/11 conflicts such as the War in Afghanistan and the U.S. Invasion of Iraq. He was the son of Teddy Bunker.

Early life[]

Cornelius was born in Texas in 1973 to Teddy Bunker and Natalie Davis.

Military service[]

After graduating high school, Bunker enlisted in the Marines.

Dates of rank[]

  • Private (1991/1992-1993)
  • Private First Class (1993-1995)
  • Lance Corporal (1995-1997)
  • Corporal (1997-1999)
  • Sergeant (1999-2001)
  • Staff Sergeant (2001-2003)
  • Gunnery Sergeant (2003-2004)

Personal life and death[]

Bunker was never officially married or known to have any children, though there have been several teenagers throughout the late 2010s and early 2020s that have claimed to be his children. He died in a car accident in 2004 at the age of 31.
