Eberhard Werner XXIII
(4704-after 4760) was a Japanese insurance agent.
Early life (4704-4742)
[
]
Werner was born in Tokyo. He was the only child of
Eberhard Werner XXII
and his ex-girlfriend Maritza Rodriguez.
In 4722, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.
Career (4742-4760)
[
]
In 4742, Werner became a junior insurance agent for Cardinal Insurance Group.
In 4745, Werner was promoted to insurance agent.
In 4748, Werner was promoted to senior insurance agent.
Werner was alive in 4760, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 4722 to 4723, Werner dated a receptionist named Tsubame Hirano (4701-after 4760). They had a daughter,
Jasmin Werner XI
.
