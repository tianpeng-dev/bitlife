Mary Grace Gambino (née: Andrews) (August 9, 1994 - TBA) was an American actress who made her television debut in 2012 in the series "The Trash Crew" which turned out to be a flop and her successful career in films started with the cancellation in 2015 when she tried casting for several series and films and it didn't work out for her then her regular talent agent Garth secured her the role of Paula Rapinoe (supporting character) in the Romantic Comedy film "30 Going On 13" in the same year. Since then she has been gaining fame until she received her first Oscar in 2019 for Best Actress and made career better.

Her best films were "30 Going On 13", and "Pretty Prostitute" directed by John Diaz but produced by other studios, which received Golden Globes in 2016 ("30 Going On 13") and 2020 ("Pretty Prostitute"), and her worst, which was called worse than Gigli by critics, was "Mom Meets My Boyfriends" despite being a box office success, from the same director but produced by Triumph Productions.

Early Life[]

Mary Grace was born on August 9, 1994 (Leo) in Cincinnati, Ohio, to single mother Molly Andrews, a 22-year-old hairdresser who went to a fertility clinic 9 months ago to get pregnant from a donor sperm. She was interested in art from a young age, when she was in kindergarten. When she was one year old, her mother married Steve Richards, a 24-year-old real estate agent, and they went on honeymoon to Manila, Philippines, leaving her and his son Tanner Richards at May Andrews' (Molly's mom's) home.

When she was 3, her stepbrother started primary school, and when she was 4, her mother took her to see a play, there Mary Grace found her destiny in art (but not theatre, but acting).

Elementary School and Youthful Love[]

Mary Grace started elementary school at Little Russell Elementary School at the age of 5, when her stepbrother Tanner later got pneumonia. When she was in first grade, during her first class and introductions, her classmate Darlene Elway came to her and they became friends. Then after playing with her cat Evelyn, she went to the Library with her to do a puzzle. When she was 6, Darlene asked her if she would cover her up so she could tell her mom that she had been with her all morning, even though it wasn't true. After that, she read Dr. Seuss' The Cat in the Hat all pages.

When she was 7, her mother was worried about her daughter's eyesight, so she took her to an optometrist for an eye exam and she passed the test. To be on good terms with the cat, she played with her and gave her a fish-flavored cat treat. When she was in the fourth grade (8 years old), then while she was on the playground, Erica McCain came to her and they became friends. At the age of 9, when her stepfather left a box of donuts (to test her patience) and said they were for her. She came to see that the donuts had carrots in them, since she was a fan of carrots, she didn't care and ate them anyway. Her stepbrother Tanner started middle school, when she was in elementary school, she learned something practical.

When she was in 5th grade (10 years old), then during one of her lunch breaks Noah Gambino came to her, who to prove he was a metalhead, asked her to be his girlfriend, with good grades, she agreed, at first hesitantly, then eagerly went on dates at the end of 5th grade.
