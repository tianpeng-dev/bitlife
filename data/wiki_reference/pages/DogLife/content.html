DogLife, known in the App Store as DogLife: BitLife Dogs is a mobile life simulator for Android and IOS created by Candywriter LLC, released on November 1, 2021 (on Android), and November 3, 2021 (on iOS). It is the first spin-off to BitLife.

On 10 December 2021, the Doglife Challenge was launched on Bitlife. Lasting for five days, the challenge's tasks included downloading the Doglife app and giving birth to 2 litters of puppies within the player's first Doglife character's life.

There are also ribbons in DogLife.

Gameplay[]

Players can play as a street, house dog (pet), pet store dog, or shelter dog. Upon clicking the "New Life" button, players will get a randomly generated dog. If the player chooses to play as a house dog, there may be other pets within their residence.

Unlike BitLife, players always age three months per clicking the "Age" button, instead of a year.

Features[]

  • Household, Street, Shelter, or Pet Store - View your home, humans and other animals.
    • The household is the owner's home.
    • The street is a place for stray dogs.
    • The shelter is like Prison, except you are only released after being adopted. You have a chance of being returned to the shelter due to bad behavior.
    • The pet store is another place where you have the chance of being adopted.
  • Scent Database - Check smell of other animals when you sniff them.
  • Hierarchy - Check other animals sorted by their respect.
  • Activities - Lets players act up, attract attention, escape, play alone, start a riot, practice a trick, and surrender.
  • You have the chance of being euthanized for bad behavior or because of old age.
    • If you manage to kill your owner, you will be seized by animal control and euthanized.

Purchases[]

  • Dog Lover - Unlocks all dog breeds. Costs $2.99 USD.
  • Cat Lover - Unlocks ability to play as a cat along with all cat breeds. Cost $2.99 USD.
  • Top Dog - Dog Lover, Cat lover, customize appearance and attributes, edit humans and other animals, locate lost animals, dark mode, and no ads. Costs $4.99 USD
