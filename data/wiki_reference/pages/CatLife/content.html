BitLife Cats — CatLife (often shortened to CatLife) is a mobile life simulator game created by developer Candywriter LLC on January 3rd, 2022. The game utilizes a text-based format to try and create a accurate simulator of how cats live. It is the second spinoff of the original game after DogLife.
