Grayson Shrub (born 1972) is an American makeup artist. He is the oldest child and only son of the late Henry Shrub, President of the United States, his younger siblings being Julia and Martha.

Early life[]

Grayson was born in 1972 to Henry and Emma Shrub. At the time of his birth, Henry was still serving in the United States Air Force as a first lieutenant, a year before his promotion to captain. Henry had been granted temporary leave so he could attend his son's birth. Grayson's grandparents were Jackie Shrub and Hannah Shrub.

Personal life[]

Shrub married writer Hannah Jenkins (born 1980) in 2003. Henry was initially hesitant about the relationship, as her name reminded him of his late mother, and her job as a writer reminded him of his late father. In the end, however, the Shrubs and Jenkins families managed to get along just fine, and Henry lovingly welcomed Hannah to the family. Grayson and Hannah have reportedly had at least one child between the mid-2000s and early-2010s.
