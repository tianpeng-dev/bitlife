Jasmine Knight (January 29 1946 - August 7 2025) was an Australian baker, thief and murderer.

Early Life

She was born through artificial insemination in Brisbane, Australia. Her mother is Anna Knight who is a real estate agent. She always spent time at the park being lonely and testing out the laws of physics with a swing. At the age of five she started Vivacious Primary School and the year later the cool girls asked her to go see 'The Unbelievables' with them at the theater. At the age of eight she watched explicit YouTube videos sent to her by someone. This turned her live into a mischievous life. Her mother took her to take acting lessons. At the age of nine her eyesight was worsening and she failed an eye test and she had to get glasses. The same year she was up to some mischief. She spat out a skyscraper window with a tattoo artist. At the age of 10 she started taking flute lessons and her mother got her a flute. At the age of 11 she asked out her classmate, Nathan but he rejected her. That year also she also asked another classmate named James Dumpton but he rejected her. At the age of 12 she asked out James Dumpton again and this time he changed his mind. When she was 13 she started going to Heath Murphy Secondary School. At the age of 15 she passed her drivers test on the first try and got her license. Her mother gave her a Kia Optima. At the age of 18 she started history in University.

Adult Life

When she was 21, James proposed to her at a marijuana farm. At the age of 22 she graduated from university and applied to get a job as a history teacher but was rejected. Instead she became an apprentice baker. Also, she gave birth to a girl named Holly Dumpton. At the age of 24 she got promoted to Baker and she married her fiancé; James at a church. At the age of 25 she went to Sublime Casino where she won $1000 from Black Jack. At the age of 28 she almost got divorced when her husband found out that she bought a Bernese mountain dog and they called him Peyton. At the age of 32 she gave birth to a boy named Clive Dumpton. At the age of 35 she had another baby named Lily Dumpton. Three years later her daughter, Lily died in a bus crash and Jasmine was diagnosed with depression from the loss. At the age of 44 she was cured of depression. When she was 45. her daughter, Holly started a job as a Travel Associate. At the age of 46 she became friends with two of her co-workers; Julia Sainsbury and Felix Brown. When she was 49 her mother died after suffering a stroke and inherited $730,000. She also became best friends with her friend, Julia. At the age of 50 she had diabolical thoughts of killing her co-worker; Molly Vega. She hired a hitman named Vincent to kill her and she died. Also she robbed a bank the same year. A year later she hired another hitman named Wolverine to kill her supervisor, Isla Spinks but the hitman turned out to be an undercover cop and she was captured. She was fired from her job. She was sent to prison for 47 years. She tried to join three prison gangs there but she was beaten by them and she finally joined a different gang. She also tried to escape from prison but failed and she bribed the guards $10000 in exchange for 5 years of protection. At the age of 52, Jasmine and James divorced. Also, she tried to escape from prison. When she was 60 her son started a job as a trucker. When she was 63 her daughter Holly got married to Fletcher Scott. The same year she got free after being heard out by a parole board. She also became a roadkill remover for Falcon Inc. Two years later she was fired and sued the company for firing her. She won the lawsuit. She started a job as a water slide tester. When she was 67 her son, Clive married Zara Jackman. When she was 70 a lot of things happened. She was fired from her job as a water slide tester. She hired a hitman named Marcus Brutus to kill her ex-husband James for $45000 but instead he ran off with her money. She started a job as a server at a restaurant instead. At the age of 71 she hired another hitman named Guy of Gisborne to kill her ex-husband, James for $10000. He killed her ex with an axe. She robbed a bank and stole $380,000 from the bank disguised as a politician. At the age of 72 she hacked into an airline pilots computer and stole $9700. She pickpocketed someone and stole $66. Also she robbed another bank disguised as a nun and stole $47,000. When she was 73 she emigrated to Leon, Nicaragua. She tried robbed a bank in Leon but was caught by the police. She bribed them $90,000 and they let her go. Later she stole a package from someone's house and sold the contents. She tried to rob another bank the same year disguised as a Persian sultan but was caught again. She tried to flirt with the cops but they assaulted her. She was sent to prison for bank robbery. It was hard for her prison and she assaulted three other prisoners. She died later in prison at the age of 79.
