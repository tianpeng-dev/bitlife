Not to be confused with his grandson, Maurizio Albano II.

Maurizio Albano I (3841-3884) was an Italian photographer and businessman.

Early life (3841-3859)[]

Albano was born in Genoa, Italy. He is the older of two children born to Aisha Kovacevic II and the only child born to her first boyfriend, Andrea Albano.

Albano graduated from secondary school in 3859.

Photography career (3859-3870)[]

In 3859, Albano became a junior photographer for Genoa Journal.

In 3870, Albano resigned.

Business career (3870-3884)[]

In 3870, Albano acquired a clothing outlet called House of Fashion.

In 3884, Albano gave his son, Paolo, his business.

Murder[]

In 3884, Albano was sucked to death with a set of plastic vampire teeth. His son, Paolo, was accused of the murder, but was acquitted.

Marriage and children[]

From 3861 to 3870, Albano married an insurance agent named Viviana Zeppeli (3840-after 3908). They had three children, Paolo Albano, Nario I (3868-3877), and Rocco Albano I (3870-3884). After Albano and Zeppeli divorced, they continued dating until his death.
