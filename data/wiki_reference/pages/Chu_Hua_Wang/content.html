Early life[]

Chu Hua Wang was born in Shenzhen, China, in 2061. Her parents are Lijuan Grigalashvili and Dong Wang (born 2043).

Adult life (pre-CNSA)[]

In 2080, Wang became a receptionist at Coastal Development. She retired in 2114, after which she enrolled in college.

In 2118, Wang enrolled at the Chinese Space Academy.

Astronaut career and death[]

In 2120, Wang became an astronaut at the China National Space Administration. During her only spacewalk, she died while performing a research experiment. She left her inheritance to her son.

Relationships and child[]

Wang was married to a photographer named Zixin Peng (2058-after 2120) from 2090 to 2114. They had no children.

Wang started dating a student named On Kao (2094-2173) in 2114. The same year, they adopted a Singaporean-Chinese boy named Louis Wang (né Jiang, born 2107).

Pet[]

In 2114, Wang bought a dingo named Charlie (born 2105) from an exotic pet dealer.
