A character originating from the life Hanako Kaneko, She followed on Social Media about the Swedish-Islamic War (2027-2033).

Bahrom Ahura (1965-2033), was the 9th President of Iran (2025-2033), Politician in the Islamic Consultative Assembly (1988-2033), Mayor of Tehran (1988-1992), and served in the Mandatory Military Service (1986-1988).

Early Life[]

Birth, and School[]

Bahrom Ahura was born in 1965 in Tehran, Iran to unknown parents, He went to school from 1971 (aged 6) to 1983 (aged 18) and then to the.

University, and Conscription[]

When Bahrom was 21 (in 1986), during his 3rd year at the University of Tehran, he was elected to compulsory military service for 2 years (in 1988, when he was 23), after which he joined the party and was elected to the Iranian parliament for long years.

First Term as President of Iran[]

Bahrom won the 2025 Presidential elections as the 9th President of Iran with only 90% of the votes.


During his term as president, he was known for inventing new harams (Prohibitions in Arabic). In 2026, Sweden organized a political slander campaign against Bahrom, which resulted in the Swedish-Islamic War, a year later (2027, at 5:30 a.m.) was the starting point of the war (for Bahrom Ahura, and supporters: Response to provocation) it carried out a devastating air campaign commanded by General of the Iranian Air Force on the city of Halmstad (which resulted in a 24-hour curfew across Sweden)

In the year 2028, Bahrom decided to fight for re-election in 2029, having to face his opponent (who is against this war).

Second Term as President of Iran, and Death[]

President Bahrom was assassinated in 2033 in the Swedish Covert Operation (Called "Operation Ahura"), and in this way ending his rule, and the Swedish-Islamic War.
