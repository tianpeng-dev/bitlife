Denmark
is a
country
that was on BitLife in March 2019. Its default cities are the capital, Copenhagen, along with Aarhus.
Denmark was part of a March 2019
update
that added 8 new countries into the game. The other 7 were
Portugal
,
Norway
,
Indonesia
,
Singapore
,
Finland
,
Estonia
, and
Romania
. This update was also the update that brought
Pets
for the first time. This update was also the first to have multiple new countries being added other than the ones originally released.
