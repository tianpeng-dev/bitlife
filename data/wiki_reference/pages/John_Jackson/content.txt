John Jackson (2135-after 2198) is a British-Canadian DJ.
Early life
[
]
Jackson was born in Bristol, England. His father is
Clarence Jackson
. He emigrated to Dallas, Texas with his parents as a baby. He went to Andreas Butterfield High.
DJ career
[
]
In 2153, Jackson emigrated back to Bristol, England. He became a DJ for Hyperbole Beats.
In 2154, Jackson resigned and emigrated to Montreal, Quebec.
In 2155, Jackson became a DJ for Handy Beats. He disappeared in 2198.
Relationship and children
[
]
In 2149, Jackson started dating a student named Ariana Williams (2135-after 2198), who later became a receptionist. They have two children, Billy (2152-2164) and
Payton Jackson
.
