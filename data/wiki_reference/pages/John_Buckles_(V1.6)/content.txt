John Buckles
(September 11, 1923 - June 6, 1984) was an American United States Army veteran who participated in the Normandy invasion of D-Day in World War II. After completing his service in the military, he went on to hold multiple jobs, and even served in three political offices, including School Board Director from 1945 to 1953, Mayor of Sacramento from 1969 to 1981, and Governor of California from 1981 until his assassination in 1984.
