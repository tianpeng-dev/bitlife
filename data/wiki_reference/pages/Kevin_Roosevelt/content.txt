Kevin Roosevelt
(2111-after 2159) was an Israeli trucker.
Early life (2111-2229)
[
]
Roosevelt was born in Tel Aviv, Israel. He was the youngest of three children born to
Carlton Roosevelt
and his ex-girlfriend Cheyenne Bonanno.
In 2117, Roosevelt enrolled at Cardinal Primary School. He called his headmaster, Tamar Hadad, a snollygoster. He received a warning.
In 2123, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Tzofit’s Middle School. He skipped school to go shopping.
In 2125, Roosevelt stole a Toyota Tacoma.
In 2126, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Netali’s Secondary School.
In 2129, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school. He briefly emigrated to Chicago, Illinois, before moving back to Tel Aviv.
Career (2148-2159)
[
]
In 2148, Roosevelt became an apprentice trucker for Kingfisher Transportation.
In 2150, Roosevelt was promoted to trucker.
Roosevelt was alive in 2159, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 2121 to 2130, Roosevelt dated a student named Leah Avraham (2111-after 2159), who became a trucker in 2130. They had three children, Venus II (2128-after 2159), Shira (2129-after 2159), and Carlton Roosevelt III (b. 2130).
