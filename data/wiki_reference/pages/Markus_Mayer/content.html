Early Life (1989-2007)[]

Markus was born 23rd October 1989 in Austria. He had a brother named Kilian (1988-1993). His mother Jasmin Mozart (born 1953) is a retired school administrator, his father Dominik Mayer (1924-2007) was a retired lawyer. In 1993 his old brother Kilian died due to whooping cough. A year later Markus was diagnosed with depression. In 1994 his parents had a girl named Bettina who was conceived at Disneyland. In 2000 Markus started dating his classmate Halimnye Ugochukwu who became his wife years later! Mayer was always interested in playing football. He was playing in school soccer club since 2000. He also was a captain of his team!

Personal Life[]

Markus lost his father in 2007, Markus was 18 and his father was 83. His dad lost the battle to cancer of anus. In 2008 Markus migrated to Germany to join one of Bundesliga's team. And he joined Borussia Mönchengladbach! In 2011 he bought a Cairn Terrier named Cherokee. In 2012 Markus has become a father, her daughter was named Emma. In 2014 her girlfriend has born his a son named Philipp. In 2017 Halimnye has born a third child named Alina Mayer. In 2019 after retirement from football Markus moved back to his hometown Salzburg. In 2021 Halimnye has born their last child named Dike Mayer. Died in 2025... 🫡

Career[]

Markus Mayer has been soccer player and actor. He played as a midfielder. Markus played in the German teams: Borussia M'Gladbach, Borussia Dortmund and Bayern Munich.
