Ufa Al-Zawahiri was born in Alexandria, Egypt and lived to 120 years old. Ufa had a very long and eventful life serving in the military, working as a lawyer and ruling the country as president. Ufa would raise two children with his wife Sohaila Sabbagh named Nefertiti Al-Zawahiri and Mohamed Al-Zawahiri. Ufa would graduate from college with a degree in Political Science and graduate from Law School soon after.
