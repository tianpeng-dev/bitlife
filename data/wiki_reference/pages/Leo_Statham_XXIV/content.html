Leo Statham XXIV (5824-after 5881) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5824-5849)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the youngest of three children born to Leo Statham XXIII and his ex-girlfriend Carrie Bulkley.

In 5842, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5846, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5849-5864)[]

In 5849, Statham graduated with a Doctorate in Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Karl Murphy.

In 5854, Statham ran for reelection and beat Oisin Yeats.

In 5859, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Worker’s Party candidate Katelyn Burke-Duffy.

In 5864, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5881, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5842 to 5843, Statham dated a student named Alison Dewey (5824-after 5881). They had a son, Leo Statham XXV.
