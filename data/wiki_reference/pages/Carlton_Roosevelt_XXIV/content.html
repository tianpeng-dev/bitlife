Carlton Roosevelt XXIV (2964-May 24, 3044) was an American fireman, porn star, movie star, politician, and convicted murderer.

Early life (2964-2982)[]

Roosevelt was born in Los Angeles, California. He was the only child born to Esteban Roosevelt and his ex-girlfriend Julia Handy.

In 2982, Roosevelt graduated high school.

Firefighting career (2982-2985)[]

In 2982, Roosevelt became a probationary firefighter for the Los Angeles Fire Department.

In 2985, Roosevelt was promoted to firefighter. He resigned.

Porn career (2985-2994)[]

In 2985, Roosevelt became a porn actor for Pony Erotica.

In 2992, Roosevelt became famous.

In 2994, Roosevelt resigned.

Acting career (2995-3012)[]

In 2995, Roosevelt played the leading role of Russell James in the film Buckshot.

In 2998, Roosevelt played Dermott the Frog in Puppets Take Manhattan.

In 3001, Roosevelt played Jim Evers in Blue Dove Down.

In 3002, Roosevelt played Arlo Nottingham in Angels in the Penalty Box.

In 3003, Roosevelt played Tommy Lee Cockford in When Our Hearts Collided.

In 3004, Roosevelt played Dan Marconi in The Blackest Midnight.

In 3005, Roosevelt played Matthew Durden in Bite Club.

In 3007, Roosevelt played Clayton Sellers in Do You Know the Way?

In 3008, Roosevelt played Dirk Haynes in Bite the Dust.

In 3009, Roosevelt played Ana Hardwood in The Moon Princess.

In 3010, Roosevelt played Quinton Dancer in Eyes Wide Open.

In 3011, Roosevelt played Mack in A Chorus Triangle, which won a Bitcademy Award for Best Musical Film.

In 3012, Roosevelt played Dale McDonald in The Perks of Baking with Cornflour. He enrolled at UCLA, where he majored in political science.

In 3016, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at UCLA School of Law.

Political career (3019-3027)[]

In 3019, Roosevelt ran for election to the LAUSD Board of Education and beat Zion Moorehead.

In 3023, Roosevelt ran for reelection and beat Hugh Robinson.

In 3027, Roosevelt was forced to resign in disgrace after being convicted of murder.

Prison sentence (3027-3044)[]

In 3027, Roosevelt murdered his daughter, Venus Roosevelt IX. He was sentenced to death. He appealed to the California Court of Appeal for the 2nd District and his sentence was overturned. He attempted to murder his son, Carlton Roosevelt XXV. He was sentenced to 41 years in prison. He appealed, but lost. He is serving his sentence at Los Angeles Penitentiary.

In 3044, Roosevelt was released from prison in exchange for snitching on another prisoner.

Suicide[]

On May 24, 3044, Roosevelt committed suicide.

Relationships and children[]

From 2984 to 2987, Roosevelt dated his co-worker, a firewoman named Viviane Dancer (2961-3046). They had three children from their first relationship, Venus IX (2985-3027), Jeanette (2986-after 3057), and Carlton XXV (2987-after 3057).

From 3016 to 3026, Roosevelt and Dancer dated again. They had three children from their second relationship, Jamila (3018-after 3057), Ramona (3020-after 3057), and Willy Roosevelt.

From 3027 to 3028, Roosevelt and Dancer dated a third time.
