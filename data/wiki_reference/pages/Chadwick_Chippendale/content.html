Chadwick Charles Chippendale (December 28, 1925 - February 19, 2022), stage named CCC (Triple C), was an American choreographer whose career spanned six decades. He spent his entire career dancing for the Vegas-based studio Valley School of Dance, where, starting in 1979, the Chippendale dancing business also performed. Inspired to do so by his own surname, Chadwick also became a Chippendale dancer.

Life and career[]

1925-1943: Early years[]

Chadwick was born as a planned pregnancy in Las Vegas. His mother, Virginia (later Pratt) (1895 - 1977) was a jewler who sold jewelry to fundraising collectors. His father, James "Jimmy" Chippendale (1892 - 1966) was a professor. Chad's parents divorced in 1934. Around that time, his half-brother, Shaggy, was born. Virginia married Ezekiel Pratt (1901 - 1966) a trucker. Jimmy married college student Chloe Price (1916 - 1991) in 1937.
