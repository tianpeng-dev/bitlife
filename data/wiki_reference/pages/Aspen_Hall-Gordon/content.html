Aspen Hall-Gordon (b. 2123) is an Australian firefighter.

Early life (2123-2137)[]

Aspen Hall-Gordon was born in Adelaide, South Australia in 2123. Her father was Jacob Jones (né Hall-Gordon).

In 2128, her father married actress Amelia Jones. He died the same year.

From 2136 to 2138, Hall-Gordon dated Kobe Watts (b. 2123). In 2138, they had a son named Mel Watts.

Firefighter (2138-present)[]

In 2138, Hall-Gordon graduated high school. She became a probationary firefighter for Adelaide Fire Department. She started dating Alexander Scott (b. 2109).
