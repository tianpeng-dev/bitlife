Career Collections


A career is collected when a character holds it for 20 consecutive years. Nearly every career in the game can be collected – the prestigious Judge and the lowly Worm Picker, from the Midwife to the Mortician and (almost) everything in between.

For quick reference, the list below includes the basic requirements to get hired in each career. Over half have an educational requirement, and some have requirements based on gender, traits, previous work experience, or other factors. See Education for info on requirements for higher education, and Careers/Jobs for additional info on each career track.

Note: "Secondary School" refers to any equivalent level of education, including various countries' High School or Upper Secondary School, as well as earning a GED.
