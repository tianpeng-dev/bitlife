Early life and education
[
]
Romet Grigorieva was born as Romet Rebane in 1974. In 1976, he was adopted by Leenart Grigorieva and Liisa Popov. Romet's last name was eventually changed to his adoptive father's soon after he was adopted.
He attended primary school from 1981 to 1990 and attended secondary school from 1990 to 1993. He also attended university from 1994 to 1998.
Career
[
]
Romet started working as a marriage counselor for Tallinn Health Services in 1998. He was forced to retire in 2062, but got the same job that same year, this time for Quantum Health and Addiction. However, he was axed from that job just one year later in 2063.
