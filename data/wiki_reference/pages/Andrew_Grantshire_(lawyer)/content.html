Andrew Robert Grantshire (born c. 1935) is an American retired attorney from New York. He is the fourth great-grandson of Caroline Grantshire, Marchioness of Winchester. He is also the brother-in-law of mobster Giuseppe Angelo through his marriage to Giuseppe's younger sister Maria.
