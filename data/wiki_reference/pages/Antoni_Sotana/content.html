Antoni Sotana (April 30th, 1965 - 1997) was the second-in-command of the Miami division of the Italian-American mafia. He served under Jonathan Zeymmo as his right-hand-man and took over Zeymmo's job as the syndicate's main enforcer after Zeymmo was promoted to Godfather. In 1997, Toni would be sent on a mission to Japan to assassinate a politician working with the US to eliminate their respective country's criminal organizations. While the hit was successful, Toni was killed by an Yakuza member known as "Murakami" for muscling in on their territory. His passing contributed to the destabilization of both Zeymmo's mental health and the Miami company of the Italian-American mafia.

Notes[]

· This character was made up to serve as an way to explain the downfall of the IA Mafia, alongside the otherwise true explanation that there was an informant.

· His name is a blending of Antonio "Tony" Montana, the protagonist of the 1983 film Scarface, and Tony Soprano, the protagonist of the 1999-2007 HBO series, The Sopranos.
