Josef Bergmann Jr. was a retired astronaut and a nobel-prized astronomer. During his careeer, he completed a total of 12 Missions in Space. He holds a Guinness World Record for being the oldest person to set foot on Mars (61 years and 28 days).

early life[]

Josef Bergmann Jr. was born on September 29th in 1996 to financial tycoon Josef Bergmann Sr. and Merle Winkler. Due to his fathers wealth, he had a very neat childhood. After graduating from high school, he studied information systems in university, fulfilling the wish of his father, who supported him financially. After graduating, he started jobbing as a busser, as he wasn´t very interested in a corporate job.

career as astronaut[]

As Josef turned 29, his father commited suicide after escaping from prison. Later that year Josef won an estimated amout of $85.000.000 in the lottery. In his first autobiography "Confessions" he stated, that "those two events really opened my eyes about who I wanna be." He then immediately quit his job as a busser, and started a training programm at the German Space Academy to become an astronaut. After two years of training, Josef applied at the ESA and joined the fleet of astronauts.
