Leilah Abdel
(born 1975) is a British police chief who has served in the Swansea Police Department since 1994. She is also the widow of British Air Force Chief Master Sergeant
Hugo Chaplin
.
Career
[
]
At 19 years old, Abdel joined the Swansea Police Department as Cadet in 1994. In her career, she recieved seven promotions every three years from 1997 to 2015:
- Patrolman (1997)
- Trooper (2000)
- Corporal (2003)
- Sergeant (2006)
- Inspector (2009)
- Lieutenant (2012)
- Chief of Police (2015)
Personal Life
[
]
Abdel and Chaplin had been friends since 1980, when they were both five years old. They began dating when they started high school at 14. They were engaged 1997 before Hugo enlisted in the Air Force. Their first child, son
Percy Chaplin
, was born in 2000. They married a year later and stayed together until Hugo's death in 2021.
