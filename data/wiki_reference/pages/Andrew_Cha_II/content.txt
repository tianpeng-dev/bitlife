Early life (3168-3187)
[
]
Cha was born in Cagliari, Italy. He was the youngest of four children born to
Chin Cha IX
and his girlfriend Rita Brown.
In 3174, Cha enrolled at St. Angelica’s Primary School.
In 3179, Cha enrolled at Marta Neri Middle School and joined the soccer team.
In 3182, Cha enrolled at Nario Toscani Secondary School and joined the soccer team.
In 3185, Cha became the captain of the Nario Toscani Secondary School soccer team.
In 3187, Cha graduated from secondary school.
Sports career (3187-3214)
[
]
In 3187, Cha joined Inter as a goalkeeper.
In 3195, Cha passed a doping test.
In 3196, 3198, and 3200, Inter finished as champions.
In 3210, Cha was released by Inter and joined Napoli.
In 3214, Cha was released by Napoli.
Realty career (3215-3234)
[
]
In 3215, Cha became a real estate agent for Eneide and Saraceno Realtors.
In 3234, Cha retired.
Retirement
[
]
Cha was alive in 3243, but his death date is unknown.
