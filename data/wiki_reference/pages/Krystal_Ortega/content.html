Krystal Ortega (December 17, 1995 - September 13, 2022) was an American hustler from Las Vegas, best known for her career as a flatulist.

Early life[]

Krystal was born in Las Vegas to jeweler Alexa Ortega (born 1968) and marine biologist Randall Ortega (1945 - 2017). The Ortegas had a cat named DeShawn (died 2009).

Personal life[]

Ortega had her first kiss with future flight attendant Diego Gerco (born 1995) when they were both ten. They eventually began dating nine years later in 2014, and married in 2018. Their son, Jacob, was born a year after their wedding.

Death[]

Ortega was murdered in prison on September 13, 2022. She was beaten to death by the Switchblade Sisterhood prison gain, whom she tried to join. She is survived by her mother, husband, and son.
