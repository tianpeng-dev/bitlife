Carlton Roosevelt XXXI (3292-after 3335) was a Romanian-American casino worker.

Early life (3292-3311)[]

Roosevelt was born in Transylvania, Romania. He was the youngest of four children born to Carlton Roosevelt XXX and his girlfriend Timea Munteanu.

In 3302, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Stefan’s Lower Secondary School.

In 3308, Roosevelt enrolled at Serenity Preparatory School.

In 3311, Roosevelt graduated from upper secondary school. He emigrated to New York City.

Career (3314-3335)[]

In 3314, Roosevelt became a dealer for Aristotle Casino Resort.

In 3320, Roosevelt was promoted to floor supervisor.

Roosevelt was alive in 3335, but his death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 3307 to 3308, Roosevelt dated a student named Cristina Radu (3292-after 3313).

From 3308 to 3313, Roosevelt dated a student named Sara Rusu (3292-after 3335). They had three children:

  1. Carlton Roosevelt XXXII (3309-after 3335)
  2. Gabriela Roosevelt (3312-after 3335)
  3. Andi Roosevelt
