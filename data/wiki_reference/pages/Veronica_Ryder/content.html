Veronica Ryder (born 1967) is an American truck driver and animal activist. She was the owner of Joy, a Himalayan cat, who shared a loving relationship with her, unlike her late husband, Lil Mountjoy.

Early life[]

Veronica was born in Massachustts. Her parents worked as healthcare professionals, specializing in animal care. Her father was a Vietnam draft dodger and came close to going to jail during Veronica's infancy.
