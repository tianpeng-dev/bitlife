Early life (5683-5701)
[
]
Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He is the only child of
Leo Statham XVI
and his ex-girlfriend Emer Lewis.
Career (5701-5721)
[
]
In 5701, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Michael Campbell.
In 5706, Statham ran for reelection and beat Veronica Quinn.
In 5711, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Worker’s Party candidate Gerald Guerin.
In 5716, Statham ran for reelection and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Eric McCarty.
In 5721, Statham did not run for a third term.
Later life
[
]
Statham was alive in 5739, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 5701 to 5702, Statham dated a student named Katie Graham (5681-after 5739). They had a son,
Leo Statham XVIII
