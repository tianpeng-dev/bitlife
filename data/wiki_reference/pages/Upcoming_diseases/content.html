The upcoming diseases are set to expand the health and life simulation in BitLife, adding more variety, realism, and challenges to gameplay. Players will encounter a mix of infectious, genetic, chronic, neurological, and rare conditions, each with unique effects on characters’ health, happiness, and lifespan.

1. Infectious / Contagious Diseases[]

  • 😬Tetanus – Could occur after injuries; rare but serious.
  • 😷Dengue – Travel-related tropical disease.
  • 😷Monkeypox – Rare, contagious viral infection.
  • 😱Toxic shock syndrome - Caused by toxins in bacteria
  • 🤢Salmonella – Food poisoning; mild to severe.
  • 😩Polio – Rare; could cause paralysis.
  • 🙁Hand Foot and Mouth Disease – Usually mild; could affect kids.
  • 🤢Zika – Travel-related; could affect pregnancy.
  • 🙁Cooties – Fun/childhood “silly” disease; could be a humorous addition.
  • 🦐Paralytic Shellfish Poisoning – Could be tied to eating risky foods.

2. Genetic / Congenital Conditions[]

  • 🧠Autism – Could influence social interactions and life choices.
  • 😐Down Syndrome – Could affect intelligence, health, and lifespan.
  • 😷Cystic Fibrosis – Serious genetic condition; affects lungs and digestion.
  • 😷Huntington’s Disease – Late-onset neurological disorder.
  • 😬Sjorgen's Syndrome - Skin dryness
  • 😬Cerebral Palsy – Affects mobility; could influence jobs and activities.
  • 😒Infertility – Could affect relationships and family planning.
  • 🦶Flat Foot – Minor effect on physical activities; fun detail.

3. Neurological / Mental Health Conditions[]

  • 🧠ADHD – Could affect focus, school, work performance.
  • 😬Parkinson’s – Late-onset movement disorder.
  • 🧠Amnesia - Substantial difficulty in recalling old memories or forming new ones
  • 😷Bacterial Meningitis - Can be Deadly if untreated
  • 🧠Psychosis – Severe mental illness affecting perception and behavior.
  • 🙁OCD – Could affect daily routines and decisions.
  • 🙁Tourette’s Syndrome – Involuntary tics; humorous or challenging events.
  • 🫨Vertigo – Dizziness affecting mobility.
  • 😀Insensitivity to Pain – Rare condition; could lead to accidental injuries.
  • 😬Tinnitus – Ringing in ears; minor annoyance but realistic.

4. Gastrointestinal / Liver / Digestive[]

  • 🤢Giardiasis – Parasite; causes diarrhea.
  • 😷Acid Reflux – A chronic condition where stomach acid flows back up into the esophagus, causing irritation and damage
  • 🤢Cholera - Caused by contaminated water
  • 😷Crohn’s Disease – Chronic bowel inflammation.
  • 😷Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) – Minor digestive issues; affects comfort.
  • 🤒Stomach Flu - Caused by Noroviruses
  • 😷Cirrhosis – Severe liver disease; could be linked to alcohol or hepatitis.
  • 😔Low Blood Pressure – Could affect stamina, fainting events.

5. Respiratory / Skin / Ear[]

  • 😷COPD – Chronic lung disease; linked to smoking.
  • 🤒Sore Throat – Minor, common illness.
  • 😷Anthrax - A rare but serious illness
  • 🙁Warts – Minor cosmetic effect; harmless.
  • 😬Eczema – Could affect appearance and happiness.
  • 😡Rosacea – Redness on face; cosmetic/embarrassment effect.
  • 🤧Sinusitis – Minor illness; affects happiness or health.

6. Rare / Deadly[]

  • 🧠vCJD – Mad-cow related disease; rare and fatal.
  • 😬Brain Eating Amoeba - Very fatal disease caused by dirty water
  • 🤢Lassa Fever – Caused by mosquitoes
  • 😫Fatal Familial Insomnia - Rare, incurable genetic prion disease causing a progressive inability to sleep and leading to rapid death
