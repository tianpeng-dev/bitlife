Yuri Aelesnarovich (Russian Юрий Алеснарович; known in the US as Yuri Johnson) (February 14, 19XX - TBD, 2022) was a Russian-American soldier and politician. He served in both the Russian and United States armies. With his college career abroad, he was also a successful player of American football.
