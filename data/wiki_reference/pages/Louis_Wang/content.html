Louis Wang (né Jiang, 2107-2230) was a Singaporean-Chinese bluegrass singer.

Early life[]

Louis Wang was born Louis Jiang in Singapore, Singapore in 2107. In 2114, he was adopted by Chu Hua Wang and her boyfriend On Kao, and his last name was changed to that of his mother. He moved to Shenzhen, China.

Wang went to public school at Yunming Ti’en Lower Secondary School and Unity Preparatory School. He also started taking voice lessons at the age of 13 and acting lessons at 14.

Music career[]

In 2125, Wang signed with Harp Entertainment as a solo country singer. He released 12 bluegrass albums and gone on 16 tours before retiring in 2187.

In 2137, he wrote a book titled True Story!

After retiring, Wang enrolled in college.

Writing career[]

In 2191, Wang became a writer for Discount Press.

Death[]

In 2230, Wang died of old age at 123. He was the world’s oldest person. As he had no children, the Bennett Legacy ended after 240 years.

Marriages[]

Wang started dating a student named Jing Kai-shek (2107-2217) in 2120. They were married from 2130 until Kai-shek’s death.

In 2217, Wang married a writer named Ru Ho (born 2165).
