Chin Cha V (2985-3057) was an American pilot and businessman.

Early life (2985-3011)[]

Cha was born in Houston, Texas. He was the younger of two children born to Chin Cha IV and his girlfriend Amanda Bush.

In 2990, Cha enrolled at Little Egbert Elementary School.

In 2996, Cha enrolled at Atmosphere Middle School. He joined the football team.

In 3000, Cha enrolled at Houston High School. He joined the football team.

In 3003, Cha enrolled at the University of Houston on an athletic scholarship. He played football for the Cougars, was a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi, and majored in political science. He got a pilot’s license.

Between 3003 and 3005, Cha donated blood three times, but it didn’t count for some reason.

In 3007, Cha graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science, but it also didn’t count. He enrolled at the University of Houston again, this time majoring in engineering.

In 3011, Cha graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Engineering.

First pilot career (3011-3012)[]

In 3011, Cha became a pilot trainee for Lugo Airlines. He gave his best friend, Addison Genovese (2985-after 3023) a blimp.

Between 3011 and 3012, Cha donated blood twice, and this time it actually counted.

In 3012, Cha gave his supervisor, Paula Gutiérrez (2961-after 3023) a jet, and gave his co-worker, a co-pilot named Anita Brady (2957-after 3023).

Business career (3012-3023)[]

In 3012, Cha acquired Kronik Cannabis Company, a marijuana dispensary. He took a $51,000 bonus and sponsored 10 team building activities.

In 3017, Cha sold Kronik Cannabis Company at a $196 million profit. He founded The Red Carpet Wagon, a food truck company.

In 3023, Cha sold The Red Carpet Wagon at a $5.4 million loss.

Second pilot career (3024-3051)[]

In 3024, Cha became a pilot trainee for Tweedy Airways.

In 3030, Cha was promoted to co-pilot.

In 3036, Cha was promoted to pilot.

In 3042, Cha was promoted to airline captain.

In 3051, Cha retired.

Death[]

In 3057, Cha died of a stroke.

Relationship and children[]

In 2996, Cha started dating a student named Aaliyah Sack (2985-after 3094), who became a photographer in 3004. They had four children:
