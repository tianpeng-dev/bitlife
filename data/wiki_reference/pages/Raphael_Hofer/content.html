Early Life[]

Raphael Hofer was born in Salzburg, Austria, on January 7, 2015. He was conceived in the Kanayama Shrine in Japan. His mother is Alina Leitner (1992-), a restaurant worker. And his father is Marco Hofer (1973-), an Air Force enlistee. He had an older sister named Lara Hofer (2011-)

Childhood[]

When he was 1 year old (2016), he began suffering from croup. When he was 2 years old (2017), he worked intensely on his tooth brushing skills until he was able to thoroughly clean his teeth without missing a spot. When he was 3 years old (2018), his mother and his father had a daughter named Emma Hofer (2018-), Raphael's new sister. She was conceived while his mother was in a sex swing. He became friends with Lukas Kainz. When he was 4 years old (2019), he began suffering from whooping cough and cured it 7 minutes later. When he was 5 years old (2020), he broke his parents's chainsaw and then lied about it. When he was 6 years old (2021), his little sister, Emma, has been diagnosed with whooping cough. His mother and his father had a daughter named Lisa Hofer (2021-), Raphael's new sister. She was conceived after his father's condom broke.

Primary School[]

When he was 6 years old (2021), he started attending Little Fischer Primary School. When he was 8 years old (2023), he was diagnosed with sickle cell disease. His little sister, Emma, died from complications associated with whooping cough.

Death[]

Tragically, Raphael Hofer died after a brief illness at the tender age of 9 surrounded by family. His funeral was attended by his mother, his father, and his two sisters, Lara and Lisa.

Friends say he struggled with the loss of his little sister, Emma.

Raphael died from complications associated with sickle cell disease.
