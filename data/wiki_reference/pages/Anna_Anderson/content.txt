Anna Anderson (3268-3348) was a French-British soccer player and museum worker.

Early life (3268-3290)
[
]
Anderson was born in Bordeaux, France. Her parents were
Aaron Anderson
and his ex-girlfriend Charlotte Durand.
In 3271, Anderson emigrated to Cardiff, Wales.
In 3286, Anderson emigrated to Dallas, Texas and enrolled at the University of Texas. She was a member of Alpha Chi Omega and played soccer for the Mavericks before graduating in 3290 with a BS in Anthropology.
Sports career (3290-3305)
[
]
In 3290, Anderson joined Houston FC as a forward.
In 3294, Anderson was released by Houston. She emigrated back to Cardiff and joined Reading.
In 3305, Anderson retired.
Museum career (3306-3334)
[
]
In 3306, Anderson became a Docent for Thoroughbred Gallery.
In 3312, Anderson was promoted to Exhibit Associate.
In 3318, Anderson was promoted to Assistant Curator.
In 3324, Anderson was promoted to Curator.
In 3330, Anderson was promoted to Museum Director.
In 3334, Anderson retired.
Death
[
]
In 3348, Anderson died of a stroke. Her funeral was held in Cardiff.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 3286 to 3287, Anderson dated a student named Ezra Thiel (3240-3309). They had a daughter,
Mary Joe Thiel
.
