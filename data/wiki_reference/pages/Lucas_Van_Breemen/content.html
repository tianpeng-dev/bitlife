They also had a Cavalier King Charles Spaniel named Brock. At the age of 4, in 2008, Lucas entered a public school. He made a lot of friends there. Since school years, or rather since 9 (year 2013) Lucas began to study singing. He had a talent for music and he was showing tremendous success. At the age of 12, Lucas realized that he was not at all attracted to female beauty and the opposite sex did not like him at all, so he identified himself as gay. At the age of 13, in 2017, Lucas started studying piano. At the age of 14, Lucas began dating a guy named David RijkaardAfter 2 years, they broke up and Lucas began dating his friend Tygo Citroen, before seducing him and having sex with him.
