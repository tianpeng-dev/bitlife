Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XVII (4571-after 4627) is a Japanese mailman.

Early life (4571-4609)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. He is the only child of Eberhard Werner XVI and Yasuko Fujita.

In 4589, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4609-4627)[]

In 4609, Werner became a junior mail carrier for Japan Post Holdings.

In 4612, Werner was promoted to mail carrier.

In 4615, Werner was promoted to senior mail carrier.

Werner was alive in 4627, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4589 to 4590, Werner dated a restaurant worker named Kanon Majima (4564-after 4627). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XVIII.
