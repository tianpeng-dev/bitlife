Canada
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the capital, Ottawa, along with Calgary, Edmonton, Halifax, Montreal, Quebec City, Regina, Saskatoon, Toronto, Vancouver, and Winnipeg.
On iOS, Canada provides free healthcare, making characters able to consult doctors and treat
diseases and ailments
free of charge.
Two
Achievements
are Canada-based;
"There's Always Canada,"
where a non-Canadian citizen emigrates to Canada, and
"Winnipeg, EH?"
where a character vacations (by themselves in
Activities
or during childhood when parents offer to take them on vacation) to Winnipeg, Canada.
Along with the
United States
, Canada allows characters in
University
to rush a sorority or fraternity.
