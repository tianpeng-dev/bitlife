Arranged Marriage

Launched on iOS:

October 19, 2019

Launched on Android:

December 20, 2019
In some countries, your parents can arrange a marriage for you to another person, regardless of whether your character is already in a relationship, engaged, or identifies as homosexual. Even if you are dating or are engaged, you parents can arrange a marriage for you. You will have the options "Agree to marry them" and "Refuse to marry them". If you refuse to marry them, your parents might either leave it there, ask you politely, or demand that you marry the person if they have a high religiousness. They might also demand you after rejecting the polite request. Refusing to marry the person after they demanded twice will result in an empty relationships bar. If they only ask once and you refuse, this will not empty the relationship bar but will lower it. When you agree to marry the person, your spouse's relationship bar will usually start off low, near the orange zone.
