Leo Statham XXX
(5940-after 5983) was an Irish-American policeman.
Early life (5940-5959)
[
]
Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the only child of
Leo Statham XXIX
and his ex-girlfriend Airi Sasaki.
In 5958, Statham graduated from secondary school.
In 5959, Statham emigrated to Newark, New Jersey.
Career (5966-5983)
[
]
In 5966, Statham became a cadet for the Newark Police Department.
In 5969, Statham was promoted to patrolman.
In 5972, Statham was promoted to trooper.
In 5975, Statham was promoted to corporal.
In 5978, Statham was promoted to sergeant.
In 5981, Statham was promoted to inspector.
Statham was alive in 5983, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 5959 to 5960, Statham dated an unemployed woman named Misty Mendoza-Buck (5941-after 5983). They had a son,
Leo Statham XXXI
.
