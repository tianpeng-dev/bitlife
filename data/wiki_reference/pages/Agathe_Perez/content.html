Agathe Perez (2997-after 3069) was a French-British soccer player, actor, and database administrator.

Early life (2997-3019)[]

Perez was born in Lyon, France. Her parents were Alicia Rolland and her ex-boyfriend Jean Claude Perez.

In 3000, Perez emigrated to Cardiff, Wales.

In 3015, Perez emigrated back to Lyon and enrolled at Claude Bernard University. She was on the soccer team and graduated in 3019 with a Licentiate in Computer Science.

Sports career (3019-3038)[]

In 3019, Perez emigrated back to Cardiff and joined Birmingham. She won three Golden Boots while on the team.

In 3033, Perez was released by Birmingham and joined City FC. She won a Golden Boot and three championships while on the team.

In 3038, Perez was released by City.

Astronautic career (3038-3040)[]

In 3038, Perez enrolled at the National Space Academy in Leicester, England. She graduated in 3040, but never worked for the UK Space Agency.

Acting career (3040-3051)[]

In 3040, Perez got her first acting job. She played Dana Szalinski in the film Honey, I Sacrificed the Kids (3041).

In 3041, Perez started playing Ceres Black on season 3 of the TV show West of Neptune. She won three Bitty Awards for Best Supporting Actress and was nominated for two more while on the show.

Database administration career (3051-3062)[]

In 3051, Perez became a junior database administrator for Atmosphere Semiconductor.

In 3061, Perez was promoted to database administrator.

In 3062, Perez resigned.

Perez was alive in 3069, but her death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 3015 to 3016, Perez dated a student named Ismael Thomas (2997-after 3041). They had a daughter, Alya (3016-3022).

From 3022 to 3023, Perez dated a museum worker named Mason Longstaff (2976-3058). They had a son, Rory Longstaff. Perez and Longstaff started dating again in 3044.
