Addison Garcia (b. 2023) is an American pilot.

Early life (2023-2045)[]

Garcia was born in Wichita, Kansas. She is the only child of Angelica Castro and her ex-boyfriend Ary Garcia.

In 2028, Garcia enrolled at Little Gulch Elementary School.

In 2034, Garcia enrolled at Thoroughbred Middle School. She befriended a popular girl named Jayla Nolan (b. 2023).

In 2037, Garcia enrolled at St. David’s High School.

In 2041, Garcia enrolled at Wichita State University. She was a member of Alpha Chi Omega.

In 2042, Garcia got her pilot’s license. She bought a Cessna 162 Skycatcher and took it for one flight before selling it.

Career (2045-present)[]

In 2045, Garcia graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Physics. She became a pilot trainee for Mesosphere Air.

Relationship and children[]

From 2033 to 2042, Garcia dated a student named Jimmy Fuentes (b. 2023). They had four children, Dale (b. 2038), Forrest (b. 2040), Benedict (b. 2041), and Alyssa Fuentes (b. 2042).
