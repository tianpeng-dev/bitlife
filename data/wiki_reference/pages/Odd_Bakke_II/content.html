Odd Bakke II (2221-2304) was a Puerto Rican-French unemployed man.

Early life (2221-2251)[]

Bakke was born in Ponce, Puerto Rico. He was the youngest of three brothers born to Tarjei Bakke V and his girlfriend Mariah Smith.

In 2239, Bakke moved to Oakland, California.

In 2251, Bakke emigrated to Lyon, France.

Death[]

In 2304, Bakke died of old age.
