Eva Grieg II (4271-December 31, 4354) was a German receptionist and convicted murderer.

Early life (4271-4289)[]

Grieg was born in Stuttgart, Germany. She was the only daughter of Camilla Grieg II and her first husband Bruno Werner.

Front desk career (4289-4330)[]

In 4289, Grieg became a receptionist for Avalon International.

On December 31, 4330, Grieg retired.

Prison sentence (4331-4354)[]

In 4331, Grieg was arrested for murdering her husbands. She was convicted and sentenced to 39 years in prison. She served her sentence at Gung Ho Max Security Prison.

On December 31, 4354, Grieg died of old age in prison.

Marriages and children[]

In 4318, Grieg married a wedding planner named Leon Winter (4262-December 31, 4330). They had two adopted children, Alexander Meier (4308-4404, adopted 4321) and Luna Konig. In 4330, Grieg murdered Winter with a set of plastic vampire teeth after making love to him.

Less than a year after murdering Winter, Grieg married an unemployed man named Jakob Heilig (4312-4331). In 4331, she murdered him with a set of plastic vampire teeth after making love to him.

Less than a year after murdering Heilig, Grieg married a wedding planner named Immanuel Horn (4312-4331). She murdered him with a set of plastic vampire teeth after making love to him.
