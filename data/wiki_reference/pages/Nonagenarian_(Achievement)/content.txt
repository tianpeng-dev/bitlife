Nonagenarian
is an
achievement
of the Longevity section. The player can earn this achievement by living till the age of 90.
Trivia
[
]
This achievement is harder to obtain than
Octogenarian
, because
death
by old age can occur more frequently when the character gets older than 80 years.
This achievement isn't directly connected to any
ribbon
, so obtaining it doesn't guarantee a certain ribbon.
