Profile
is a feature of
BitLife
which shows more information about your Character and their
relationships
, including their
stats
. Family, lovers, exes,
Pets
, and people at your character's school and work have their own profiles. As of February 2020, the character faces have a redesign.
Profiles are useful to see Basic, Detailed, Obscure and Important Information about Characters, or your Character. It mostly contains information such as Relationship Level, Relationship with Your character, Age, Sexuality, Time knowing each other, Birthplace, Education, Occupation and much more.
Other Statistics are seen as 'More Important' Like Looks, Smarts and Craziness, These are shown when Your Character meets another Character, being the only 3 Statistics you are get to judge a Character.

In BitLife, parents (as with all family) are randomly generated, meaning some lives are born with married parents and others have an unknown father or are the result of a
one-night stand
. Some lives are born with married parents that later get divorced, this also allows for the parents to remarry, resulting in step-families.
Biological parents
[
]
An example of a character's mother
For a character's biological parents, it will show information such as their age, level of education (if listed as university, it will also list their degree in brackets), marital status and their current occupation. It will also display the four parent stats: Relationship, Religiousness, Generosity and Money.
Divorce
[
]
An example of a parental divorce pop-up
If a character's parents get divorced, it will significantly decrease the character's Happiness stat. However, divorce allows the parents to marry other people, resulting in step-families.
Step-Parents
[
]
An example of a parent getting remarried
When one of the character's parents gets remarried, a pop-up will appear containing the name, age and occupation of the new step-parent and the location of their honeymoon (if they have one), it will then show any new step-siblings and two of the three step-parent stats: Kindness and Money.
An example of a stepfather's profile
When clicking on a step-parent's profile, it will display their age, level of education and current occupation along with the three step-parent stats: Relationship, Kindness and Money.
School Faculty members include any teachers, professors, principals, and deans. It will display their first and last name, gender, job title, age, sexuality (if known), relationship status (if known), and nationality (if they were from a different
nation
than the one your character is currently from. It will also show their four teacher stats: Relationship, Looks, Strictness, and Popularity.
