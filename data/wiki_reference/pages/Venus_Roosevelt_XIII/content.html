Venus Roosevelt XIII (3077-3163) was an American-Canadian receptionist and convicted assailant.

Early life (3077-3095)[]

Roosevelt was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. She was the second of 17 children born to Kiana Roosevelt and the eldest of three born to her first boyfriend Cleveland Browser.

In 3091, Roosevelt enrolled at Flores High School.

In 3095, Roosevelt graduated from high school.

Prison sentence (3120-3122)[]

In 3120, Roosevelt pinched two of her sisters, Sophie Roosevelt and Alice Browser. She was arrested for assault and sentenced to 2 years in prison. She served her sentence at New Orleans Max Security Prison.

In 3122, Roosevelt was released from prison. She pinched five of her siblings, Bonnie Browser, Charles Eder, Donna Eder, Emma Eder, and Frank Eder. She bought seven green cars. She watched Jacksepticeye with her friend, a business analyst named Martha Packard-Simpson (b. 3078). She emigrated to Cork, Ireland, then to Halifax, Nova Scotia.

Career (3125-3144)[]

In 3125, Roosevelt became a receptionist for Neon Investments.

In 3144, Roosevelt retired.

Death[]

In 3163, Roosevelt died of a stroke.
