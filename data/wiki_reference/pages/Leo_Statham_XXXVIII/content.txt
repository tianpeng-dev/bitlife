Early life (6099-6717)
[
]
Statham was born in Kobe, Japan. He was the youngest of three children born to
Leo Statham XXXVI
and the younger of two born to his third girlfriend Yuki Samawura.
In 6705, Statham enrolled at Yuito Yamamoto Elementary School.
In 6711, Statham enrolled at Masaru Konosuke Lower Secondary School.
In 6714, Statham enrolled at St. Kazuo’s Upper Secondary School.
In 6717, Statham graduated from upper secondary school. He emigrated to Birmingham, England.
Career (6729-6756)
[
]
In 6729, Statham became a junior insurance agent for Royalty Insurance Group.
In 6732, Statham was promoted to insurance agent.
In 6735, Statham was promoted to senior insurance agent.
Cancer and death
[
]
In 6751, Statham was diagnosed with heart cancer.
In 6756, Statham died from heart cancer. His son traveled to his funeral in Birmingham.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 6709 to 6718, Statham started dating a student named Akemi Kimura (6099-after 6774), who became a camerawoman in 6718. They had two sons, Leo XXXIX (6716-after 6774) and
Yuto Statham
