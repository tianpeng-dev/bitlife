Fadwa
(2003-2025) was a Moroccan Savannah cat who spent an loving live on Rabat

Fadwa was born at Rabbaj Residence as a result she was conceived when her father plowed her mother in her owner's garden by the turnips. She became acquainted with a mutt named Kaoutar, Her owner is Yassin Rabbaj (born 1984), a restaurant worker.
When Fadwa was 9 months old, she became friends with Kaoutar the mutt, she played "Chase" with Kaoutar the mutt in the backyard.
In 2005, Yassin married a woman named Nawal Zarrouk (born 1987) Fadwa showed love to Nawal, Also they had a baby named Anass Rabbaj, In 6 months, When she took a nap, Fadwa had a dream that she was fist bumping, So she woke up and practiced fist bumping.
In 2006, When Fadwa took a cool, cozy nap, The burglar broke into the Rabbaj-Zarrouk's House when they're sleeping, The burglar woke Fadwa the cat, Fadwa saw him, And she attacked the burglar, Nibbling his arm, The attack scared the burglar away, Yassin praised Fadwa. In 6 months she mated with a stray cat named Tarik, She peacefully mated Tarik the cat in a street, When Fadwa tried she went back into her home
In 2007. Fadwa gave birth to 3 kittens, Fadwa called the kittens Soumaya, Jaa and Isla, 3 months, she went into heat where she released many pheromones to attract a male, She saw a stray cat named Jasper, Fadwa mated with Jasper the cat, After mating, She went back into his home qagain, 3 Months, She gave birth to 3 kittens again, they named Monsef, Chaib and Padme, In 3 months, His owner, Yassin hid Fadwa's kitten, Jaa in the rig of a tractor at a gas station and let a trucker carry Jaa away,
In 2008, His owner Nawaz, has been promoted to Makeup Artist, In 3 months, His owner gave Fadwa's Kitten, Padme away for a hush money payment, A day she let his owners sleep together in a cozy bed, Fadwa took a nice nap.
In 2019, Fadwa diagnosed with heartworm, His owners didn't notice when Fadwa is behaving.
In 2020, His owner, Yassin took Fadwa to the vet, Where Fadwa diagnosed with heartworm, The treatment cost $199, His owner, Yassin, accepted to pay for his treatment, Despite this, she continued to suffer from heartworm,
In 2025, His owner, Yassin took Fadwa a last car ride to the vet, Since they couldn't done about his heartworm, she was going to be put down, Fadwa accepted his love, and the loving fate and his owner began to cry she went out of the euthanasia room, Fadwa was euthanized that day, Yassin took Fadwa's body and dropped out on the flower bed in his backyard
