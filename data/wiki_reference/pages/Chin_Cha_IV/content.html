Chin Cha IV (2968-July 4, 2990) was an Australian-American pilot.

Early life (2968-2990)[]

Cha was born in Townsville, Queensland. He was the only child of Scarlett Cha, formerly Sung Cha, and her ex-girlfriend Matilda Mulgrave.

In 2972, Cha emigrated to Houston, Texas.

In 2973, Cha enrolled at Hicks Elementary School.

In 2981, Cha enrolled at St. Chloe’s High School.

In 2982, Cha joined the St. Chloe’s High School football team.

In 2986, Cha enrolled at the University of Houston. He was a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi and played football for the Cougars.

In 2990, Cha graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Physics.

Career and death (2990)[]

In 2990, Cha became a pilot trainee for Tweedy Airways. He donated blood and contracted HIV. He tried to cure the disease by eating toad offered by a witch doctor, but died as a result.

Relationships and children[]

In 2978, Cha started dating a student named Amber Thrasher (2968-2980).

In 2980, Cha started dating a student named Amanda Bush (2968-after 3023), who became a grocer in 2991. They had two children:

  2. Chin Cha V
