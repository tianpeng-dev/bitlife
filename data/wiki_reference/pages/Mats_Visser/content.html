Background[]

Youngest son of former supermodels Adrian Visser and Ariana Pinto, Mats always had a passion for the arts and specifically acting,

Having lost his father at the young age of 16, Mats quickly decided to follow his dream of becoming a famous and successful actor.

He emigrated to Monaco at the age of 18 after securing some acting jobs as an extra in his home country.

He started his career at 18 and appeared in his first movie at 20.

Life and career[]

His dream came true when Mats debuted in the big screen at 20.

Mats had the versatility of a star, starring from Childrens movies and Musicals to the most terrifying Thriller and the most exceptional Sci-Fi movies, always delivering magic through the big screen.

He had his golden years from the age of 28 until the age of 35, having participated in 8 back-to-back award winning films.

With the support of his older brother and his still living mother, Mats fulfilled his desire of being successful and won multiple awards throughout his amazing career.

Mats lost his mother, his biggest fan, at the age of 52.

Amazingly, in all of his participations, Mats was either awarded or nominated for an award, another proof of his undeniable natural talent.

Mats accumulated 16 Best Actor awards of 26 nominations and 6 Best Supporting Actor awards of 14 nominations, alongside 13 award-winning films.

Loved by all industry and fellow cast members and directors, Mats was described as a kind soul, prompt to help everyone in need and passionate for his job.

Although he had multiple lovers in his prime years, Mats never married and chose to stay dedicated to acting and his friends and family.

He also wrote a series of books about his success and trajectory.

At 76, during his acceptance speech for the Best Actor award as Ezekiel Woodward-Cross in "Disregardable Me", Mats announced his retirement from acting after an excellent and memorable career, that ended the best way possible. He chose to end his career the same way it started, with a Childrens movie.

At 76, Mats also lost his older brother, Mason, to a stroke.

After retiring, he spent years travelling the world.

At 84, he had his first and only child, his son Thomas Visser, from his relationship with fellow actress and former co-star, Emma Bocelli.

He accumulated over 6 million followers on Facebook, 13 million on Instagram, 23 million subscribers on OnlyFans, 8 million followers on TikTok, 10 million on Twitch, 3 million on Twitter and 82 million subscribers on YouTube.

Mats Visser passed away at the age of 100, after suffering a stroke. His net worth at the time was of 12.69 Billion €.
