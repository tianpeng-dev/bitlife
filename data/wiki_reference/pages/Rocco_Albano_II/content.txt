Rocco Albano II
(3910-3989) was an American-Swedish environmental scientist, basketball player, businessman, and astronaut.

Early life (3910-3932)
[
]
Albano was born in New York City. He is the younger of two sons born to
Maurizio Albano II
and his girlfriend Lara Ricci. He was named for his granduncle, Rocco Albano I, who died as a child.
Albano went to Morning Glory Preparatory School and graduated in 3928.
In 3928, Albano was accepted to Brooklyn College on an athletic scholarship. He played basketball for the Bulldogs and was a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi. He graduated in 3932 with a Bachelor of Science in Physics.
Environmental science and sports careers (3932-3951)
[
]
In 3932, Albano briefly became an environmental scientist for Dover Corporation. He resigned shortly thereafter to join the Los Angeles Lickers as a center.
In 3934, Albano was traded to the Golden State Brawlers. He won two Sixth Man Awards, two championships, and Defensive Player of the Year while on the team.
In 3945, Albano was traded to the Denver Nugs. He won Defensive Player of the Year while on the team.
In 3947, Albano was traded to the Phoenix Fusions.
In 3949, Albano was released by the Fusions and joined the Washington Blizzards.
In 3951, Albano was released by the Blizzards.
Business career (3951-3954)
[
]
In 3951, Albano acquired a kitchenware company called Arcadia Kitchen World.
In 3954, Albano sold Arcadia Kitchen World, making a $29 million profit.
Astronaut career (3954-3973)
[
]
In 3954, Albano emigrated to Malmö, Skåne, Sweden. He enrolled at Umeå University in Umeå, Västerbotten.
In 3956, Albano graduated with an Associate’s Degree in Space Technology. He joined the Swedish National Space Agency as a cadet. He went on his first research mission, taking Taurus III to the International Space Station.
In 3957, Albano went on his first spacewalk, taking the Starship Nebula to repair Zelda III.
In 3960, Albano was promoted to captain.
In 3964, Albano was promoted to major.
In 3967, Albano went on his first lunar mission, taking the Starfinder to Mare Humorum.
In 3968, Albano was promoted to general.
In 3969, Albano went on his first Martian mission, taking the Galactic Nova to Memnonia Quadrangle.
In 3973, Albano resigned from SNSA.
Death
[
]
In 3989, Albano died from a stroke. His funeral was held in Malmö and his body was donated to science.
Relationships and children
[
]
From 3922 to 3954, Albano dated a student named Diana Palermo (3910-after 3973), who became a grocer in 3928. They had eight children, Monica II (3929-after 3990), Paolo II (3930-after 3990), Abigail (3931-after 3990), Ace (3932-after 3990), Aaliyah (3934-after 3990), Addison I (3935-3946), Allison (3935-after 3990), and Aisha Albano (3935-after 3990). Addison, Allison, and Aisha were identical triplets.
From 3954 to 3955, Albano dated a student named Ines Lindbergh (3924-3960). They had a daughter,
Addison Albano II
.
