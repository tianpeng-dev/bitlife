Virgil Kovacevic I (né Pratt, 3645-November 6, 3768) was an American real estate agent, baker, businessman, and politician who became the Governor of California.

Early life (3645-3663)[]

Kovacevic was born Virgil Pratt in Oakland, California.

In 3651, Kovacevic was adopted by Gloria Kovacevic II and her husband Ruben Rosales. His last name was changed to hers.

In 3663, Kovacevic graduated high school.

Real estate career (3663-3667)[]

In 3663, Kovacevic became a real estate agent for Desiree Hymen Realty.

In 3667, Kovacevic seduced his co-worker, Sara Feingold (3597-3684). He resigned shortly thereafter.

Baking and business careers (3667-3746)[]

In 3667, Kovacevic became a baker for Creed and Sons. He seduced four of his co-workers, Bradley Monroe (3610-3690), Brett Stewart (3598-3673), Reggie James-Legend (3598-3669), and Olivia Gobble (3597-3679). He resigned afterwards to acquire a cosmetics company called Ultimate Cosmetics.

In 3725, Kovacevic sold Ultimate Cosmetics and acquired a footwear company called Saguaro Sole Outlet.

In 3743, Kovacevic sold Saguaro and acquired a robotics company called AI Robotics.

In 3746, Kovacevic sold AI Robotics.

Political career (3746-3768)[]

In 3746, Kovacevic ran for election to the California State Board of Education. He beat Paula Cruise and was inaugurated.

In 3750, Kovacevic ran for reelection and beat Matt Evans.

In 3754, Kovacevic ran for Mayor of Oakland as a Democrat. He beat Republican candidate Vernon Grace and was inaugurated in January 3755.

In 3758, Kovacevic ran for reelection and beat Republican candidate Gabby Grace.

In 3762, Kovacevic ran for Governor of California as a Democrat, but lost the primary. He enrolled at Lincoln University and graduated in 3766 with a BA in English.

In 3766, Kovacevic ran for Governor of California as a Democrat and beat Republican candidate Al Fitzgerald.

Death[]

On November 6, 3768, Kovacevic died of old age.
