Leo Statham XXVII (5882-after 5938) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5882-5907)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He is the only child of Leo Statham XXVI and his ex-girlfriend Kathleen Taylor.

In 5900, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5904, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5907-5922)[]

In 5907, Statham graduated with a Doctorate in Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Shannon Brown.

In 5912, Statham ran for reelection and beat Lorna McCarthy.

In 5917, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Green Party candidate Jonathan O’Reilly.

In 5922, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5938, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5900 to 5901, Statham dated a student named Agnes Smyth (5882-after 5938). They had a son, Leo Statham XXVIII.
