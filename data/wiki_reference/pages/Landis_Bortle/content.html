Childhood and beginnings[]

Landis was born in Nashville to the prominent Bortle family during their historically and statistically wealthiest period. His father, Lawrence "Larry" Bortle (1946- TBD) was an Army officer with a huge passion for art history, which he studied in college. His mother, Abbigal "Abby" (neé TBD; 1946 - TBD) was a sommelier for Hareem's who, according to legend, introduced Landis to wine tasting at a young age. Landis had an older brother named Jimbo (born 1980), a police officer, a pet boxer named Payaso (TBD - TBD) and a German Shepherd named Payaso (TBD - TBD).

...

Bortle attended Vanderbilt University, Class of 2004, majoring in music. Having been awarded an athletic scholarship from high school, he was a proud member of the Commodores football team. He also joined the performing arts program, offering his acting skills in various fall/winter plays and spring musicals. Bortle further cemented his legacy at Vanderbilt as a member of Kappa Alpha Psi.
