Trinity Poland Peryak was an American and illegally Polish, Peruvian and Japanese criminal, hooligan, explorer and camp counsellor involved with the Russians. Born Trinity McCartney on February 22nd in New York City to Samantha and Mike McCartney, she grew up going on many hiking/outdoor trips, mainly to Poland, Peru, and Yakushima Island, which influenced her many hikes and partying sprees there when she grew up.

Trinity, always having a love of the outdoors, became a camp counsellor at 16 years old.

Immediately after turning 18, Trinity changed her name to Trinity Poland Peryak, a compound name after the “trinity” of her ideals of nature: the forests of Poland, the trails of Peru, and the island of Yakushima. The rest of her camp counsellor money mostly went towards running off to Poland, Peru, and Yakushima Island any chance she could get.

In her mid-40s she became affiliated with the Vasiliev family, a Russian spy family with headquarters in Kyoto.

Early life[]

Trinity was born to Samantha McCartney and Mike McCartney in New York on February 22nd. Her older sister Venus was 10 years old at the time.

The very next year, when Trinity was only 1 year old, Samantha and Mike were divorced. A year later Samantha would marry Brendon McQueen, changing her name to Samantha McQueen.

Trinity perhaps began her quite outdoorsy lifestyle at 7 years old after her mother Samantha bought her a pink Specialized Rockhopper bike, a sports bicycle named after a penquin, in her favourite colour, pink. On her first bike ride, Trinity took home a cricket that she found. She later found herself invited to compete in a bicycle race thrown by some random teenagers on the street and won though she was 7. Trinity loved beautiful rivers and lakes, usually riding past them to admire them on her bike rides. She would go on to pet a deer and take home an anteater.

Trinity was most likely also a 42ist, hence her randomness. Trinity’s best friend, Scarlett Lukes, once invited her over for a sleepover, in which at one point Scarlett asked her what the meaning of life was. Trinity responded with “The meaning of life is either 42 or everything is pointless!” After that Scarlett did not speak to her for the rest of the entire evening.

At 9 years old Trinity became a vegetarian.
