Carlton Roosevelt VIII (2290-2366) was an American baker, football player, politician, and actor.

Early life (2290-2312)[]

Roosevelt was born in Los Angeles, California. He was the second of 19 children born to Carlton Roosevelt VII and his girlfriend Bobbie Sue Banner.

In 2296, Roosevelt enrolled at O’Connor Elementary School.

In 2301, Roosevelt enrolled at Altitude Middle School.

In 2304, Roosevelt enrolled at Baseline Preparatory School.

In 2308, Roosevelt enrolled at the University of California, Los Angeles. He is a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi and plays football for the Bruins.

First baking and sports careers (2312-2332)[]

In 2312, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice. He was briefly a baker for the Palmer Company, then an apprentice baker for the Roberts Group. He joined the Baltimore Baddies as a safety.

In 2324, Roosevelt was traded to the Tennessee Dwarves.

In 2327, Roosevelt was traded to the Las Vegas Gamblers.

In 2330, Roosevelt was released by the Gamblers and joined the Los Angeles Batteries.

In 2332, Roosevelt was released by the Batteries and enrolled at UCLA School of Law.

Political career (2335-2343)[]

In 2335, Roosevelt graduated with a Doctorate of Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Los Angeles Unified School District Board of Education and beat Claire Butler.

In 2339, Roosevelt ran for reelection and beat Zion Tinkle.

Acting career (2343-2350)[]

In 2343, Roosevelt started playing Hayes in the TV show Wack Mirror.

In 2349, Roosevelt started playing Jesse Shufflebottom in Medieval Midsummers.

In 2350, Roosevelt retired from acting.

Second baking career (2351-2356)[]

In 2351, Roosevelt became an apprentice baker for Maestro, Inc.

In 2353, Roosevelt was promoted to baker.

In 2356, Roosevelt resigned.

Death[]

In 2366, Roosevelt died of natural causes. His body was donated to science.
