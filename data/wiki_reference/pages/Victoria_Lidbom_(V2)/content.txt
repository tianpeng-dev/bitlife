Victoria Lidbom
(October 31, TBD - June TBD, 2025) was an Australian wildlife expert, outdoorswoman, zookeeper, veterinarian, and military officer. A curious explorer since childhood, she initially struggled to afford her passions due to various financial responsibilities. This changed when she received a big inheritance from her late father, which she used to open her zoo, Lidbom's Sanctuary.
