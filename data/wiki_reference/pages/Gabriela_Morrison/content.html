Gabriela Morrison (née Mendoza, 3407-3502) was a Mexican mail carrier.

Early life (3407-3425)[]

Morrison was born Gabriela Mendoza in Nogales, Sonora. In 3410, she was adopted by Estrella Morrison, and the former’s name was changed to that of the latter.

Morrison graduated from high school in 3425.

Career (3425-3472)[]

In 3425, Morrison became a mail carrier for the City of Nogales. She retired in 3472.

Death[]

In 3502, Morrison died of old age.

Relationships and children[]

Morrison had a son, Fabian Hernandez (3432-3517), from a brief relationship with a man named Angel Hernandez.

In 3481, Morrison married an enlistee in the Mexican Army named Matias Cabrera (3461-3481). She has two stepchildren, Jaime (3479-3546) and Rosa Cabrera from Cabrera’s previous relationship with a woman named Benesh Kabir.
