Ronald, Marquess of Cockwallace (1944-June 13, 2029) was a British choreographer and nobleman.

Early life (1944-1968)[]

Ronald was born in Southampton, England. He was the only child of Ronald Ali, Sr. and his ex-girlfriend Spun Amy.

In 1962, Ronald enrolled at the University of Southampton.

In 1966, Ronald graduated with a Bachelor of Accountancy. He enrolled at London Business School.

Choreography career (1968-2012)[]

In 1968, Ronald graduated with a Master of Business Administration. He became a junior choreographer for Acoustic Dance Studio.

In 1974, Ronald was promoted to choreographer.

In 1980, Ronald was promoted to senior choreographer.

In 2012, Ronald retired.

Marquess of Cockwallace (2025-2029)[]

In 2025, Ronald married Eliza, Marchioness of Cockwallace (2002-2059), becoming Marquess of Cockwallace jure uxoris. They have two daughters:

  1. Mel, Countess of Warwick
  2. Ruby, Countess of Oxford (2027-2115)

On June 13, 2029, Ronald died of a stroke.
