Wakumi Kurosaku (February 9, 1992 - May 11, 2024) was a Japanese animator.

Early life and education[]

Wakumi was born in Fukuoka, Japan to hairdresser Makoto Kurosaki and trucker Yoshio Kurosaki (both born 1955). She had an older sister named Rin (born 1990), a financial analyst for Atlantic Dynamicss, and a pussycat named Jasper (1990 - 2009).

Career[]

Personal life and death[]

In 2017, Kurosaki married college classmate Daichi Suzuki (born 1992), a police detective. Their daughter, Tsubame, was born in 2022.

Kurosaki died of a stroke on May 11, 2024. She is survived by her parents, sister, husband, and daughter.
