Hunter Von Stroke (1970 - 2025) was an American lawyer, socialite, and serial killer.

Early Life[]

Hunter was born to the Von Stroke family and grew up spoiled by them.

In school, Hunter was a popular student, but had often become a victim of bullying, which led to him getting his sister, who would violently attack the bullies. This caused Hunter to grow an ego and feel like he was invincible.

Personal Life[]

Hunter had grown up with two friends, Laquitta Sprinkle and Juan Bonanno, but Hunter's egotism and petty tendencies drove them away, with Hunter and Juan becoming enemies before Hunter killed them both.

When he was 28, he was ghosted on a date with an influencer and developing a hatred for TikTok in particular because of it.

When he was 32, he formed a relationship with famous actress, Manila Cendes before the two would marry five years later, giving Hunter a massive fortune and connections. He also gained a stepson named Jesse, who he raised responsibly and mourned his death. After Hunter was sentenced to death, Manila tried two times to divorce Hunter, but he always convinced her to stay with him.

Career[]

In High School, Hunter briefly worked as a part time amusement park attendant up until he graduated Law School.

After graduating Law School, he worked as a lawyer for Thrasher & Romano for more than twenty years before he was arrested and sentenced to prison.

Thanks to his marriage with Manila, Hunter is famous as a socialite, although he ironically would not be recognized as such until after he was convicted.

Murders[]

When he was 14, he had gotten into an argument with Laquitta about sports and was so upset that he lured her to the woods, where he impaled her with a stick and ran away.

When he was 28, he was so angry that the influencer ghosted him that he did a Drive-By on a random astronomer.

When he was 37, he was so mad at a TikToker that he pushed her off a cliff.

When he was 39, he left a Full Moon party and shot the bouncer. This time, he was arrested and charged with two counts of murder, but he managed to be found not guilty and walked away Scott free.

A year later, Hunter had grown tired of Juan and impaled him with a stick he found just like Laquitta.

At age 42, Hunter decided to spice things up and killed a newspaper editor with fake teeth. He was arrested for this, but like before, he managed to get away with it.

At age 44, he killed a biker chick with the teeth and it cured his migraines somehow.

When he was 47, he beat up a male prostitute to death.

At age 50, new evidence would allow Hunter to be arrested and charged with murder and this time, Hunter was unable to talk himself out of it and he was sentenced to death when he was 58.

Death[]

Although he was scheduled to be executed on his 58th birthday, Hunter wound up being diagnosed with cancer in his bladder and died 3 years earlier than expected. Nobody attended his funeral, but the prisoners held him to a high regard because of his crimes.
