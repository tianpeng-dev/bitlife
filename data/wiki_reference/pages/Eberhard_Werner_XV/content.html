Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XV (4514-after 4570) was a Japanese hairdresser.

Early life (4514-4552)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. He is the only child of Eberhard Werner XIV and his ex-girlfriend Tsunade Nomura.

In 4532, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4552-4570)[]

In 4552, Werner became an apprentice hairdresser for Secret Salon Tokyo.

In 4554, Werner was promoted to hairdresser.

Werner was alive in 4570, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 4532 to 4533, Werner dated an Iraqi-born Japan Ground Self-Defense Force enlistee named Saida Hajjar (4514-after 4570). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner IX.

Werner has a son, Oda Nishikiyama (4541-after 4570), from a one-night stand with a museum worker named Chiaki Nishikiyama (4507-after 4551).
