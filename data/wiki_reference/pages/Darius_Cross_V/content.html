Darius Cross V (2217-2327) was an American-Argentinian supercentenarian.

Early life (2217-2240)[]

Cross was born in San Francisco, California. He was the youngest of four children born to Stephen Cross I and his girlfriend Ally Fudge.

Cross went to Blair Mendez High School and was captain of the football team.

In 2235, Cross enrolled at San Francisco State University on an athletic scholarship. He rushed AE Pi and played football for the Gators.

In 2239, Cross graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He briefly worked as a lifeguard for Matt Frost-Fisher Swimming Pool. He emigrated to Oranjestad, Aruba; then to Valladolid, Spain. He went to the gym. He donated blood. He bought a Golden Retriever puppy named Poker (2239-2247).

In 2240, Cross emigrated to Buenos Aires, Argentina. He went on vacation to the Maldives.

Death[]

In 2327, Cross died of a stroke.
