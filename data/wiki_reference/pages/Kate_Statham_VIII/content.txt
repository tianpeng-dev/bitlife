Kate Statham VIII
(5398-after 5457) was an Italian politician and jeweler.

Early life (5398-5417)
[
]
Statham was born in Naples, Italy. She was the only child of
Leo Statham V
and his ex-girlfriend Angelica Gallo.
Political career (5417-5437)
[
]
In 5417, Statham graduated from secondary school. She ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Liliana Cristoforetti.
In 5422, Statham ran for reelection and beat Emily Canali.
In 5427, Statham ran for Metropolitan Mayor of Naples as a Democrat and beat Italian Left candidate Thomas Verdi.
In 5432, Statham ran for reelection and beat 5 Star candidate Elena Gentileschi.
In 5437, Statham did not run for a third term.
Jewelry career (5438-5457)
[
]
In 5438, Statham became a sales associate for Nilde Jewelry.
In 5444, Statham was promoted to assistant manager.
In 5450, Statham was promoted to store manager.
Statham was alive in 5457, but her death date is unknown.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 5417 to 5418, Statham dated a jeweler named Emanuele Vicoli (5397-after 5457). They had two daughters,
Kate Statham IX
and Giulia Statham (5418-after 5457).
