Camilla Grieg II
(4254-December 31, 4330) was a German afro-punk singer, politician, and convicted murderer.

Early life (4254-4272)
[
]
Grieg was born in Stuttgart, Germany. She was the younger of two children born to
Liv Grieg
and the only born to her second boyfriend Toni Kraus.
In 4260, Grieg enrolled at Little Eggemann Primary School.
In 4264, Grieg enrolled at Johanna Voigt Middle School.
In 4267, Grieg created her Facebook, Twitch, and YouTube accounts. She trolled Steve Carell, Jim Gaffigan, Chris Hemsworth, Daniel Radcliffe, and Travis Scott on Facebook.
In 4269, Grieg enrolled at Werner Secondary School.
Music career (4272-4274)
[
]
In 4272, Grieg graduated from secondary school. She became addicted to gambling after playing blackjack at Kayak Stuttgart Casino. She joined the rock band Exodus. She released her first afro-punk album with the band,
Awhile on Main Street
. She became addicted to alcohol after drinking cognac at Timo’s Disco.
In 4273, Grieg released her second afro-punk album with Exodus,
The Short Run
, which was certified diamond by the Federal Music Industry Association in 4275. She was verified on Facebook and YouTube and became a Twitch Affiliate.
Political career and prison sentences (4274-present)
[
]
In 4274, Grieg left Exodus. She ran for election to the Baden-Württemberg School Board and beat Nora Simon. Less than a year later, she was arrested for murdering her first two husbands. She was convicted and sentenced to 39 years in prison. She was released in 4301 after snitching on another inmate.
Less than a year after being released from prison, Grieg was arrested for attempting to murder her fourth husband. She was convicted and sentenced to life in prison.
In 4309, Grieg escaped from prison in a riot. After she murdered her fourth husband, she was captured by German authorities and sent back to prison. She appealed her case to the Constitutional Court of Baden-Württemberg, and her sentence was reduced to 40 years. She served her sentence at Gung Ho Max Security Prison.
On December 31, 4330, Grieg died of old age in prison.

In 4274, Grieg and Werner started dating again and were married less than a year later. After they made love, she murdered him with a set of plastic vampire fangs.
Less than a year after murdering Werner, Grieg married an unemployed man named Lucas Billman (4256-4274). After they made love for the first time, she murdered him with a set of plastic vampire fangs.
In 4301, Grieg married a student named Alexander Klein (4282-4301). After they made love for the first time, she murdered him with a set of plastic vampire fangs.
Less than a year after murdering Klein, Grieg married a real estate agent named Luis Banner (4282-4309). After they made love for the first time after her escape from prison, she murdered him with a set of vampire fangs.
