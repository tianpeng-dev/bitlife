Wendy
(2011 - 2025) was an American Australian Cattle dog.
Life
[
]
Wendy was born in Salt Lake City, Utah in the backseat of her mother's owner's car. She and her mother bonded during the car ride together before they parted ways. Wendy's owners were the Stone-McDonagh family, which consisted of Logan Stone (born 1989), Victoria McDonagh (born 1991) and their two children, Camille (2010 - 2023) and Keenan (born 2013). Stone works as a dental hygienist, and McDonagh is a photographer. Logan and the kids had a loving relationship with Wendy. Victoria, on the other hand, did not show such kindness towards her for heavily inconspicuous reasons.
