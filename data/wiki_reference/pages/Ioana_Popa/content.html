Ioana Popa or Șerban (1427-1474) was the adoptive daughter of Vlad Șerban and Oana Constantin. Her parents divorced in 1435. Vlad died in 1439, when Ioana was only 12 and her mother died in 1446.

Painting[]

Ioana was a painter between 1451 until her death in 1474, an unusual job for a noble from that time.

Personal Life[]

She began dating Dorian Mureșan (1427-1496) in 1441, when both were only 14. They married in 1445. Together they got 5 children:

Ana Mureșan (1446-1460), who was murdered

Alexandra Mureșan (1447-?)

Elena Mureșan (1451-1468), who died of cancer of the eyeball

Barbu Mureșan (1453-1507)

Ilinca Mureșan (1459-?)

Love Affair[]

Ioana was known for her love adventure with Ștefan Stoica in 1463, though the relationship didn't last long, because Ioana didn't want to ruin her marriage with Dorian.

After Death[]

Ioana died in 1474 and her husband, Dorian, remarried to Timea Nagy (1460-1511) in 1478. They got no children.
