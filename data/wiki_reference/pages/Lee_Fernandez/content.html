Early life[]

Fernandez was born in Sheffield, England. His father is Alexander Fernandez. He is the ninth of 26 children. He went to First Secondary.

In 2334, Fernandez went to the University of Sheffield. He joined the art club. The same year, he accidentally switched to his son Albert because I need to get more sleep.

Career[]

In 2338, Fernandez graduated with a BS in Psychology. He became a school psychologist for the City of Sheffield. He retired in 2381.

Death[]

In 2396, Fernandez died of natural causes.

Relationship and children[]

In 2332, Fernandez started dating a student named Clair Wood-Ali (2316-2378), who later became a hairdresser. They have two sons, Owen Fernandez (b. 2333) and Albert Fernandez.
