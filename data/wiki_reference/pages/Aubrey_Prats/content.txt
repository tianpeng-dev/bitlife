Early life
[
]
Prats was born in Baltimore, Maryland. Her mother was
Nevaeh Stevens-Watson
. She went to the University of Maryland and graduated with a BS in finance. She then went to ESPC Business School in France and to space academy.
Astronaut career
[
]
In 1857, Prats became an astronaut and made her first publication. She would go on three spacewalks. She resigned in 1892.
Death
[
]
In 1916, Prats died of a stroke. Her body was donated to science.
Relationship and child
[
]
In 1853, Prats started dating a writer named William Meyer (1835-1924). They have a son,
Eddie Meyer
.
