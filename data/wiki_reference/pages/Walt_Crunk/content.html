Walter Crunk (1992-2075) was an American-British lobbyist and church singer.

Early life[]

Crunk was born in Baltimore, Maryland. Lauren Gulch became his stepmother when he was one. He went to the University of Maryland and graduated with a BS in Political Science in 2014.

Lobbying career[]

In 2014, Crunk became a lobbyist for Begonia Financial. He retired in 2035.

Music career[]

In 2040, Crunk signed with Impact Entertainment as a worship singer and released his debut church album. He retired after releasing his last album in 2058 and emigrated to Bristol, England.

Death[]

In 2075, Crunk died of old age. His funeral was held in Bristol.

Relationships and children[]

From 2013 to 2025, Crunk was married to a university student named Lily Smith (1986-after 2059), who later became an internal auditor. They had a son, Peyton Crunk (2025-after 2076). Less than a year after they divorced, Crunk and Smith started dating again. They broke up in 2058.

In 2058, Crunk started dating a trucker named Felicity Thompson (2030-after 2076). They had a daughter, Carson Crunk.
