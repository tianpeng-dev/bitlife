Sopa (2007 - 2025) was a Siamese cat who lived in Thailand.

Life[]

Sopa was born in Phuket, Thailand on the back porch of her mother's owner, immediately after which she was sold to Chaiya's Pet Company, where she was put on sale for 5,744.30 Thai Baht ($170 USD; $257.19 in 2025).
