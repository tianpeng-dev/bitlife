Leo Statham XXXII
(5978-after 6025) was an American-British real estate agent.
Early life (5978-5997)
[
]
Statham was born in Newark, New Jersey. He was the younger of two children born to
Leo Statham XXXI
and his second girlfriend Desiree Green.
In 5983, Statham enrolled at St. Bob’s Elementary School.
In 5989, Statham enrolled at St. James’s Middle School.
In 5990, a bully named Odell did not let Statham play soccer with the other kids at his school. In response, Statham roundhouse kicked Odell in the skull.
In 5992, Statham enrolled at Montgomery McCain High School. He learned to perform a karate chop.
In 5996, Statham graduated from high school. He earned a black belt in karate.
In 5997, Statham emigrated to Birmingham, England.
Career (6021-6025)
[
]
In 6021, Statham became a real estate agent for Woodcock and Bexley Real Estate.
Statham outlived his son, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 5988 to 6002, Statham dated a student named Carly Miller (5978-after 6025), who had 100% popularity. They had eight children, Kate XXIII (5995-after 6025), Katelyn (5995-after 6025), Brooklyn (5997-after 6025), Olivia (5998-after 6025), Anita (5999-after 6025), Jessalyn (6000-after 6025), Paula (6001-after 6025), and
Leo Statham XXXIII
