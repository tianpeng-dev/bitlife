Early life (2380-2398)[]

Cross was born Elias Johansson in Malmö, Sweden. In 2386, she was adopted by Kaelynn Cross II, making her the younger of her two children. Her last name was changed.

In 2396, Cross came out of the closet as a transgender girl. She changed her first name to Edith.

Cross went to Malmö Secondary School and graduated in 2398. After graduation, she emigrated to Jacksonville, Florida.

Career (2401-2446)[]

In 2401, Cross became a porn actor for First Erotica.

In 2446, Cross retired.

Death[]

In 2465, Cross died of natural causes.
