Jasmin Werner XVII
(4885-February 8, 4902) was a Greek convicted murderer.
Life (4885-4902)
[
]
Werner was born in Santorini, Greece. She is the youngest of eight children born to
Eberhard Werner XXV
and the only one born to his second girlfriend Kate Doukas.
In 4891, Werner enrolled at St. Olga’s Primary School.
In 4897, Werner enrolled at Sofia Hero Middle School.
In 4900, Werner enrolled at Diligent Preparatory School.
In 4901, Werner strangled two of her enemies, Sofia Kalafas (4885-4901) and Elizabeth Kellis (4885-4901), to death. She was sentenced to two years in juvenile detention and 21 years in prison.
On February 8, 4902, Werner escaped from juvenile detention. She tried to strangle her enemy, Irene Demopoulos (4885-after February 8, 4902). Demopoulos killed Werner in self-defense.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 4895 to 4901, Werner dated a student named Homer Kairis (4885-after 4921). They had a son,
Eberhard Werner XXVI
.
