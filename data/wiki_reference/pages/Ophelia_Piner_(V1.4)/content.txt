Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (1957 - 2031) and Cooper Piner (1958 - 2043). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist. She had an older brother, Steven (1979 - 2055), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 2015).
