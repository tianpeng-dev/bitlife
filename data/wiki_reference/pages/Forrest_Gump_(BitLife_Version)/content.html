Forrest Gump (February 9, 1949 - October 10, 2022) was an American football player, soldier, and fisherman.

Early life[]

Forrest was born in Greenbow, Alabama to LaSaondra (1917 - 2004) and Paul Gump (1891 - 1962). His father was a firefighter, and his mother was a receptionist. He had a big sister named Tara (born 1940), a mailwoman. The Gumps had an Irish setter named Ebody (d. 1951), and a goldfish named Kyrie (d. 1957).

Personal life and death[]

Gump met Jenna Henderson (better known as Jenny) at the age of five, who became his best friend. They then started a romantic relationship at age eleven. During his third year in the Army in 1969, Forrest proposed to Jenny, to which she accepted. They married a year later and went on to have three children and six grandchildren together.
