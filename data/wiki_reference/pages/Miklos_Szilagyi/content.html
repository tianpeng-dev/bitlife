Early life (up to 18 years)[]

Miklos Szilagyi was born on April 25, 2001 in Budapest, Hungary. He was born into a simple family. His parents divorced when the boy was 2 years old. Then both his parents got married, but both his stepmother and stepfather hated Miklos. They had their own children. Miklos studied perfectly at school, he had many friends and was very popular at school. When Miklos was 11 years old, his father died, and it was very hard for Miklos because his stepfather hated him, but before his father's death, he maintained good communication with his dad. At the age of 10, Miklos had a girlfriend, with whom they were together for 9 years until Miklos died.

Personal life[]

Quite a few things have happened in Miklos' personal life. At the age of 18, he moved to Hamburg, Germany. There he bought his house and was already preparing to propose to the girl. At the age of 18, by the way, his childhood dream came true! He became a professional football player. He played for Hamburg in the Bundesliga, although he played only 1 year...

Soccer career[]

Miklos joined Hamburg in 2019 when he was 18. Then Hamburg was in last place in the league, but Miklos didn't care, it didn't really matter to him whether he would be a top footballer or play at an average level, the main thing for him was that the dream had come true. In 2020, at the end of the 19/20 season, Hamburg took 11th place out of 18.

Death[]

2020. Miklos Szilagyi was preparing for a new match, when suddenly he receives an offer. It says: we pay you 500,000 euros, and you deliberately lose. Miklos refused and reported the proponent... And as a result, the proponent shot Miklos. On October 9, 2020, Miklos Szilagyi died at the age of 19, his life was cut short and it was quite sad, but Hamburg fans will remember the striker as one of the brightest, albeit unrealized talent. RIP, Miklos Szilagyi!
