Childhood diseases and childhood ailments are diseases and ailments that can be contracted as a child. Some of the diseases can also be contracted by adults, while others cannot. In the School Update, school nurses may treat some of these if they are mild enough. Given that the condition is not curable by the school nurse, she will instead advise you to seek medical treatment. Some can be cured by Medical Doctor while others go away after a few years of aging up. If the character ages up, they will die in a fatal disease as young as the age of 2. Dying from one of these when the character is under 30 will give the Unlucky ribbon.
