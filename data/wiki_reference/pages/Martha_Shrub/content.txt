Martha Shrub
(born 1986) is a member of New Haven Public Schools who started her career as an assistant principal. She is the youngest child of the late U.S. President
Henry Shrub
, her older siblings being
Grayson
and
Julia
.
Early life and education
[
]
Martha was born in 1986 to Henry Shrub and
Emma Smith
, during her father's second year in office as Mayor of New Haven. Her grandparents were
Jackie Shrub
and
Hannah Shrub
.
Career
[
]
Shrub completed graduate school in 2011. She then got a job at New Haven Public Schools as an assistant principal.
