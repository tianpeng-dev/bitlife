Early life[]

At, age 2 he got a stepfather Dante Vasquez, a 24-year old unemployed person (1899-) they went to Hongkong for their honeymoon. He got a new, Staffordshire named Spike (1908-). At age, 5 he suffered a mild ear infection. At age 6, he got enrolled into Valentina Alvarez Primary School, a public school. At age, 7 he came back home from school to notice Spike escaped, so he looked for him, and found him across the street. At age, 28 while lighting fireworks, one of the fireworks were faulty, and while his mom set them up it exploded and killed her instantly, Jose and his stepfather attended her funeral, at age 9 after the sadden unfortunate he took a walk to calm himself down. Around, age 10 Spike passed away in his sleep. At age 13, in music class he played the violin, at age 14 he skipped school, and helped girl cheat on her test, around that year he started dating a girl named Carmen Ramos, at age 15 he got pneumonia bedridden, once he got better age 16, he got a part time job as a newspaper delivery person, unfortunaly Carmen broke up with him so he just let it slide. At age 18, he graduated, and quit his part time job, and got into the army.

Military career[]

At, age 19 he got promoted to Private second class, with a salary of $7,208. The next year he got into deployment to a treacherous region of south Africa, he served his country by successfully completing it and got a award of honor. At age, 21 he got promoted to Private First class with a salary of $8,064. Shortly, months after his 23rd birthday he got promoted to Specialist. After, he walked 5 grueling miles of marching in the desert, his mind was delirious, His feet are blistering, and he is falling behind the rest of the platoon, so he caught up. At around age 35, he got promoted to Sergeant Major with a salary of $25,927. He served his country by, completing a deployment to hostile territory deep within Serbia. He screamed at a fellow soldier to stop just before she was about to pick up a IED. His platoon starts taking heavy fire when suddenly his M4 rifle jams, At age, 59 he grabbed his backup pistol after. Around age 62, he retired from the Mexican Army.
