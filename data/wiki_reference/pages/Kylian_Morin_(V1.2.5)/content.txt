Kylian Morin
(June 25, 1917 - May 24, 1984) was a French Navy sailor and politician who, prior to joining the military, served as the school board director of his hometown in Nice. He was initially believed to be killed in action in May of 1944 during World War II. However, forty years after his "death," it was discovered that he had faked his death so he could defect to the United States, where he spent the last decade of his life under a new identity,
Kyle Marvinson
, with a fabricated background of being a US Navy war hero and a local fisherman in a coastal New England seaside town. Fearing that he would be considered a traitor by his country, Morin committed suicide.
