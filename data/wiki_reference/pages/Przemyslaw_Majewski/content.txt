Early life (1973 - 1991)
[
]
Przemyslaw Majewski was born on June 1, 1973 in Warsaw, the capital of Poland. His parents are Wojciech Majewski (1935-2020) and Julia Wojciechowski (1938-2014). The family was quite rich, and they also had a cat, Purri. At the age of 4 , he had his first friend .At the age of 6, the boy went to a boys-only school. He studied perfectly, and this greatly influenced his life in the future. At the age of 17, Przemyslaw got a girlfriend, Milena Zawadzki. He graduated from high school in 1991.
Personal life
[
]
Since childhood, Przemyslaw dreamed of becoming a politician. He entered the university in 1995 at the age of 22 because there were no places to enter a political university. He graduated at the age of 26 (1999). And at 27 he became a dad! He had his first child, a boy Tobias Majewski (born in 2000). In the same year he became a wedding planner. He didn't want to work there, but he decided to wait a few years until there was a headmaster election at the school. In 2001, he bought a house for himself. In 2003, he was elected headmaster of the school! And then the most interesting part of his life began.
Politician career
[
]
When he was elected director, he began to make every effort to become the mayor of the city in the future. His dream came true at the age of 34 (in 2007). He became a politician, or rather the mayor of Warsaw! He began to invest efforts, to develop the city, the standard of living. And most citizens supported him. In 2011, his term came to an end, and he did not want to become the mayor of Warsaw again, he wanted to become the governor of the province. He lost the election, but a year later he became mayor again. A lot of residents supported all his decisions. In 2016, his second term came to an end, and he tried to become governor again, but he was not elected. And in the same year he again became the mayor of Warsaw. In 2020, he tried to become governor again, but after failure, he again became mayor of the city. It was his last term.
Death
[
]
Przemyslaw Majewski died unexpectedly at the age of 50 from a stroke. His wife found his body and immediately called an ambulance, but it was not possible to save him. RIP, Przemyslaw!
