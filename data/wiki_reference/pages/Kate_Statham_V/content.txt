Kate Statham IV
and her ex-boyfriend Simon Henderson.
Pornography career (5333-5337)
[

In 5333, Statham graduated from secondary school. She emigrated to Tampa, Florida. She became a porn cameraman for Kingfisher Adult Theater.
In 5337, Statham resigned and enrolled at the University of South Florida.
Music career (5341-5375)
[
]
In 5341, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. She signed with Crown Studios as a rapper and adopted the stage name Savagery. She released her first album,
Gangster’s Condominium
, which went gold in 5342.
In 5342, Savagery released her second album,
Right Outta BitLife
, which went gold.
In 5344, Savagery released her third album,
Deadly Scuffle for Love
, which went gold in 5345.
In 5347, Savagery released her fourth album,
Confessions of Kate Statham V
, which went double platinum within a year and went diamond in 5349.
Savagery was alive in 5375, but her death date is unknown.
