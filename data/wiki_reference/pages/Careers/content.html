Careers

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)


Careers are forms of employment that provide characters with varying levels of income. Certain careers pay more than others but normally require a greater level of education and certain schooling. Income is the same number of money, no matter what currency. Some careers rapidly provide more social media followers and a massive increase in income after reaching a certain 'breakthrough' (Acting and Composing) while others rely on a gradually increasing wage (Doctor and Corporate's President). Most medical careers require a PhD but can give a rewarding wage. Choosing careers relies on steady planning and where you live. Emigration also forces you to get a new career. Most careers also provide raises based on performance or, depending on the job, looks and smarts. Another key thing to remember is that jobs typically go from Jr. X to X to Sr. X or Apprentice X to X. Careers may be collected if the character spends at least 20 years in the career. There are certain Achievements a player can get if they become a certain career and as of April 2020, there is a whole section in achievements about jobs and military.

Interviews[]

Characters are required to attend an interview to get hired. If the character has previously been in a fraternity/sorority either in United States or Canada, they could be hired on the spot without an interview. You may or may not be hired based on the choices you choose and sometimes randomized results of each choice.
