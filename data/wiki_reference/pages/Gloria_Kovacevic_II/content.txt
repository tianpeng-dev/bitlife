Gloria Kovacevic II (3597-3681) was a Mexican-American travel agent and businesswoman.

Early life (3597-3619)
[
]
Kovacevic was born in Nogales, Sonora. Her parents were
José Antonio Kovacevic
and his girlfriend Petra Espinoza. She was named for her aunt.
Kovacevic went to the University of Sonora and graduated in 3619 with a BA in Dance.
Travel agency career (3619-3651)
[
]
In 3617, Kovacevic became a travel agent for Nogales Travel. She became a regional manager before retiring in 3651.
Business career (3651-3668)
[
]
In 3651, Kovacevic emigrated to Oakland, California. She acquired a pharmaceutical company called Nurse Hyde’s Pharmaceuticals.
In 3668, Nurse Hyde’s went bankrupt and Kovacevic retired.
Death
[
]
In 3681, Kovacevic died of natural causes. Her funeral was held in Oakland and her ashes were scattered on a golf course.
Marriage and children
[
]
In 3641, Kovacevic married a fireman named Ruben Rosales (3587-3663). They had three adopted children: Juan del Rio (3633-3704, adopted c. 3641), Victoria Rosas (3641-3730, adopted c. 3642), and
Virgil Kovacevic
.
