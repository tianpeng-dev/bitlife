Sabrina Mayweather (January 25, 1979 - May 19, 2021) was an American pilot.

Early life[]

Mayweather was born on January 25, 1979 in Los Angels to makeup artist Riley Mayweather and police officer Master Mayweather. She had a close friendship with her best friend, Mikiyah Basio-Evans.

Career[]

In 2001, upon graduating from university, Mayweather took the job as a pilot trainee for Butler Airways with a salary of nearly $50K. She served their airline for eighteen years, receiving two promotions:

- Co-Pilot (2009)

- Pilot (2015)

Personal life[]

Mayweather began dating her friend, Biff Hymen, in 1989, when they were ten. Despite his several attempts to break up with her during high school, she always managed to win him back. They married in 1999. They had five children together and remained married until Mayweather's death.

=Legal issues[]

In 2018, Mayweather showed concern for her son, Caleb's, qualifications for college. She bribed a college official $12K to doctor Caleb. She was arrested and charged with bribery, but she was found not guilty. Until her death, Mayweather never stopped believing that she had done the right the right thing for Caleb.

Death[]

On May 19, 2021, while Mayweather was on a joyride in her glider, an anonymous hacker overtook control of the plane. She tried her best to remain calm, but was unable to avoid a fatal crash. She was killed almost instantly. She was 42. The cause of death was ruled as murder. Mayweather is survived by her mother, her husband, her children, her siblings, half siblings and her neices and nephews.

Eventually, Sabrina's family recieved a hefty life insurance policy of over $1 million from the crash.
