Early Life
[
]
Bobby Stein was born on October 14th, 1931 to Willy Stein (1873-1950) and Angelica Stein (1913) in Buffalo, New York. He had an older brother named Alexander Stein (1930).In 1936, Bobby was enrolled in Little Frost Elementary School. 6 years later, he started middle school at Meridian Middle School. One year after that, his parents would divorce. In 1944 his mom married Pervis Garfield (1910), who had two daughters, Autumn (1935) and Parker (1937) Garfield. In 1945, Bobby started high school at Shaft High School, where he played baseball and eventually became the co-captain in 1948. In 1949, Bobby graduated from high school and went to University to get a degree in Marketing, he tutored people, took care of the elder, and mowed lawns that year. In 1950, Bobby’s father died. Two years later, Bobby became leader of the cross country team and started dating Ava, he encountered a stray cat that same year and took him in and named him Connor (1947 - 1957).
Family Life
[
]
In 1953, Bobby proposed to Ava at an Elegant Restaurant and got married in a church. They went to Venice, Italy for their honeymoon. 3 years later, Bobby and Ava bought their first house. On March 23rd, 1960, Ava gave birth to Henry Stein. And 3 years later, on July 5th, 1963, his daughter, Elizabeth, would be born.
Career
[
]
In 1968, Green Grove was founded, but it went bankrupt a year later. In 1973, Bobby was offered and accepted the position of CEO at DadCorps Robotics. One year later, he became famous. He remained CEO of DadCorps until 1988. That same year, Natural Market was founded. A few years later, he sold the company to a hipster. In 1993, Bobby became the CEO of Buffalo Brewing Company and served for 6 years. In 2010, Bobby became the CEO of Mary Juana Cannabis Co.
Later Life
[
]
In 1993, his son, Henry, married Miley Lukes. 2 years later, Miley gave birth to Bobby’s first grandson, Derek on the 12th of March, 1995. In 1997, Parker died after being trapped and killed in a bridge that collapsed. Bobby’s niece, Hailey, died from cancer of the pancreas that same year. In 2009, Robby witnessed his little sister, Oprah, choking on a cucumber, and he successfully performed the Heimlich maneuver on her. In 2014, Bobby got into a major car accident and burst his spine, his mouth was grazed, and his torso pierced, he barely survived the incident. He would pass away peacefully on November 25th, 2031.
