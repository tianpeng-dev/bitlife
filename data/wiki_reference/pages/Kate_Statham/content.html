Kate Statham, Sr. (5069-5178) was a British insurance agent, physician, baker, and politician.

Early life (5069-5087)[]

Statham was born in Blackpool, England. She was the youngest of three children born to Jesse Statham and the youngest of two born to his second wife, Amelia Seaman. In 5075, Jesse Statham married Jasmin Werner XXX, making Kate the seventh oldest of her 19 children.

Insurance career (5087-5098)[]

In 5087, Statham became a junior insurance agent for Absolute Insurance Group.

In 5098, Statham resigned and enrolled at Lancaster University. She graduated with a Bachelor in Psychology in 5102 and a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery in 5111.

Medical and baking careers (5111-5112)[]

In 5111, Statham became a family physician for Arrival Medical Center. She became best friends with her co-worker, a physician named Anna Webb-Beardsley (b. 5062). She resigned to become a baker for Mr. Choice. She robbed Blackpool Savings and Loan with a bayonet while disguised as a police officer, and escaped in a Bentley Mulsanne.

Political career (5112-5167)[]

In 5112, Statham ran for election to the Lancashire County Council as an independent candidate. She beat Chelsea Stevens, another independent candidate, and was inaugurated.

In 5117, Statham ran for reelection and beat Charlie Cox.

In 5122, Statham ran for a third term and beat Melissa Bowman.

In 5127, Statham ran for a fourth term and beat Lucrecia Gonzales.

In 5132, Statham ran for a fifth term and beat Heidi Ladywell.

In 5137, Statham ran for a sixth term and beat Simon Carter.

In 5142, Statham ran for a seventh term and beat Bertrand Kane.

In 5147, Statham ran for an eighth term and beat Joan Patel-Suckley.

In 5152, Statham ran for a ninth term and beat Stanley Thigpen.

In 5157, Statham ran for a 10th term and beat Nathan Tomlinson.

In 5162, Statham ran for an 11th term and beat Poppy Grimwood.

In 5167, Statham did not run for a 12th term.

Death[]

In 5178, Statham died of natural causes.

Relationships and children[]

From 5097 to 5099, Statham dated her co-worker, an insurance agent named Rod Sheringham, Sr. (5067-5138). They had two children, Rod Sheringham, Jr. (5098-5188) and Kate Statham, Jr.

In 5111, Statham started dating her co-worker, a baker named Samuel Wallace (5036-5111), who she murdered with a set of plastic vampire teeth.

In 5111, Statham briefly dated her co-worker, a baker named Holly Malik (5087-after 5162). The former cheated on the latter with a receptionist named Hunter Hayes (5076-after 5162). After Statham and Malik broke up, they had a one night stand.

From 5111 to 5112, Statham dated a mailman named Rupert Cockwallace (5092-after 5162). In 5112, they had a threesome with a database administrator named Leo Richards-Blunt (5049-5161).
