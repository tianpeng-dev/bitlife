Heirlooms
Launched on iOS:
2019
Family Heirlooms
are a variety of items a player may find in the attic minigame at the beginning of the game by searching the room with a flashlight. Once detected with the flashlight, these heirlooms will be added into the player's
Assets
with a brief description, estimated price value, and rarity. Players will then have the option to: Discard, Donate, Play, Refurbish or Sell Items. As of 2024, there are 182 different heirlooms.
Certain heirlooms may give additional bonuses for your character. An example is the 'Lucky Dice', which despite has a low selling point if sold, should be kept to increase success in the
Lottery
and
Casino
.
Note: This list is not complete as the number of heirlooms are constantly subjected to change by the developers.

Prices may vary based on the character's country, duration in the family, and condition of the heirloom.

Tips
[
]
Heirlooms only exist within
Generations
of BitLife, so starting a new life outside the family legacy will not let allow players to keep acquired heirlooms, yet they may still be viewed in the "Heirlooms" category located in the Main Menu.
If player cheats (e.g. change the time) to get more heirlooms, they will only obtain common items such as the alarm clock, fine China set, prayer beads, teddy bear, umbrella, and wind chime.
There is no limit to how many heirlooms a character can possess.
The character who a player selects to start the new generation will receive all the heirlooms, unless the player writes their will decreeing all assets to whichever child they choose.
The value of the heirlooms are added to your character's net worth even if the character does not sell the heirlooms. Holding valuable heirlooms can help a character achieve the
Rich
or
Loaded
Ribbon
.
Characters do not have access to their heirlooms until they are 11.
Heirloom condition may decline over the years or decline suddenly if a character damages it while playing with it. Condition will also improve if the heirloom is refurbished yet this does not apply to all items.
Lending an heirloom to a friend may result in a decline of condition, or result in complete loss if they lose it.
Donating heirlooms increases
Karma
.
If you play with the Holy Grail, you get all stats maxed and you get the
Sacrilege
Achievement
.
Originally the Heirloom search minigame popped up as soon as the game was opened, but after an update, the Heirloom minigame appeared in a yellow tab located on the bottom left screen and resets at 12am.
Players can occasionally attain an additional heirloom through watching an ad.
