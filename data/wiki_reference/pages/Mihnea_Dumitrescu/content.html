Mihnea Dumitrescu (1608-1696) was a son of Maria Sandu-Mureșan and Andi Dumitrescu.

Career[]

He led a luxurios life, beofre being bankrupt with millions in debt. In 1685 he became a great Logofethe (Vice President) until his death in 1696.

Private life[]

He married Ralu Anghel (1613-?) in 1658 and adopted her daughter,

Anastasia (1645-?), who had an illegitimate son in 1689,Radu (1689-?) and in 1690 she adopted another son, Octavian (1689-?).

The couple didn't have biological children, so they adopted a boy in 1659:

David Dumitrescu (1654-?).
