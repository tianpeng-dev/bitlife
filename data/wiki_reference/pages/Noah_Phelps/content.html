Noah Phelps (born 1944) is an American businessman and retired operations analyst. He is the younger child of firefighter and cameraman Stanley Phelps.

Early Life[]

Noah was born in 1944 in Houston, the second child of Stanley Phelps and Gretchen Hanks, two years after the birth of his big brother, Colt. The family spent much time together, even when their parents were busy. Noah studied finance at his university.

Career[]

Noah was hired as a junior operations analyst for First Electronics in 1967. He was promoted to Operations Analyst in 1977, and then to Sr. Operations Analyst in 1987.

Noah retired in 2010, having spanned a successful 43 year career in business.

Personal life[]

Noah met and dated his future wife, Gissile Goodhead, when she was in college. They married in 1983. Their first daughter, Lisa, was born in 1984, and their second daughter, Breanna, was born in 1991. Their first son, Garrett, was born in 1988, and their second son, Luis, was born in 1993. He also has two nieces, Colt's daughters Mikiyah and Serena.

Noah was traumatized by Colt's sudden death in 1996 following a snake bite. Some friends say it was an accident, while others say it was murder. As of today, Noah still maintains some confusion, in addition to his grief, over the loss of his brother.
