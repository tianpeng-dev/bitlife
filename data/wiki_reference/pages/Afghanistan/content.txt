Afghanistan
is a country added to BitLife in June 2019. Its three default cities are the capital, Kabul, along with Jalalabad and Kandahar.

Information
[
]
Being an Islam-based country, female characters in BitLife will wear hijabs when they are adults. This is shared with females in
Iran
,
Iraq
,
UAE
, or
Saudi Arabia
. If a female does not originally have one before emigrating, her emoji will be replaced with one that does. If she emigrates to a non-Islam based country, her hijab may be removed. Female lovers or hook-ups who originate from one of these countries will have her hijab.

Death
[
]
Afghanistan's war zones are mirrored in the game. It is common to see family, or even your own character, killed by stray bullets or other symbols that represent the warfare seen in real life. As a result, emigrating from Afghanistan is highly suggested.
