Leo Statham XX (5740-after 5800) was an Irish politician and insurance agent.

Early life (5740-5758)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the only child of Leo Statham XIX and his ex-girlfriend Aisling Taffe-O’Connor.

Political career (5758-5778)[]

In 5758, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Amelia O’Reilly.

In 5763, Statham ran for reelection and beat Donal Price-Joyce.

In 5768, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Nicola Skinnider.

In 5773, Statham ran for reelection and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Noah Fithen.

In 5778, Statham did not run for a third term.

Insurance agency career (5779-5800)[]

In 5779, Statham became a junior insurance agent for Seamen Insurance.

In 5782, Statham was promoted to insurance agent.

In 5785, Statham was promoted to senior insurance agent.

Statham was alive in 5800, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 5758 to 5760, Statham dated a firewoman named Erin Murphy (5740-after 5800). They had two children, Kate XVII (5759-after 5800) and Leo Statham XXI.
