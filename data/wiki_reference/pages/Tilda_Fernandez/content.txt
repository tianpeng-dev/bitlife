Early life (2474-2492)
[
]
Fernandez was born in Berlin, Germany. Her parents are
Finn Fernandez
and his ex-girlfriend Elisa Sauer. She went to Bauer Secondary School.
Political career (2492-2512)
[
]
In 2492, Fernandez ran for election to the Berlin GEB. She defeated Freda Roth and was inaugurated the same year.
In 2496, Fernandez ran for reelection and beat Ritter Moller.
In 2500, Fernandez ran for a third term and beat Mia Bahn.
In 2504, Fernandez ran for a fourth term and beat Liam Sauer.
In 2508, Fernandez ran for a fifth term and beat Leopold Thomas.
In 2512, Fernandez announced that she was not running for a sixth term.
Grocery career (2513-2531)
[
]
In 2513, Fernandez became an apprentice grocer for Aria Supermarket.
In 2515, Fernandez was promoted to grocer.
Fernandez was alive in 2531, but her date of death is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 2484 to 2491, Fernandez dated a student named Carlo Achen (2474-after 2531), who later became a receptionist. They had a daughter,
Lisa Achen
.
