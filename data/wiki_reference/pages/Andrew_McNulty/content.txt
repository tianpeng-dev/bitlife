Andrew McNulty
(November 2nd, 2021 - December 17th, 2144) was a famous rockstar. He was born after his mother’s birth control failed.
He was the son of Autumn McNulty and Dustin McNulty, younger brother of Jason McNulty, and stepbrother of Zach and DeAndre Reyes

Early Life
[
]
Andrew McNulty was born on November 2nd, 2021, in New York, United States to Autumn McNulty/Reyes (1996-2079) a firefighter, and Dustin McNulty (1995-2078), a police officer.
Personal Life
[
]
At the age of 14, Andrew McNulty began dating his future wife, Claire Bush. At the age of 19, their first child, Riley Bush-McNulty, was born out of wedlock. 10 years later, Andrew married Claire and stayed together until Claire’s death in 2131. He had 6 other children and 8 grandchildren.
Career
[
]
At the age of 22, Andrew McNulty became a writer for Stevens Books. He first went viral at the age of 26 after posting a video of himself doing a choreographed dance to a Lil Baby Song. He became a famous author at the age of 29, occasionally appearing in TV commercials. At the age of 34, Andrew McNulty joined the band known then as The Boll Weevils, which would then be renamed to The Tasmanian Devils 3 years later.
