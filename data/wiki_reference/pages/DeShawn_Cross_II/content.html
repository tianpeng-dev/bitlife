DeShawn Cross II (2642-October 6, 2742) was an American football player.

Early life (2642-2664)[]

Cross was born in Miami, Florida. He was the only child of Boomer Cross II and his second partner Oprah Copeland. He was named for his 9x great-granduncle.

Cross went to Horseshoe Preparatory School and was captain of the football team before graduating in 2660.

Cross went to Florida International University and played football for the Panthers before graduating in 2664 with a BA in Finance.

Career (2664-2682)[]

In 2664, Cross joined the Jacksonville Jackers as a quarterback.

In 2667, Cross was traded to the Indianapolis Stallions.

In 2668, Cross was released by the Stallions and joined the Las Vegas Burglars.

In 2670, Cross was traded to the Carolina T’Challas.

In 2682, Cross was released by the T’Challas.

Death[]

On October 6, 2742, Cross died of old age.
