Oliver Burger-Cocker was a history teacher in Halifax, Canada. He worked was a teacher for 75 years, making him the longest tenured teacher in the school, city, state, and country history.

Early Years[]

On November 13, 1979 after 9 entire months of pain, 30 year old Nina Burger gave birth to her son whom she named Oliver. He was born out of wedlock and never learned who his father was. The year after he was born, his mother got married to James Evans, a 38 year old physical therapist, but that marriage ended in 1984. Nina never got remarried. He went to the Radiance Primary School from ages 6 to 12. In 1987, his mother gave birth to his younger brother named Dickie. He started dating Justine Beaver when he was 10 but would later come out as gay.

1997-2079[]

He graduated from high school in 1997. He went to study history at university, his mother paid for his tuition. He came out as a gay in 1999, broke up with his childhood girlfriend Justine, and started dating Ethan Bieber. In 2001, he became a teacher after 4 years of hard work. A year later he got engaged to Ethan, but the pair would not get married until 2008. He invested into many bonds. In 2030, his mother died at the age of 81. In 2036, him and Ethan got a divorce despite Oliver's persistence and trust in the marriage, Ethan still went ahead and made him sign the divorce papers. Oliver however did not stay single for long and later that same year started dating Percy King. They got engaged in 2039 but Percy died in 2045. Percy's death left Oliver depressed, but he stayed strong for himself and his students. He acquired an alcohol addiction in 2057, and never got over it. He repeatedly rejected attempts to retire, and the city allowed him to stay despite the fact that he was now in his eighties. His alcohol addiction got so terrible to the point where he got alcohol poisoning and had several more stays at the doctor after that. After 75 years of teaching, Oliver was actually forced to retire from being a teacher in 2076 when he was 97 years old, by the end of his career, he had taught at least 4 of his earliest student's great-great-great grandchildren. The year before he was forced to retire, he had got engaged to a man named Arthur Cocker, having dated since 2071. They did not get married until Oliver was 105 in the year 2084.

Later years and death[]

Oliver did not do much in his later years except spend time with his husband and at the gym. He died on December 23, 2091 in his sleep. This was just 3 weeks after his husband had died. Nobody attended his funeral, and he had outlived his younger brother by 23 years.
