Characters, also commonly referred to as 'Bitizens' are the human characters of the game. The player controls one of these characters and has a number of close relationships that includes blood relatives and partners while the rest of humanity is normally seen in one time actions or events.

Originally starting off as yellow emojis with simple hair depending on the gender, over the past year, the emojis have since evolved into a fully diverse selection, with over 19 possible skin choices, 9 eye colors, and 34 hairstyle options.

Based on Unicode's diverse human emojis, the appearance changes as it ages.

New emojis[]

Starting from update 1.30 (iOS), Bitizens have since received new emojis, while in 1.32 (iOS), customization has been implemented onto the new emojis. Before the updates, the emojis had remained uniform with either brown, blonde, or red hair, blue or brown eyes, and either straight or curly hair for both genders.
