Jasmin Werner XIII (4761-after 4810) was a Japanese receptionist.

Early life (4761-4799)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. She was the only child of Jasmin Werner XII and her ex-boyfriend Riku Matsumoto.

In 4779, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4799-4810)[]

In 4799, Werner became a receptionist for Atlantic Holdings.

Werner was alive in 4810, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4779 to 4780, Werner dated an unemployed man named Kota Yokoyama (4761-after 4810). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner XIV.
