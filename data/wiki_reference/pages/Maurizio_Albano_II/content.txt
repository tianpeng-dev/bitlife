Early life (3890-3909)
[
]
Albano was born in Genoa, Italy. He is the younger of two children born to
Paolo Albano
and his girlfriend Alice Ferrari.
Albano graduated secondary school in 3909.
Business career (3909-3922)
[
]
In 3909, Albano emigrated to New York City. He acquired a clothing outlet called New York House of Fashion.
In 3922, Albano sold New York House of Fashion. He made a profit of $161 million.
Jewelry career (3923-3956)
[
]
In 3923, Albano became a sales associate for Crunk Brothers Jewelry.
In 3929, Albano was promoted to assistant manager.
In 3935, Albano was promoted to store manager.
In 3956, Albano retired.
Death
[
]
In 3963, Albano died of natural causes. His funeral was held in New York.
Relationship and children
[
]
In 3908, Albano started dating a student named Lara Ricci (3890-after 3973). They had two sons, Nario II (3909-after 3973) and
Rocco Albano II
.
