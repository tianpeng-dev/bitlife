Henrik Larsson (4113-after 4160) was a Swedish museum worker.

Early life (4113-4135)[]

Larsson was born in Malmö, Sweden. He is the younger of two children born to Olivia Jansson and her boyfriend Mohamed Larsson.

In 4120, Larsson enrolled at Samuelsson Primary School.

In 4129, Larsson enrolled at Matt Qvist Secondary School. He graduated in 4131.

In 4131, Larsson ran for election to the Malmö School Board, but lost to Liam Jakobsson. He enrolled at Malmö University Faculty of Culture and Society and graduated in 4135 with a BA in English.

Career (4135-4160)[]

In 4135, Larsson became a docent for Malmö Smith Museum.

In 4141, Larsson was promoted to exhibit associate.

In 4147, Larsson was promoted to assistant curator.

In 4153, Larsson was promoted to curator.

In 4160, Larsson was promoted to museum director. His death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 4123 to 4128, Larsson dated a student named Lilly Vikander (4113-after 4132). They had a daughter from their first relationship, Alva (4127-after 4160).

In 4131, Larsson and Vikander started dating again. They have a daughter from their second relationship, Nellie Larsson.
