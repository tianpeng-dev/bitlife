Early life and education[]

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (2007 - 2081) and Cooper Piner (2008 - 2093). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist. She had an older brother, Steven (2029 - 2105), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 2055).
