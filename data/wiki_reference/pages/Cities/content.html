Cities

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)

Last Updated on iOS:

October 2020
Cities in BitLife are where characters live, work and socialize in the game. Most cities are relatively the same, with the same types of jobs and amenities. However, depending on which city you live in can change many factors in the game such as name, race, schooling stages, and nationality. Many things within the city, such as the public library, school district, and community college are named after whatever city the character is in. It is expensive to move to another city, however, it is possible with the “emigrate” option. You don’t have any control over what city you go to other than the country it is located in and characters cannot move to another city in the country. Cities can also be traveled to using the “vacation“ option, however, there is no effect depending on the city and some cities can be vacation spots but not default cities to live in. In June 2019, an option to add custom cities was featured in the game. There is a secret ribbon called the Teammate ribbon if your character is from either city of the United States where Candywriter produces BitLife and your character is an App Developer. There is an achievement if a character visits Winnipeg, Canada, on vacation. The Teammate ribbon can be unlocked if a character is an app developer from Tucson or Miami in the United States.
