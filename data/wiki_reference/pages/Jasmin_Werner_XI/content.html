Jasmin Werner XI (4723-after 4779) was a Japanese photographer.

Early life (4723-4761)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. She was the only child of Eberhard Werner XXIII and his ex-girlfriend Tsubame Hirano.

In 4741, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4761-4779)[]

In 4761, Werner became an apprentice photographer for Tokyo Gazette.

In 4763, Werner was promoted to photographer.

Werner was alive in 4779, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4741 to 4742, Werner dated a wedding planner named Sora Imai (4717-after 4779). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner XII.
