Anita Garcia (2295 - 2330) was an American exotic dancer, museum worker and businesswoman. She was the daughter of baseball player Dash Garcia, granddaughter of football player Jeremy Garcia, great-granddaughter of actor Jorge Garcia, great-great-granddaughter of actress Desiree Garcia, great-great-great-granddaughter of art gallery professional Lynn Garcia, great-great-great-great-granddaughter of magician Odell Garcia, great-great-great-great-great-granddaughter of businessman Eduardo Garcia, great-great-great-great-great-great-granddaughter of baseball player Colt Garcia, and great-great-great-great-great-great-great-granddaughter of musician Donald Garcia.
