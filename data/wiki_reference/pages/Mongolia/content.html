When was it added to iOS?

December 19, 2019
Mongolia is a country that was on BitLife in December 2019. Its default cities are only the capital, Ulaanbaatar.

In December 2019 on iOS; new countries were added. Along with Mongolia; there was also Austria, Bahamas, Costa Rica, DR Congo, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Kuwait, Lebanon, Nepal, Ukraine, and Vietnam. This was added in the Friends Update.

Information[]

Name List[]

Mongolia, along with Nigeria, have some of the longest names for people in the game. Some names base on Mongolia as the Mongol Empire, such as Kublai, Temujin (Temujiin), Genghis, Khan, Mandukhai, etc.
