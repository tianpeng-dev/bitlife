Mongolia

country
that was on BitLife in December 2019. Its default cities are only the capital, Ulaanbaatar.
In December 2019 on iOS; new countries were added. Along with Mongolia; there was also Austria, Bahamas, Costa Rica, DR Congo, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Kuwait, Lebanon, Nepal, Ukraine, and Vietnam. This was added in the
Friends
Update.
