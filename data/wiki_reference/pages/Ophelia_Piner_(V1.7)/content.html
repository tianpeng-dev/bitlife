Ophelia Piner (October 22, 1962 - December 2, 2061) was an American professional basketball player.

Early life and education[]

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (1927 - 2001) and Cooper Piner (1928 - 2003). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist and Korean War veteran of the Marine Corps. She had an older brother, Steven (1949 - 2025), a Vietnam veteran-turned-grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 1985).
