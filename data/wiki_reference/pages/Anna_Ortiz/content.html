Anna Ortiz-Washington (born 1952) is a retired American banker. She is the widow of reggae musician and military officer Toby Washington.

Personal life[]

Oritz had med Washington during her freshman year of high school. After being friends for thirteen years, they started their romantic relationship in 1979. That same year, Anna accepted Toby's proposal, and they married in a church, going to Honolulu for their honeymoon. They had four children together and remained married until Washington's death in 2022:

  • Autumn (born 1981) - Marriage counselor for Alamo Psychiatric Clinic
  • DJ (born 1982) - Graphic designer for Hunter & Sons
  • Serinity (born 1984) - Mailwoman
  • Honey (born 1991) - Legal secretary for Gump & Reed
