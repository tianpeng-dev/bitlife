Not to be confused with his grandfather, Otello Werner.

Otello Werner II (4695-after 4743) was a Japanese male stripper.

Early life (4695-4715)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. He is the younger of two children born to Greta Werner V and her ex-boyfriend Masahisa Konosuke.

In 4701, Werner enrolled at Toucan Elementary School.

In 4707, Werner enrolled at Sugiyama Lower Secondary School.

In 4710, Werner enrolled at Tokyo Upper Secondary School.

In 4713, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4715-4743)[]

In 4715, Werner became a male stripper for Natsuki’s Showgirls.

Werner was alive in 4743, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 4705 to 4712, Werner dated a student named Fuka Yamada (4695-after 4743). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner VI (4712-after 4743).

From 4713 to 4714, Werner and Yamada started dating again. They had a son, Eberhard Werner X, formerly known as Greta Werner VI.
