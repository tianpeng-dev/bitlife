Merchandise

Merchandise pertaining to BitLife: Life Simulator was added to Redbubble[1] in October 2019. It was said to have clothing and stickers and can be found when tapping Bitlife Community... on the lower left screen of the character's journal. Its store's headquarters is based in Miami, Florida.
