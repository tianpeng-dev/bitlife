The Alternative Doctor is a group of other types of doctors that are not a regular doctor. There are four alternative doctors; the acupuncturist, the chiropractor, colon hydrotherapy, and the homeopathic doctor. The acupuncturist works best on conditions such as fibromyalgia, insomnia, and scoliosis. The chiropractor works best on gout, bunions, and a few conditions that the acupuncturist works on as well. The homeopathic doctor can sometimes treat conditions but is mostly ineffective and very rarely works, although visiting him improves health. Colon hydrotherapy improves health and has a chance to cure colon cancer, but does not cure any other illnesses.
