Maxwell Thomas Brewer (September 7, 1922 - August 12, 1951), also known by the stage name Brewster Max, was an American disk jockey. Originally wanting to be a professional singer, he became a DJ after being mercilessly rejected by labels. In 1948, he decided to take a break from his career to join the Marines; he wanted to become a war hero to lift his chances of becoming a singer He died in combat in Korea in August 1951.
