Kiana Roosevelt (3054-after 3124) was a German-American mover, nun, and convicted burglar.

Early life (3054-3072)[]

Roosevelt was born in Dortmund, Germany. She was the youngest of eight children born to Willy Roosevelt and his ex-girlfriend Becky Nix.

In 3060, Roosevelt enrolled at Little Pfeiffer Primary School.

In 3061, Roosevelt adopted a goldfish named Dixie (3060-3066).

In 3064, Roosevelt enrolled at Eggemann Middle School.

In 3069, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Florian’s Secondary School.

In 3072, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school.

Prison sentence (3075-3076)[]

In 3075, Roosevelt was briefly a mover for Degler and Sons. She burgled a daycare worker’s house. She plead guilty and was sentenced to 1 year in prison. She was caught attempting to escape and her sentence was increased to 3 years. She is serving her sentence at Fassbender Detention Center.

In 3076, Roosevelt escaped from prison. She murdered a drunk. She emigrated to New Orleans, Louisiana and briefly became a nun for St. Ellie Disciples.

Convent (3095-3120)[]

In 3095, Roosevelt became a nun for St. Lisa Convent.

In 3120, Roosevelt retired.

Later life[]

Roosevelt was alive in 3124, but her death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

Roosevelt has an adopted daughter named Sophie Roosevelt (née Walter, 3067-3124, adopted 3075).

From 3076 to 3079, Roosevelt dated a student named Cleveland Browser (3058-after 3124). They had three daughters, Venus Roosevelt XIII, Alice Browser (3078-after 3124), and Bonnie Browser (3079-after 3124).

From 3079 to 3094, Roosevelt dated an Austrian-American massage therapist named Florian Eder (3055-after 3094). They had 13 children, Charles (3080-after 3124), Donna (3081-after 3124), Emma (3082-after 3124), Frank (3083-after 3124), George (3084-3096), Hilda (3085-after 3124), Isabelle (3085-after 3124), Jolene (3086-after 3124), Kate (3087-after 3124), Lana (3088-after 3124), Mina (3090-after 3124), Natalie (3091-after 3124), and Olivia Eder (3094-after 3124).
