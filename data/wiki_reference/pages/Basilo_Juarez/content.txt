Basilo Juarez
(October 29, 1971 - January 12, 2022) was a Mexican choreographer who danced for Discount Dance Troup for three decades.

Early life
[
]
Born in Malatzán during the honeymoon of his parents, Luz Mendoza (born 1951), a sailor in the Mexican Navy, and Vincente Juarez (born 1948), a retail salesman, Basilo was the first child of three (biological). His two younger brothers are Juan (born 1972), a Mexican Coast Guard enlistee, and Marco (born 1977), a restaurant worker for Thoroughbred Diner. Shortly after Marco's birth, Basilo's parents divorced. In 1980, Vincente married Isabel Pacheco (born 1947), a developer with two children.
Basilo grew up watching performances from the greatest dancers, including Michael Jackson, Gene Kelly, James Brown, Patrick Swayze, among others. It inspired him to try dancing himself. Although he never found a dance club or theater program throughout the educational period of his life, he found a place in the drama kids clique. He graduated university with a dance major in 1993.
Career
[
]
Juarez was hired for the role of Jr. Choreographer at Discount Dance Troupe in 1993. He got promoted in 1999 to Choreographer, and in 2005 to Sr. Choreographer.
Personal life
[
]
Juarez enjoyed parties. Besides throwing house parties, he went clubbing at several night clubs, showing off his dance skills to other guests.
Juarez enjoyed the single life until his mid-to-late thirties. In 2007, while clubbing at Oak Tree, he met Victoria Campos (born 1969), a mail carrier, and had a one-night stand with her. The following year, they began dating after her first husband's death. Their twin daughters, Mariangel and Mayra, were born in 2014, and they married two years later.
Health issues and death
[
]
In 2006, Juarez and a friend, Sergio Martinez, got drunk at a pool party and twerked on a partygoer who passed out. From that time until his death, Juarez was addicted to alcohol.
In 2021, Juarez was diagnosed with throat cancer. He died on January 12, 2022. He was survived by his parents, siblings, wife and children.
