Treasures are part of the Black Market Expansion Pack, released on July 31, 2023. Treasures are rare items acquired by the player at estate sales, auctions, OnlyFans or on the black market. Some treasures are found only via one method whereas others can be found via multiple. Once collected and appraised, they can be displayed at the player's museum. As of September 2023, there are 351 unique treasures. The sections below contain lists of all known antiques. These lists are not complete.
