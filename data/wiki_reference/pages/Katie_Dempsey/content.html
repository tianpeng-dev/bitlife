Katherine "Katie" Dempsey (1910 - 1951) was a famous actress.

Youth and career[]

Dempsey was born in 1910. She began acting between 18 and 22 years old, having took lessons during childhood and from higher education. Her professional film career began during the early stages of the Great Depression.

Suicide[]

In March of 1951, a new film starring Dempsey was being filmed in San Lucas, Cuba. She was found dead by fellow co-stars in her trailer, having committed suicide. The circumstances were mysterious, and the reason for Katie's suicide remains unsolved.
