Edward Gulch Jr. (May 30, 1948 - July 11, 2021) was an American professional football player. He spent his 19-season career (1970-1989) as a linebacker for the Indianapolis Fillies.

Early life[]

Eddie was born in New York to the latest generation of the prominent Gulch family. His mother, Aretha (1919 - 2008), was a real estate agent. His father, Eddie Sr. (1920 - 1999), was a schoolteacher who was lucky enough to have Jr. in some of his classes and served in numerous educational divisions of the Armed Forces during World War II. The second child of five, his four siblings include sister Luna (born 1943), sister Marilyn (born 1951), brother Dwayne (born 1955), and brother Jerry (born 1958). Luna is a make-up artist for Wonfrey Productions, Marilyn is a restaurant worker, Dwayne is a wedding planner, and Jerry is an insurance agent for Sapphire Insurance, a subsidiary of one of the big insurance companies, though exactly which is currently undisclosed.

Playing career[]

Gulch signed up for the 1970 NFL draft. He was picked by the Indianapolis Fillies as a linebacker.
