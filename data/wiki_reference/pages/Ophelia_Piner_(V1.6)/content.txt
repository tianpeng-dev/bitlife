Early life and education

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (1937 - 2011) and Cooper Piner (1938 - 2013). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist and Vietnam veteran of the Marine Corps. She had an older brother, Steven (1959 - 2035), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 1995).
