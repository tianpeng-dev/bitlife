Not to be confused with Greta Werner or her other namesakes.

Greta Werner V (4676-after 4714) was a Japanese firefighter.

Early life (4676-4696)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. She was the younger of two children born to Otello Werner and his ex-girlfriend Aika Tanaka.

In 4682, Werner enrolled at Kakuji Kubo Elementary School.

In 4688, Werner enrolled at Sugiyama Lower Secondary School.

In 4691, Werner enrolled at Marathon Preparatory School.

In 4694, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4696-4714)[]

In 4696, Werner became a probationary firefighter for the Tokyo Fire Department.

In 4698, Werner was promoted to firefighter.

In 4700, Werner was promoted to fire equipment operator.

In 4702, Werner was promoted to lieutenant.

In 4704, Werner was promoted to captain.

In 4706, Werner was promoted to battalion chief.

In 4708, Werner was promoted to assistant chief.

In 4710, Werner was promoted to fire chief.

Werner was alive in 4714, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 4686 to 4693, Werner dated a student named Masahisa Konosuke (4676-after 4714). They had a son, Eberhard Werner IX (4693-after 4714).

From 4694 to 4695, Werner and Konosuke dated again. They had a son, Otello Werner II.
