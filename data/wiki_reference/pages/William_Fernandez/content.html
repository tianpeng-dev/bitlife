William Fernandez (2205-2278) was an American-British soccer player.

Early life[]

Fernandez was born in Detroit, Michigan. His mother is Ana Jones. In 2223, he emigrated to Sheffield, England. In 2227, he graduated from the University of Sheffield with a BS in Political Science.

Career[]

In 2227, Fernandez joined Palace FC as a defender. He retired in 2237.

Death[]

In 2278, Fernandez died of a stroke. He was buried in Sheffield. At the time of his death, his net worth was over 900 million pounds.

Relationship and child[]

In 2223, Fernandez started dating a student named Amelia Woodgate (2205-2259), who later became a chef. They have a son, Bob Fernandez.
