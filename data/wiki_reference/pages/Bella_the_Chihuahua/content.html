Bella (2004 - 2025) was a chihuahua who lived in Kansas City with the Fuentes-Shaft family.

Life background[]

Pet store years[]

Bella was born in Sonata Pet Shop, a Kansas City pet store, specifically in the staff bathroom. She was put up for sale ay a starting price of $205. She got along well with one of the Sonata's employees, Mr. Egbert. Bella made good friends with some of the other pets for sale, including Pepper, a Siberian husky, and Nathan, a border collie. Between six and nine months old, she practiced every type of dog trick until she was decently skilled at it.

When she was nine months old, Bella caught the attention of a flight attendant named Antonio Fuentes-Shaft (born 1980). He purchased her and took her in to live with him and his family, including his then-wife, Giselle Phelps (born 1974), a real estate agent, and their sons, Scott, who now works as a mail carrier, and Rocky. Antonio and Giselle had another son named Dennis (born 2005) before divorcing in 2008. Shortly after the divorce, Antonio had a daughter named Faith.
