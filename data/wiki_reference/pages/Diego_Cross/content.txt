Diego Cross
(2412-after 2491) was an American hairdresser.

Early life (2412-2430)
[
]
Cross was born Diego Alvarez in Jacksonville, Florida.
In 2415, Cross was adopted by
Raven Cross
, making him her only child. His last name was changed.
In 2430, Cross graduated from high school.
Career (2432-2474)
[
]
In 2432, Cross became an apprentice hairdresser for Nucleus Salon Jacksonville.
In 2435, Cross was promoted to hairdresser.
In 2474, Cross retired.
Prison stint (2489)
[
]
In 2489, Cross burgled an opera singer’s house and was sentenced to one year in prison. He briefly served a sentence at Light Jail before escaping.
Cross was alive in 2491, but his death date is unknown.
Marriage and children
[
]
From 2433 to 2457, Cross was married to a grocer named Ginger Roberts (2408-2489). They had three children:
Robert Cross (2449-after 2491), graphic designer
Zander Pistof (2452-after 2491, adopted), jeweler
Quincy Cross
