Eberhard Werner XXIX (4998-5078) was a Mexican-Canadian crime scene analyst.

Early life (4998-5024)[]

Werner was born in Guadalajara, Mexico. He is the second of 30 children born to Greta Werner VIII and the oldest of 29 born to her husband Oscar Chavez.

In 5016, Werner enrolled at the University of Guadalajara and majored in biology.

In 5020, Werner graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology. He enrolled at Autonomous University of Guadalajara Dental School.

First career (5024-5028)[]

In 5024, Werner graduated with a Doctorate in Dental Science. He became a crime scene analyst for the Guadalajara Police Department.

In 5028, Werner resigned. He emigrated to Saskatoon, Saskatchewan.

Second career (5032-5054)[]

In 5032, Werner became a crime scene analyst for the Saskatoon Police Service.

In 5038, Werner was promoted to crime scene technician.

In 5044, Werner was promoted to forensic investigator.

In 5054, Werner retired.

Death[]

In 5078, Werner died of old age. His daughter could not attend his funeral as she was in prison.

Relationship and children[]

From 5028 to 5031, Werner started dating a trucker named Ellen Singh (5004-5084). They had three children, Eberhard XXX (5029-after 5097), Laurent (5030-after 5097), and Jasmin Werner XXX.
