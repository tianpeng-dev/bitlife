Early life and education
[
]
Emma was born in San Francisco and grew up in Oregon. Her mother was a homemaker while her father worked as a car salesman, often producing and selling military vehicles to contribute to the war effort. She was a cheerleader in high school and later attended University of New Haven (UNH), studying in political science and graduated at the top of her class. She attended Cornell for law school.
