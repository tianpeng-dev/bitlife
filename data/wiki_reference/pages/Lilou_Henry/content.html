Lilou Henry (2921-after 2977) was a French-British soccer player.

Early life (2921-2943)[]

Henry was born in Lyon, France. Her parents were Ambre Marie and her ex-boyfriend Adam Henry.

In 2923, Henry emigrated to Manchester, England.

In 2939, Henry emigrated back to Lyon. She enrolled at Les Chartreux and was on the rugby team. She graduated in 2943 with a Licentiate in Psychology.

Sports career (2943-2958)[]

In 2943, Henry emigrated back to Manchester and joined Blues FC as a forward. She has won five championships, two Golden Boots, and a Ballon d’Or while on the team.

In 2958, Henry retired.

Psychology career (2959-2977)[]

In 2959, Henry became a school psychologist for Académie de Lyon.

Henry was alive in 2977, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2939 to 2940, Henry dated a student named Antoine Rolland (b. 2913), who later became an interior designer. They have a son, Jean Paul Rolland.
