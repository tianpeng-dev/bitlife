Mason Smart (September 6, 1922 - January 14, 2022) was an American computer programmer, factory worker, IT Support, cashier and mortician.

Early life[]

Mason was born in Bridgeport. His mother, Penelope (1900 - 1978) was a flight attenant. His father, Levi (1879 - 1965), was a museum worker. He had two sisters, Raven (1931 - 2015), a hairdresser for Secret Salon Bridgeport, and Sandra (born 1940), a camerawoman for Eden Broadcasting Service.
