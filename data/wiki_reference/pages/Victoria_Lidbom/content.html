Victoria Lidbom (October 31, 1980 - June 20, 2025) was an Australian wildlife expert, outdoorswoman, zookeeper and veterinarian. A curious explorer since childhood, she initially struggled to afford her passions due to various financial responsibilities. This changed when she received a big inheritance from her late father, which she used to open her zoo, Lidbom's Sanctuary, which she ran for twenty years from 2002 until selling it in 2022.

Childhood[]

Lidbom was born in the City of Knox to firewoman Aurora Lidbom (born 1946) and senior Army enlistee Gavin Lidbom (1925 - 2002). She had an older brother named Eric (born 1976), a teacher.
