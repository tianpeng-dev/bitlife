Ha-Eun Cha XII (3427-July 18, 3444) was an American student.

Life (3427-3444)[]

Cha was born in Phoenix, Arizona. She was the only child of Benjamin Cha II and his girlfriend Holly Chapman.

In 3438, Cha enrolled at Robert Banks Middle School.

In 3441, Cha enrolled at Phoenix High School.

In 3444, Cha insulted her classmate, Ava. In response, Ava attacked her with a lightsaber, killing her.

Relationship and child[]

In 3438, Cha started dating a student named Blair Lawless, Sr. (3427-after 3452), who became an insurance agent in 3446. They had a son:
