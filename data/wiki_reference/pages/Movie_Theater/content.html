Movie Theatre

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)

Movie Theater is an activity in BitLife. You can go to the movies with someone, or by yourself. In "Activities", there is an option for "Movie Theater" and adult characters can watch a comedy movie or a documentary movie on iOS or a random movie on Android. Comedy movies boost happiness on iOS and documentary movies boost smarts. There are different types of movie genres on Android and they all will boost Happiness. Child characters will watch Family movies only. As a child, friends and cool kids may ask your character to watch a movie with them and your character may accept or decline. Custom characters' names may appear in movie titles.

If you choose to go to the movie theater with someone else, it will boost your relationship with them regardless of how you both enjoyed the movie. A person with a low relationship will not want to go to the theater with your character. A player who is not a Bitizen will have to watch an ad to use the Movie Theater. Your character may only attend one movie with a family member or lover per year. The Movie Buff ribbon is available to iOS players who watch many movies, both alone and with other people.
