Lila Campbell (born 1936) is an American businesswoman and retired insurance agent. She is the ex-wife of the late dancer Donald Howsen and ex-sister-in-law of attorney Derek Howsen.

Early life[]

Lila was born in Virginia around the time of one of the many dust storms of the Great Depression. Her family moved around a lot due to the migration, sometimes living in the safer states like California. Eventually, after the Depression came to an end, they resettled back in Virginia to repair their family's heritage.

Personal life[]

Campbell and Howsen met in 1954 when she was 18 and he was 20. They married a year later and had four children together before divorcing in 1964:

  • Tyreese (born 1958) - Wedding planner for Wood & Sons
  • Gabby (born 1959) - Hairdresser for Atmosphere Salon Arlington County
  • DJ (born 1960) - Sales associate for Long-Rojas Jeweler
  • Isabella (born 1962) - Marine biologist for Bush & Sons

When Donald died in 2019, Campbell was invited to the funeral. She accepted the invitation and spoke at the services, saying that she had still loved him, even though they were not together anymore.
