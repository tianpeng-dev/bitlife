Kylian Morin
(June 25, 1997 - May 24, 2024) was a French Navy sailor and politician who, prior to joining the military, served as the school board director of his hometown in Nice.

Early life
[
]
Kylian was born in Nice to a restaurant worker named Sarah Morin (born 1970). His father was unknown. A year after Kylian's birth, Sarah married
Napoleon Antionette
(born 1970), a soldier in the French Army, particularly in the French Foreign Legion. He had a half-sister, Julia, born in 2007, a year before Sarah and Napoleon divorced. Kylian also grew up with a loving pet cat named Mavis (d. 2010).
Political career
[
]
Morin graduated from secondary school in 2015. That same summer, he announced that he was running for the position of school board director for Nice's public school system. Using part of his fortune, he budgeted ₤100,000 towards his campaign against his American-born opponent, Mary Hatch Bailey (born 1953), whom he treated with indifference. He won the election, thus becoming one the country's youngest politicians at the age of 18.

Personal life
[
]
One day in 2017, after completing his daily duties as school board director, Morin engaged in a threesome with a childhood friend Louis Muller (born 1997) and pornographic actress Francesca Vera (born 1993). He supposedly forgot about the interaction for a few years until he suddenly remembered one day in 2020. At that point, he got back in touch with Vera, and the two began a romantic relationship that would last until Morin's death.
