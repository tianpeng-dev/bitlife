Marshall Cross II (2852-2933) was an Italian-French soccer player and financial analyst.

Early life (2852-2875)[]

Cross was born in Genoa, Italy. He was the younger of two brothers born to Cristiano Cross II and his girlfriend Bobbie Sue Morgan-Johnson. He was named for his 13x great-granduncle.

Cross went to Caruso Secondary School and was on the soccer team for a year before graduating in 2871.

Cross went to the University of Genoa and played soccer for CUS Genoa before graduating with a BS in Mathematics in 2875.

Sports career (2875-2892)[]

In 2875, Cross joined Genoa as a forward.

In 2881, Cross was released by Genoa and joined Milan.

In 2892, Cross was released by Milan and emigrated to Lyon, France.

Financial analysis career (2905-2914)[]

In 2905, Cross became a junior financial analyst for Eager Beaver Designs.

In 2914, Cross retired.

Death[]

In 2933, Cross died of old age.

Relationships and children[]

From 2870 to 2892, Cross dated his Qatari-Italian classmate, Aisha Ansari (2850-after 2904), who became a mailwoman in 2872. They had four children:

  1. Miranda Cross II (2871-2879)
  2. Tina Cross II (2872-2960), authoress
  3. Ramona Cross II (2873-2877)
  4. Jorge Cross II (2874-2955), fireman

In 2892, Cross started dating a student named Mila Martinez (2868-2951), who became a marine biologist in 2893. They had two children:

  1. Natalie Cross II (2893-after 2983), marriage counselor
  2. Luther Cross II
