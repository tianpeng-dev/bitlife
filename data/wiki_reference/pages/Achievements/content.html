Achievements

Launched on iOS:

January 12, 2019

Launched on Android:

September 23, 2019
Achievements are special rewards for completing tasks in BitLife. Achievements can only be unlocked once. Some achievements take hard work while some are easy and others are random chance.

As of May 3, 2026, there are 410 achievements to unlock and 45 different categories of achievements.(ADD NEW BADGES AND CATEGORIES)

Only one has been removed from the game, listed in the end of the page.
