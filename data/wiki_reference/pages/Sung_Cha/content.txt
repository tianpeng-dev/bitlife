Scarlett Cha
, formerly
Sung Cha
(2951-after 2990) was an Australian-American exotic dancer, soldier, and fast food worker.

Early life (2951-2969)
[
]
Cha was born Sung Cha in Townsville, Queensland. She was the youngest of three children born to
Cha Chin, Jr.
and his girlfriend Han Oh Ae.
In 2956, Cha enrolled at Martin Primary School.
In 2964, Cha enrolled at Townsville Secondary School.
In 2969, Cha graduated from secondary school.
Stripping career (2969-2972)
[
]
In 2969, Cha came out of the closet as a transgender woman and changed her first name to Scarlett. She became an exotic dancer for the Red Carpet Club.
In 2972, Cha emigrated to Houston, Texas. She bought a Ford Mustang, rode a bicycle, got a spray tan, and prayed to the BitLife developers.
Military career (2972-2976)
[
]
In 2972, Cha joined the US Army as a private.
In 2973, Cha was promoted to private second class.
In 2975, Cha was promoted to private first class.
In 2976, Cha was honorably discharged. She went on vacation to Boston, Massachusetts and went to the horse races.
Fast food career (2976-2990)
[
]
In 2976, Cha became a crew member for McCoastal.
In 2978, Cha was promoted to shift manager.
In 2980, Cha was promoted to restaurant manager.
In 2982, Cha was promoted to general manager.
Cha outlived her son, but her death date is unknown.
Relationships and child
[
]
From 2961 to 2972, Cha dated a student named Matilda Mulgrave (2951-after 2990). They had a son:
Chin Cha IV
In 2969, Cha seduced her co-worker, an exotic dancer named Ariana White (2949-after 2976).
