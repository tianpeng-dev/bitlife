Julian Cross II (2523-2603) was a Peruvian-American insurance agent.

Early life (2523-2541)[]

Cross was born in Lima, Peru. He was the youngest of four children born to Amani Cross II and his girlfriend Ally Parker. He was named after his 6x great-granduncle.

Cross went to Neon Preparatory School and graduated in 2541. After graduating, he emigrated to Miami, Florida.

Career (2556-2589)[]

In 2556, Cross became a junior insurance agent for Bronze Insurance Group.

In 2559, Cross was promoted to insurance agent.

In 2562, Cross was promoted to senior insurance agent.

In 2589, Cross retired.

Death[]

In 2603, Cross died of a stroke.

Marriage, relationship, and child[]

In 2540, Cross started dating his classmate, Cynthia Chicote (2523-2601), who became a hairdresser in 2542. They had two children:

  1. Aubrey Cross II (2541-2616), Navy enlistee
  2. Andreas Cross III

In 2603, Cross married an environmental scientist named Kim Hux (2580-after 2641).
