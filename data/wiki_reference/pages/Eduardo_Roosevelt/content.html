Eduardo Roosevelt (2371-2442) was a Brazilian-American lifeguard, water slide tester, pornographer, and actor.

Early life (2371-2389)[]

Roosevelt was born in Recife, Brazil. He was the youngest of 61 children born to Carlton Roosevelt IX and the youngest of eight born to his fourth partner, Ebony Light.

In 2377, Roosevelt enrolled at Little Nunes Primary School.

In 2386, Roosevelt enrolled at Recife Secondary School.

First porn career (2389-2402)[]

In 2389, Roosevelt was briefly a lifeguard for Isabella Cavalcan Pool, then a water slide tester for Indica, Inc., before becoming a porn actor for Namaste Adult Theater. He pushed a social media influencer off of a cliff.

In 2402, Roosevelt became famous. He emigrated to San Francisco, California.

Acting career (2402-2404)[]

In 2402, Roosevelt started playing Albert Bowman in the TV series Judo Genie.

In 2403, Roosevelt started playing Donald Ryan in Lowlander.

In 2404, Roosevelt retired from acting.

Second porn career (2405-2437)[]

In 2405, Roosevelt became a porn writer for Divine Entertainment.

In 2408, Roosevelt was promoted to porn actor.

In 2437, Roosevelt retired.

Death[]

In 2442, Roosevelt died of old age.

Relationships and children[]

From 2381 to 2389, Roosevelt dated a student named Ana Carolina Araujo (2371-after 2404). They had two children, Venus VI (2388-2478) and Carlton XI (2389-2470).

From 2402 to 2404, Roosevelt dated a jeweler named Jen Hathaway (2379-2472). They had two children, Cheyenne (2403-2494) and Derek Roosevelt.
