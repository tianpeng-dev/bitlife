Early life (1898-1916)[]

Birth and family background (1898-1901)[]

Yosef Levi was born on February 26, 1883 in Jerusalem, Ottoman Empire to Baila Levi. His mother was unemployed.

Childhood (1901-1916)[]

Growing up, Yosefs mother married Omri Dahan in 1901 and she also became a firefighter the same year. At 15 Yosef joined the astronomy club and became treasurer, he also went to space camp. His half-sister was born in 1916.

Adult years (1916-1952)[]

As an adult, Yosef Levi Emigrated to Tampa, USA in 1916 and changed his name to Joseph Levy. His half-brother was born in 1918 and Josephs second half-sister was born in 1920 and became a police officer in 1932

Post retirement (1952-1994)[]

After retirement in 1952, Levy saved someone's life in 1958. He died in 1994, aged 96.

Personal life[]

In 1929 he married Avery Gray and had 9 children with her.
