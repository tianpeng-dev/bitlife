Early life
[
]
Alexey was born in Moscow in a simple family. His mother is a policeman, and his father is a military man. He also had an older sister Ekaterina Korzhova (2006-2029). At the age of 6, Alexey went to school, where he studied very well. At school, he made many friends with whom he is friends to this day. While still at school, he met Chernova Igorevna, who later became his wife. If we turn to the sad, then when the boy was 4 years old, his parents divorced. His mother married Vladimir Olegovich, and his father married Olga Tarasovna. Vladimir treats his stepson well, and his stepmother treats his stepson terribly. By the way, Alexey's stepmother had three children: Ruchkina Tarasovna (born in 2004), Lilova Tarasovna (born in 2011) and Chernova Tarasovna (2016-2026). In 2026, Alexey's 10-year-old half-sister died. Chernova died due to a snake bite. Alexey was 12 years old then. In 2029, his sister Ekaterina died, she was eaten by a shark. In 2032 Alexey Korzhov graduated from high school and entered the university.
