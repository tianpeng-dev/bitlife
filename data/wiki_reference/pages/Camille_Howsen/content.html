Camille Howsen (1906 - 1994) was an American magician and the mother of Marine and lawyer Derek Howsen.

Early life[]

Camille was born in Virginia to a family of popular entertainers, with both of her parents being multimedia actors, performing in film and on stage.

Magic career[]

By the time she graduated high school in 1924, Camille had completed her apprenticeship. After a few more final training lessons over the summer, she became a full-time magician.

Camille retired in 1972.

Personal life[]

In the late 1920s, Camille met writer Dennis Howsen. After several years of dating, they married in the early 1930s. They had three sons together, including Donald (1934 - 2019), Derek (April 1, 1935 - May 14, 2024), and Vernon (born 1942). Dennis died in 1976, and Camille followed in 1994.
