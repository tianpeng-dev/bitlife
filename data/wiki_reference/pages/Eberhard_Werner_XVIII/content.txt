Eberhard Werner XVIII
(4590-after 4646) is a Japanese real estate agent.
Early life (4590-4628)
[
]
Werner was born in Tokyo. He is the only child of
Eberhard Werner XVII
and his ex-girlfriend Kanon Majima.
In 4608, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.
Career (4628-4646)
[
]
In 4628, Werner became a real estate agent for Motoko Gota Realty.
Werner was alive in 4646, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 4608 to 4609, Werner dated a Finnish-born unemployed woman named Essi Hirvonen (4590-after 4646). They had a son,
Eberhard Werner XIX
.
