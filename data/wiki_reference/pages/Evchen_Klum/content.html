Evchen Klum (2570-after 2622) was a German-American basketball player and real estate agent.

Early life (2570-2592)[]

Klum was born in Berlin, Germany. Her parents are Helena Schmidt and her ex-boyfriend Ben Klum. She graduated from high school in 2588.

In 2588, Klum emigrated to Jacksonville, Florida. She went to the University of North Florida and played women’s basketball for the Ospreys. She graduated in 2592 with a BS in Political Science.

Sports career (2592-2604)[]

In 2592, Klum joined the Connecticut Stars as a point guard.

After Klum’s rookie season, the Stars finished in 10th place in the BitLife Women’s Basketball League.

During Klum’s second season, she missed 46 games after having MCL surgery. However, the Stars finished in eighth.

After Klum’s third season, the Stars finished in seventh and she was given a new contract.

During Klum’s fourth season, she missed 31 games after having hip surgery. The Stars finished in eighth and she was traded to the Las Vegas Showgirls.

After Klum’s fifth season, the Showgirls finished in 11th.

After Klum’s sixth season, the Showgirls finished in sixth.

After Klum’s seventh season, the Showgirls finished in sixth and she won the Sixth Woman Award.

After Klum’s eighth season, the Showgirls finished in sixth.

After Klum’s ninth season, the Showgirls finished as runners up and she won the Offensive Player of the Year Award.

After Klum’s 10th season, the Showgirls finished in sixth and she won the Defensive Player of the Year Award.

After Klum’s 11th season, the Showgirls finished in fifth and she won Defensive Player of the Year.

During Klum’s 12th season, she missed 76 games after having surgery for a spinal fracture. The Showgirls finished in fifth and Klum retired.

Real estate career (2605-2622)[]

In 2605, Klum became a real estate agent for Mary Grace Rios Realty.

Klum was alive in 2622, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 2588 to 2593, Klum started dating a student named Casey Krueger (b. 2570), who became a grocer in 2592. They had five children:

  1. Parker (2589-after 2622), an accountant
  2. Steffi (2590-after 2622)
  3. Fabienne (2591-after 2622), a wedding planner
  4. Josephina (2592-after 2622), a legal secretary
  5. Jacob (b. 2593)
