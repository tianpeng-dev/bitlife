Dylan Hyde (20 January 1960 - 9 May 2022) was a British chemical engineer. He was convicted for the attempted murder of a mobster in 1990 and sentenced to almost four decades in prison.

Early life and education[]

Hyde was born in London to Brian (1918 - 1995), an insurance agent, and Zoe Hyde (1939 - 2015), a trucker. He had seven younger siblings, five biological and two stepbrothers from his mother's second marriage to school administraor Harold "Harry" Suckley (born 1944):

  • Layla (born 1964) - Agent for Melissa Humphreys Real Estate
  • Maviah (born 1968) - Mailwoman
  • Mel (born 1971) - Receptionist
  • Samantha (born 1973) - Restauranter
  • Mark (born 1976) - Brain surgeon at Nucleus Hospital
  • George (born 1980) - Marine biologist at Blithe Inc.
  • Zachary (born 1984) - Flight attendant

Personal life[]

Hyde met Tyana Champion when they were nine. After being friends for nineteen years, they started dating. After five years of dating, they got married in 1990. That same year, their daughter, Joan, was born. Joan is a wedding planner for Gravesend Media. A year later, while Hyde was in prison, he and Champion divorced.
