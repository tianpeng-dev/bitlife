Mary Elizabeth Houston
(February 6, 1917 - May 26, 1997) was an American musician and actress.
Beginnings
[
]
Mary Elizabeth Houston was born on February 6, 1917, in Los Angeles, California, to Bob Houston and Genevieve. She had one older brother, Clint. Her mother died in 1919 from a fire in the home, and her father remarried to Betsy Phelps in 1922, who had two daughters, Anna and Viviane, from a previous marriage. Betsy was described as a evil and controlling woman. She would prank Mary by saying she was invisible and as a teen, would tell her about how her father "likes it". She told her father about these incidents but he didn't seem to care. Mary was sent to juvenile at 9 after attacking a bully, being released at 10, then transferring school. In 1933, she dropped out of high school and got a job as a dancer.
