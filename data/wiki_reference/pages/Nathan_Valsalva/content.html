He has no father, as his father was unknown. He has a family Persian cat named Juno. During his early childhood he was known for his rudeness and his violence. At his later childhood he was known for his crimes. Around 14 and 17 he committed murderer against two people his younger brother and his Italian Teacher. Around September 17th, 1949 he got killed by his gang members after accusing somebody for being a rat, from his suspicious acts, getting beated along suffering massive injuries.
