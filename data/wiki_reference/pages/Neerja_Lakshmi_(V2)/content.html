Neerja Laksmi (March 15, 1990 - May 16, 2024) was an Indian wedding planner. She also had a side-job as a small-time actress, appearing as an extra for numerous films and television programs.

Early life[]

Neerja was born in Srinagar, India to insurance agent Anushree Gayakvad (1952 - 2024) and jeweler Sachin Laksmi (born 1954). She had two brothers, Ansh (born 1988), a receptionist for Manhole Corp, and Anand (born 1992), an agent for Sign & Parachute Real Estate. She also had two pets growing up, a Great Pyrenees named Dixie (1989 - 2000) and a cat named Molly (2001 - 2009).

Death[]

On May 16, 2024, sometime after her mother's funeral, Lakshmi conducted a routine property inspection on her house. She found her tenant, Rajesh Upasani, running a gambling den out of the living room. Lakshmi warned Upasani that she would evict him if he didn't stop. In retaliation, Upasani gunned her down with a machine gun. She was rushed to the emergency room as quickly as possible, but it was determined that the bullet wounds killed her instantly. Laksmi is survived by her father, husband, two daughters, and two brothers.

On May 17, Upasani was charged with Lakshmi's murder. His trial is expected to begin either in late 2024 or early 2025.
