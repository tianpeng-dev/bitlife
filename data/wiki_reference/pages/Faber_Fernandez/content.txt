Early life (2437-2459)
[
]
Fernandez was born in Berlin, Germany. His parents were
Paul Fernandez
and his ex-girlfriend Helena Pfeiffer. He went to the Free University of Berlin and graduated in 2459 with a BS in computer science.
Career (2460-2464)
[
]
In 2460, Fernandez emigrated to Los Angeles, California and joined the Chicago Steers as a point guard. After his rookie season, the Steers finished in eighth place in the BitLife Basketball League and he was given a new contract.
During Fernandez’s second season, he missed 23 games after having back spasm surgery. However, the Steers finished in sixth place.
After Fernandez’s third season, the Steers finished in 10th place. He was traded to the Milwaukee Walkers, who had finished in 19th.
After Fernandez’s fourth season, the Walkers finished in fourth place.
Death
[
]
In 2464, Fernandez developed plantar fasciitis and had a fasciotomy. He died from complications associated with the surgery on September 8, 2464. He was 27 years old. His funeral was held in Los Angeles.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 2456 to 2457, Fernandez dated a student named Lisa Busch (2437-after 2475), who later became an accountant. They had a son,
Finn Fernandez
.
