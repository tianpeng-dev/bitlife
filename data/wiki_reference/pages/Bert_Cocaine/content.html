Health and addiction[]

He struggled with being addicted to cocaine. He was in intense rehabilitation several times but still went back to being addicted to doing drugs. He also suffered from lung cancer and heart disease.

Death[]

Bert was found dead in his hotel room in 2022. He died from a cocaine overdose which heart disease worsened the situation.
