In BitLife, the player can get trolled or troll someone. When the player gets trolled, the player can block them, troll them back, ignore them, or ask them to meet in person. On YouTube and TikTok you cannot troll anyone and no one can troll you, but on social media like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter you can troll people and people can troll you. Even if you’re famous or have full fame you can still get trolled.

Trolling can get your account suspended for Abusive Behavior if you troll people too much. You can appeal your suspension with five choices, apologize, claim a misunderstanding, act confused, write them an angry email, or threaten them. The best thing to do is apologize, claim a misunderstanding, or act confused, afterways they can unsuspend your account, or your account will still be suspended. Trolling isn't the only reason you can get suspended, if you buy followers too much you can get suspended for Spamming (on all social media except Facebook; players do not have the option to buy followers on Facebook). You can also troll BitLife but doing this gives you a disease. Sometimes you can also get suspended for an “unspecified reason”, which is rare but possible.

When you try to troll someone, the game gives you eight choices. You can troll your siblings/parents/friends/enemies, doing this will lower/get empty relationships with the sibling you trolled, and you can get suspended for doing this.
