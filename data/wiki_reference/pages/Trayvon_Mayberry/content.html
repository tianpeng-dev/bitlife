Early life[]

Trayvon Burns was born in Chicago to camerawoman Anita Burns. His father, whose whereabouts in the months leading up to Taryvon's birth remain a mystery, was an explorer who gave up his opportunity of fatherhood to uncover secrets of the world in variois locations. He had written to his son and Anita that he spent every day regretting that he wasn't there for his family, and wished them the very best in life and to explore with passion. Trayvon shared a strong bondage with his mongrel, Peachy (died 1934) and his Great Pyrenees, Reba (died 1947).

Trayvon attended Chicago University in the class of 1950. He studied anthropology. He met many lovers in college, men and women alike. After graduating from college, he went to graduate school for two years.
