Nevaeh Stevens-Watson (1792-1858) was an American porn star and magician.

Early life
[
]
Stevens-Watson was born in Baltimore, Maryland. Her mother was
Joana Reis
. She went to the University of Maryland and graduated with a BS in finance. She then graduated from business school.
Porn career
[
]
In 1817, Stevens-Watson became a porn actor for Smith Entertainment. She became famous in 1830. She retired in 1846.
Magic career
[
]
In 1850, Stevens-Watson became a magician for Nucleus Brothers Circus.
Death
[
]
In 1858, Stevens-Watson died of cervical cancer. Her body was donated to science.
Relationship and child
[
]
In 1829, Stevens-Watson started dating a university student named Pedro Prats (1810-1872), who later became a business analyst. They had a daughter,
Aubrey Prats
.
