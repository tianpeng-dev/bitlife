Wolfgang Statham (6859-6959) was an Austrian country singer, actor, politician, and lawyer.

Early life (6859-6877)[]

Statham was born in Salzburg, Austria. He was the youngest of four children born to Marcus Statham and the youngest of three born to his second girlfriend Viktoria Lang.

In 6865, Statham enrolled at Unger Primary School. He started taking piano lessons.

In 6867, Statham started taking voice lessons.

In 6869, Statham enrolled at Frontal Middle School.

In 6873, Statham enrolled at Schmidt Secondary School.

In 6874, Statham mastered the piano.

Music career (6877-6889)[]

In 6877, Statham signed with Color Records as a solo artist. He released his first country album, Go On Inside, which went gold in 6883.

In 6878, Statham released his second album, Getting Dressed Giggling, which went gold in 6880.

In 6880, Statham released his third album, The Unsung Ballad of Wolfgang Statham, which went double platinum in less than a year and diamond in 6884. He started taking acting lessons.

In 6883, Statham released his fourth album, I Knew You Were a Dumpster Fire, which went double platinum in less than a year.

In 6886, Statham released his fifth album, Sloppin’ the Stalls, which went double platinum in less than a year.

In 6889, Statham did not renew his contract. He enrolled at the University of Salzburg.

In 6893, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at University of Salzburg Faculty of Law.

In 6896, Statham graduated with a Doctorate of Jurisprudence.

Acting career (6897-6900)[]

In 6897, Statham played a massage therapist in Big in Bangkok.

In 6898, Statham played a degenerate in The Kung Fu Kid.

In 6899, Statham played a funeral home owner in I Fell for a Mortician. He became famous.

In 6900, Statham played Jim Blanchard in Safari on the Savanna.

Political and legal careers (6900-6924)[]

In 6900, Statham emigrated to Charleston, South Carolina. He ran for election to the Charleston County School District Board of Trustees and beat D’Brickashaw Licker.

In 6904, Statham ran for reelection and beat Javier Torres.

In 6908, Statham ran for Mayor of Charleston as a Democrat, but lost to Republican candidate Carlton Burger. He became a junior associate for Alvarez and Associates.

In 6912, Statham ran for Mayor of Charleston and beat Green Party candidate Karla Davis.

In 6916, Statham ran for reelection and beat Republican candidate Montgomery Pena.

In 6918, Statham started going to the gym annually.

In 6920, Statham ran for Governor of South Carolina as a Republican and beat Democratic candidate Violet Clinton.

In 6924, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

In 6948, Statham emigrated back to Salzburg.

Marriage, relationships, and children[]

From 6869 to 6876, Statham started dating a student named Emma Schuster (6859-6936). They had a son, Leo Statham LXII (6876-6948).

From 6877 to 6948, Statham was married to a restaurateur named Lorenz Berger (6859-after 6950).

From 6948 to 6950, Statham dated a student named Marie Steiner (6929-7005). They have four children, Kate XXIX (6949-after 7017), Hannah (6949-after 7017), Leo Statham LXIII, and Matteo Statham (6950-after 7017).
