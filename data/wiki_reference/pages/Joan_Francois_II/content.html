Joan Francois II (3493-December 15, 3511) was a French wedding planner.

Early life (3493-3511)[]

Francois was born in Lyon, France. She was the youngest of three children born to Soan Francois III and the only born to his third girlfriend Coco Vuitton.

Francois went to Jubilee Preparatory School and graduated in 3511.

Career and murder (3511)[]

In 3511, Francois became a wedding planner for Paris and Sons. She insulted her co-worker, Coco, nine times. In response, Coco attacked Francois with a pipe bomb. Francois died from her injuries on December 15.
