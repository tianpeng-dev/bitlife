Early life (1921-1939)[]

Birth and family background (1921-1927)[]

James Adams was born on May 25, 1921 in Dallas, Texas to Darell Adams and Margie Adams. His mother was a wedding planner, and his father was a librarian.

Childhood (1927-1939)[]

Growing up, Adams' parents divorced in 1929 when he was 8, in 1931 at age 10, his mother married Larry Walker. In 1932, his half-sister, Misty was born. His father died in 1937 at age 78.

Coast guard years (1939-1975)[]

As an adult, Adams became Coast Guard, in 1943 Adams accidentally detonated a mine in Columbia, severely injuring him, He retired in 1975.

Post retirement (1975-2017)[]

After retirement in 1975, most of his children were born, except John. He died in 2017 at 96.

Personal life[]

In 1964 he married Kendra Ibanez and had 11 children with her.
