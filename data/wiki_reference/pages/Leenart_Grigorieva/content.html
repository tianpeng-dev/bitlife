Leenart Grigorieva (August 20th, 1935-March 31st, 2026) was an Estonian reporter and PR manager.

Early life and education[]

Leenart Grigorieva was born through artificial insemination in Tallinn, Estonia. His single mother was impregnated by a sperm load that was anonymously donated to a fertility clinic.

He attended Kirigiri Primary School from 1942 to 1951 and Predrik Popov Secondary School from 1951 to 1954. He also attended university from 1954 to 1958.

Career[]

In 1958, he was hired for the position of Reporter for Swagger TV. However, he was eventually axed from his job in 1962, but hired for the position of PR Assistant for Transcendent Investments just one year later in 1963.
