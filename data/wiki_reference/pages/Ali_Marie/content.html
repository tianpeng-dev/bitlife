Ali Marie (2882-after 2939) was a French-Australian rugby player and mechanical engineer.

Early life (2882-2904)[]

Marie was born in Lyon, France. His parents were Luka Marie and his ex-girlfriend Baligha al-Hajri.

In 2900, Marie enrolled at the École Centrale de Lyon. He was on the rugby team. He graduated in 2904 with a Diplôme d’Ingénieur.

Sports career (2904-2919)[]

In 2904, Marie emigrated to Sydney, Australia and joined the Parramatta Reels as a prop.

In 2919, Marie retired.

Engineering career (2920-2939)[]

In 2920, Marie became an Engineer I for the Allegro Company.

In 2924, Marie was promoted to Engineer II.

In 2928, Marie was promoted to Engineer III.

In 2932, Marie was promoted to assistant engineering manager.

In 2936, Marie was promoted to engineering manager.

Marie was alive in 2939, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2900 to 2901, Marie dated a student named Laura Marie (2882-2924), who later became a pilot. They had a daughter, Ambre Marie.
