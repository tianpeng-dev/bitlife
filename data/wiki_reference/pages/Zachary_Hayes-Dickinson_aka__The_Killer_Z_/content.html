Life:

Zachary Hayes-Dickinson was the youngest child of late microbiologist David Hayes-Dickinson and accountant Matilda Baldock.

From an early age he had a passion for music and started practicing singing at age 12, he then became a rapper when he finished secondary school, at 18.

That same year he started his life as a famous rapper and social media influencer as well as a New Age cult named "The Hayes-Dickinson Way" which he led for some decades and had his first 5 children within.

He never commited to a relationship longterm and was always a wild spirit.

He achieved his dreams as a famous rapper releasing over 65 albums during his carreer and achieving a net worth of over 36.58 billion £.

As an influencer he accumulated over 62 million followers on Facebook, 84 million followers on Instagram, 60 million subscribers on Only Fans, 225 million followers on TikTok, 194 million followers on Twitch, 7 million followers on Twitter and 267 million subscribers on YouTube.
