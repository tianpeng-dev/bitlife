Jane Statham II (5258-after 5314) was a British politician.

Early life (5258-5276)[]

Statham was born in Blackpool, England. She was the younger of two daughters born to Leo Statham and his ex-girlfriend Emilia Burton.

In 5276, Statham graduated from secondary school.

Career (5276-5296)[]

In 5276, Statham ran for election to the Lancaster County Council. She beat Louis Matthews and served alongside her father for the first year of her first term.

In 5281, Statham ran for reelection and beat Omar Dragomir.

In 5286, Statham ran for Mayor of Blackpool as a member of the Labour Party and beat Conservative candidate Russell Crayford.

In 5291, Statham ran for reelection and beat Conservative candidate Kate Dartford.

In 5296, Statham did not run for a third term.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5314, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5276 to 5277, Statham dated Terry Hayes, Baron of Buckley (5249-after 5314). They had a son, Leo Statham II.
