Septimus Allen-Creed (July 31st, 1994 - April 4th, 2023) was a British domestic terrorist and former police officer. He was known for committing the April 4th vehicle attacks, in which he and his colleagues drove an SUV around the streets of London and fired a light machine gun at crowds of citizens, before crashing into a construction site and being crushed by a crane that fell on top of them.
