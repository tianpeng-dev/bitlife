Early life (2565-2589)
[
]
Cross was born in Miami, Florida. He was the fifth of 20 children born to
Andreas Cross III
and his girlfriend Crystal Schitt. He was named for his 8x great-granduncle.
Cross graduated from Florida International University in 2587 with a BS in Information Systems and in 2589 with an MBA.
Museum career (2589-2631)
[
]
In 2589, Cross became a docent for Imperial Gallery.
In 2595, Cross was promoted to exhibit associate.
In 2601, Cross was promoted to assistant curator.
In 2607, Cross was promoted to curator.
In 2631, Cross retired.
Acting career (2641-2655)
[
]
From 2641 to 2642, Cross played Louis Sinclair in the TV show
Unusual Things.
From 2644 to 2647, Cross played Foster in
Better Call Paul.
From 2647 to 2652, Cross played Tom in
Wizards of Wonderly Place.
From 2652 to 2655, Cross played Joanna Noble in
Nurse Who.
Death
[
]
On October 5, 2655, Cross died of old age.
Marriage, relationship, and child
[
]
From 2592 to 2612, Cross was married to a real estate agent named Breanna Colon (2566-2644).
In 2641, Cross started dating a student named Oprah Copeland (2623-2721), who became a PR manager in 2646. They had a son:
DeShawn Cross II
