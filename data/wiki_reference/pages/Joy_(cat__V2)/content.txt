Joy
(2006 - 2025) was an American Himalayan cat from Boston, Massachusetts. She spent her entire life in the loving confines of the Mountjoy-Ryder residence.
Life
[
]
Joy was born in Boston as a surprise arrival to her mother's human family, who believed that their dog was merely gaining weight. Her owners were army enlistee
Lil Mountjoy
(born 1952) and trucker
Veronica Ryder
(born 1955). The couple's son, Elijah (born 1974), would often visit. Joy also shared the Mountjoy-Ryder residence with an Australian shepherd named Minnie. Veronica loved Joy very much, but Lil, a Vietnam veteran, had a more negative relationship with her. Some say that Lil's abusive nature was inherited from his father, a hardened veteran of World War II.
