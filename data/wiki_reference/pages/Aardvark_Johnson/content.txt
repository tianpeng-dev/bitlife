Aardvark Johnson
(August 1, 1979 - July 24, 2024) was a New Zealander pornographer, convicted attempted murderer, and founder of the Johnson Legacy.

Early life
[
]
Johnson was born in Auckland. His father, Isaac Johnson (1938-2016) was a general manager at a restaurant. His mother, Kara Lee-Singh (née Johnson, 1938-2014) was a camera operator. They divorced in 1980. In 1982, Kara married an assistant manager at a jewelry store named Kahurangi Lee-Singh (1941-1991). Johnson had an older sister named Aaria Johnson (1977-after 2024).
Johnson went to all-boys schools called St. Ava’s Primary School and Spunky Secondary School. He also started taking accordion lessons at age six, piano lessons at seven, acting and voice lessons at eight, and modeling lessons at 11.
In 1995, Johnson did some freelance work as a tutor.
Porn career
[
]
In 1998, Johnson was hired as a porn actor at Krypton Studios. He resigned after less than a year. After resigning, Johnson briefly went to a community college.
Legal issues
[
]
In 1997, Johnson attempted to murder a crackhead with a set of plastic vampire teeth. He tried and failed to bribe the police, and was sentenced to 31 years in prison. He was paroled in 2024.
In 2024, Johnson was tried for attempting to murder an opera singer, but was acquitted.
Death
[
]
On July 24, 2024, Johnson attempted to murder an opera singer, likely as revenge for his previous trial, but was killed in the attempt.
