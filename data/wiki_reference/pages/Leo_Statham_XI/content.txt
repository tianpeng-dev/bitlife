Leo Statham XI
(5497-5570) was an Italian-Irish politician and flight attendant.

Early life (5497-5516)
[
]
Statham was born in Naples, Italy. He is the youngest of 13 children born to
Kate Statham XI
and the youngest of 12 born to her second boyfriend Carlo Altera.
Political career (5516-5521)
[
]
In 5516, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Gemma Alighieri.
In 5521, Statham did not run for reelection. He emigrated to Dublin, Ireland.
Flight attendant career (5539-5563)
[
]
In 5539, Statham became a junior flight attendant for O’Banion Airways.
In 5541, Statham was promoted to flight attendant.
In 5543, Statham was promoted to senior flight attendant.
In 5545, Statham was promoted to cabin crew supervisor.
In 5563, Statham retired.
Death
[
]
In 5570, Statham died of old age.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 5516 to 5522, Statham dated a Kazakh-Irish reporter named Gulaiym Majceev (5495-5583). They had three children, Kate XII (5517-5607), Priscilla (5518-5599), and
Leo Statham XII
.
