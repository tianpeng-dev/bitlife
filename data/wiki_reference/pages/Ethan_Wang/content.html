Admiral Ethan Wang (21 February 1912-25 May 2026) was a navy officer in 1934-1974, WWII veteran and longest-living New Zealander.

History[]

He was born in Auckland, News Zealand in 21 February 1912. He was conceived on parents' honeymoon. He study journalism at university. In 1934 Ethan fulfilled his dreams as a distinguished officer in the navy.

In 1935 he become going with student named Anahera Thompson. In 1939 they decided to wedding after Ethan proposed Diamond Ring in a field of tulips, they married at a winery and went to the Honeymoon in Crete, Greece.

In 1940 he become father for his daughter Laura. In 1944 he become father for his daughter Lucy. In 1946 he become father for his son Blake. In 1949 Anahera lost first-trimester miscarriege.

In 1956 Ethan has a dream, that he got killed in the battle.

In 1968 Ethan for first time become a grandpa for Ana Lowe (Laura's daughter). Year later borned his second granddaughter Hazel (Laura's daughter).

In 1974 Ethan retired from News Zealand Navy after reaching the mandatory retirement age. I began earning retirement pay of $187,959 per year.

In 1981 Ethan become attented to alcohol, but in this same year he overcose his addiction to alcohol.

In 1986 his son Blake fathered a girl named Hazel Wang.

In 1995 Ethan heard his neighbor screaming from inside her burning house. He called The fire departament but they were too late!

In 2017 Ethan and Anahera divorced. Ethan begged her to stay, but she insisted. As a part of the divorce settlement, the judge ordered they assets to be divided equally.

In 22 December 2022 Ethan's son Blake died after suffering a stroke on the age of 76.

In 25 May 2026 Ethan Wang passed away peacefully at they age of 114. His two daughters Laura and Lucy and his three granddaughters couldn't be bothered to attend his service.
