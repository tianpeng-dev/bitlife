Dementia in Bitlife is a disease in which your health becomes low and your smarts. Dementia can lead to death, however it is curable by frequent visits to the doctor's office, however this is somewhat rare. You, your parents, your spouse, child or any other relative aged 60 or over have a risk of getting dementia.


In less than most cases, a one - time visit to the normal medical doctor can cure dementia, pretty rarely, has happened to long time Bitlife players.
