Harvey Dent (February 14, 1968 - September 11, 2022) was an American lawyer was the district attorney of Gotham, New Jersey. He was also known as a ruthless vigilante, targeting the corruption of Gotham, under the alias "Two-Face", referring to a bizarre disfigurement on the right side of his face caused by a tragic incident.
