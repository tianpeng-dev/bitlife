Adrian Achen (June 16, 1998 - May 13, 2021) was a German soldier and undergraduate in engineering.

Early life[]

Achen was born on June 16, 1998 to Frieda Kong, a flight attendant, and Fritz Achen, a firefighter. He had a loving relationship with most of his family, especially the family dog, Jude. His stepfather, receptionost named Trevor Phillips (not to be confused with the character of the same name from GTA 5), never spent time with him.

Achen was a high school and college wrestler, and a college drama student. He was eventually kicked out of the drama club and off the wrestling team due to his bad grades.

Personal life[]

In 2016, Achen started dating his best friend, Kadejah Hakim, a wedding planner. They dated for five years until Achen's death.

Military service and death[]

Achen enlisted in the marines in 2020, at age 22. About a year later, on May 13, 2021, he accepted a deployment to a dangerous militant sector of Nepal, where he accidently detonated a mine, causing a seared ear, split lips, a severed fist and a charred nostril. Achen's death was 34 days short of his 23rd birthday. He is survived by his parents, his girlfriend and his two siblings, brother Friedrich and sister Katharina. Achen was posthumously awarded a medal of honor.
