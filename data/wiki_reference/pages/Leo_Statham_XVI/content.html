Leo Statham XVI (5664-after 5720) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5664-5682)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the youngest of four children born to Charlie Statham II and his ex-girlfriend Peggy O’Hara.

Career (5682-5702)[]

In 5682, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Gary Phillips.

In 5687, Statham ran for reelection and beat Dorothy O’Brien.

In 5692, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Harry Wilson-Duffy.

In 5697, Statham ran for reelection and beat Worker’s Party candidate Kyle Rees.

In 5702, Statham did not run for a third term.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5720, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5682 to 5683, Statham dated a jeweler named Emer Lewis (5664-after 5720). They had a son, Leo Statham XVII.
