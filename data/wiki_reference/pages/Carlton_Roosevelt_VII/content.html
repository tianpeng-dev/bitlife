Carlton Roosvelt VII (2272-April 15, 2296) was an American baker and football player.

Early life (2272-2294)[]

Roosevelt was born in Los Angeles, California. He was the first of 246 children born to Carlton Roosevelt VI and the first of four born to his first girlfriend, Ally Cocker.

In 2277, Roosevelt enrolled at O’Connor Elementary School.

In 2283, Roosevelt enrolled at Altitude Middle School.

In 2286, Roosevelt enrolled at Baseline Preparatory School.

In 2290, Roosevelt enrolled at the University of California, Los Angeles. He was a member of Alpha Sigma Phi and played football for the Bruins.

Baking career (2294-2295)[]

In 2294, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. He became an apprentice baker for James and Sons.

In 2295, Roosevelt was fired for sexual harassment.

Sports career (2295-2296)[]

In 2295, Roosevelt joined the New England Tea Parties as a safety.

In 2296, Roosevelt reported a mafia boss, who retaliated by attacking him with a guided missile. He died from his injuries on April 15.
