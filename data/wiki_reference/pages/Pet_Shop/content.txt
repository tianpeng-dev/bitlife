Pet shops are a type of shelter in the games
DogLife
and
CatLife
. Pet shops are usually the second-safest habitat in the games. Pet shops are the most ideal place if you want to get adopted
quickly.
Gameplay
[
]
To get into a pet store, you must already have selected your spawning point. There is no way to be sent to a pet store in any other habitat.
Characters
[
]
Pet shop characters will be: a caretaker (Mr, Mx or Ms), a shopper and other animals.
