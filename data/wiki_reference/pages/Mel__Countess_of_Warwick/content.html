Lady Mel, Countess of Warwick (2026-2147) was a British noblewoman.

Countess of Warwick (2026-present)[]

Mel was born in Southampton, England. She was the Countess of Warwick suo jure. She was the elder of two sisters born to Ronald, Marquess of Cockwallace.

In 2032, Mel enrolled at Amelia Matthews Primary School.

In 2037, Mel enrolled at Webb Secondary School.

In 2044, Mel graduated from secondary school.

Marriages and issue[]
