Diseases/Deadly diseases

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)
Deadly diseases are diseases that can end in death for your character. Some are easy to treat but must be treated to avoid dying. Others are harder to treat and a character must live with it for many years. Many can be contracted while a character is a child or teen and dying from one of these under age 30 will get the Unlucky ribbon. All are randomly contracted when aging up except rabies via animal encounters, and HIV via hook-ups or making love with inflicted mates. A web search may diagnose a deadly disease but it does not necessarily mean that your character will have one. Also, there are some diseases that are deadly in real life that are not deadly in the game, some of these include the Flu, Pneumonia, Cirrhosis, Staph Infection, Food Posioning and Ulcers. A player is more likely to die from complications from a disease if a player has low health.
