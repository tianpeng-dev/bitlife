Victor Rolland (2959-after 3015) was a French-Australian rugby player and biotechnologist.

Early life (2959-2981)[]

Rolland was born in Lyon, France. His parents were Jean Paul Rolland and his ex-girlfriend Faustine Mathieu.

In 2977, Rolland enrolled at Claude Bernard University. He was on the rugby team. He graduated in 2981 with a Licentiate in Chemistry.

Sports career (2981-2996)[]

In 2981, Rolland emigrated to Brisbane, Australia and joined the Parramatta Congers.

In 2987, Rolland was traded to the Gold Coast Panners. He has won a championship while on the team and two Player of the Year awards.

In 2996, Rolland retired.

Biotechnology career (2997-3015)[]

In 2997, Rolland became a junior biotechnologist at Ryan Corporation.

In 3003, Rolland was promoted to biotechnologist.

In 3009, Rolland was promoted to senior biotechnologist.

Rolland was alive in 3015, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2977 to 2978, Rolland dated a student named Avril Schmitt (b. 2959). They had a daughter, Alicia Rolland.
