Mathilde Krueger (2806-after 2862) was a French bartender.

Early life (2806-2828)[]

Krueger was born in Lyon, France. Her parents are Kendrick Krueger and his second girlfriend Leana Marchand. She has an older half sister named Aisha, who was a real estate agent in the United States. She went to Lyon Secondary School.

In 2824, Krueger enrolled at Claude Bernard University. She graduated in 2828 with a BS in Chemistry.

Bartending career (2828-2862)[]

In 2828, Krueger became a junior bartender at Rainier Diner.

In 2831, Krueger was promoted to bartender.

In 2834, Krueger was promoted to senior bartender.

Krueger was alive in 2682, but her date of death is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

In 2816, Krueger started dating a student named Gabriel Masson (2806-after 2862), who later became a fireman. They have two daughters, Alice (2823-after 2862) and Lana Masson.
