Caleb Gravesend
(1982-April 10th, 2068) was a British firefighter.
Early life and education
[
]
Caleb Gravesend was born to Rod Gravesend and Scarlett Woodbridge in 1982.
He attended primary school from 1987 to 1993 and secondary school from 1993 to 2000.
Career
[
]
Caleb Gravesend started a new position as Probationary Firefighter for Swansea Fire Department in 2001 and was eventually laid-off from the job in 2035. However, he was hired for the same job at the same department the same year, but was laid-off again in 2037.
