Karma

Karma is an element to BitLife. Doing good actions will increase karma and doing bad actions will lower karma. Karma helps a player live longer and get through tough situations easier. After a character dies, it will say the level of karma they had along with their overall happiness. You can view your character's karma by meditation, or after they die. Choosing the nicest option in random scenarios improves karma while choosing the rudest will lower it.

Things that increase karma[]

  • Apologizing
  • Calling police
  • Complimenting
  • Donating Heirlooms
  • Working on your Mind & Body
  • Giving gifts and cars to people
  • Giving money to family
  • Giving money to homeless
  • Helping someone, like a friend
  • Supporting friends when dealing with a problem and reporting them when they do illegal actions
  • Saving a life
  • Serving the military

Things that decrease karma[]

  • Arguing
  • Assaulting
  • Crime
  • Insulting
  • Not saving a life in danger.
  • Passing on STDs
  • Cheating on a lover
  • Abandoning children or pets
  • Going to prison
  • Skipping a funeral
  • Trying drugs
  • Trying to get away with bad things done in a Life.
  • Anything that is bad
