In Bitlife, you can do delinquent actions. When you try to do delinquent actions, the character's journal will say 'I felt immature inclinations'.

Sometimes, a relative with high craziness or a sibling will insist you to engage you as a mischief, with a risk of being reprimanded by a parent, being arrested or addicted to alcohol. When you refused to be a mischief, your relationship bar will lower.

Disorderly Conduct[]

These are things like pitch invading at games, pulling fire alarms in apartment complexes and releasing pigs into high schools. Such actions may get you 1 year in jail.

Criminal Mischief[]

These are things like tagging places, writing things like “Sorry about your wall!” or “Tag! You're it!” Others include blowing up mailboxes and vandalizing stop signs.

Reckless Endangerment[]

These include throwing beer bottles onto oncoming traffic, setting dog feces on fire, or dancing in the middle of a crosswalk.

Assault and Larceny[]

These involve slapping people down the street or stealing street signs/bikes.

Piracy and Illegal Gambling[]

Piracy is illegal film theft. This involves selling pirated copies of a movie/ Illegal gambling involves taking bets while residing in Nations that do not allow gambling.

Twerking[]

These involve crashing a party and twerking on a stranger. Funnily enough, it can get you life in prison!

Consequences[]

Sometimes when you do these actions, there are many popups who can appear :

  • Argue: A parent with high religiousness will argue with your character.
  • Arrested: Sometimes you can be arrested for your crime, if you are minor, you will be send to Juvenile Center without a trial.
  • Being addicted to alcohol: Likely go on party with your friends/best friend, you can suffer from an addiction to alcohol.
