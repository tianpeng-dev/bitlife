In this Korean name, the surname is Na.

Na Kang Mi I (2516-June 20, 2636) was a South Korean flight attendant and actress.

Early life (2516-2534)[]

Na was born in Gangnam, Seoul. She was the only child of Hamilton Tilda IV and her boyfriend Na Joo-won.

In 2534, Na graduated from upper secondary school.

Flight attendant career (2535-2568)[]

In 2535, Na became a junior flight attendant for Vitality Airlines. She was promoted to Cabin Crew Supervisor by 2568.

In 2568, Na retired.

Acting career (2568-2636)[]

From 2568 to 2569, Na played Jessica Anderson in the TV show Glee Club.

From 2569 to 2574, Na played Crystal Zapata in 10th Heaven.

From 2574 to 2580, Na played Aria Chatman in Battlefield Galactica.

From 2580 to 2588, Na played Joan Fisher in Seven Feet Under.

From 2588 to 2589, Na played Ruiz in Life Below Freezing.

From 2589 to 2592, Na played Jayla Zhang-Monroe in Saved by the Smell.

From 2592 to 2600, Na played Jen Ortega in In for a Penny.

In 2601, Na played Viviane Roach in the film Rachel Getting Buried.

In 2602, Na played Scarlett Gambino in Man in the Forest.

In 2603, Na played Rachel in Disregardable Me.

In 2604, Na played Carly Ramirez in Heck or High Water.

From 2604 to 2605, Na played Eliza Dartford in the TV show Fast Cars.

From 2605 to 2610, Na played Darryl Ingalls in Little Family on the Prairie.

From 2610 to 2612, Na played Jane Bennett in Baxter.

From 2612 to 2614, Na played Malik Browser in Losing My Marbles.

From 2614 to 2615, Na played Honey in Better Call Paul.

From 2615 to 2616, Na played Anna Selhurst in Foxfire.

In 2617, Na played a revolutionist in the film Les Venerables.

In 2618, Na played a ranch hand in Bite the Dust.

From 2618 to 2624, Na played Tyson Rose in the TV show Smitt’s Creek.

From 2624 to 2625, Na played Virgil in Asian Horror Story.

In 2626, Na played Lady Gladria in the film Lord of the Strings.

From 2626 to 2627, Na played Agatha Rhee in the TV show The Waking Dead.

In 2627, Na started playing Anna Young in The Fresh Prince of Wel-Fare.

On June 20, 2636, Na died of old age.
