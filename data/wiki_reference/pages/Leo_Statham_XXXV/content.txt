(6044-March 30, 6074) was a British-Japanese army officer.

Early life (6044-6066)
[
]
Statham was born in Birmingham, England. He was the youngest of three children born to
Leo Statham XXXIV
and his ex-girlfriend Mei Mazda.
In 6055, Statham enrolled at Hourglass Secondary School.
In 6062, Statham emigrated to Kobe, Japan. He enrolled at Kobe University. He earned his boating license and bought a fishing boat, which he named
Nothing But Net
. He was hit by a speed boat, causing
Nothing But Net
to catch fire. Statham abandoned ship.
Career (6066-6074)
[
]
In 6066, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. He joined the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force as a
santō rikui
, or second lieutenant.
In 6067, Statham was promoted to
nitō rikui
, or first lieutenant.
In 6070, Statham was promoted to
ittō rikui
, or captain.
In 6073, Statham bought a pirate ship, which he named
Saitama’s Grog
.
Death
[
]
On March 30, 6074, Statham was deployed to the Philippines. He stepped on a landmine and died from his injuries.
Relationship and children
[
]
From 6054 to 6062, Statham dated a student named Amber Earl (6044-after 6704). They had three children, Kate XXVI (6061-after 6704), Eva (6061-after 6704), and
Leo Statham XXXVI
.
