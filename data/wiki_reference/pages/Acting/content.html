Acting[]

Acting is a feature from the Movie Star Update. Released on March 2022, it comes with the ability to act in a movie or TV show. This feature, along with the "job pack" comes with a price as of right now, up to $5 per item or $10 for the Job Pack.

As of December 2022, the new "Stock Market Update" will be released this month.

Starting to Act[]

If you have God Mode, you are able to choose "Acting" from the Special Talents tab.

If you choose "Acting" or able to get the acting special talent, then a symbol (🎭) will appear next to the characters name.

Up to the age of 12, you can ask your parents for them to pay for your Acting Lessons.

Past the age of 18, you must buy your own. The price varies by country.

Participating in Drama Club in school will boost your acting ability for free.

Selecting A TV Show/Movie[]

When you reach 18, you are able to act as an extra, cast on a TV show/Movie, or find an agent.

EXTRA

When casting an extra, you'll see some information, like what the title of the movie/TV show is, how much is the budget, and what is your role. You can choose to accept or deny the extra roll.

This will increase your chance to be noticed by an agent.

If an agent notices you during your extra role, you have the opportunity to join.
