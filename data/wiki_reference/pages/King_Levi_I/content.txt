Levi I (January 12, -2025) was a King of the Netherlands. He was the first King of the House of Rynsburger.

Birth
[
]
Levi was born on January 12th, with a fortune of €9.1m as of birth. He is a Capricorn. He was born to Naomi Hendriks, the Queen of the Netherlands, and Mason Rynsburger, the King Consort of the Netherlands. While Levi was the first proper King of the Rynsburger Dynasty, he wasn’t the first royal.
Infancy
[
]
Nothing major really happened to Levi in the first year years of his life, though he did witness events such as his older brother, Stijn, starting Primary School and his Mother attempting to stop the crisis that started between Turkey and Serbia. However, on his second year, he met and became friends with Noud Ruis, and discovered his passion for music. The Turkey-Serbia crisis escalated further during his life, and the royal family faced protests as Queen Naomi’s peace efforts didn’t work out.
