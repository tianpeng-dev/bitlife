Early life (3241-3267)[]

Roosevelt was born in Transylvania, Romania. He is the youngest of four children born to Carlton Roosevelt XXVIII and the younger of two born to his third girlfriend, Nadia Bashir.

In 3247, Roosevelt enrolled at Fruzsi Barbu Primary School.

In 3251, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Matyas’s Lower Secondary School.

In 3257, Roosevelt enrolled at Serenity Preparatory School.

In 3260, Roosevelt enrolled at Babeș-Bolyai University. He majored in political science.

In 3264, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at Babeș-Bolyai University Faculty of Law.

In 3267, Roosevelt graduated with a Doctor of Jurisprudence.

First porn career (3267-3274)[]

In 3267, Roosevelt became a porn actor for Luxe Studios.

In 3274, Roosevelt resigned.

Acting career (3275-3287)[]

In 3275, Roosevelt played Carl Gillooly in the film They, Tonya.

From 3275 to 3287, Roosevelt played Jim Suzuki in the TV show The Campaign.

Second porn career (3288-3291)[]

In 3288, Roosevelt became a porn writer for Peacock Studios.

In 3291, Roosevelt was promoted to porn cameraman.

Murder[]

In 3291, Roosevelt was murdered by a hitman hired by his son.

Relationship and children[]

From 3251 to 3261, Roosevelt dated a student named Adelina Lupu (3241-3291), who became an insurance agent in 3260. They had four children from their first relationship:

  1. Venus Roosevelt XVI (3258-3276)
  2. Anna Roosevelt (3259-3277)
  3. Alexandra Roosevelt (3260-3278)
  4. Carlton Roosevelt XXX

In 3274, Roosevelt and Lupu started dating again.
