Eberhard Werner I (4443-January 1, 4465) was a German clown.

Early life (4443-4465)[]

Werner was born in Stuttgart, Germany. He was the younger of two sons born to Anton Werner and Jana Debus.

In 4444, Werner’s father married Anna Bauer. However, he was murdered and Bauer committed suicide in 4445, so Eberhard Werner and his older sister Jasmin were sent to Stuttgart Orphan Asylum.

In 4449, Werner enrolled at Little Eggemann Primary School.

In 4453, Werner enrolled at St. Juna’s Middle School.

In 4458, Werner enrolled at Epsilon Secondary School.

In 4461, Werner graduated from secondary school.

Clowning career and death (4465)[]

In 4465, Werner became a clown for Secret Bros Circus. He bought a Lamborghini Aventador, which he sold less than a year later. He called his enemy, Ben Neuer, a lamebrain. In response, Neuer attacked Werner with a baton. Werner died from his injuries.

Marriages, relationship, and child[]

From 4454 to 4460, Werner dated a student named Greta Ziegler (4443-after 4480). They had a daughter, Greta Werner.

In 4461, Werner started dating a student named Melain Pohl (4442-after 4465). They were briefly married in 4463. Werner gained over $2 million from the divorce.

Less than a year after divorcing Pohl, Werner married a student named Lilly Kaiser (4444-after 4465). They divorced after less than a year.

Less than a year after divorcing Kaiser, Werner married a student named Jule Banner (4444-4463). She was murdered less than a year later.
