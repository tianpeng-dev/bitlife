William Chapman (September 18, 1966 - December 24, 2024), also known as the Santa Claus Killer, was an American elf and serial killer. Between 1984 and 1991, he murdered at least eight people who he considered to be "naughty." It is speculated that he killed eight additional people on Christmas Eve of 1984, similarly to his movie character counterpart in the classic holiday horror flick Silent Night, Deadly Night. After his eighth documented murder in 1991, which was his stepfather, Chapman would end his killing spree and continued his career as an elf for the next three decades before retiring in 2024, which was followed by his own murder.

Childhood[]

Billy was born in Heber City, Utah to stay-at-home parents Bud Chapman (1933 - 2004) and Ally Chapman (1943 - 2021). Bud was a Korean War veteran who supported the family with his service benefits. He had two younger sisters, Tiara (biological; born 1973) and Valeria (half; born 1983). The family had a pet cat named Lily (1965 - 1983). Bud and Ally divorced when Billy was twelve years old. Bud married travel agent Abby Colbert (born 1952) and Ally married veterinarian Sam Wainwright (1925 - 1991), who coincidently shared the name with a minor character in the film It's A Wonderful Life. Having a wealthy background, Sam avoided serving in World War II by intentionally underperforming at the military academy so he could keep his comfortable lifestyle and avoid being scarred by war. Billy tried to spend time with Sam, but the latter would refuse despite having some responsibility in his life, making Billy secretly resent him. Growing up, Billy enjoyed the holiday traditions and festivities, though some parts of it made him feel uneasy, such as the idea of Santa Claus watching children sleep. Every Christmas, Bud dressed as Santa, while Ally dressed as Mrs. Claus.

On Christmas Eve of 1971, while driving home from a visit with Billy's grandparents, the Chapmans were stopped by a man dressed as Santa Claus, who appeared to be stranded with a flat tire. "Santa," then attempted to murder Bud and sexually grope Ally by ripping off her shirt and fondling her breasts, but the 5-year-old Billy stopped him by attacking him into unconsciousness, calling the police from the car phone, and then hiding in the bushes until the police arrived. Upon the Santa's apprehension, it was discovered that he was a thug who robbed a nearby gas station and killed the owner. The experience scarred Billy for the rest of his life, prompting him to seek vengeance on those he saw as naughty.

Elven career and murders[]

After graduating from high school in 1984, Chapman started to look for work. He discovered a job posting at "Santa's Workshop" and applied there. After displaying his passion for Christmas and talents at acting and making toys, he was hired as an elf.

Later life[]

After 1991, Chapman would never kill again. He would spend the rest of his career as an elf, and would unsuccessfully try to become an official mall Santa. In his later years, he would rack up a lot of debt and try to climb out of it through a criminal career of bank robberies and burglaries, but to no avail. In 2024, after working as an elf for forty years, Billy retired.

Death[]

In December 2024, shortly after his retirement, Chapman continued his spree of money theft, hoping to climb out of his massive debt. On Christmas Eve, he met his final victim, a drug dealer. Chapman tried to pickpocket him, only to get attacked with a dildo. He died from a whacked thumb. All his family attended the funeral.
