Raymond "Ray" Pincock
(July 11, 2002 - May 15, 2021) was an American college student who was brutally murdered by one of his classmates in a fight. The incident has caused mass outrage within the community.

Early Life
[
]
Pincock was born July 11, 2002 in Washington to Cassandra, a financial analyst, and Bart, a wedding planner. He enjoyed playing with his friends, his older brother Beavis, and his pet rabbit, Thumper (supposedly named after the character in Walt Disney's 1942 film
Bambi
).
High School Years
[
]
Throughout his high school days, Pincock was infamous for harrassing classmates that he percieved as threats to his friends and family. His behavior shocked his parents.
Death
[
]
After graduating high school, Pincock enrolled in community college. His juvenile behavior continued, resulting in more reprimanding.
On May 15, 2021, Pincock suspected that his classmate, Hugh, was an evil person (perhaps a terrorist), after seeing his picture(s) in the news. He attacked him with a rubber band. Hugh fought back, bruising Pincock's nostril and smacking his back. Ray was taken to the emergency room, where he eventually died from his injuries. He was 18 years old.
Aftermath
[
]
Ray's death had sparked violent protests, for many of the public believed that his college, and not just Hugh, was responsible for his death. Ray's parents, who are against these protests, created an anti violence organization, dedicated to his memory.
