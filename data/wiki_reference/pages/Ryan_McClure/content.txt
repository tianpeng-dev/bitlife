Ryan McClure
(neé Crunk, 1964 - May 31st, 2052) was an American photographer.
Early life and education
[
]
Ryan McClure was born as Ryan Crunk in Newark, United States in 1964. His single mother then died in a car crash. When he was 4 years old, he was adopted by Chester McClure and Emily Ruiz. His last name was then changed to his adoptive father's.
He attended elementary school from 1969 to 1975, middle school from 1975 to 1978 and high school from 1978 to 1982.
Career
[
]
In 1983, McClure started a new position as Apprentice Photographer for The Newark Inquirer.
