Randall Guidry was an American bank robber, burglar, serial killer and gang member active between 2000 and 2025 before passing away of cancer of the mouth while serving time in prison.

Childhood[]

Randall John Guidry was born in Lafayette, Louisiana on February 1st, 1978 to a photographer named Perry Guidry and a police officer named Jenna Guidry. Not much else is know about his childhood except his father was very cruel and his mother was absent for the most part

Military Service[]

Randall served a mandatory 4 years in the United States Marines.

Death[]

Randall died of mouth cancer at age 47 in a Norwegian prison where he was placed after the murder of a woman which happened after he fled the US to Norway.
