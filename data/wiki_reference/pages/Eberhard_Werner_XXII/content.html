Eberhard Werner XXII (4685-after 4741) was a Japanese grocer.

Early life (4685-4723)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. He is the only child of Jasmin Werner X and her ex-boyfriend Cristiano Carvalho.

In 4703, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4723-4741)[]

In 4723, Werner became an apprentice grocer for Wanderlust Grocery.

In 4725, Werner was promoted to grocer.

Werner was alive in 4741, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4703 to 4704, Werner dated a Nicaraguan-born hairdresser named Maritza Rodriguez (4685-after 4741). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XXIII.
