South Korea
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the capital, Seoul, along with Busan, Daegu, Gangnam, and Incheon. An
achievement
called
K-Pop
can be achieved for a character in South Korea who achieves
fame
as a singer.
