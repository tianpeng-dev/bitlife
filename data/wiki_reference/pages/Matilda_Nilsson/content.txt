Matilda Nilsson
(b. June 3, 1897 — d. ???) is a de facto member of the Swedish branch of the Nilssons, although she is not even a baroness. She was born into the family of
Lars Nilsson
and Molly Nilsson before their wedding, which is why she did not join the royal family.
Childhood
[
]
Matilda at age 7
Matilda at age 12
Matilda was born in Gothenburg, Sweden in 1897. Her father and mother were not married at that time, so she was not born a baroness, but an ordinary girl, which is why the public denounced Lars Nilsson. Due to her life circumstances, Matilda was subjected to hate from the public and primary school students. At age 16 she enrolled in Secondary School continuing to experience oppression from society. After 2 years, she entered the university in the field of information systems, asking her father to pay for the tuition in full.
Adult Life
[
]
In 1916, Matilda came out of the closet, declaring her bisexuality to her father and not receiving any aggression towards her. Two years later she moved out in Karlstad. Three years later she was diagnosed with hemorrhoids. At 23, she graduated from university with an undergraduate degree in information systems and began graduate school. Then she got a job Jr. Marine Biologist at The Buttcherson Company being promoted to Marine Biologist a year later.
In 1927, she was promoted to Sr. Marine Biologist.
