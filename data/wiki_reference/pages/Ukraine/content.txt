Ukraine
is a
country
that was on BitLife in December 2019. Its default cities are the capital, Kyiv along with Dnipro, Donetsk, Kharkiv, and Odesa.
In December 2019 on iOS; new countries were added. Along with Ukraine; there was also Austria, Bahamas, Costa Rica, DR Congo, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Kuwait, Lebanon, Mongolia, Nepal, and Vietnam. This was added in the
Friends
Update.
Despite that the country is facing an ongoing war against Russia in real life, Ukraine has no
deaths
related to terrorist or battlefield attacks in Bitlife.
