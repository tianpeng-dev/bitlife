Cristiano Cross II (2814-2886) was an American-Italian soccer player.

Early life (2814-2836)[]

Cross was born in Miami, Florida. He was the youngest of four children born to Keith Cross II and his girlfriend Cruella Mayberry. He was named for his 12x great-granduncle.

Cross went to Florida International University and played soccer for the Panthers before graduating with a BA in English in 2836.

Career (2836-2855)[]

In 2836, Cross emigrated to Genoa, Italy. He joined La Spezia as a midfielder.

In 2838, Cross was traded to Sassuolo.

In 2844, Cross was traded to Milan.

In 2849, Cross was traded back to La Spezia.

In 2851, Cross was released by La Spezia and joined Genoa.

In 2855, Cross was released by Genoa.

Death[]

in 2886, Cross died of old age.

Relationship and children[]

In 2832, Cross started dating his classmate, Bobbie Sue Morgan-Johnson (2814-2889), who became a forensic scientist in 2837. They had two sons:

  1. Gaylord Cross II (2833-after 2904), Air Force enlistee
  2. Marshall Cross II
