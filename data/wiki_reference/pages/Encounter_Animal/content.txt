An
Encounter
is a scenario where a character is in close range of an animal, either domestic or wild. The options of how to react are either to rescue/adopt them as a pet, run away, slowly retreat, or pet it. The results of the action are random and may or may not succeed. The player may get attacked and could even
die
from the attack. The player could also get
rabies
if the animal gives them rabies. Following the
Pets
update, animals who are pets can become your character's new pet if the character chooses and the animal is alright with it. An
achievement
can be unlocked if your character contracts rabies and another achievement if they get killed by a hippopotamus.
