An Encounter is a scenario where a character is in close range of an animal, either domestic or wild. The options of how to react are either to rescue/adopt them as a pet, run away, slowly retreat, or pet it. The results of the action are random and may or may not succeed. The player may get attacked and could even die from the attack. The player could also get rabies if the animal gives them rabies. Following the Pets update, animals who are pets can become your character's new pet if the character chooses and the animal is alright with it. An achievement can be unlocked if your character contracts rabies and another achievement if they get killed by a hippopotamus.

Actions animals do[]

  • Become your pet/be rescued by your character
  • Kill your character, if they did not successfully evade it, sometimes maul your character.
    • Some animals are always deadly if rescued, such as the poison dart frog, as touching it will instantly kill a human.
  • Give your character rabies, if rabid
  • Refuse to be your pet or bite your character, but with little to no damage to your character
  • Attack your character, lowering their health.
  • Sometimes it can do nothing, but your attempts at catching it will be unsuccessful.
