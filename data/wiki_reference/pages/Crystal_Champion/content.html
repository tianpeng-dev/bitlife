Early life and education[]

Note: All crimes and accounts are direct confessions from Champion's memoir.

1938-1956: Birth, adolescence and early struggles[]

Crystal Champion was born on October 6, 1938 in Cleveland, Ohio, through artificial insemination to travel associate Miranda Champion (1917-), who married Champion's long-term stepfather Apollo Zucker. Champion did not get along with her stepfather and his son Owen throughout the entirety of her adolescence, despite efforts from Champion to have a good relationship with the pair, specifically her stepfather who never grew to like her until his death. Champion became an older sister to three half siblings and remained close with her mother for her whole life, later naming her dog in honour of her mother following her passing.

In her first year of middle school, Champion had a run-in with her principal following a disagreement with a classmate which led to their relationship souring and Champion purposefully acting out, leading to an expulsion early in the 1949 school year. She met her second boyfriend, Hunter Castro at her second school that year whom she would later re-kindle with, although at the time they dated for less than 3 weeks. Champion found middle school tough, being shunned out from hanging with the 'popular girls' and participating in the schools cheer team (which she tried out for multiple times over different years), which she later attributed to "not being the cutest kid".

Champion's school difficulties continued in her sophomore year of high school, failing to befriend popular students and becoming disliked and bullied for her desperation, paired with continued failure to join her schools cheerleading team. She decided to act out and get expelled as a way to change schools, not wanting to share with her mother what had been taking place at school. Champion faired much better after enrolling at Ramirez High School in 1954. That following year, aged 16 she faced more personal troubles, breaking up with her at the time longest boyfriend, as well as facing difficulties in her relationship with her mother after she had been expelled and her relationship with her stepfather reached a boiling point, culminating in a heated fight.

Champion admitted 16 was one of the hardest years of her life, and she found an escape with partying with her friends away from home and making rash decisions like when she hooked up with her childhood friend Keenan and became pregnant, opting to abort. Soon after she hooked up with her ex-boyfriend Hunter Castro and became pregnant again, opting to abort. Following this she re-kindled her relationship with Castro, with them breaking up two years later. By 17, she had stopped caring about trying to climb the social ranks and learnt to enjoy her teenage years.

1957: College education, first ever crime[]

After graduating from high school in May 1957, Champion failed to be accepted to any universities due to her records of numerous expulsions throughout her childhood and thus decided to opt for community college first. She enrolled at Barnard College in accounting in 1959 following her community college completion. In 1960, Champion underwent her first ever cosmetic surgeries which translated into a better general opinion for her in college, which she attributes to being what started her addiction to perfection in her appearance. She also had an extra role in Mermaids (1961) which she took for extra cash at the time.
