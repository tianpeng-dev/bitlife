Early Life[]

Mats Walkowiak was born in the year 1888 , in Bonn,Germany, as the second Child of Lotta Kant (29) (1861-1893) and Rudolf Walkowiak (43) (1845-1929) His Mother was the breadwinner of the Family while the Father strugled to get a stable job. Sadly his Mother died in an aciicdent when he was five. Thsi caused the Family to fall into Poverty.

Adult Life[]

Life until WW2[]

After he turned 18 he was able to get a Scholarship for the Cologne University where he studied Physics. After completing his Studies in the Year 1911 he went on to work at the university. This stopped with the Outbreak of WW1 and Mats was drafted into the Military. He fought well even earning a few Medals before losing an Eye at the Battle of Verdun. After the War he continued to teach at the University of Cologne, while also working with other famous Physicians like Albert Einstein on different Theories. It was an Open Secret that he was gay. This is why the people close to him werent really surprised when he adopted a little Girl, Hilda, together with his "Roommate and close Friend" Jonas Gross. He also became active in politics and joined the SPD

During WW2[]

In the Year 1934 Mats, His Daughter, Jonas Gross, His Sister and her Nephew all fled to Switzerland, because of them all being "Social Liberals" Here he spend his Time aiding the Underground Resistance in Germany and France. He returned to Germany in the Year 1951

After WW2[]

After WW2 Mats continued researching Astrophysics and making some Breakthroughs in the Field. However, his main objective was known to prevent War. He became a voice against the Cold War advocating against nuclear weapons and for the conflict to quiet down. He died in the Year 1972 at age 84 surrounded by his daughter, nephews and 3 grandsons.
