Father Travis Ovule (July 29, 1969 - November 27, 2023) was an American cult leader.

Early life[]

Ovule was born in San Fransisco to makeup artist Irene Ovule (1927 - 2012) and an unknown father. He had a half-sister, Hannah Pousa (born 1965), a sailor in the Navy, a stepfather named Vincent "Vinnie" Jones (1937 - 1980), a grocer, and two stepsisters (Vinnie's daughters), Ciara (born 1958), a camerawoman, and Hailey (born 1964), an IT Support.
