When was it added to iOS?

March 2019

Finland is a country that was on BitLife in March 2019. Its default cities are only the capital, Helsinki.

Finland was part of a March 2019 update that added 8 new countries into the game. The other 7 were Portugal, Norway, Indonesia, Denmark, Singapore, Estonia, and Romania. This update was also the update that brought Pets for the first time. This update was also the first to have multiple new countries being added other than the ones originally released.
