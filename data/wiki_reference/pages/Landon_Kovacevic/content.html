Landon Kovacevic (3742-3838) was an American-Italian trucker, baker, politician, lawyer, judge, and businessman who became the President of the United States.

Early life (3742-3760)[]

Kovacevic was born in Oakland, California. He was the 73rd of 97 children born to Virgil Kovacevic I and the fifth of 23 born to his fourth girlfriend Elyse Schitt.

Kovacevic graduated high school in 3760.

Trucking career (3760-3768)[]

In 3760, Kovacevic became a trucker for Shamrock Trucking and Services.

In 3768, Kovacevic hooked up with five of his co-workers, Giselle Colbert (3696-3787), Jalen Cox (3695-3773), Giselle Gallo (3742-after 3824), Precious Riviera (3718-3793), and Nathan Velazquez (3728-3807). He resigned shortly thereafter.

Baking, political, legal, and judicial careers (3768-3821)[]

In 3768, Kovacevic became a baker for Oliver Ideas. He resigned less than a year later to run for election to the California State Board of Education. He beat Larry Vega and was inaugurated.

In 3772, Kovacevic ran for reelection and beat Sabrina Sandoval.

In 3774, Kovacevic ran for election as Mayor of Oakland as a Democrat. He beat Republican candidate Joy Cunningham and was inaugurated on January 9, 3775.

In 3778, Kovacevic ran for reelection and beat Green candidate Marquis Gay.

In 3782, Kovacevic ran for Governor of California as a Democrat, but lost the primary. He enrolled at Lincoln University and graduated in 3786 with a BS in Political Science.

In 3786, Kovacevic ran for Governor a second time and was nominated, but lost to Republican candidate Landon Gobble. He enrolled at UC Berkeley School of Law and graduated in 3789 with a JD.

In 3789, Kovacevic became a junior associate for Richardson and Associates.

In 3790, Kovacevic ran for Governor a third time, but lost to Republican candidate Tyson Young.

In 3794, Kovacevic ran for Governor a fourth time, but lost to Republican candidate Bailey Gulch-Simpson.

In 3795, Kovacevic was promoted to associate.

In 3798, Kovacevic ran for Governor a fifth time and beat Republican candidate Bobbie Sue Li. He was inaugurated on January 7, 3799.

In 3802, Kovacevic ran for reelection, but lost to Republican candidate Martha Sprinkle.

In 3804, Kovacevic became a junior associate for Clarkson and Springer.

In 3805, Kovacevic became a magistrate.

In 3806, Kovacevic ran for reelection a second time, but lost to Republican candidate Connor Kardashian.

In 3810, Kovacevic ran for reelection a third time and beat Libertarian candidate Louis Garfield. He was inaugurated on January 7, 3811.

In 3816, Kovacevic ran for President of the United States as a Democrat and beat Socialist candidate Bobbie Sue Reynolds. He was inaugurated on January 20, 3817.

In 3820, Kovacevic announced that he would not run for reelection.

Business career (3821-3823)[]

Shortly after leaving office in 3821, Kovacevic acquired a cosmetics company called Oakland Beauty Supply.

In 3823, Kovacevic sold Oakland Beauty Supply. He lost $122 million from the investments and sale. He retired from business and emigrated to Genoa, Italy.

Death[]

In 3838, Kovacevic died of old age. His funeral was held in Genoa.
