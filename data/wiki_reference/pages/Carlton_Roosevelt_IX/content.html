Carlton Roosevelt IX (2307-2391) was an American-Brazilian accountant, microbiologist, baker, and astronaut.

Early life (2307-2329)[]

Roosevelt was born in Los Angeles, California. He was the eldest of 178 children born to Carlton Roosevelt VIII and the eldest of 27 born to his second girlfriend Jenny Bumgarner.

In 2325, Roosevelt enrolled at the University of California, Los Angeles.

Accounting career (2329-2350)[]

In 2329, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics. He became a junior accountant for Oasis Corp.

Roosevelt became a senior accountant before retiring in 2350. He enrolled at UCLA again.

Microbiology and baking careers (2354-2355)[]

In 2354, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Biology. He became a junior microbiologist for Medley Engineers.

In 2355, Roosevelt was briefly an apprentice baker for Dickinson Ideas, then an accountant for Celestial Labs.

Astronautic career (2356-2370)[]

In 2356, Roosevelt began training for NASA.

In 2358, Roosevelt joined NASA as a cadet.

In 2361, Roosevelt was promoted to captain.

In 2363, Roosevelt was promoted to major. He became famous.

In 2368, Roosevelt was promoted to general.

In 2370, Roosevelt resigned and emigrated to Recife, Brazil.

Death[]

In 2391, Roosevelt died of old age.

Marriage, relationships, and children[]

From 2334 to 2340, Roosevelt was married to a makeup artist named Venus Hanks (2311-after 2371). They had a son, Junior (2339-after 2404).

From 2350 to 2353, Roosevelt and Hanks dated again. They had three children, Isabella (2351-after 2404), Payton (2352-after 2404), and Carlton X (2353-after 2404).

In 2354, Roosevelt had one night stands with an Andorran-born restaurateur named Lidia Casal (2306-after 2371) and a Croatian-born library clerk named Ana Ivankovic (2300-after 2371). Roosevelt got syphilis from Casal and gave it to Ivankovic. Roosevelt and Casal briefly dated, and he bought her a bouquet of tulips.

In 2355, Roosevelt had one night stands with a hairdresser named Marilyn Martin (2306-after 2370) and a chef named Dixie McQueen-Walsh (2315-after 2370). Roosevelt got gonorrhea from Martin and chlamydia from McQueen-Walsh. He seduced five of his co-workers: a telemarketer named Ashley Clarkson (2284-2364), a biotechnologist named Luna Pyle (2284-2360), a telemarketer named Mariah McCain (2323-after 2371), a baker named Goober Cunningham (2324-after 2371), and a telemarketer named Nevaeh Washington (2331-after 2371).
