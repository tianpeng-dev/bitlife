Early life (6768-6790)[]

Statham was born in Salzburg, Austria. He was the younger of two brothers born to Yuto Statham and the only one born to his second girlfriend Johanna Wimmer.

In 6774, Statham enrolled at Unger Primary School. He started taking piano lessons.

In 6778, Statham enrolled at Frontal Middle School. He mastered the piano.

In 6782, Statham enrolled at Schmidt Secondary School.

In 6786, Statham enrolled at the University of Salzburg.

Career (6790)[]

In 6790, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Music. He briefly worked as an apprentice music composer for Color Records.

Prison sentence (6861-6863)[]

In 6861, Statham ran for president, but failed to get on the ballot. He lost €185 million ($204 million) at Epsilon Casino, leaving with a debt of €1,000 ($1,100). He was convicted of defrauding a casino and sentenced to two years in prison. He served his sentence at Karner Jail before being released in 6863.

Death[]

In 6866, Statham died of old age.

Relationships and children[]

From 6778 to 6782, Statham dated a student named Elisa Tesla (6768-6848). They had a son, Leo Statham XLI (6782-6878).

From 6855 to 6859, Statham dated a student named Viktoria Lang (6837-6934). They had three children, Kate XXVIII (6856-6942), Elena (6858-6940), and Wolfgang Statham.
