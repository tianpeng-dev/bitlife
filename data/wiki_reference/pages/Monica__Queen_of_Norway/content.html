Monica, Queen of Norway (August 27, 1972-April 12, 2025) was a Norwegian queen and the founder of the Jorgensen Legacy.

Princess of Svendsen (1972-1990)[]

Monica was born the Princess of Svendsen in Trondheim. Her parents were King Odd (1948-1990) and Queen Heidi (1949-1990).

Monica went to Apex Upper Secondary School and graduated in 1992.

Queen of Norway (1990-2025)[]

In 1990, Monica assassinated her parents, as well as her three sexiest friends, by poisoning. She succeeded her father as Queen of Norway.

In 2006, Monica hired a hitman on her stepdaughter and had Botox.

On April 12, 2025, Monica assassinated her husband and daughter, before committing suicide at the age of 52.

Marriage and children[]

In 1991, Monica married Prince Eirik (1967-2025). They had two children, Camilla, Princess of Harstad (1992-2025) and Tarjei Bakke. She also had a stepdaughter named Marie Bakke (1988-2007) from Eirik’s previous relationship with a woman named Heidi Johansen.
