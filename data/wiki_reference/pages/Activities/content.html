Activities

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)

Activities are one of the most important mechanics of BitLife, as from here the player will have access to further gameplay, which gives your character things to do and shape them for Ribbons. Activities are greyed out if the character is too young to do them, and will stop being greyed out when they reach the minimum age to do these activities. Many activities can have mixed results and some include scenarios with them. Some actions are dependent on the nation your character is from, such as the driving age per nation, and the laws on doing gambling. You can add activities into Favourite Activities in the Settings which put them at the very top, which can reduce time scrolling and searching for an activity you commonly do.
