Singapore
is a
city-state
added to BitLife in March 2019. Its only default city is itself.
Singapore was part of a March 2019
update
that added 8 new countries into the game. The other 7 were
Portugal
,
Norway
,
Indonesia
,
Denmark
,
Finland
,
Estonia
, and
Romania
. This update was also the update that brought
Pets
for the first time, and was also the first to have multiple new countries being added other than the ones originally released.
