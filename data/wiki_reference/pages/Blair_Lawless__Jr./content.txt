Blair Lawless, Jr.
(3444-July 18, 3452) was an American student.
Life (3444-3452)
[
]
Lawless was born in Phoenix, Arizona. He was the only son of
Ha-Eun Cha XII
and her boyfriend Brian Lawless, Sr.
In 3449, Lawless enrolled at Jada Vazquez Elementary School.
On July 18, 3452, Lawless saw his classmate, Cameron, acting up in class and reported her to the principal. Cameron shot Lawless with a Gatling gun, killing him and ending the Ali Legacy after over 1,500 years.
