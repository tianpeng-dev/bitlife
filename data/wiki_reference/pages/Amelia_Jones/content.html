Early life (2041-2058)[]

Jones was born in Adelaide, South Australia in 2041. Her father was Chase Jones.

From 2051 to 2058, she dated Jack Miller (2041-2128). In 2058, they had a son named Kobe (d. 2139).

Acting (first career, 2059-2073)[]

In 2059, Jones graduated high school. She briefly worked as a roadkill remover for Davies of Adelaide before she began playing Mikayla Fisher in Medieval Memoirs. She created her YouTube channel.

In 2062, Medieval Memoirs was cancelled. Jones played Michelle Pratt in How to Get Away with Reading.

In 2063, Jones wrote the book Uncovered, to mixed reviews.

In 2069, How to Get Away with Reading was cancelled. Jones played Mercy Hopkins in Tailors Who Shear.

In 2072, Jones uploaded a video of her completing the 100 baby challenge in BitLife. It went viral on YouTube, with over 3 million views.

Music (2074-2122)[]

In 2074, Tailors Who Shear was cancelled. Jones signed as a worship singer with Back Door Entertainment. She released her first album, and would continue releasing albums into the 22nd century.

She released her last album in 2116, and went on her last world tour in 2120.

Acting (second career, 2123-present)[]

In 2123, she was released by Back Door Entertainment. She played Layla Rooney in The Sight of Cinema, for which she won her first award, for Best Supporting Actress. She would continue playing film roles every year until 2128, when she started playing Ned Mosley in How I Met Your Brother.

In 2128, she married, and then killed, Jacob Jones (né Hall-Gordon, 2105-2128). He had a daughter named Aspen Hall-Gordon (b. 2123).
