Billy Cooper, Sr. (b. 2356) is an American retired teacher and pornographer.

Early life (2356-2378)[]

Cooper was born in Nashville, Tennessee. He is the youngest of three brothers born to Wyatt Cooper and Keisha Montana. Wyatt married Celestina Bakke, making Billy the youngest of her nine children.

In 2367, Cooper enrolled at Mayfield Middle School.

In 2370, Cooper enrolled at Amplitude Preparatory School.

In 2374, Cooper enrolled at Tennessee State University. He majored in education.

In 2378, Cooper graduated with a Bachelor of Education.

Careers (2378-2415)[]

In 2378, Cooper was briefly a school teacher for Metropolitan Nashville Public Schools. He then became a porn writer for Cum Laude Erotica.

In 2382, Cooper was promoted to porn cameraman.

In 2385, Cooper was promoted to porn actor.

In 2415, Cooper was forced to retire.
