Jeremy Garcia (2213 - 2264) was an American professional football player. He was the son actor Jorge Garcia, grandson of actress Desiree Garcia, great-grandson of art gallery professional Lynn Garcia, great-great-grandson of magician Odell Garcia, great-great-great-grandson of businessman Eduardo Garcia, great-great-great-great-grandson of baseball player Colt Garcia, and great-great-great-great-great-grandson of musician Donald Garcia.
