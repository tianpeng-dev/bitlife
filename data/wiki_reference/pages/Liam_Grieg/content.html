Early life (4199-4222)[]

Grieg was born in Bergen, Norway. He is the younger of two children born to Sydney Cook and the only born to her second boyfriend Arne Grieg.

In 4205, Grieg enrolled at St. Morten’s Primary School.

In 4212, Grieg enrolled at Medley Lower Secondary School.

In 4215, Grieg enrolled at Bergen Upper Secondary School.

In 4216, Grieg successfully burgled five houses.

In 4218, Grieg graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Vestland School Board, but lost to Anna Holm. He enrolled at the University of Bergen.

Political career (4222-4234)[]

In 4222, Grieg graduated from university with a BS in Political Science. He ran for election to the Vestland School Board and beat Bente Hagen.

In 4226, Grieg ran for reelection and beat Espen Rasmussen.

In 4230, Grieg ran for a third term and beat Erik Pettersen. He was caught burgling a nerd’s house and tried for burglary, decreasing his happiness to 49%. However, he was found not guilty.

In 4234, Grieg did not run for a fourth term. He emigrated to Stuttgart, Germany.

Lobbying career (4236-4254)[]

In 4236, Grieg became a junior lobbyist for Kayak Mills.

In 4243, Grieg resigned and emigrated to Brussels, Belgium.

In 4244, Grieg became a junior lobbyist for Ash Data.

In 4247, Grieg was promoted to lobbyist.

Grieg was alive in 4254, but his death date is unknown.
