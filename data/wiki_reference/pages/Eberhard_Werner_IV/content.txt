Eberhard Werner IV
(4584-after January 4, 4631) was a German-Italian cameraman.
Early life (4584-4605)
[
]
Werner was born in Stuttgart, Germany. He is the younger of two children born to
Eberhard Werner III
and his ex-girlfriend Carla Weber.
In 4602, Werner illegally emigrated to Turin, Italy.
Career (4605-4631)
[
]
In 4605, Werner became an apprentice cameraman for Rhythm Broadcasting Corporation.
In 4607, Werner was promoted to cameraman.
Werner outlived his son, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and children
[
]
In 4602, Werner started dating a Dutch-Italian trucker named Julia van Nistelrooy (4584-after January 4, 4631). They have two children, Jasmin Werner IV (4603-after January 4, 4631) and
Eberhard Werner V
.
