Julia Shrub (born 1974) is an American computer programmer who works for Amigo Dynamics. She is the middle child and elder daughter of late U.S. President Henry Shrub, her siblings being Grayson and Martha. Julia is also an activist and advocate for LGBT rights.

Early life[]

Julia was born in 1974 to Henry and Emma Shrub, between the time of Henry's honorable discharge from the Air Force and the time he started working as a lawyer. Her grandparents were Jackie Shrub and Hannah Shrub.

Career[]

Shrub graduated from business school in 1999. She subsequently started a position at Amigo Dynamics as a junior computer programmer.

Personal life[]

After being closeted for many years, Julia came out as a lesbian to her family in 2003. Henry offered her his unconditional support. In 2012, she married receptionist and fellow LGBT activist Olivia Diggler (born 1978).
