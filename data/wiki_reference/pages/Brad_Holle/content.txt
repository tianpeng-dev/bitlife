Early life
[

Brad was born in St. Louis, Missouri to Asia Harris, a Jeweler (Born in 1928) and James Harris, an Army enlistee (Also born in 1928). He was born in August 24, 1953. He was a planned pregnancy.
When he was  2 years old (1955), he befriended Joseph Smart when visiting Disneyland on a vacation. When he was 5 years old (1960), he entered elementary school at Little Handy Elementary. When he was 7 years old (1962), he started taking bass guitar lessons.
When he was 9 years old (1964), his classmate, Clarence, acted up in class, Harris decided to ignore his antics.
When he was 12 years old (1967), he tried to apply to his school’s football team, but failed.
When he was 16 years old (1971), he successfully entered his school’s baseball team and he had a blast playing there.
When he was 18 years old (1973), he started university and studied Computer Science and applied his university’s baseball team.
