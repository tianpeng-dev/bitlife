Darius Cross III (2104-2205) was an American-British computer programmer.

Early life (2104-2128)[]

Cross was born in Dallas, Texas. He was the younger of two children born to Darius Cross, Jr. and his girlfriend Miley Richardson.

In 2109, Cross started wearing glasses.

Cross was the vice president of the Dungeons and Dragons club at Bautista Middle School.

Cross went to Transcendent High School and graduated in 2122.

Cross went to the University of North Texas and graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 2126. He was a member of the science club before graduating with a Master of Science in Computer Science in 2128.

Career (2128)[]

In 2128, Cross became a computer programmer for Valley Corp. He resigned less than a year later and emigrated to Ipswich, England.

Death[]

In 2205, Cross died of old age.
