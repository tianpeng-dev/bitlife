Early life (5458-5477)
[
]
Statham was born in Naples, Italy. She is the only child of
Kate Statham X
and her ex-boyfriend Claudio Cutolo.
Career (5477-5517)
[
]
In 5477, Statham graduated from secondary school. She ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Stefania Angelo.
In 5482, Statham ran for reelection and beat Federico Bellini.
In 5487, Statham ran for Mayor of Naples as a Democrat and beat Brothers of Italy candidate Valentina Rizzuto.
In 5492, Statham ran for reelection and beat Italian Left candidate Cosimo Napolitano.
In 5497, Statham ran for a third term, but lost to FdI candidate Giacomo Jilani. She ran for a third term on the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Pietro Angelo.
In 5502, Statham ran for a fourth term and beat Matteo Bucca.
In 5507, Statham ran for a third term as mayor and beat SI candidate Lia Alighieri.
In 5512, Statham ran for a fourth term and beat FdI candidate Leone Polo.
In 5517, Statham did not run for a fifth term.
Death
[
]
In 5536, Statham died of old age. Her funeral was held in Naples.
Relationships and children
[
]
From 5477 to 5478, Statham dated a student named Remo Rizzuto (5459-after 5516). They had a son, Leo Statham VI (5478-after 5538).
From 5485 to 5497, Statham dated a mailman named Carlo Altera (5463-after 5538). They had 12 children, Claudia Altera (5486-after 5538), Leo Statham VII (5487-after 5538), Leo Statham VIII (5488-after 5538), Lia Altera (5489-5497), Rosa Altera (5490-5510), Druscilla Altera (5491-after 5538), Veronica Altera (5492-5522), Gioia Altera (5493-after 5538), Francesca Altera (5494-after 5538), Leo Statham IX (5495-after 5538), Leo Statham X (5496-after 5538), and
Leo Statham XI
.
