Bogdan Stepaniak (August 29, 1978 - September 16, 2051) was a Ukrainian military man, born in Kharkiv (USSR) and lived there all his life until his death.

Early age (up to 18 years)[]

Bogdan was born in Kharkiv on August 29, 1978. His mother, Olena Stepaniak (1950 - 2021) became pregnant after soaking in filthy public hot tub. His father was unknown and also they had a family cat named Simba. When he was 1 year old, his mother married Pavlo Chura (1947-1993), a 32-year-old retailer with a child named Lyudmila Chura (born in 1978). Bogdan's stepfather treated him badly, was often angry with him and refused to spend time with him. At the age of 4, he became friends with his first friend Mikhailo Pavlik (1978 - 2049). At the age of 6, he was enrolled in primary school at Alexander Conrad Primary School, also he became friends with Joseph Pitoniak (born in 1978), Fadeyka Fedak (1978 - 2038). When he was 8, his mother gave birth to a sister Olena Chura (born in 1986). When Bogdan was 9, his beloved cat Simba died. Bogdan asked his parents to buy him a dog, but they refused. When Bogdan was 10 years old, his sister Olena was diagnosed with cancer of buttocks, but 1 year later she was cured. At the age of 9 he became friends with his beautiful classmate Zhadana Petrenko (born in 1978). At the age of 10 he asked out to Zhadana Petrenko. Bogdan started dating with her. Pavel Chura, Bogdan's stepfather, died at the age of 46. He was killed by Bogdan. Bogdan could no longer stand such an attitude towards himself. Also at the age of 15 Bogdan started upper secondary school. When he was 18, he graduated from upper secondary school.

During these 73 years, Bogdan has lived a fairly happy life, he has been abroad, at sea, although he did not live very long. Rest in peace, Bogdan!
