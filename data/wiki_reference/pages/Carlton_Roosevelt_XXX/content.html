Capt. Carlton Roosevelt XXX (3261-3306) was a Romanian bubblegum singer and veteran.

Early life (3261-3284)[]

Roosevelt was born in Transylvania, Romania. He was the youngest of four children born to Liam Roosevelt and his girlfriend Adelina Lupu.

In 3280, Roosevelt enrolled at Babeș-Bolyai University. He majored in political science.

In 3284, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science.

Military service (3284-3289)[]

In 3284, Roosevelt joined the Romanian Land Forces as a Sublieutenant.

In 3285, Roosevelt was promoted to Lieutenant.

In 3289, Roosevelt was promoted to Captain. He was honorably discharged.

Music career (3292-3306)[]

In 3292, Roosevelt signed with Rosebud Records as a pop singer. He released his debut bubblegum album, I’ve Got the Flower, which went gold in less than a year.

In 3293, Roosevelt released his second album, Daydreaming of Nowhere Else, which went gold in 3296.

In 3296, Roosevelt released his third album, Gelatinous Valley, which went gold in less than a year.

In 3299, Roosevelt released his fourth album, Playing Dress-Up, which went diamond in less than a year.

In 3303, Roosevelt released his fifth album, CARTPOP, which went diamond in less than a year.

Death[]

In 3306, Roosevelt tried to murder his daughter, Venus, with a set of plastic vampire teeth. She took out a set of her own and killed him in self defense.

Relationship and children[]

In 3287, Roosevelt started dating a graphic designer named Timea Munteanu (3257-after 3313). They had four children:

  2. Alina Roosevelt (3290-after 3313)
