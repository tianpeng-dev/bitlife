An exotic pet in BitLife is a pet that is not found in the pet store, and usually in the wild. You can get these pets by going to the Exotic Pet Dealer. This is impossible if you are not a Bitizen or a god mode player.

If you have more than 20 exotic pets, you can get the Tarzan Ribbon. You can also get exotic pets from an Encounter/Animal. You can also get mauled by a pet such as a tiger , lion or a jaguar.
