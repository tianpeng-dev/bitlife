Clinton "Clint" Buckles (born 1948) is an American historian and retired truck driver. A Vietnam War veteran, he is the oldest child of D-Day veteran John Buckles.

Early life[]

Clinton was born in 1948 to John Buckles (September 11, 1923 - June 6, 2024) and his wife, Farah Groper (born 1923), a firefighter. His younger sister was Mercedes (1950 - 2022), and his younger brother is Lee (born 1958).

Vietnam War[]

Despite applying to become a trucker after graduating high school in 1966, Clinton was still subject to the draft. After dodging the draft for a little while by starting his trucking career as early as possible, he ultimately decided to enlist in the Army, as he felt pressured to serve his country just like his father in World War II.

Trucking career[]

After leaving the Army in 1974, Buckles officially began his career as a truck driver.
