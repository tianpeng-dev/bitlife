Gabrielle
(born 1962) and
Gretchen Hawskon
(1962 - 1997), collectively known as the
Hawkson Twins
, were an American duo of twin sisters. Their father was soldier-turned-spy
Eagle Hawkson
. Gabrielle is the surviving twin following Gretchen's death in 1997 from a car accident.
