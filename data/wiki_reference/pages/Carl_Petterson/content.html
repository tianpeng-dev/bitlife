Carl Petterson (4037-after 4075) was a Swedish marriage counselor.

Early life (4037-4059)[]

Petterson was born in Malmö, Sweden. He is the only child of Esteban Petterson and his wife Olivia Persson.

In 4044, Petterson enrolled at Avalon Primary School.

In 4053, Petterson enrolled at Malmö Secondary School.

In 4055, Petterson graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Malmö School Board, but lost to Alva Vikander. He enrolled at Malmö University Faculty of Health and Society and graduated in 4059 with a BS in Psychology.

Career (4059-4075)[]

In 4059, Petterson became a marriage counselor for Kayak Health Services.

Petterson was alive in 4075, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4047 to 4053, Petterson dated a student named Ines Berggren (4037-after 4075). They had a daughter, Matilda Petterson II (4053-after 4075).

In 4055, Petterson and Berggren started dating again. They have a daughter, Isabella Petterson II.
