Michael Hurt-Sack
(1943 - April 8, 2020) was a United States Army veteran.
Early life
[
]
Michael was born in 1943 to parents who were both doing their part in World War II. His father was a soldier, and his mother was a military nurse and medical expert.
Military service
[
]
Hurt-Sack first enlisted in the Army in 1961 at the age of 18.
...
After four decades of service, Hurt-Sack retired from the Army in 2005 at the mandatory retirement age of 62.
Personal life
[
]
Michael was married to a woman named Rebeca. They had three children together. He died on April 8, 2020, and is survived by his three children and two grandchildren.
