Marcos Sossegado
, born in January 26, 2006, was an Brazilian bishop of the Recife Community Church for 93 years, passing away at 114 in October 3, 2120
Insipired by his name, in which means "peaceful" in Portuguese, he led a quiet life with his friends and family, while working at his local church.
One impactful thing was when his best friend, Giovanna Do Vale, died from cancer of the buttocks at 34 years old.
At 52 years old, Mr. Sossegado found an unconscious woman at the grocery store and saved her life.
