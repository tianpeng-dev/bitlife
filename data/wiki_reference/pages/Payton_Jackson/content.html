Payton Jackson (2154-after 2222) was a British-American soccer player.

Early life[]

Jackson was born in Bristol, England. Her father is John Jackson. She emigrated to Montreal, Quebec with her father as a baby. She was on the St. Louis’s Secondary School soccer team from 2169 to 2172 and the McGill Martlets from 2172 to 2176.

Career[]

In 2176, Jackson emigrated back to Bristol and joined Goodison FC as a forward.

In 2183, Jackson was traded to Brighton.

In 2187, Jackson was released by Brighton, emigrated to Detroit, Michigan, and joined Orlando FC.

In 2189, Jackson was traded to Tacoma FC.

In 2194, Jackson was released by Tacoma, emigrated back to Bristol again, and joined Villa FC.

In 2195, Jackson was released by Villa and emigrated back to Detroit.

Relationships and children[]

From 2169 to 2187, Jackson started dating a student named Edouard Fortin (b. 2154), who became a policeman in 2172. They had a son, Riley Fortin (b. 2171).

In 2187, Jackson started dating a retail salesman named Steven Jones (b. 2152). They have a daughter, Ana Jones.
