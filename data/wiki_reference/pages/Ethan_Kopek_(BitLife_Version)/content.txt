Ethan Aaron Kopek
(August 9, 1989 - December 25, 2024) was an American police officer, security guard, TSA officer, and former airport baggage handler. He gained prominence as a hero during Christmas 2023 when he saved many people from getting killed by a nerve agent at Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) by a mysterious traveler. The event served as partial inspiration for the 2024 holiday action thriller film
Carry-On
, in which he was portrayed by Taron Egerton.
Following his heroic actions, Kopek became an LAPD officer and would serve for a full year. During his time as a cop, he would embark on a crusade of vigilantism, targeting dangerous criminals on the streets by stealing from them. This led to his murder on Christmas Day 2024, a mere couple of weeks after the release of the film.

Early life and education
[

Kopek in his younger years as a baggage handler.
Ethan was born in Los Angeles to real estate agent Viviane Kopek (born 1942) and restaurateur Aaron Kopek (born 1944), who allegedly served in Vietnam. He has an older brother named Tyson (born 1980), a mailman. The family owned a beagle named Paisley (1988 - 1999). Ethan's best friend is Malik Cruise (born 1988), a flight attendant.
Baggage career
[
]
After graduation, Kopek landed an interview with Colombo Air for their baggage handler opening position in 2007. He was hired, thus beginning his career in the business of transport safety and inspection.
Security and TSA
[
]
Following his completion of community college, Kopek found a new job as a security guard for Weed Media. His experience as a baggage handler also made him eligible to work as a TSA officer at LAX.
