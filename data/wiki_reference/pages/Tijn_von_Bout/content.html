Tijn von Bout (born 1994) is a Dutch writer. He specializes in the crime genre, writing about both true crime and fiction. Many of his stories are inspired by the activities of his notorious father, Dar von Bout.

Early life[]

Tijn was born in Amsterdam to Dar Von Bout and his wife, restaurateur Tess Bijl, whom he married a year before in 1993. At the time of his birth, his father was among the top dealers of the Powder Bruises drug cartel. His grandparents were Femke Dekker (1935 - 2015), who, like his mother, worked in the restaurant business, and Jens von Bout (1936 - 2019), a fighter pilot in the Royal Netherlands Air Force. His aunt was Lianne von Bout (1971 - 2009), a firewoman.

Tijn is the oldest child of his nine other siblings:

  • Emma (born 1997) - College professor
  • Hugo (born 1999) - Architect
  • Femke (born 2002) - Communications student
  • Lieke (2004 - 2024) - Engineering student; died from liver cancer
  • Jens (born 2006)
  • James (born 2012) - Secondary school student
  • Tom (born 2014) - Primary school student
  • Martin (born 2016) - Primary school student
  • Amy (born 2018) - Primary school student
