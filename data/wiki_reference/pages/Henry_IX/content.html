Prince Henry IX of Curry (born Henry Tudor; 1 August 2003 - 12 October 2022) was an heir to the throne of the United Kingdom. He was the first "Henry" in the royal family since Henry VIII. In the last years of his life, Henry went mad and murdered his parents as a means to ascend to the throne early, but he later committed suicide out of regret.

Early life[]

Henry Tudor was born in Greenwich to Queen Sara (1965 - 2022) and Prince Consort Billy (1950 - 2021).

Marriage and issue[]

Henry met Rita Scott (born 2003) in secondary school. After five years of dating, they married in 2021 when they were both 18. Their only child, a daughter, Princess Ellie, was born a year after their wedding.
