Finn Fernandez (2457-after 2512) was a German grocer.

Early life (2457-2474)[]

Fernandez was born in Berlin, Germany. His parents were Faber Fernandez and his ex-girlfriend Lisa Busch. He went to St. Luther’s Secondary School.

Career (2475-2512)[]

In 2475, Fernandez became an apprentice grocer for Wanderlust Grocery.

In 2477, Fernandez was promoted to grocer.

Fernandez retired in 2512, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 2467 to 2474, Fernandez dated a student named Elisa Sauer (2457-after 2512), who later became a policy analyst. They had a daughter, Tilda Fernandez.
