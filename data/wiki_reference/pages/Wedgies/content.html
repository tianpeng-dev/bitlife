Wedgies

Scenario Type

Mess | Murder

Description

A randomized message appears when you mess with someone saying you wedgied them or you can attempt to murder someone via an atomic wedgie.

Also on Android?

Yes


Wedgies are when you yank someone's underwear upwards forcefully in this game. Atomic Wedgies are when you try to pull the underwear over their heads, an attempt to suffocate someone.

In school, you can be bullied and the bully can give you a wedgie. You can choose to "mess" with someone, one of your classmates, and randomly the 'messing' will say you gave the person a wedgie.

In prison, you can "mess" with one of the prison yard members and it will randomly say you gave them a wedgie.

You can attack someone and randomly it will say that you hurt that person in the selected method of attack (say you were kicking the groin), it would then say (note: randomly, not all the time) "You kicked Jester in the groin and then gave him a wedgie."

Murdering someone with an Atomic Wedgie is an interesting method of murdering someone but it is not without its faults. On a success, you pull their underwear over their head and they suffocate. On a failure, the cops are called and you are injured. Should there be some form of a backfire, their waistband may break and it may be stuffed into the mouth as a method to choke.

Used in: Ultimate Bully Challenge (must murder someone with an atomic wedgie)
