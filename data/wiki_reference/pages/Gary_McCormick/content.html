Gareth Denver McCormick (April 24, 1953 - October 16, 2021) was an American football player and convicted felon. He played as a linebacker for the Dallas Buckaroos and the New York Pets. He eventually retired from the Pets to pursue an army career, and later became a jack of all trades after his military service.

McCormick's positive reputation was tarnished in 1993 when he attempted to kill his former wife, Carrie, over their divorce a year prior. He was convicted of attempted murder and spent the last 28 years of his life in prison until his death in 2021.

Death[]

McCormick died in prison at the age of 68 from complications of old age. His funeral was attended by his siblings and niece. His children, still heartbroken by their father's murderous intent, did not attend his services.
