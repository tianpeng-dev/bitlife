Genesis Roosevelt (3330-3364) was an American business analyst.

Early life (3330-3353)[]

Roosevelt was born in New York City. She was the only child of Andi Roosevelt and his girlfriend Aretha Rossi.

In 3335, Roosevelt enrolled at Payton Scrooge Elementary School.

In 3341, Roosevelt enrolled at Magnitude Middle School.

In 3344, Roosevelt enrolled at Empire High School.

In 3347, Roosevelt was sent to the New York Orphanage after murdering her parents.

In 3349, Roosevelt enrolled at Empire State University. She majored in information systems.

In 3353, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems.

Career (3353-3364)[]

In 3353, Roosevelt became a junior business analyst for Fuji Energy.

In 3364, Roosevelt and her boyfriend were murdered by their daughter.
