Mind & Body
is an
activity
that can give small bursts of health and smarts to increase your
Stats
and can even help raise
Karma
. In addition, they may be a little more happy as a result of self-care. Mind and Body was a standard default top fave go-to activity, until an
update
allowed players to choose which activities should go on the top for their own games. In most locations, characters need to be at least 12-13 for meditating or library or 16 to work out at the gym or to take a memory test. Later it was changed to 12 for all 4: meditating, library, gym, and memory test. This can be done in
Prison
and juvenile detention (except for a memory test) with gym being free. There was an update which added 5 more Mind and Body activities: Reading books, Going on a Diet, Gardening, Martial Arts, and Walks.
Mind & Body Activities
[
]
Books
- The player can choose a book for their character and tap the screen to read the pages. They can also get a manga (Fruits Basket, Vol. 1). This can improve Smarts for the character and have them say how much they enjoyed the book. If a character does not want to finish a book, they may abandon it or find a different book. All books are based on real-life books. Children may only read children's books. An achievement can be unlocked for reading the entire dictionary. Unlike the Library, reading multiple individual books in one year can improve Smarts every time rather than just once a year.

Diet
- Go on a diet. Some diets can improve health while others lower health. Diets with lower health benefits could lead to
Health problems
. Each diet has a monthly price.
List of diets and its effects (May not be accurate)
Atkins diet: Slightly lowers health.
High Calorie diet: Raises happiness, lowers health and appearance (-16 health per year)
Hot Cheetos diet: Lowers health rapidly (-50 health per year)
Jenny Craig diet: Raises happiness and health
Keto diet: Raises health, lowers happiness slightly
Liquid diet: Lowers looks, happiness and health, could result in being diagnosed with malnutrition
Mediterranean diet: Raises happiness and health
Paleo diet: Raises health and looks, lowers happiness
South Beach diet: Raises health and looks
Tapeworm diet: Lowers health and looks rapidly (-50 health, -50 looks per year)
Vegan diet: Character gets malnutrition more easily, raises health, lowers happiness
Vegetarian diet: Character gets malnutrition more easily, raises health
Weight Watchers diet: Raises happiness, health, and looks
Whole30 diet: Raises health, lowers happiness.
Gardening
- If the character has
a house
, they may do some gardening in their garden. You can garden in your parents' house until you're 18, when you need to own a house. This will show a bar showing how relaxing it was while gardening. Gardening increases happiness by 8%.
List of Gardening actions
Cutting back the plants
Fertilizing the shrubs
Filling all the birdbaths with water and bird feeders with seeds
Planting some [plant]
Pruning the trees
Pulling all of the weeds
Raking the leaves
Trimming the shrubs
Gym
- Going to the gym costs $5–20 per visit and increases health and appearance. If your character has less than 10% health, they will be too weak to work out and unable to do so. Some
physical ailments
can be contracted at the gym such as ringworm or athlete's foot, but these go away quickly after
aging
or seeing
the doctor.
Sometimes a player can find
Love
at the gym, where they have an opportunity to ask someone on a date or accept a date request. It is available free on
Prison
.
Memory Test
- It is a test with a
mini-game
where you need to press the flashing colors after they're done flashing, the same colors the same amount of times. An easy way to win this to write the initials of the colors on a paper. This either increases or decreases your smarts depending on your score.
Library
- Going to the city's public library increases smarts. This tends to be faster increase when the character is younger rather than old. Sometimes a player can find
Love
at the library, where they have an opportunity to ask someone on a date or accept a date request.
Martial arts
- The player can rank up in belts and use moves on their enemies in assault situations. They can learn jiu-jitsu, taekwondo, judo, karate, or kung fu. Killing someone with one of these moves can earn an
achievement
. If the character has less than 60% health, they will probably not rank up in belts, and if their health is below 10%, they will be unable to attend these classes altogether.
Meditation
- Meditation increases health and improves karma. You can also find a "deep awareness" of yourself, meaning you will know a random stat like, say, athleticism or fertility.
Walks
- The character can choose from various walks. Walking improves one's health. They might encounter scenarios such as finding a purse or an animal. However, in some unfortunate cases, the character could suffer a massive heart
attack
, which usually happens if the character is old and walks at too fast a pace.
