Leo Statham XXXI (5960-after 6020) was an American receptionist.

Early life (5960-5978)[]

Statham was born in Newark, New Jersey. He was the only child of Leo Statham XXX and his ex-girlfriend Misty Mendoza-Buck.

In 5965, Statham enrolled at St. Bob’s Elementary School.

In 5971, Statham enrolled at Onyx Middle School.

In 5974, Statham enrolled at Renaissance Preparatory School. He learned how to perform a karate chop.

In 5978, Statham graduated from high school.

Career (5984-6020)[]

In 5984, Statham became a receptionist for Diversity Fashions.

Statham was alive in 6020, but his death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 5970 to 5974, Statham dated a student named Faith Bigg-Sprinkle (5960-after 5983).

From 5974 to 5978, Statham dated a student named Desiree Green (5960-after 6020), who had 51% popularity. They had two children, Kate XXII (5977-after 6020) and Leo Statham XXXII.
