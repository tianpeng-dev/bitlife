Jasmin Werner XIV (4780-after 4833) was a Japanese-American wedding planner.

Early life (4780-4811)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. She was the only child of Jasmin Werner XIII and her ex-boyfriend Kota Yokoyama.

In 4798, Werner graduated from upper secondary school. She emigrated to Buffalo, New York.

Career (4811-4833)[]

In 4811, Werner became a junior wedding planner for Mr. Comrade.

In 4814, Werner was promoted to wedding planner.

In 4817, Werner was promoted to senior wedding planner.

Werner was alive in 4833, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4798 to 4800, Werner dated a British-American student named Lucas Hayes (4780-after 4833). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner XV.
