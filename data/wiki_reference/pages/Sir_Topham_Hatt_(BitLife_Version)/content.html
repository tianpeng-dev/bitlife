Personal life[]

Hatt married Jane Brown, brother of Sir Handel Brown, the owner of the Skarloey Railway. They had two children, Barbara Jane and Charles, and at least two granchildren, including Stephen and Bridget. Barbara Jane later married Henry Regaby, a Colonel in the Sodor Regiment during World War II.
