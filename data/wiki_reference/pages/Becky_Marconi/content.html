Rebecca "Becky" Marconi (1903 - 1989) was an American school teacher and the mother of rockstar musician Chris Marconi (CMarc).

Death[]

Becky died in 1989, at age 86. Chris cremated her, sprinkling her ashes in a lavender field. He and his little brother, Moe, each inherited over $3.5 million. Later that year, Chris made a song dedicated to her, titled "Bye Bye Mommy." In 1990, the song was used in a commercial for a child hunger hotline, which Chris had also starred in.
