Edward Nygma (November 18, 1976 - September 16, 2022) was a forensic scientist from Gotham City. He was also known as an intelligent vigilante under the alias "Riddler". Most, if not all of his riddles focused on the corruption within Gotham.

Early life[]

Edward was born to GCPD officer Arleen Nygma (neé Nashton) (1934 - 2011) and financial analyst and occasional scientist Woodrow Bartholomew "Woody" Nygma (1930 - 2003). He had an older sister, Tiana (1974 - 2021), a policy analyst, accountant and businesswoman.
