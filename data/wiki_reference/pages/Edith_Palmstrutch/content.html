Edith Palmstrutch (August 31, 1928 - February 24, 2022) was a Swedish history teacher and housekeeper.

Childhood years and education[]

Edith was born in Halmstad as the result of a failed pull-out between Elsa Dahlberg (1889 - 1970) and Mohamed Palmstrutch (1861 - 1940). Her father was a former financial advisor, having retired a couple years or so before Edith's birth. Her mother was a museum worker.

History teaching career[]

Straight out of college, Palmstrutch began working for Halmstsd Public Schools as a history teacher in 1950. The first topics she began to teach were of the Second World War, the brewing Korean War and the baby boom following World War Two.
