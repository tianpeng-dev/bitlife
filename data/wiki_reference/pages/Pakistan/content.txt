Pakistan
is a
country
that was on BitLife in October 2019. Its default cities are the capital, Islamabad, along with Abbotabad, Karachi, Lahore, and Peshawar.
Pakistan was added as a new country to BitLife in late October 2019 during the Haunted Update, as the only non-Haunted addition to the game.

Information
[
]
Being an Islam-based country, female characters in BitLife will wear hijabs when they are adults. However unlike
Afghanistan
,
Iran
,
Iraq
,
Saudi Arabia
, or
UAE
, females do not have hijabs in Pakistan.
Pakistan, along with
Afghanistan
,
Angola
,
Belize
,
El Salvador
,
Guatemala
,
Guyana
,
Haiti
,
Iraq
,
Papua New Guinea
,
Syria
and
Venezuela
, are the 12 countries in the game where
deaths
are influenced by the country a character is from, in this case, characters can die from unexpected terrorist or battlefield attacks. Being killed by an attack under the age of 30 will give your character the
Unlucky
ribbon, making it easier to obtain (though there is a chance that they may end up getting a different ribbon besides
Unlucky
).
Family
members can also die from these as well and it is likely that a parent or parents will die while your character is still very young. However, the death toll by terrorist and battlefield attacks are much lower in Pakistan, and don't happen as often.
