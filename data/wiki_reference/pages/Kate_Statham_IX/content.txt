Kate Statham IX
(5418-after 5477) was an Italian politician and receptionist.

Early life (5418-5437)
[
]
Statham was born in Naples. She was the older of two daughters born to
Kate Statham VIII
and her ex-boyfriend Emanuele Vicoli.
Political career (5437-5457)
[
]
In 5437, Statham graduated from secondary school. She ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Ludovica Ferro.
In 5442, Statham ran for reelection and beat Claudia Opizzi.
In 5447, Statham ran for Metropolitan Mayor of Naples as a Democrat and beat Brothers of Italy candidate Giuseppe Toscano.
In 5452, Statham ran for reelection and beat FdI candidate Gioele Columbus.
In 5457, Statham did not run for a third term.
Front desk career (5458-5477)
[
]
In 5458, Statham became a receptionist for Altitude Communications.
Statham was alive in 5477, but her death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 5437 to 5438, Statham dated a wedding planner named Herberto Valsalva (5413-after 5477). They had a daughter,
Kate Statham X
.
