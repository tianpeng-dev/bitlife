Eleanor Hollywood (October 6th, 2120 - Present), also known as Elly Hollywood, is a famous punk rockstar. She is the daughter of the famous actress, Stephanie Groper.

Early Life[]

Eleanor Hollywood was born on October 6th, 2078 to Stephanie Groper (2078-2162) a famous actress, and Nicholas Hollywood (2078-2166), a Sr. Financial Advisor.

Personal Life[]

Hollywood first experimented with music at the age of 12, where she practiced playing her great-grandfather’s, Jolan Soro’s, Ash guitar. She would go on to take singing lessons before graduating in 2138. She would go on to have 5 children through artificial insemination. In 2151, Hollywood got married to Jenna Mendoza, a runway model.

Career[]

After graduating high school in 2138, Hollywood auditioned for multiple record contracts before signing a deal with the company Uranus Studios. She is known for her hits such as Psycho-Logical, Saint Maria, Goodbye, Wake Up Call, Good Mourning, In-N-Out, Elly’s Hellhole, and Mother to Daughter.

Trivia[]

  • Hollywood is an open lesbian.
  • Hollywood wrote the album Stage Rage after she was attacked and harassed by an obsessive fan while using the restroom and failing to sue the fan.
  • Mother to Daughter is a tribute to Hollywood’s mother, who passed away at the age of 84.
