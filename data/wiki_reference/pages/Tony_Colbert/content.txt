Anthony Jorge Colbert
(December 23, 1927 - 2001) was an American restaurant manager. His twin brother was police officer
Tom Colbert
.
Life and career
[
]
Tony was born with his twin on December 23, 1927 in Brooklyn.
Death
[
]
Tony died in 2001 at the age of 74. Initially rumored to have been one of the restaurant workers of Windows on The World of the World Trade Center that perished in the September 11 attacks, it was later proven that he had died naturally and not when the attacks were even taking place.
