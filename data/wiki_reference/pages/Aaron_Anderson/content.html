Aaron Anderson (3249-after 3305) was a French-British rugby player and pilot.

Early life (3249-3271)[]

Anderson was born in Bordeaux, France. His parents were Yusei Anderson and his ex-girlfriend Amelia Moreau.

In 3252, Anderson emigrated to Sydney, Australia.

In 3259, Anderson emigrated to Cardiff, Wales.

In 3267, Anderson emigrated back to Bordeaux and enrolled at the University of Bordeaux. He was on the rugby team and graduated in 3271 with a Licentiate in Chemistry.

Sports career (3271-3286)[]

In 3271, Anderson emigrated back to Cardiff and joined the Hull Kingston Rollers as a flanker. He won four Most Improved Player awards, four Man of Steel awards, and a championship on the team.

In 3285, Anderson was released by the Rollers and joined the Leeds Hornbills.

In 3286, Anderson retired.

Pilot career (3287-3305)[]

In 3287, Anderson became a Pilot Trainee for Sativa Airways.

In 3292, Anderson was promoted to Co-Pilot.

Anderson was alive in 3305, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 3267 to 3268, Anderson dated a student named Charlotte Durand (b. 3249). They had a daughter, Anna Anderson.
