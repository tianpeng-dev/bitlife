Early life and education[]

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (2027 - 2101) and Cooper Piner (2028 - 2113). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist. She had an older brother, Steven (2049 - 2125), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 2075).
