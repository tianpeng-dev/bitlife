Leon Fernandez (2379-2454) was a German-American politician and dental hygienist.

Early life
[
]
Fernandez was born in Berlin, Germany. His father was
Albert Fernandez
. He went to the Free University of Berlin and graduated with a BS in History in 2401.
Political career
[
]
In 2402, Fernandez was elected to the Berlin GEB. He served eight terms before retiring in 2434.
After retiring, Fernandez emigrated to San Antonio, Texas.
Dental hygienist career
[
]
In 2435, Fernandez became a dental hygienist for Dentistry San Antonio. He resigned in 2445.
Death
[
]
In 2454, Fernandez died of natural causes. He was 75 years old. His funeral was held in San Antonio.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 2398 to 2434, Fernandez dated a student named Annika Lorenz (b. 2379), who became a mechanical engineer in 2401. They had a son,
Paul Fernandez
.
