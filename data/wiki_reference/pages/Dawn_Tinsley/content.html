Early life and education[]

Dawn was born on June 13, 1951 in Los Angeles, California to Veronica and John Tinsley. Her mother was a music composer, while her father worked as a banker until his retirement. She has a distant relationship with her half-brother Forrest, but they are currently on better terms.

At the age of 8, Tinsley took acting lessons and joined her high school drama club at Los Angeles High School.

Career[]

In 1976, at age 18, Hunter's career began as a voice actress for Dreamscape Films. She was promoted to Actress in 1982, and then Lead Actress in 1988. She became officially famous in 1991. However, she was still struggling to make money.

Personal life[]

Health issues[]

During the 1990s, Tinsley was a victim of cocaine addiction, originated from several parties with her friends. She was able to recover from her first addiction with the help of a luxury rehab. She was unable to recover from her drug addiction, as Dreamscape Films kept rejecting her requests to attend rehab.
