Samuel Hable (1943 - July 18, 2019) was a United States Marine Corps officer.

Service[]

After graduating from college, Hable joined the Marines as an officer in 1965. He was among the many first ground troops that arrived in Vietnam on March 8, in the 3rd Battalion, 9th Marines of the 3rd Marine Division.

...

Hable retired in 2005.

Personal life[]

Hable was married and had two children. At some point in his life, he killed someone. Hable died on July 18, 2019. He is survived by his two children and eight grandchildren.
