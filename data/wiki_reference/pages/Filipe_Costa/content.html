Filipe Costa (1954-1981) born on November 12, 1954 in Lisbon, Portugal was held with the hands of two parents, his mother name is Sabine Alfonso, a sergeant at Lisbon Police Department (age 44.) His father name is Gustavo Costa, a makeup artist at Coelho Studios (age 45). Filipe has one big sister named Beatriz (age 6}. During his childhood, he loved planes and wanted to become a pilot.

Early life[]

At, age 1 his mother married Diogo Ribeiro, a 32-year old archaeologist with 2 children, Afonso which he never really liked him, and Jonas the two would play with each other. At age, 3 he wanted to feel how a airplane would move so he ran and jumped in place. At age, 4 his stepfather was trying to convince him he was invisible, so he told his mother. Around age, 6 his mother and his stepfather divorced, as Jonas started ignoring him, as he got lonely. At age 6 he got enrolled at Little Coelho Primary School., an all boys-school. At age 11, he started violin lessons and got a violin, from his mother.

Personal life[]

At age, 18 he got enrolled in English in university, to start his journey as a pilot he also had a part time job as a newspaper delivery person. But at age 19 he soon quit because he had a bad schedule, for 50 hours he took flight school, and then decided to get a pilot license, he went to graduate school at age 22, and is now dating a girl name Flavia Reis, a 18 year old good looking unemployed chick, and got a job at age 24, as a flight attendant, age 25 he and Flavia got pregnant, and named the baby Vicente and got him circumcised. At age, 26 his father passed away from Natural causes, he got buried and the funeral was the following week. Flavia became pregnant with his baby and he bought a Robinson R22 Helicopter for $186,600,


Career[]

At age 18, he worked part time as a newspaper delivery person, At 21, during college he was a lab assistant until he got fired. At age 24, he got hired for Alves Air, flight attendant.

Diseases[]

At age 14, he got diagnosed with Acne. at age 22, he got diagnosed with pneumonia, at age 25, 2 years before his accident he suffered from hives.

Death[]

See also: R22 Coasta Crash.

On March 4th, 1981 While, flying his helicopter, a flock of birds got in the way, causing the craft to yaw dangerously back and forth as it span, falling. He tried spinning his aircraft, and tried to escape fast, but unfortunaly his aircraft crashed onto a forest and sadly passed away at age 27. His wife and son heard, the news but nobody ever found him till this day. The crash was famously named after him, "R22 Costa crash" The, mayor of Lisbon, Portugal felt so bad hearing the loss, against Filipe's family so he decided to build a statue of Filipe on his helicopter. The funeral ceremony was on March 7th, and was attended by his, mother, his girlfriend, Flavia, his son Vicente, and his three siblings. Unfortunaly due to the fathers death, his wife lost the baby.
