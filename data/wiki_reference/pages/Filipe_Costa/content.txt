Filipe Costa (1954-1981) born on November 12, 1954 in Lisbon, Portugal was held with the hands of two parents, his mother name is Sabine Alfonso, a sergeant at Lisbon Police Department (age 44.) His father name is Gustavo Costa, a makeup artist at Coelho Studios (age 45). Filipe has one big sister named Beatriz (age 6}. During his childhood, he loved planes and wanted to become a pilot.

R22 Coasta Crash
.
On March 4th, 1981 While, flying his helicopter, a flock of birds got in the way, causing the craft to yaw dangerously back and forth as it span, falling. He tried spinning his aircraft, and tried to escape fast, but unfortunaly his aircraft crashed onto a forest and sadly passed away at age 27. His wife and son heard, the news but nobody ever found him till this day. The crash was famously named after him, "R22 Costa crash" The, mayor of Lisbon, Portugal felt so bad hearing the loss, against Filipe's family so he decided to build a statue of Filipe on his helicopter. The funeral ceremony was on March 7th, and was attended by his, mother, his girlfriend, Flavia, his son Vicente, and his three siblings. Unfortunaly due to the fathers death, his wife lost the baby. R.I.P Filipe Costa.
