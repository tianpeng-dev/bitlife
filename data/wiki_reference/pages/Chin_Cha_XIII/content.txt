Early life (3256-3274)
[
]
Cha was born in London, England. He was the youngest of five children born to
Andrew Cha IV
and his girlfriend Elizabeth Nottingham.
In 3261, Cha enrolled at St. Nathan’s Primary School.
In 3267, Cha enrolled at Seamen Preparatory School and joined the soccer team.
In 3270, Cha became the captain of the Seamen Prep soccer team.
In 3274, Cha graduated from secondary school.
Sports career (3274-3296)
[
]
In 3274, Cha joined Villa FC as a midfielder.
In 3278, Cha passed a doping test.
In 3279, Cha was traded to West Ham.
West Ham won the 3284 and 3289 League Championships.
In 3288, Cha won the Ballon d’Or.
In 3294, Cha was released by West Ham and joined Liverpool.
Liverpool won the 3295 League Championship.
In 3296, Cha was released by Liverpool. He emigrated to Las Vegas, Nevada.
Travel agency career (3316-3322)
[
]
In 3316, Cha became a travel associate for Spectrum Travel and Cruises.
In 3320, Cha was promoted to tour operator.
In 3322, Cha retired.
Death
[
]
In 3332, Cha died of natural causes.
Relationships and children
[
]
From 3266 to 3296, Cha dated a student named Gracie Sheringham (3256-after 3315), who became a wedding planner in 3275. They had three children:
Ha-Eun Cha IX (3270-3349), Royal Navy enlistee
Chin Cha XIV (3272-3352), flight attendant
Andrew Cha V (3285-3309), male stripper

Benjamin Cha
