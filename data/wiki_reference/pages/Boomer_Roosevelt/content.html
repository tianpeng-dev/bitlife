Boomer Roosevelt (2751-2828) was an American marine biologist.

Early life (2751-2775)[]

Roosevelt was born in San Francisco, California. He was the youngest of five children born to Venus Roosevelt XI and the younger of two born to her second boyfriend Angel Hole.

In 2769, Roosevelt enrolled at San Francisco State University.

In 2773, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Economics. He enrolled at San Francisco State University Graduate School.

In 2775, Roosevelt graduated with a Master’s Degree.

Career (2775-2817)[]

In 2775, Roosevelt became a junior marine biologist for the Hardwood Group.

In 2784, Roosevelt was promoted to marine biologist.

In 2787, Roosevelt was promoted to senior marine biologist.

In 2817, Roosevelt retired.

Death[]

In 2828, Roosevelt died of a stroke.

Relationship and children[]

From 2781 to 2784, Roosevelt dated a restaurateur named Monica Geller (2756-2835). They had three children, Venus XIII (2782-2786), Sydney (2783-2857), and Carlton Roosevelt XIX.
