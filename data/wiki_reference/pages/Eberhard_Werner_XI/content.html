Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XI (4751-after 4513) was a Japanese real estate agent.

Early life (4751-4789)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. He is the younger of two children born to Eita Werner and his ex-girlfriend Aratani Onoda.

In 4757, Werner enrolled at Toucan Elementary School.

In 4763, Werner enrolled at Matsuo Lower Secondary School.

In 4766, Werner enrolled at Marathon Preparatory School.

In 4769, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4789-4513)[]

In 4789, Werner became a real estate agent for Kurosaki and Harada Realty.

In 4513, Werner retired. His death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 4761 to 4767, Werner dated a student named Hina Harada (4751-after 4788).

From 4767 to 4768, Werner dated a student named Aia Taniguchi (4751-after 4513). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner VIII (4768-after 4513).

From 4769 to 4770, Werner and Taniguchi dated again. They had a son, Eberhard Werner XII.
