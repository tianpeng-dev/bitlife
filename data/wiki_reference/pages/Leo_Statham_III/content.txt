Early life (5335-5353)
[
]
Statham was born in Tampa, Florida. He was the younger of two children born to
Kate Statham V
and her ex-boyfriend Jimbo Huang.
In 5349, Statham enrolled at Tampa High School.
In 5352, Statham murdered his first two girlfriends, along with his two enemies, Claire Dickinson (5335-5352) and Jaime Chicote (5335-5352).
First police career (5353-5356)
[
]
In 5353, Statham became a trooper for the Tampa Police Department.
In 5356, Statham seduced his supervisor, Jude Evans (5306-after 5375). He emigrated to Naples, Italy and enrolled at the University of Naples Federico II. He played soccer for CUS Napoli.
Sports career (5360-5375)
[
]
In 5360, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Psychology. He joined Firenze as a defender.
In 5375, Statham was released by Firenze.
Second police career (5376-5395)
[
]
In 5376, Statham became a cadet for the Naples Police Department.
In 5379, Statham was promoted to patrolman.
In 5382, Statham was promoted to trooper.
In 5385, Statham was promoted to corporal.
In 5388, Statham was promoted to sergeant.
In 5391, Statham was promoted to inspector.
In 5394, Statham was promoted to lieutenant.
Statham was alive in 5395, but his death date is unknown.
Relationships and children
[

In 5349, Statham started dating a Dominican-American student named Bianca Silverio (5335-5352). They had a daughter, Kate Statham VII (5349-after 5395).
In 5352, Statham started dating an Indonesian-American  student named Hana Amuata (5335-5352).
From 5352 to 5353, Statham dated a Jordanian-American student named Mouna Hassan (5335-after 5375). They had a son, Leo Statham IV (5353-after 5395).
From 5356 to 5357, Statham dated a student named Greta Barsanti (5335-after 5395). They had a son,
