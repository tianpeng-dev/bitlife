Sengi Johnson (1997-2071) is a New Zealander convicted murderer.

Early life[]

Johnson’s parents were Aardvark Johnson and Eva Johnson (née Shepherd, 1980-2062).

Johnson got a degree in graphic design in 2019.

Murders[]

In 2024, Johnson murdered a hippie with a set of plastic vampire teeth. He tried and failed to bribe the police, and was sentenced to 30 years in prison. He was paroled in 2044.

In 2044, Johnson murdered a club promoter. He was sentenced to life in prison, even though life imprisonment is illegal in New Zealand. He appealed the conviction in 2061, but lost.

Death[]

Johnson died in prison in 2071, ending the Johnson Legacy after 91 years.

Relationships[]

Johnson dated a Qatari-born trucker named Reema Ahamed from 2024 to 2035. She later married a woman named Tama White and hyphenated her surname.

Johnson dated a trucker named Brooklyn Young (born 2000) from 2044 to 2052.

Pets[]

In 2024, Johnson bought a gorilla named Sydney (1971-2025) from an exotic pet dealer.

In 2044, Johnson bought a Siberian tiger named Lynn (2025-2045).
