Barbu Sandu-Mureșan (1528-1587), was the son of Alina Mureșan and Andreas Sandu.

Career[]

Barbu went bankrupt after his mother's death. In 1580 he was named a Logofethe (lobbyist) 1580-1587.

Personal Life[]

In 1581 Barbu married Ionela Ilie (1557-1637), a wealthy heiress who got him some money. The couple got 2 daughters:

Maria Sandu-Mureșan (1584-1684)

Caplea Sandu-Mureșan (1585-1668)

Barbu died in 1587.

Ionela remarried in 1610 to Radu Vasile (1560-?), a knight (army enlistee), but divorced him in 1624.
