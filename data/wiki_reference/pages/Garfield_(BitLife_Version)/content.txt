Garfield
(2007 - 2025) was an American tabby cat who lived in Cleveland, Ohio. He lived an idle life, much like his comic strip counterpart.
Life
[
]
Garfield was born in Cleveland as the result of his mother whispering "pspsps" to his father while she was in heat. His owner was Tia Lawrence (born 1983), a lobbyist, who had another pet tabby cat named Pixie.
