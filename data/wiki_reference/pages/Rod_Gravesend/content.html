Rod Gravesend (May 17th, 1952-April 6th, 2026) was a British financial advisor.

Early life and education[]

Rod Gravesend was born on May 17th, 1952 in Swansea, United Kingdom. He was conceived after his mother toured the fire station and tested every hose. It is unknown which hero was his father.

He attended St. Bella's Primary School from 1957 to 1963 and Zodiac Secondary School from 1963 to 1970, where he joined the Video Games club. He also attended university from 1970 to 1974.

Career[]

In 1974, Rod Gravesend was hired for the position of Financial Advisor for Swansea Credit Union. He worked there for 35 years before being ousted from the job in 2009. However, he was hired for the same job, this time for Gatwick Community Bank, the same year he was ousted from his previous job.
