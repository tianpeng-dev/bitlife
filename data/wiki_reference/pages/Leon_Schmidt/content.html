Leon Joel Schmidt (1911-1921) was born in Vienna, Austria as the only child, until his brother Ludwig Schmidt was born. He lived an amazing life, until at age 7 he got diagnosed with the Measles, he also was known for accidently killing the family cat, 7 months later from an overdose. At age 11, he died from complications of the measles, surrounded by his family, and friends. His funeral was attended by, his mother, father, and Ludwig, and his younger sister Angelina, and his younger brother Phillip.
