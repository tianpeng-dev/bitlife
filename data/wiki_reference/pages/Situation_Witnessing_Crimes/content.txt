A
Situation
is an event where your character sees
someone
committing a crime or doing something in public and your character can try to intervene, call the police, or do nothing. If your character calls the police, it will help them get closer to the
Model Bitizen
ribbon.
Sometimes if your character intervenes or is caught calling the police on the stranger, that stranger may argue or assault your character. If they argue, your character can argue back, ignore, or assault them. If the stranger chooses to assault your character, your character can call the police on that, fight back, or walk away. The police can be called again if the stranger assaults your character and the police will either arrest the stranger, complain at you for "wasting their time", or come too late and the stranger will flee. Your character can
sue
if assaulted regardless of result and will have a higher chance of winning if they did not fight back and did very little to provoke the assault. Your character could also face
prison
if found
guilty
of assault even if they were not the first to assault. Unlike the
SOS Situation,
your character does not have a risk of dying if they choose wrong (besides assault), but will not directly get the
Hero
ribbon for intervening. In the January 2020 crime update, your character can do delinquent things similar to what the strangers have done.
