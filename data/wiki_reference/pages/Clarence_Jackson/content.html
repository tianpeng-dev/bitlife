Clarence Jackson (2093-after 2154) was a British-American football player and restaurant worker.

Early life[]

Jackson was born in Bristol, England. His mother was Harriet Tzadua. He went to Dynamite Preparatory School and was on its football team from 2104 to 2111.

Football career[]

In 2111, Jackson became an association football player for Leicester.

In 2126, Jackson was released by Leicester and joined Derby.

In 2133, Jackson was released by Derby and joined Sunderland.

In 2134, Jackson was released by Sunderland.

In 2135, Jackson emigrated to Dallas, Texas with his second girlfriend and their son.

Restaurant career[]

In 2136, Jackson became a dishwasher for Casa Big Load. He became a general manager before disappearing.

Relationships and children[]

From 2104 to 2134, Jackson dated a student named Esme Maguire (2093-after 2135), who became a restaurant worker in 2111. They had two children, George (2111-2116) and Rebecca Jackson (b. 2118).

In 2134, Jackson started dating a university student named Gemma Burns (2113-after 2154), who later became a restaurant worker. They have a son, John Jackson.
