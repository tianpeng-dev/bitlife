Leo Statham (né Barker, 5234-after 5295) was a British politician and chef.

Early life (5234-5252)[]

Statham was born Leo Barker in Blackpool, England. In 5246, he was adopted by Jane Statham and her husband Rufus Woodgate.

Political career (5252-5277)[]

In 5252, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Lancashire County Council. He beat Helena Price and was inaugurated.

In 5257, Statham ran for reelection and beat Delmar Ortiz.

In 5262, Statham ran for a third term and beat Lexi Grimwood.

In 5267, Statham ran for a fourth term and beat Molly Brackley.

In 5272, Statham ran for a fifth term and beat Keira Winehouse.

In 5277, Statham did not run for a sixth term.

Cooking career (5278-5295)[]

In 5278, Statham became a line cook for Sara’s Restaurant.

In 5281, Statham was promoted to short order cook.

In 5284, Statham was promoted to apprentice chef.

In 5287, Statham was promoted to chef tournant.

In 5290, Statham was promoted to chef garde manger.

In 5293, Statham was promoted to junior sous chef.

Statham was alive in 5295, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 5251 to 5252, Statham dated a student named Emilia Burton (5234-after 5295). They had a daughter, Kate Statham III (5252-after 5295).

From 5257 to 5258, Statham and Burton dated again. They had a daughter, Jane Statham II.
