Andree Colbert (1908 - 1984) was a US Army soldier who mothered police officer and chief Tom Colbert.

Military career[]

Andree joined the US Army in the early to mid 1920s. She became a Sergeant First Class in 1938. Tom joined her in 1945 after he graduated from high school. Tom served until 1955 when the Vietnam War broke out.

She retired from the army in 1970.

Personal life[]

Andree married her high school boyfriend, Bill Colbert, around the time Tom and his twin brother, Tony, were born. Their third son, Cole, was born in 1936.

Death[]

Andree passed away in 1984, aged 76.
