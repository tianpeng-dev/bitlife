Early life (2543-2565)
[
]
Cross was born in Miami, Florida. He was the younger of two children born to
Julian Cross II
and his girlfriend Cynthia Chicote. He was named after his 7x great-granduncle.
Cross went to Florida International University and graduated in 2565 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
App development career (2565-2629)
[
]
In 2565, Cross became a junior app developer for BitLife.
In 2571, Cross was promoted to app developer.
In 2577, Cross was promoted to senior app developer.
In 2590, Cross was promoted to app designer.
In 2593, Cross became famous.
In 2629, Cross was forced to retire.
Acting career (2634-2641)
[
]
In 2634, Cross played an astronomer in the film
The Silly Sorcerer.
In 2635, Cross played a hairstylist in
That’s My Star.
In 2636, Cross played Rob Tibwell in
You Had Me at Hello.
He became famous.
From 2636 to 2637, Cross played Jayden Morrisette in the TV show
Sugar Babies.
From 2637 to 2641, Cross played Joshua Baldock in
In Front of My Salad?
Death
[
]
On October 5, 2641, Cross died of old age.
