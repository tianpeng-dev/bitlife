Red Black (February 3rd, 2016 - February, 3rd 2051) was a man in the United States known for killing 2 people in his life before executed from a lethal injection.

Early Life[]

Red Black was adopted in Las Vegas, United States to Ebony Miller and Sabrina Black on February 3rd, 2016. When Ebony Miller took him to get vaccinated he threw a tantrum.

He was enrolled at St.Valeria’s Elementary School.
