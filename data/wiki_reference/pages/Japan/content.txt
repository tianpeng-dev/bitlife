Japan
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the capital, Tokyo, along with Fukuoka, Hiroshima, Kobe, Kyoto, Nagoya, Osaka, and Yokohama.
Japan provides free healthcare, making characters able to consult doctors and treat
diseases and ailments
free of charge.
Japan has a royal family.
