Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner III (4498-January 4, 4602) was a German mailman and newspaper editor.

Early life (4498-4520)[]

Werner was born in Stuttgart, Germany. He was the younger of two children born to Eberhard Werner II and his ex-girlfriend Helma Friedrich.

In 4516, Werner enrolled at the University of Stuttgart.

Mailman career (4520-4563)[]

In 4520, Werner graduated from university with a Bachelor of Arts in Communications. He became a junior mail carrier for the German Federal Post.

In 4523, Werner was promoted to mail carrier.

In 4526, Werner was promoted to senior mail carrier.

In 4563, Werner retired.

Editing career (4582)[]

In 4582, Werner was briefly a junior editor for the Stuttgart Globe.

Death[]

On January 4, 4602, Werner committed suicide.

Relationship and children[]

From 4582 to 4584, Werner dated a newspaper editor named Carla Weber (4550-after 4604). They had two children, Greta Werner III (4583-after 4604) and Eberhard Werner IV.
