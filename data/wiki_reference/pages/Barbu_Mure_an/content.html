Barbu Mureșan (1453-1507) was the son of Ioana Popa and Dorian Mureșan.

Career[]

Barbu was a Logofethe (lobbyist) between 1501 until his death in 1507.

Personal Life[]

In 1476, Barbu married Carmen Barbu (1455-1527). They weren't able to get children, Carmen having some misscariages. In the end they got a son:

Petru Mureșan (1493-1501), who died young of sickle cell disease. 4 years later, Carmen gave birth to a daughter:

Alina Mureșan (1505-1576)

Barbu died in 1507.
