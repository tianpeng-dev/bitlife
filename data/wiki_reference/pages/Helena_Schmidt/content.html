Helena Schmidt (2551-after 2604) was a German politician and policy analyst.

Early life (2551-2573)[]

Schmidt was born in Berlin, Germany. Her parents are Evchen Altmann and her ex-boyfriend Fritz Schmidt. She graduated high school in 2569.

In 2570, Schmidt ran for election to the Berlin GEB, but lost to Marlene Wagner. She then enrolled at the Free University of Berlin. She graduated in 2573 with a BS in Political Science.

Political career (2573-2589)[]

In 2573, Schmidt ran again for election to the Berlin GEB. She beat Reinald Schmidt (unrelated) and was inaugurated.

In 2577, Schmidt ran for reelection and beat Lias Kohler.

In 2581, Schmidt ran for a third term and beat Marlon Ziegler.

In 2585, Schmidt ran for a fourth term and beat Josephina Haas.

In 2589, Schmidt did not run for a fifth term.

Policy analysis career (2590-2604)[]

In 2590, Schmidt became a policy analyst for the City of Berlin.

Schmidt was alive in 2604, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2569 to 2570, Schmidt dated a student named Ben Klum (2551-after 2604), who later became a massage therapist. They had a daughter named Evchen Klum.
