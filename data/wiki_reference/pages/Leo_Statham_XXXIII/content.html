Leo Statham XXXIII (6002-March 29, 6025) was a British-Japanese army officer.

Early life (6002-6024)[]

Statham was born in Birmingham, England. He was the youngest of eight children born to Leo Statham XXXII and his ex-girlfriend Carly Miller.

In 6020, Statham graduated from secondary school. He emigrated to Kobe, Japan. He enrolled at Kobe University.

Career (6024-6025)[]

In 6024, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. He joined the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force as a santō rikui (second lieutenant).

In 6025, Statham was promoted to nitō rikui (first lieutenant).

Death[]

In 6025, Statham took his fishing boat, which he named Argo, for a ride. He got into a shipwreck and called the Japan Coast Guard, but died before they could arrive.

Relationship and children[]

From 6020 to 6025, Statham started dating a student named Hibiki Sugimoto (6002-after 6054), who became a restaurateur in 6024. They had four children, Kate XXIV (6021-after 6054), Mei (6023-after 6054), Nami (6024-after 6054), and Leo Statham XXXIV.
