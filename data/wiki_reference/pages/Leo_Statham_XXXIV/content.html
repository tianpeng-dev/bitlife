Leo Statham XXXIV (6025-after 6074) is was Japanese-British wedding planner.

Early life (6025-6043)[]

Statham was born in Kobe, Japan. He was the youngest of four children born to Leo Statham XXXIII and his ex-girlfriend Hibiki Sugimoto.

In 6031, Statham enrolled at Little Kikuchi Elementary School.

In 6037, Statham enrolled at St. Hinata’s Lower Secondary School.

In 6040, Statham enrolled at St. Miyu’s Upper Secondary School.

In 6043, Statham graduated from upper secondary school. He emigrated to Birmingham, England.

Career (6055-6074)[]

In 6055, Statham became a junior wedding planner for Davis and Sons.

In 6058, Statham was promoted to wedding planner.

In 6061, Statham was promoted to senior wedding planner.

Statham outlived his son, but his death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

In 6035, Statham started dating a student named Nami Fukuda (6025-6040).

From 6040 to 6044, Statham dated a student named Mei Mazda (6025-after 6074). They had three children, Kate XXV (6042-after 6074), Yuna (6043-after 6074), and Leo Statham XXXV.
