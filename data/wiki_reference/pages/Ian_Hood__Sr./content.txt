Ian Hood, Sr.
(September 7, 1929-2011) was a British-American king, drug kingpin, and founder of the Hood Legacy.

Prince of Bastard and King of England (1930-1948)
[
]
Hood was born the Prince of Bastard in Manchester, England. He was the only child of King Clarence (1893-1948) and Queen Amber (1893-1978).
Hood went to Heirloom Secondary School and graduated in 1948.
In 1948, Hood assassinated his father and briefly became King of England before abdicating.
Drug dealing career (1948-2004)
[
]
In 1948, Hood joined the Crash Overrides as a dealer, and was promoted to runner in less than a year. His nickname was
Krayzee Steven
.
Between 1948 and 1950, Hood sold £842 ($50,000 in 2025) worth of glitter.
In 1951, Hood was promoted to lieutenant.
In 2004, Hood was chosen as the new kingpin. He dropped out of the cartel and emigrated to Phoenix, Arizona.
Death
[
]
In 2011, Hood died of old age.
