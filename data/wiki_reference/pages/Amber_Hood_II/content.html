Amber Hood II (2008-August 17, 2025) was an American student.

Life (2008-2025)[]

Hood was born in Phoenix, Arizona. She was the youngest of four children born to Ian Hood, Sr., and the youngest of three born to his second girlfriend Sara Wayne.

Hood went to St. Kawhi’s High School.

On August 17, 2025, Hood called her classmate, Ben, a dingleberry. In response, he assaulted her. She died of her injuries.
