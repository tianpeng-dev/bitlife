Australia
is an original
country
that was on BitLife since September 2018. Its default cities are the capital of Canberra, along with the cities of Adelaide, Brisbane, Canberra,  Gold Coast, Hobart, Melbourne, Newcastle, Perth, Sydney, Townsville,  and Wollongong.
As of July 2022, on both iOS and Android, Australia provides universal single-payer healthcare, making characters able to consult doctors and treat
diseases and ailments
free of charge.
