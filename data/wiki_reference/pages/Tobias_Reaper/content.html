Tobias Reaper (August 9, 1987 - April 7, 2022) was a Romanian hitman and mobster.

Early life and education[]

Tobias was born in Satu Mare to graphic designer Bianca Radu (1940 - 2020) and school administrator Zolton Reaper (1937 - 2019). He had a twin brother named Tudor, a restaurant worker who died in 2009 from a crack overdose. The family also owned a cat named Hallow (d. 2000).

...

Reaper attended Babeș-Bolyai University.

Military career[]

After graduating from UBB, Reaper joined the Romanian Land Forces in 2010, starting out as a sublocotenent. He was promoted to locotenent a year later.

...

Upon completing the minimum four years of service, Reaper successfully applied for his honorable discharge, shortly after being promoted to căpitan.
