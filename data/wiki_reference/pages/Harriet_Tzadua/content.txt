Harriet Tzadua (2076-after 2134) was a British chef.
Early life
[
]
Tzadua was born in Bristol, England. Her mother was
Carson Crunk
. She graduated from the University of Bristol in 2098 with a BA in Art History.
Career
[
]
In 2098, Tzadua became a line cook for Crimson Restaurant. She became an executive chef before mysteriously disappearing in 2134.
Relationship
[
]
In 2087, Tzadua started dating a student named Finley Jackson (2076-2094). They have a son,
Clarence Jackson
.
