Adam Fong (April 6, 1976 - April 25, 2022) was a Malaysian politician and former royal.

Early life and reign[]

Adam was born Prince of Sughalia with a fortune of over $4.2 million in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. His parents were Queen Natasha Wang (born 1952) and Prince Consort Ishtiqal Fong (1932 - 2009). The family owned a pet kitty named Jazz (d. 1988).

Fong was described as an enthusiastic and productive child. An athletic protege in his youth, he joined the basketball teams of his all-boys lower and upper secondary schools.

Abdication and political career[]

In 1994, upon turning 18 years old and completing upper secondary school, Fong, who had grown bored of his life of royalty, abdicated his title and surrendered Wang Palace. His parents showed him overwhelming love and supported his decision immensely, wishing him all the best in his future endeavors. Soon afterwards, Fong embarked on his political career, running for School Board Director.
