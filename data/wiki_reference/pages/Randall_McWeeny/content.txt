Randall McWeeny
(né
Clark
, 3480-3508) was an American magician.
Early life (3480-3503)
[
]
McWeeny was born
Randall Clark
in Los Angeles, California.
In 3483, McWeeny was adopted by
Victoria McWeeny
and her boyfriend Jeff Sack, making him their only child. His last name was changed.
In 3485, McWeeny enrolled at Little Blanchard Elementary School.
In 3491, McWeeny enrolled at Mariah Hunter Middle School.
In 3494, McWeeny enrolled at Los Angeles High School. He murdered his parents and was sent to the Coastal Home.
In 3498, McWeeny graduated from high school.
In 3499, McWeeny enrolled at the University of California, Los Angeles. He majored in finance.
In 3503, McWeeny graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Finance.
Career (3503-3508)
[
]
In 3503, McWeeny became a magician for Eclipse World Circus.
In 3508, McWeeny was murdered by his daughter.
Relationship and child
[
]
