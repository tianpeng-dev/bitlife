Kylian Morin
(June 25, 1917 - May 24, 1944) was a French Navy sailor and politician who, prior to joining the military, served as the school board director of his hometown in Nice. He was killed in action during World War II.

Early life
[
]
Kylian was born in Nice to a restaurant worker named Sarah Morin (1890 - 1980). His unknown father was believed to be a French Navy sailor who was killed in action during World War I. A year after Kylian's birth, Sarah married
Napoleon Antionette
(1890 - 1966), a soldier in the French Foreign Legion who served in the war, allegedly alongside Kylian's biological father. He had a half-sister, Julia (1927 - 2020). Sarah and Napoleon divorced a year after Julia's birth. Kylian also grew up with a loving pet cat named Mavis (d. 1930).
Political career
[
]
Morin graduated from secondary school in 1935. That same summer, he announced that he was running for the position of school board director for Nice's public school system. Using part of his fortune, he budgeted ₤100,000 towards his campaign against his American-born opponent, Mary Hatch Bailey (1873 - 1954), whom he treated with indifference. He won the election.

Personal life
[
]
One day in 1937, after completing his daily duties as school board director, Morin engaged in a threesome with a childhood friend and eventual fellow sailor Louis Muller (1917 - 1990) and pornographic actress Francesca Vera (1913 - 1993). He supposedly forgot about the interaction for a few years until he suddenly remembered one day in 1940. At that point, he got back in touch with Vera, and the two began a romantic relationship that would last until Morin's death.
