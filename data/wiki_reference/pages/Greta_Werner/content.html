Greta Werner, Sr. (4460-4549) was a German magician.

Early life (4460-4478)[]

Werner was born in Stuttgart, Germany. She was the only child of Eberhard Werner and his girlfriend Greta Ziegler.

In 4466, Werner enrolled at Hartmann Primary School.

In 4470, Werner enrolled at Johanna Voigt Middle School.

In 4475, Werner enrolled at Werner Secondary School.

Magic career (4478-4525)[]

In 4478, Werner became a magician for Circus Spectrum.

In 4525, Werner retired.

Death[]

In 4549, Werner died of old age.

Relationship and children[]

In 4470, Werner started dating a student named Fiete Martin (4460-4538). They had four children, Jasmin Werner II (4477-4557), Greta Werner Jr. (4478-4558), Anna Martin (4479-4501), and Eberhard Werner II.
