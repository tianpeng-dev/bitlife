In this Korean name, the surname is Hamilton.

Hamilton Tilda IV BA (2492-2585) was a South Korean anime singer.

Early life (2492-2515)[]

Hamilton was born in Gangnam, Seoul. She is the only child of Theo Hamilton IV and his girlfriend Mun Uh Se.

In 2511, Hamilton enrolled at Seoul National University. She majored in communications.

In 2515, Hamilton graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication.

Career (2520-2568)[]

In 2520, Hamilton signed with Celestial Records as a K-pop singer. She released her debut anime album, Shhhh, which went gold in less than a year and platinum in 2555.

In 2521, Hamilton released her second album, The Naptime Chapter, which went gold in 2522.

In 2522, Hamilton released her third album, Inside Castle, which went gold in 2523.

In 2525, Hamilton released her fourth album, PerSOULna.

In 2530, Hamilton released her fifth album, PlaSTIK Dreams, which went gold in 2532.

In 2533, Hamilton released her sixth album, Matter in Black, which went platinum in less than a year and double platinum in 2535.

In 2538, Hamilton released her seventh album, We Bang, which went gold in 2540.

In 2545, Hamilton released her eighth album, Go Back Home, which went gold in less than a year.

In 2551, Hamilton released her ninth album, Reality in Green, which went platinum in less than a year and double platinum in 2553.

In 2556, Hamilton released her 10th album, BIT WORLD.

In 2561, Hamilton released her 11th album, DNC: GLXY, which went gold in 2567.

In 2566, Hamilton released her 12th album, Uni:VERSE, which went gold in 2567.

In 2568, Hamilton retired.

Death[]

In 2585, Hamilton died of old age.
