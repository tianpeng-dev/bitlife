1998-2011: Early childhood
[

Eno was born in Redmond, Washington, the headquarters of Microsoft and Nintendo of America. His mother, Tiara (born 1964) is a jeweler. He never met his father, who left him at birth, but was still looked after by his mother and his stepfather, Marcus Gasser (born 1961). He also had a stepbrother named Julius (born 1998), an accountant.

2016-2020: College and continued success

As he was graduating from high school, Xobx enrolled in DigiPen Institute of Technology, Class of 2020. He received his acceptance letter the day after graduation, and he celebrated with a party vlog.
2020-present: Technology professionalism
[
]
After graduating from DigiPen, Xobx found a job as an IT Support for Sedona Electronics.
Personal life
[
]
In his freshman year of college in 2016, Xobx met his future wife, Tiana Gay (born 1998), who currently works as a dealer for the Anytime Redmond Casino. After five years of dating, Gay proposed to him in 2021, to which Xobx accepted, and they got married later that year. They have two children together, including a son named Jermaine born in 2023, and a daughter named Aaliyah (named after singer Aaliyah Haughton) born in 2024.
