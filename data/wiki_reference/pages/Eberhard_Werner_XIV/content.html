Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XIV (b. 4795) is a Japanese real estate agent.

Early life (4795-4533)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. He is the younger of two children born to Eberhard Werner XII and his ex-girlfriend Chiasa Nakanishi.

In 4513, Werner graduated upper secondary school.

Career (4533-4551)[]

In 4533, Werner became a real estate agent for Motoko Gota Realty.

Werner was alive in 4551, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4513 to 4514, Werner dated a firefighter named Tsunade Nomura (4795-after 4551). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XV.
