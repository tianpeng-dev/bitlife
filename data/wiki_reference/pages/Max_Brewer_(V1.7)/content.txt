Maxwell Thomas Brewer
(September 7, 1962 - August 12, 1991), also known by the stage name
Brewster Max
, was an American disk jockey. Originally wanting to be a professional singer, he became a DJ after being mercilessly rejected by labels. In 1988, he decided to take a break from his career to join the Marines; he wanted to become a war hero to lift his chances of becoming a singer. He died in combat in August 1991.
