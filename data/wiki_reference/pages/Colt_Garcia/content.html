Colt Garcia (2021 - 2116) was an American professional baseball player and graphic designer. He was the youngest son of singer Donald Garcia.

Early life and education[]

Colt was born in Hawaii to Donald Garcia (1908 - 2022) and his second wife, Kierra Santos (1971 - 2072), a United States Air Force fighter pilot.

Personal life[]

Garcia met his first wife, Anita Roberts, in 2040 during his rookie season. She was a fan who attended games to check out Colt's butt. After dating for five years, Roberts proposed to him in 2045. Garcia accepted, and they got married that same year. They had a good marriage for several years, but Garcia eventually began to fall out of love with Roberts. One day in 2050, after finishing his 11th season, he had a one-night stand with Oprah Boozer, who got pregnant as a result. A year later, Oprah lost the baby to a second-trimester miscarriage. That same year, Garcia and Roberts divorced, and he married Boozer. Colt and Oprah went on to have four children and five grandchildren together:
