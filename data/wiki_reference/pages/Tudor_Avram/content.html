Tudor Avram (September 4, 1957 - October 31, 2024) was a Romanian dentist, actor, musician, and alleged vampire from Transylvania. A theatrical and organ protege from a young age, Avram grew up with a fascination for vampires, and aspired to become one himself. On the side, he moonlighted as a serial killer who sucked the blood of his victims, targeting people he saw as evil for numerous reasons.

Childhood and early adulthood[]

Tudor was born in Transylvania to real estate agent Ramona Stefan (1932 - 2019) and cameraman Costin Avram (1934 - 2004), both of whom got married in 1960 when Tudor turned three years old. He had three sisters, including Denisa (1956 - 2018), Andreea (born 1962), and Georgiana (born 1968). Denisa was a restaurateur at Mahogany Restaurant, Andreea is an insurance agent for Nourish Insurance Group, and Georgiana is a trucker for Titanium Trucking & Services.

Drug cartel stint[]

In the early 1980s, Avram briefly worked as a drug dealer. This detail of his life is arguably the most overlooked. He was a member of the Căpcăuni țipând cartel (Screaming Ogres), having joined in 1980 within the first few days after graduating from university.

Acting career[]

At the end of his tenure as a dentist, Avram became a vampire actor for the Transylvania Community Church.

Personal life[]

When he was twelve, Avram met a classmate named Lavinia Cristea. She would grow up to become a restaurateur for Mahogany Restaurant. The pair started dating and would maintain a loving relationship throughout their school years. Cristea would often speak fondly of Avram's vampire fantasies. After graduation from university in 1980, Tudor proposed to Lavinia in a hot tub. She accepted, and they got married that summer at the courthouse. They travelled to America for their honeymoon, enjoying the beaches of Honolulu, Hawaii. Avram's only complaint of the spot was the exposure to sunlight, which seemed to bother him a little. Killing spree[]

Avram and Cristea's relationship has often been compared to popular Halloween and horror-themed couple characters, such as Gomez and Morticia Addams.

Death[]

Avram's health problems continued as he grew older. He was suffering from high blood pressure and was unable to have it cured with standard treatment. Thus, he turned to the witch doctor, who offered him the blood of a lion. As a vampire, he happily accepted the offering on several different occasions. However, his health detriments would prove not to be his ultimate downfall; it was on Halloween 2024 when he would supposedly pick a new victim. Avram approached said victim, and was supposedly killed then and there. Other accounts say it was one of his lion blood drinkings that caused his death, but conflicting reports make it hard to discern.

Tudor is survived by his wife, twelve children, four grandchildren, and two surviving sisters, all of whom attended his services. He had a net worth of 8,236,370.95 Roman Leu ($1,724,064 USD). His death is under investigation as of December 2024.
