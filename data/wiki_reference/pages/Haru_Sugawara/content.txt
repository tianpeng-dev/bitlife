Biography
[
]
Early life
[
]
Haru was born on January 16, 2023, in Kyoto, Japan, his father "Izuku Sugawara", the Prince Consort of Japan, born in 1994, his mother "Haruka Sugawara", the Empress of Japan, born in 1996, and his older brother "Yamato Sugawara", born in 2018.
At the age of 6, Haru enrolled in elementary school at Easy Elementary School. At the age of 15, Haru selected for basketball team in upper secondary school. Three years later, he graduated from upper secondary school and applied to university. Then later, he passed driving test, and Haru's parents bought Haru's car named "Porsche GT2-RS".
Personal life
[
]
Haru before emperor
At the age of 23, a ghost named "Prince Toma" appeared and Haru called the police, then 4 years later, a ghost appeared again. In 2053, he married a 23 years old girl named "Rinko". Then 7 years later, his daughter named "Nana" was born.
In 2074, Haru's father passed away at the age of 80 due to heart disease. Then 7 years later, Haru's mother passed away at the age of 85 due to natural causes and his brother "Yamato" became emperor of Japan. In 2090, Rinko and Haru bought a new car named "Mercedes-Benz AMG GT".
Reign & Death
[
]
In 2106, Haru's wife, Rinko and his older brother, Yamato, died, then Haru became Emperor of Japan. Then 17 years later, Emperor Haru died in his sleep.
