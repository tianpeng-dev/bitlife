Derek Charles Howsen (April 1, 1925 - May 14, 2014) was a United States Marine Corps veteran, lawyer, judge, and college football player. As a Marine, he is best remembered for his deployment to the Phillipines in 1974, where he successfully disarmed several landmines, earning him a Medal of Honor. After retiring from the Marines after three decades of service at the rank of Sergeant Major, Howsen started his new career as a lawyer in the 1980s, and it lasted for another three decades.
