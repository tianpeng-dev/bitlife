Early life and education[]

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (2037 - 2111) and Cooper Piner (2038 - 2123). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist. She had an older brother, Steven (2059 - 2135), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 2085).
