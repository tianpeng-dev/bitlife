Every animal and every human in
DogLife
has a scent. The player can discover someone's scent by sniffing them: doing this will add the animal to the Scent Database (currently only pets can be added to it, not humans).
The Scent Database can show the player the animal's current status (if they are alive, dead or missing) and if they paid to unlock TopDog you can access the ability to locate a missing animal.
