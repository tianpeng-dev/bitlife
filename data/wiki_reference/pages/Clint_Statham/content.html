Clint Statham (5357-after 5417) was an Italian politician and casino worker.

Early life (5357-5376)[]

Statham was born in Naples, Italy. He was the youngest of three children born to Leo Statham III and the only born to his fourth girlfriend Greta Barsanti.

Political career (5376-5396)[]

In 5376, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Naples Provincial School Administration and beat Serena Columbus.

In 5381, Statham ran for reelection and beat Ottavio Cafaro.

In 5386, Statham ran for a third term and beat Aurora Scalice.

In 5391, Statham ran for Metropolitan Mayor of Naples as a Democrat and beat Brothers of Italy candidate Samuele Andretti.

Casino career (5396-5417)[]

In 5396, Statham did not run for reelection. He became a dealer for Swift Resort and Gaming.

In 5402, Statham was promoted to floor supervisor.

In 5408, Statham was promoted to pit boss.

In 5414, Statham was promoted to assistant casino manager.

Statham was alive in 5417, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5376 to 5377, Statham dated an Army enlistee named Paola Alighieri (5353-5400). They had a son, Leo Statham V.
