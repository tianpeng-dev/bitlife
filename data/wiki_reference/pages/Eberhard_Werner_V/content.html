Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner V (4604-January 4, 4631) was an Italian soccer player.

Early life (4604-4623)[]

Werner was born in Turin, Italy. He was the younger of two children born to Eberhard Werner IV and his girlfriend Julia van Nistelrooy.

In 4610, Werner enrolled at Little Borsellino Primary School.

In 4615, Werner enrolled at Verdi Middle School. He was on the soccer team.

In 4618, Werner enrolled at Canali Secondary School. He was the captain of the soccer team.

Career (4623-4631)[]

In 4623, Werner graduated secondary school. He joined Catania as a forward.

On January 4, 4631, Werner hit on a fan named Enzo Bocchi after a game. In response, Bocchi attacked Werner with a grenade. Werner died of his injuries.

Relationship and children[]

From 4614 to 4621, Werner dated a student named Angela Marconi (4604-after 4653). They had two children, Greta Werner IV (4620-after 4653) and Eberhard Werner VI.
