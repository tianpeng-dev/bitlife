Leo Statham XXVI (5863-after 5909) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5863-5888)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the younger of two children born to Leo Statham XXV and his ex-girlfriend Amelia Thomas.

In 5881, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5885, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5888-5903)[]

In 5888, Statham graduated with a Doctorate of Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Shannon Dewey.

In 5893, Statham ran for reelection and beat Danielle Kelly.

In 5898, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Green Party candidate Mick Driscoll.

In 5903, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5919, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5881 to 5882, Statham dated a student named Kathleen Taylor (5863-after 5919). They had a son, Leo Statham XXVII.
