In this Korean name, the surname is Na.

Na Pin (né Song, 2566-2648) was a South Korean trucker.

Early life (2566-2584)[]

Na was born Song Pin in Gangnam, Seoul.

In 2568, Na was adopted by Na Kang Mi and her husband Kim Ji-hu, becoming their only child.

In 2584, Na graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (2585-2632)[]

In 2632, Na became an apprentice trucker for Kama’aina Trucking.

In 2634, Na was promoted to trucker.

In 2632, Na retired.

Death[]

In 2648, Na died of a stroke.
