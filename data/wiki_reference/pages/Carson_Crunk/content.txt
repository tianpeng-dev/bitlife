Carson Crunk (2059-after 2093) was a British lobbyist.
Early life
[
]
Crunk was born in Bristol, England. Her father was
Walt Crunk
. She graduated from the University of Bristol in 2082 with a BA in Marketing.
Lobbying career
[
]
In 2082, Crunk became a lobbyist for Binary Properties.
In 2093, Crunk disappeared. Her date of death is unknown.
Relationship
[
]
From 2070 to 2075, Crunk dated a student named Christian Woodcock (2059-after 2076).
In 2075, Crunk started dating an Ethiopian-British student named Gedeyon Tzadua (2059-after 2093), who later became an insurance agent. They have a daughter,
Harriet Tzadua
.
