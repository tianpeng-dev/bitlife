Chester McClure (July 7th, 1930 - May 30th, 2026) was an American accountant.

Early life and education[]

Chester McClure was born on July 7th, 1930 in Newark, United States. He was conceived on a camping trip.

He attended DeShawn's Elementary School from 1935 to 1941, Biff Valdez Middle School from 1941 to 1944 and Fuentes High School from 1944 to 1948. He also attended university from 1948 to 1952.

Career[]

In 1952, McClure was hired for the position of Accountant for Equinox Global.
