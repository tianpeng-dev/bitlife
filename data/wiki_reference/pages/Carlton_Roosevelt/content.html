Carlton Roosevelt, Sr. (2092-after 2147) was an American-Israeli trucker.

Early life (2092-2110)[]

Roosevelt was born in Los Angeles, California. He is the only child of Salvador Roosevelt and Tiana Fingerwell.

In 2097, Salvador Roosevelt married Grace Cluckerberg, making Carlton the younger of her two children. Carlton Roosevelt enrolled at Hole Elementary School.

In 2103, Roosevelt enrolled at Creed Middle School.

In 2106, Roosevelt enrolled at Vivacious Preparatory School.

In 2110, Roosevelt graduated from high school and emigrated to Tel Aviv, Israel.

Career (2112-2147)[]

In 2112, Roosevelt became an apprentice trucker for Sunbeam Transportation.

In 2114, Roosevelt was promoted to trucker.

Roosevelt was alive in 2147, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 2102 to 2111, Roosevelt dated a student named Cheyenne Bonanno (2092-after 2147), who became a restaurateur in 2111. They had three children, Venus (2109-after 2147), Carlton, Jr. (2110-after 2147), and Kevin Roosevelt.
