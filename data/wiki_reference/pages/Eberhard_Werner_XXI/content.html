Eberhard Werner XXI (4647-after 4703) was a Japanese travel agent.

Early life (4647-4685)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. He is the only child of Eberhard Werner XX and his ex-girlfriend Saki Masuda.

In 4665, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4685-4703)[]

In 4685, Werner became a travel associate for Tokyo Travel Service.

In 4689, Werner was promoted to tour operator.

In 4693, Werner was promoted to general manager.

In 4697, Werner was promoted to regional manager.

In 4701, Werner was promoted to agency operator.

Werner was alive in 4703, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4665 to 4666, Werner dated a mailwoman named Rin Taniguchi (4641-after 4703). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner X.
