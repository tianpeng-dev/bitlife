Finn Bexley is a former Australian Rugby player born in Wollongong, Australia. Finn was known as one of the greatest players of his time winning Player of the Year four times in a row. Finn and the teams he played on would also win 9 Championship titles throughout his career. Finn was raised by a religious single mother with a half-brother and two half-sisters. Finn would first start his Rugby journey playing on the Rugby team in Secondary School. Finn would eventually be chosen as team captain by his teammates at the age of 16. Finn would also participate in the Wrestling team and become captain of the Wrestling team at the age of 17.

Rugby Career[]

Finn would play for four different Rugby teams throughout his career. Finn would play for the Penrith Panthers, Melbourne Squall, St. George Illawarra Wyverns and the South Sydney Cottontails. Finn would play the position of Winger for all of these teams. At the start of Finn's career he was criticized by his coach of the Penrith Panthers for causing the team to lose but Finn wouldn't let this bring him down. Finn would continue to practice extremely hard even if it would cause him to be injured sometimes. After Finn's 3rd season he would be traded to the Melbourne Squall team. Eventually Finn and the Melbourne Squall would win a Championship title in Finn's 5th season playing. Finn and the Melbourne Squall wouldn't win another Championship title until Finn's 9th season. After Finn's 10th season he would be traded to the St. George Illawarra Wyverns where Finn and the team would win four consecutive Championship titles. This point in time is considered by many as Finn's peak in his Rugby career. Finn and the St. George Illawarra Wyverns would also win two more Championship titles before Finn would be traded to the South Sydney Cottontails. When Finn started playing for the Cottontails many noticed that he wasn't playing as well due to his older age. Finn didn't let this slow him down as he would continue to practice as hard as before and still do very well in Rugby. On Finn's final season of his Rugby career the South Sydney Cottontails would win a Championship title marking a strong ending to a long and great career.

Personal Life and Family[]

Finn would marry a woman named Bethany at the age of 22. Bethany would change her last name to Bexley after marriage. Finn and Bethany have 3 children named Charlotte, Xavier and Steve. Finn would go on many vacations with his wife Bethany and his kids. Sadly, Bethany would die at the age of 35 after eating pufferfish that wasn't properly prepared. Finn and his children were devastated by this sudden loss and a funeral and burial was paid for by Finn. Finn would continue to be a great father to his children by spending time with them constantly and taking them on many vacations around the world.

After Rugby[]

After Finn's rugby career he would open a casino named Bexley's Big Wins. This casino would become very popular but also unprofitable which would prove to be a large problem for the casino. The casino would be forced to shut down after being bankrupt for 5 years. After the failure of the casino Finn would get a job as a mall Santa and worked that job for many years until his death.

Death[]

At the age of 93 Finn would die after being bitten by a presumably poisonous mosquito. Finn's health was in very bad condition after a recent near fatal car crash which played a factor in the mosquito bite being fatal. Finn's three children and four grandchildren would attend his funeral and his book "My Life: Finn Bexley" was handed out as his funeral service.
