Garfield is an American sports biographical drama film about the life and career of Philadelphia Kites pitcher Perry Garfield.

Plot[]

In 2019, fifty year old Perry Garfield, a former professional baseball player, gets hired as a psychologist for his public school system in Compton. Most of the children he cares for are either mentally ill or being bullied, sometimes both. As he watches some of them playing sports, he reflects on his own life on the mound.

In 1983, Garfield, a freshman, starts playing baseball at his local high school.
