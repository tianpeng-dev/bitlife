Maciej Piotrowski
(1954-TBA) was a History Teacher at Kief Junior High School (2009-2019), who was also one of the teachers at Junior High School no.25 Adam Mickiewicz in Poznań since 1978.
Early Life
[
]
Piotrowski was born in 1954 in Poznań in the Polish People's Republic (Poland).  He comes from a family.  His Father: Adam Piotrowski (1929-2002) was a middle-class worker, and his Mother: Anna Piotrowski (1934-2015) was a stay-at-home wife who took good care of him.
Career as Teacher
[
]
Piotrowski started working as a teacher at Junior High School no.25 Adam Mickiewicz in Poznań from 1978. His first day of job in 1979, when he was a Homeroom teacher of Class 3G, his teaching method was different than other teachers, about 73% of students passed the Junior High School Examination as a result of the above  60% and making a souvenir with his favorite students.  He sometimes had mishaps, including the school principal in 1989 when, without the knowledge of his colleague (IT teacher Mr. Bolesław Marucki), he accidentally destroyed one of the Commodore 64 computers while writing grades.
