Early life (3312-3337)
[
]
Francois was born in Lyon, France. She was the youngest of three children born to
OG Rippa
and the younger of two born to his second girlfriend Julia Denis. She was named after Joan of Arc, one of the patron saints of France.
Francois went to Sciences Po Lyon and graduated with a Licence in Political Science in 3334 and an LLM in 3337.
Career (3337-3365)
[
]
In 3337, Francois became a porn cameraman for Atmosphere Pictures.
In 3340, Francois was promoted to porn actor.
In 3365, Francois was forced to retire.
Death
[
]
In 3398, Francois died from a stroke. Her ashes were scattered at her favorite camping spot.
