Fransisco "Franco" Naranja (Spanish for Franklin "Frank" Orange; April 14, 1940 - April 23, 2024) was a Spanish-American basketball player.

Primeros años[]

Fransico Naranja was born in Palma, Spain to a single mother and college student named Olivia (1920 - 1998), who would later become an assistant principal.

Después de la NBA[]

Following his retirement from the NBA, Orange found work as a primary school teacher and a university professor.

Vida personal[]

Between his fourth and fifth seasons, Orange met receptionist Pheobe Marshall (born 1942) in 1966. It proved to be a story of true love, as they got married that same year. They had three sons, including Ignacio (born 1968), and twins Mario and Manuel (born 1970). Ignacio is a translator for Serenade Financial. Mario is a private investigator for Richards Design, while Manuel is a clerk.

Muerte[]

From complications of multiple diseases, Orange died on April 23, 2024 at the age of 84, just nine days after his birthday. He is survived by his wife, three sons, half-sister, and four granddaughters.
