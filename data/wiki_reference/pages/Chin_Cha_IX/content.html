Chin Cha IX (3134-after 3214) was an American-Italian soccer player and real estate agent.

Early life (3134-3152)[]

Cha was born in Houston, Texas in 3134. He was the only child of Chin Cha VIII and his girlfriend Nicole Foreman.

In 3139, Cha enrolled at Adams Elementary School.

In 3145, Cha enrolled at St. Gretchen’s Middle School and joined the football team.

In 3148, Cha enrolled at Kristen Grande High School and joined the soccer team.

In 3151, Cha became captain of the Kristen Grande High School soccer team.

In 3152, Cha graduated from high school.

Sports career (3152-3175)[]

In 3152, Cha emigrated to Cagliari, Italy. He joined Cagliari as a midfielder.

In 3162, Cha passed a doping test.

In 3169, Cha was released by Cagliari and joined Verona.

In 3175, Cha was released by Verona.

Realty career (3176-3200)[]

In 3176, Cha became a real estate agent for Eneide and Saraceno Realtors.

In 3200, Cha retired.

Later life[]

Cha was alive in 3214, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

In 3144, Cha started dating a student named Rita Brown (3144-after 3214), who became an insurance agent in 3157. They had four children:

  1. Chin Cha X (3151-after 3214), policy analyst
  2. Ha-Eun Cha V (3166-after 3214), hairdresser
  3. Scarlett Cha IV (3167-after 3214), librarian
  4. Andrew Cha II
