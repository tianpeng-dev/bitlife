The United Kingdom
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the capital, London, along with Aberdeen, Belfast, Birmingham, Blackpool, Brighton, Bristol, Cambridge, Cardiff, Edinburgh, Glasgow, Hull, Ipswich, Leeds, Leicester, Liverpool, Manchester, Milton Keynes, Newcastle, Norwich, Nottingham, Oxford, Sheffield, Southhampton, Staines, Sunderland, Swansea, and Wolverhampton. This adds to a total of 22 English cities, 3 Scottish cities, 2 Welsh cities, and 1 Northern Irish city for a total of 28 cities.

On iOS, the United Kingdom provides free healthcare, making characters able to consult doctors and treat
diseases and ailments
free of charge. However university is not free and characters will still have a tuition fee.
