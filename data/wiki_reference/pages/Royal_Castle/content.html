What Is A Royal Castle?[]

A Royal Castle is a Real Estate that can be obtained if the Player is born as a Royal. The Royal Castle's Size is 30br/32ba to 39br/40ba.

Appearance[]

The Royal Castle has a large entrance With 2 towers, and 1 Building where the Royal Family lives. It has Large stone/brick walls with windows to see the view or firing arrows. It has a blue hue, alongside 4 blue flags in the tower.

The Haunted One has a large moon on its background and bats surrounding it. It is very rusty.

Obtainment[]

It Can be Obtained when the player is born as A Royal.

Trivia[]

  • The Royal Castle is one of the buildings that can only be obtained trough Royalty.
  • It cannot be sold, added amenities, or rented.
  • But Players can still throw a party at the castle.
  • It also has the same activities and options as the Royal Palace.
