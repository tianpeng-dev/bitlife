Thomas Washton (1941 - 2025) was an American author and lobbyist. He was the father of serial killer Doug Washton.

Early life[]

Tom was born in Colorado in 1941. His father was a young soldier in the military who watched his son's birth before going to serve in World War II. After the war, his father worked numerous odd jobs to help support the family, as well as his mother.
