Puerto Rico
is a
country
that was on BitLife in June 2019. Its default cities are the capital, San Juan, along with Ponce.
On June 9, 2019 on
iOS
; new countries were added. Along with Puerto Rico; there was also
Afghanistan
,
Belgium
,
Djibouti
,
Dominican Republic
,
Iraq

Poland
,
Switzerland

Taiwan
,
Thailand
,
United Arab Emirates
, and
Uruguay
. It was added to Android September 21, 2019. Although Puerto Rico is a territory of the United States, it is treated as a country. And unlike its real life counterpart where emigrating from Puerto Rico to the United States is straightforward, the BitLife version may accept emigrants at a fee (or may not accept if they either have a criminal record or simply refuse entry).
