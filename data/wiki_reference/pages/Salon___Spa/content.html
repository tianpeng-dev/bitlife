September 2018 (default)

The Saln & Spa is an activity that can increase looks, except for the massages, which can increase health. A player that has a character 18 years or older can do any of the procedures, while the Hair Salon and Nail Salon are the only two services that juvenile characters can go to. Juvenile characters will have to have their own money to pay for hair and nail salons, as parents only pay for seeing the doctor.
