Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XII (4770-after 4532) was a Japanese hairdresser.

Early life (4770-4514)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo, Japan. He is the younger of two children born to Eberhard Werner XI and his second girlfriend Aia Taniguchi.

In 4788, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4514-4532)[]

In 4514, Werner became an apprentice hairdresser for Celestial Salon.

In 4516, Werner was promoted to hairdresser.

In 4532, Werner retired. His death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4788 to 4790, Werner dated a hairdresser named Chiasa Nakanishi (4769-after 4532). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XIII (4790-4794).

From 4794 to 4795, Werner and Nakanishi dated again. They had a son, Eberhard Werner XIV.
