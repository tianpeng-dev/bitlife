Turkey
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the capital, Ankara, along with Istanbul and Izmir.
There is a Turkey-based
achievement
called
"Midnight Express"
if a character residing in Turkey gets sent to prison. It is based from the 1978 film
Midnight Express
.
