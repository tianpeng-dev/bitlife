Annabelle Longstaff (3051-after 3108) was a British soccer player and environmental scientist.

Early life (3051-3073)[]

Longstaff was born in Cardiff, Wales. Her parents were Rory Longstaff and his ex-girlfriend Ella Kane (née Batty-Murphy).

In 3069, Longstaff enrolled at Cardiff University. She was on the soccer team. She graduated in 3073 with a BS in Biology.

Sports career (3073-3088)[]

In 3073, Longstaff joined Bristol as a defender.

In 3088, Longstaff retired.

Environmental science career (3089-3108)[]

In 3089, Longstaff became a Jr. Environmental Scientist for Interstellar Engineering.

In 3095, Longstaff was promoted to Environmental Scientist.

Longstaff was alive in 3108, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 3069 to 3070, Longstaff dated a student named Billy Crook (3051-after 3108). They had a son, Jenson Crook.
