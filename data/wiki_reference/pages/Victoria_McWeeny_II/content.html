Victoria McWeeny II (3494-June 11, 3508) was an American student.

Life (3494-3508)[]

McWeeny was born in Los Angeles, California. She was the only child of Randall McWeeny and his girlfriend Desiree O’Connor.

In 3499, McWeeny enrolled at Little Blanchard Elementary School.

In 3505, McWeeny enrolled at Essence Middle School.

In 3508, McWeeny murdered her father with a set of plastic vampire teeth. On June 11, she tried to kill her mother in the same way, but O’Connor sucked McWeeny’s blood instead, ending the Harris Legacy after 1,598 years.

Relationship[]

In 3504, McWeeny started dating a Congolese-born student named Kabala Luba (3494-after June 11, 3508).
