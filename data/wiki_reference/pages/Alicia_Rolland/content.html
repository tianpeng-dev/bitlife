Alicia Rolland (2978-after 3049) was a French-British soccer player and biotechnologist.

Early life (2978-3000)[]

Rolland was born in Lyon, France. Her parents were Victor Rolland and his ex-girlfriend Avril Schmitt.

In 2996, Rolland enrolled at Claude Bernard University. She was on the soccer team. She graduated in 3000 with a Licentiate in Chemistry.

Sports career (3000-3015)[]

In 3000, Rolland emigrated to Cardiff, Wales and joined Blues FC as a midfielder.

In 3002, Rolland was released by Blues and joined Gunners FC.

In 3004, Rolland was traded to Bristol. She has won two championships while on the team.

In 3015, Rolland retired.

Biotechnology career (3016-3034)[]

In 3016, Rolland became a junior biotechnologist at Interstellar Engineering.

In 3022, Rolland was promoted to biotechnologist.

In 3028, Rolland was promoted to senior biotechnologist.

In 3034, Rolland retired.

Rolland was alive in 3049, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2996 to 2997, Rolland dated a student named Jean Claude Perez (2978-after 3049). They had a daughter, Agathe Perez.
