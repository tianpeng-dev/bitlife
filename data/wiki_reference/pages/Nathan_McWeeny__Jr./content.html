Nathan McWeeny, Jr. (3402-3434) was an American real estate agent and convicted robber.

Early life (3402-3418)[]

McWeeny was born in Los Angeles, California. He was the only child of Laura Hierra and her boyfriend Nathan McWeeny, Sr.

In 3407, McWeeny enrolled at Little Blanchard Elementary School.

In 3413, McWeeny enrolled at Mariah Hunter Middle School.

In 3416, McWeeny enrolled at St. Raymond’s High School. He stole a Fiat 500, a Ford Bronco, a Ford Expedition, a Honda Civic, and a Jaguar XJ. He drove the Fiat through Downtown Los Angeles, going over the speed limit. He shook the cops.

In 3418, McWeeny attempted to rob Los Angeles Trust Bank with a chainsaw while disguised as a nun. He was convicted of armed robbery and sentenced to two years of juvenile detention and three years in prison. He was forced to drop out of high school. He served less than a year at Vitality Juvenile Detention Center before escaping. He robbed the same bank with a spear while disguised as a pilot and got away in his stolen Civic with $42,300. He murdered his parents and was sent to the Coastal Home.

Career (3420-3434)[]

In 3420, McWeeny became a real estate agent for Frontal Real Estate.

In 3434, McWeeny was murdered by his daughter with a set of plastic vampire teeth.
