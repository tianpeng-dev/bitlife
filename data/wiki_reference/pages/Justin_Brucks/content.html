Justin Brucks (born November 19, TBD) is an American astronaut, Space Force pilot, former Air Force fighter pilot and former professional basketball player. He played in the NBA for three years, winning several awards and helping his team to win many games, even a championship or two. After his early retirement from basketball, he served in the Air Force for two years before attending the Space Academy, after which he became a Space Force pilot and eventually an astronaut for NASA.

Buckles had a passion for both sports and space in his youth. He first went to space camp at the age of eleven during his first year of middle school, which was also when he started playing basketball. He also played basketball in high school and played very well, becoming captain. After graduating from high school, he enrolled in Cleveland State University, Class of (TBD), joining the Vikings basketball team.
