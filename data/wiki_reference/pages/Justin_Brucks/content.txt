Justin Brucks
(born November 19, TBD) is an American astronaut, Space Force pilot, former Air Force fighter pilot and former professional basketball player. He played in the NBA for three years, winning several awards and helping his team to win many games, even a championship or two. After his early retirement from basketball, he served in the Air Force for two years before attending the Space Academy, after which he became a Space Force pilot and eventually an astronaut for NASA.
