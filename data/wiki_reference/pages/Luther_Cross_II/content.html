Luther Cross II (2894-2990) was a French pornographer, politician, lawyer, and judge who became President of the Regional Council of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes.

Early life (2894-2912)[]

Cross was born in Lyon, France. He was the youngest of six children born to Marshall Cross II and the younger of two born to his second girlfriend, Mila Martinez. He was named for his 14x great-uncle.

Cross went to Watchtower Preparatory School and graduated in 2912.

Porn career (2912-2926)[]

In 2912, Cross became a porn cameraman for Gamma Erotica.

In 2915, Cross was promoted to porn actor.

In 2926, Cross became famous. He resigned and enrolled at Sciences Po Lyon, from which he graduated with an Licence in Political Science in 2930. He then went to Lyon 3, from which he graduated with an LLM in 2933.

First political career (2933-2943)[]

In 2933, Cross ran for election to his arrondissement’s school board and beat Lila Gerard.

In 2938, Cross ran for reelection and beat Charles Boyer.

In 2943, Cross ran for Mayor of Lyon as a member of La France Insoumise, but lost to Ecologist candidate Joseph Picard.

Legal career (2943-2948)[]

In 2943, Cross became a junior associate for David and Associates.

In 2947, Cross was promoted to associate.

Second political career (2948-2968)[]

In 2948, Cross ran for Mayor of Lyon as a member of LFI and beat Socialist candidate Alicia Bernard.

In 2953, Cross ran for reelection and beat Republican candidate Alya Pasteur.

In 2958, Cross ran for President of the Regional Council of Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes as a Republican and beat Renaissance candidate Lyam Gaillard.

In 2963, Cross ran for reelection and beat Renaissance candidate Milo da Silva.

In 2968, Cross ran for President of France, but lost to Renaissance candidate Victoria Francois.

Judicial career (2968-2973)[]

In 2968, Cross was appointed as a police court judge.

In 2973, Cross ran for President of France, but lost to LFI candidate Mohamed Simon. He resigned.

Later life (2973-2990)

In 2990, Cross died of old age.
