Gavin Grigalashvili (born Georgia Grigalashvili, 2024-2081) was an Australian-Chinese church singer.

Early life[]

Grigalashvili was born in Newcastle, Australia. His father, Koba Grigalashvili (2007-after 2046), is a makeup artist. His mother, Julia Bennett (2007-July 18, 2024), was a murderer infamous for killing her own father and escaping from prison.

In 2028, Grigalashvili came out of the closet as a transgender boy and changed his first name to Gavin.

Grigalashvili attended public school at Quantum Primary School and Macquarie Secondary School. He started taking accordion lessons at age six, both voice and acting lessons at age 10, and modeling classes at 16.

Music career[]

In 2042, Grigalashvili signed with Smith Beats as a solo worship singer. He released one church album with Smith Beats.

In 2043, he created a YouTube channel, which has 38,000 subscribers.

In 2044, he was released by Smith Beats and signed with Abstract Studios. He released two church albums with Abstract.

In 2046, he left his contract to emigrate to Shenzhen, China. He joined the Reekin’ Deacons and released one church album with them.

Murder[]

In 2081, Grigalashvili was found dead in his apartment. The case was ruled a murder, but the perpetrator was never found.

Relationship and children[]

In 2042, Grigalashvili started dating Aliana Puffington (2023-2109), the Chief of Police of Newcastle Police Department. They met on a celebrity dating app. After emigrating, Puffington joined the People’s Liberation Army Navy.

In 2045, Grigalashvili and Puffington adopted a boy named Clive Grigalashvili (né Milat, b. 2039). Clive would later join the PLAN like his mother, date a woman named Hilde Eide, and father a daughter named Shu Grigalashvili (born 2064).

In 2046, Grigalashvili and Puffington adopted a girl named Lijuan Grigalashvili (née Dou, b. 2043).

Pets[]

In 2042, Grigalashvili adopted a mutt named Connor (2038-2050). Grigalashvili gave Connor to his daughter, Lijuan, in 2046.

In 2045, Grigalashvili bought a wildcat named Ninja (2042-after 2046) from an exotic pet dealer. Grigalashvili had to sell Ninja to emigrate to China.
