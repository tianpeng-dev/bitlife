Early life and education
[

Piner was born in Houston, Texas to Diana (1917 - 1991) and Cooper Piner (1918 - 1993). Her mother was a school administrator, and her father was a marine biologist and Marin Corps veteran of World War II. She had an older brother, Steven (1939 - 2015), a grocer for Henderson Foods, and a Cavalier King Charles spaniel named Lanika (d. 1975).
