Nico Eidman (1925 - April 8, 2020) was a German man known for being a gold-digger.

Boyhood[]

Nico was born and raised in Cologne. He spent much of his childhood engaging with the Hitler Youth, showing strong support for Nazism, though he would later change his ideology and views upon realizing the evil nature of the Party. Nico had at least two siblings, including a sister named Anne and a brother named Leopold.

Adult life[]

Eidmen reportedly joined the military after graduating secondary school to fight in the war. Although no official records of his service are known to exist, numerous ex-Nazi veterans claimed to remember him fighting alongside them. Aside from his supposed valor, Eidmen did not appear to engage in any other formal employment.

Personal life[]

Throughout his life, Eidman engaged in seven romances, a few of which developed into marriage. He had a total of seven children, supposedly one from each relationship. Eidman died on April 8, 2020, and is survived by any of his surviving children, as well as his nine grandchildren. He had a net worth of almost €13 million (nearly $14 million USD).
