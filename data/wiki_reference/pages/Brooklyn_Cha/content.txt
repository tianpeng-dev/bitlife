Brooklyn Cha
(3358-after 3438) was an American-Aruban mobile app developer.
Early life (3358-3380)
[
]
Cha was born
Brooklyn Marconi
in Las Vegas, Nevada.
In 3364, Cha was adopted by
Ha-Eun Cha X
, making her the youngest of her five children and her only child with her second partner Ronald Blanchard.
In 3372, Cha enrolled at Jimmy McLaughlin High School.
In 3376, Cha graduated from high school. She enrolled at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas.
In 3380, Cha graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.
Career (3380-3405)
[
]
In 3380, Cha became a junior app developer for Paramount. She earned $1 million from the casino. She was cured of constipation after taking toe of frog prescribed by a witch doctor.
In 3386, Cha was promoted to app developer.
In 3392, Cha was promoted to senior app developer.
In 3398, Cha was promoted to app designer.
In 3399, Cha became famous.
In 3400, Cha ran for President of the United States, but lost the Nazi primary.
In 3401, Cha ran for both Governor of Nevada and Mayor of Las Vegas, but lost the Libertarian primaries.
In 3405, Cha emigrated to Oranjestad, Aruba.
Marriages, relationship, and children
[
]
From 3371 to 3376, Cha dated a student named Bobby Roosevelt (3358-after 3425).
Cha had three children conceived by sperm donation:
Chin Cha XVI (3377-after 3438), receptionist
Ha-Eun Cha XI (3380-after 3438), marine biologist
Scarlett Cha VIII (3403-after 3438), pornographer
Cha has an adopted son:
Benjamin Cha II
In 3401, Cha was briefly married to a teacher named John Begay-Newton (3370-after 3245) and an animator named Clark Gooch (3372-after 3245).
In 3404, Cha was briefly married to a marine biologist named Tyreese Mayberry (3368-after 3245).
In 3405, Cha was briefly married to an unemployed man named Crispin Vazquez (3382-after 3245). She earned over $5 million from divorce settlements.
