Andrew Cha (3023-July 5, 3094) was an American convicted burglar and car thief.

Early life (3023-3041)[]

Cha was born in Houston, Texas. He was the youngest of four children born to Chin Cha V and his girlfriend Aaliyah Sack.

In 3028, Cha enrolled at Little Egbert Elementary School.

In 3034, Cha enrolled at Bronze Middle School and joined the football team.

In 3037, Cha enrolled at Houston High School and joined the football team.

In 3040, Cha went to the movies and went to a Jason Aldean concert with his best friend, Randy Casanova (b. 3023).

In 3041, Cha graduated from high school.

Prison sentences (3041-3094)[]

In 3041, Cha burgled an old man‘s house. He tried to run from the police, but was caught. He was sentenced to 3 years in prison for burglary. He escaped, but was later caught and sent back to prison. He appealed to the Texas 1st Court of Appeals, but lost. He served his first sentence at Pacific Supermax.

In 3044, Cha was released from prison. He stole a Toyota Tundra and tried to run from the police, but was caught. He was sentenced to 11 years in prison for theft. He appealed, but lost. He served his second sentence at Rabbit’s Foot Regional Prison.

In 3055, Cha was released. He stole a Hyundai Sonata and tried to run from the police, but was caught. He was sentenced to 15 years in prison for theft. He appealed, but lost. He served his third sentence at Rabbit’s Foot Regional Prison.

In 3068, Cha was released on parole. He stole a Subaru Outback and tried to run from the police, but was caught. He was sentenced to 19 years in prison for theft. He appealed, but lost. He served his fourth sentence at Rabbit’s Foot Regional Prison.

In 3082, Cha was released on parole. He burgled a farmer’s house and tried to run from the police, but was caught. He was sentenced to 22 years in prison for burglary. He appealed, but lost. He served his fifth sentence at Rabbit’s Foot Regional Prison.

On July 5, 3094, Cha died of old age.
