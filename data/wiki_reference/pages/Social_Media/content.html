Overview[]

Social Media is a feature in BitLife which is accessible through Assets or Activities. There are currently seven social media platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Onlyfans (added in 2023), TikTok, Twitch (added in 2023), Twitter, and YouTube. Like real life, not every country has access to social media sites.

Social media is essential for getting the Influencer ribbon. For characters who are famous, posting will increase their fame. If they are no longer famous, however, posting will make them lose followers.

Onlyfans[]

Onlyfans functions a lot differently from the other platforms. Onlyfans is easy to grow, and rewards heaps of money. Players can manage a monthly subscription fee, but be careful not to set it too high, as it will cause people to unsubscribe. Players can also collect tips, which will usually open a scenario where the person requests a private video from the player. Refusing a private video will result in the player losing a lot of subscribers. But, the holy grail of Onlyfans, is the wishlist. Players can request the following for their wishlist:

  • Properties
  • Cars
  • Exotic Cars
  • Cash
  • Rings
  • Earrings
  • Airplanes
  • Boats
  • Helicopters
  • Jetskis
  • Necklaces
  • Bracelets

If done properly, Onyfans can rake in millions more than all of the other job packs COMBINED. Onlyfans is one of the best ways to make money in the modern game.
