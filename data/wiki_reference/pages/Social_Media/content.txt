Social Media
is a feature in BitLife which is accessible through Assets or Activities. There are currently seven social media platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Onlyfans (added in 2023), TikTok, Twitch (added in 2023), Twitter, and YouTube. Like real life, not every country has access to social media sites.
Social media is essential for getting the Influencer
ribbon
. For characters who are
famous
, posting will increase their
fame
. If they are no longer famous, however, posting will make them lose followers.
