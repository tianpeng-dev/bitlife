Precious Murphy
(1928 - 1953) was a famous actress.
Early life
[
]
Murphy was born in 1928 to a family prominent in the performing arts.
Career
[
]
When Murphy began her movie career is disputed. She either went straight into the business at age 18 in 1946, or she went to college and then started acting at age 22 in 1950. The latter possibility would have given her only three years of time to rise to popularity.
Suicide
[
]
Murphy died by suicide in 1953, aged 25, while filming a production in Lagos, Nigeria.
