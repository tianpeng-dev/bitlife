Early life (3202-3221)
[
]
Cha was born in Cagliari, Italy. He is the youngest of five children born to
Andrew Cha II
and his girlfriend Annabella Vespucci.
In 3213, Cha enrolled at Garibaldi Middle School.
In 3214, Cha joined the Garibaldi Middle School soccer team.
In 3216, Cha enrolled at St. Remo’s Secondary School and joined the soccer team.
In 3219, Cha became captain of the St. Remo’s Secondary School soccer team.
In 3221, Cha graduated from secondary school.
Sports career (3221-3243)
[
]
In 3221, Cha emigrated to Seville, Spain. He joined Sevilla as a midfielder.
In 3224, Cha passed a doping test.
In 3238, Cha was released by Sevilla. He emigrated to London, England and joined West Ham.
In 3242, Cha was released by West Ham. He emigrated to Fortaleza, Brazil and joined Ceará.
In 3243, Cha was released by Ceará. He emigrated back to London.
Police career (3244-3256)
[
]
In 3244, Cha became a constable for the Metropolitan Police.
In 3247, Cha was promoted to sergeant.
In 3250, Cha was promoted to inspector.
In 3253, Cha was promoted to chief inspector.
In 3256, Cha was promoted to superintendent. His death date is unknown.
Relationships and children
[
]
From 3214 to 3242, Cha dated a student named Laura Campagna (3202-after 3256), who became a receptionist in 3222. They had four children:
Lieutenant Colonel Ha-Eun Cha VII (3216-after 3256), Italian Air Force officer
Chin Cha XII (3219-after 3256), pilot
Scarlett Cha VI (3234-after 3256)
Andrew Cha IV
From 3242 to 3243, Cha dated a student named Mariana Carvalho (3220-after 3243).
In 3243, Cha and Campagna started dating again.
