Leo Statham XXIX (5920-after 5965) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5920-5945)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the only child of Leo Statham XXVIII and his ex-girlfriend Clara Campbell.

In 5938, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5942, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5945-5960)[]

In 5945, Statham graduated with a Doctorate of Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Clara Patten.

In 5950, Statham ran for reelection and beat Nathan Popkin.

In 5955, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Isabella Osgood.

In 5960, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5965, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5938 to 5940, Statham dated a Japanese-born student named Airi Sasaki (5920-after 5965). They had a son, Leo Statham XXX.
