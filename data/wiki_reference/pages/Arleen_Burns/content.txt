Arleen was born to two parents. Arleen's father, Montgomery Burns, a photographer, was 30 years old at the time of her birth. Her mother, Veronica Burns, a nude art model, was 29 years old at the time of her birth.

Age 6 (2022): Arleen was enrolled at Crown Elementary School.
Age 8 (2024): Arleen was diagnosed with the measles.
Age 9 (2025): Arleen was diagnosed with depression.
Age 10 (2026): Arleen's parents bought a cat for her named Luke. She is no longer suffering from depression.
Age 13 (2029): Arleen's cat, Luke, escaped from home. She couldn't be bothered to find him.
Age 15 (2031): Arleen was enrolled in High School.
Age 16 (2032): Arleen applied for a driving license. She passed her test and got a license!
Age 18 (2034): Arleen applied for university, but was rejected. She wanted to major in Nursing.

Age 40 (2056): Arleen gave birth to a baby boy. She named the baby 'David Moorehead'.

Age 55 (2071): Arleen was diagnosed with depression.
Age 58 (2074): Arleen's mother, Veronica, died after suffering a stroke. Dion Moorehead proposed to Arleen. Arleen accepted.
Age 62 (2078): After 40 years together, Arleen Burns and Dion Moorehead married at a Church. Arleen changed her name to Arleen Moorehead.
