Early Life (2016-2034)[]

Arleen was born to two parents. Arleen's father, Montgomery Burns, a photographer, was 30 years old at the time of her birth. Her mother, Veronica Burns, a nude art model, was 29 years old at the time of her birth.

Age 5 (2021): Arleen's family mutt, Nymeria, died from intestinal issues at the age of 9.

Age 6 (2022): Arleen was enrolled at Crown Elementary School.

Age 8 (2024): Arleen was diagnosed with the measles.

Age 9 (2025): Arleen was diagnosed with depression.

Age 10 (2026): Arleen's parents bought a cat for her named Luke. She is no longer suffering from depression.

Age 13 (2029): Arleen's cat, Luke, escaped from home. She couldn't be bothered to find him.

Age 15 (2031): Arleen was enrolled in High School.

Age 16 (2032): Arleen applied for a driving license. She passed her test and got a license!

Age 18 (2034): Arleen applied for university, but was rejected. She wanted to major in Nursing.

Adult Life (2034-2080)[]

Age 19 (2035): Arleen got an interview for The City of Baltimore's Bus Driver opening. She was hired for Bus Driver for the City of Baltimore, with a salary of $19,815.

Age 20 (2036): Arleen is no longer suffering from the measles.

Age 22 (2038): Arleen is now dating Dion Moorehead.

Age 26 (2042): Arleen was assaulted. She called the police but the woman fled before they arrived.

Age 27 (2043): Arleen was diagnosed with depression.

Age 30 (2046): Arleen became pregnant with Dion's baby!

Age 31 (2047): Arleen gave birth to a baby boy. She named the baby 'Marcus Moorehead'.

Age 33 (2049): Arleen chose to vaccinate her son, Marcus.

Age 37 (2053): Arleen's mother, Veronica, retired.

Age 39 (2055): Arleen became pregnant with Dion's baby!

Age 40 (2056): Arleen gave birth to a baby boy. She named the baby 'David Moorehead'.

Age 54 (2070): Arleen's father, Montgomery, died from complications of old age. Arleen's son, David, died from being eaten by a shark. She attended both funerals.

Age 55 (2071): Arleen was diagnosed with depression.

Age 58 (2074): Arleen's mother, Veronica, died after suffering a stroke. Dion Moorehead proposed to Arleen. Arleen accepted.

Age 62 (2078): After 40 years together, Arleen Burns and Dion Moorehead married at a Church. Arleen changed her name to Arleen Moorehead.

Retirement To Death (2079-2088)[]

Age 67 (2083): Arleen retired from her position as Bus Driver. She had spent 47 years in the role.

Age 71 (2087): Arleen was diagnosed with the Bubonic Plague.

Age 72 (2088): Arleen died with complications associated with the Bubonic Plague. Her net worth was $124,803 at the time of her death.[]
