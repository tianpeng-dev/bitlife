In this Korean name, the surname is Ji.

Ji Jung (2822-2907) was a South Korean photographer and actress.

Early life (2822-2840)[]

Ji was born in Gangnam, Seoul. She was the only child of Na Kang Mi IV and her boyfriend Ji Ha-joon.

In 2840, Ji graduated from upper secondary school.

Photography career (2841-2877)[]

In 2841, Ji became a photographer for Gangnam Tribune.

In 2877, Ji retired.

Acting career (2880-2886)[]

From 2880 to 2882, Ji played Aaron in the TV show The Fresh Prince of Wel-Fare.

From 2882 to 2883, Ji played Poppy James in Thin Peaks.

From 2883 to 2886, Ji started playing Breanna Alvarez in Melrose Bungalows.

Murder[]

In 2907, Ji was murdered by a hitman named Desmond Jones, who was hired by her daughter.

Marriage, relationship, and children[]

From 2858 to 2879, Ji was married to an insurance agent named Jun Sol, Sr. (2817-after 2886). They had a son:

  1. Jun Sol, Jr. (2878-2904), Jungwi (Lieutenant Junior Grade) in the ROK Navy

In 2882, Ji started dating a casino worker named Yu Yoongi (2838-2912). They had a daughter:

  1. Yu Kok Sun
