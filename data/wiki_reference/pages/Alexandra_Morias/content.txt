Alexandra Morias
(March 17, 1960 - May 14, 2022) was an American Coast Guard officer and flutist.

Early life
[
]
Alexandra was born in Harbor Beach, Michigan to Kylie (1921 - 2011) and
Richard "Ricky" Morias
(1923 - 1998). Her mother was a wedding planner, and her father was a World War II fighter pilot-turned flight attendant. She had an older brother, Dylan (1955 - 2018), a marriage counselor. She had a pet pussycat named Chico (d. 1966). Alexandra also had a stepfather, former flight attendant and combat pilot Anthony Ho (1923 - 2006), whom Kylie married a few years after Ricky's death; Ho was also a good friend of Ricky's, having served alongside him in the war.

Personal life
[
]
When she was five years old, Morias became friends with a fellow first-year elementary school classmate Santino "Sonny" Burton (born 1960), who would later become a truck driver for Legacy Trucking Company. After five years of friendship, they started dating at the age of ten. In 1981, after sixteen years of friendship and romance, Burton proposed to Morias with a 0.43-carat diamond ring on a mountain, to which she accepted. They married a year later and went on to have three sons, including Moe (born 1988),
Antonio
(born 1997), and Buddy (born 2004). Moe is a wedding planner for The Tyson Company. Antonio, influenced by his mother, enlisted in the Coast Guard after graduating high school. Buddy is a professional in the field of English, having earned the degree in college. Morias currently has one grandson named Forrest (born 2011), Moe's son from his marriage to mail carrier Ginger Rojas (born 1980).
Alexandra also had a niece and two nephews, Crystal (born 1982), Elon (born 1986), and Dean (born 1988). Crystal and Elon were born to Dylan when he was still single. He adopted Dean with his then-wife, interior designer Latoya Doolittle (born 1938). Dylan and Doolittle divorced in 1999. Crystal is an Air Force pilot, inspired by the service of her Grandpa Ricky. Elon is a trucker for Sunbeam Trucking. Dean is a receptionist at Legacy Automotive. Elon is married to translator Victoria Lord (born 1984).
Death
[
]
Morias died on May 14, 2022 at the age of 62. She is survived by her husband, three sons and grandson. In addition to her parents, she was predeceased by her brother, Dylan, who died in 2018 after mistakenly drinking window cleaner.
