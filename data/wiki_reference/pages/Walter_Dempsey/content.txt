Walter Dempsey
(November 22, 1925 - January 14, 2022) was an American and Canadian professional hockey player who played for the New York Slanders, the St. Louis Greens, the Las Vegas Dealers, the Columbus Black V-Necks, the Dallas Sunshines, the Florida Panties, the Montreal Beachcombers, and the Toronto Pancakes, all as a defender. He was also a successful travel agent for the Minneapolis Travel Agency from 1971 to 1991.

Personal life

In 1947, Dempsey hooked up with a fan, Karen O'Donnell-Poindexter (1913 - 2023), when she couldn't stop staring at his butt. Three years later, after her husband had passed away, they married. Aside from several step children, Walter had two of his own children with Karen: Slim (born 1951), an app developer, and Kiera (born 1953), a librarian.
Death
[
]
Dempsey died peacefully in his sleep on January 14, 2022. He is survived by his children and grandchildren.
