Darien von Bout (October 9, 1972 - December 27, 2024), also known as "Quick Fix" and "Dizzy Dan," was a Dutch drug dealer and mafia associate who reigned terror on Amsterdam for many years. He was also a prolific gambler who briefly owned a casino called No Doubt Von Bout from the early-to-mid-2000s.

Early life and beginnings[]

Dar was born in Amsterdam to Jens Von Bout (1936 - 2019), a senior enlisted pilot in the Royal Netherlands Air Force, and Femke Dekker (1935 - 2015), a restaurant businesswoman who managed the Casa Bellflower. He had an older sister, Lianne (1971 - 2009), a firefighter. The von Bouts also had a pet cat named Poppy (1970 - 1985).

Career[]

Powder Bruises cartel[]

Von Bout graduated from secondary school in 1990. He attempted to join the Feather Weights cartel but was rejected. So, he joined the Powder Bruises instead.

Canali crime family and casino business[]

In 2002, Von Bout's appeal was rejected. He started a prison riot that injured eighteen people and killed two others. Dar managed to escape afterwards and subsequently left the Powder Bruises cartel. Shortly later, he joined the notorious mafia family Canali as an associate after providing alibis whenever the police asked about shady activity in the vicinity. That same year, Von Bout bought a gambling den for €158,000 ($179,484.27 USD; $319,059.98 in 2025), naming it the No Doubt Von Bout Casino.

Worm picking[]

After being kicked out of the Canali family, Von Bout tried to look for a legitimate job so he could lay low for a while. He had lost access to numerous positions because of his criminal record. Eventually, he found a steady job as a worm picker for 24-karaats opgenomen (24 Karat Incorporated). Dar didn't find as much excitement in the picking of worms as he did in the crime business, but he was glad to have a change in pace, whereas he worked in a peaceful job that lacked life-risking responsibilities. He worked for 24 Karar Inc. until resigning in 2012.

Personal life[]

Von Bout shared his first kiss with classmate Tess Bijl. He later tried asking her out, but she came out as a lesbian. Unbothered by this, he started going out with other childhood friend Evelien Rijkaard, who would become a firefighter. After dating for eight years, they married in 1993 when they were both 21.
