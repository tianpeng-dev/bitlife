(June 18, 1908 - April 22, 2024) was an American baseball player and supercentenarian.

Early life
[
]
Perry was born in Compton to Imani (1879 - 1953) and Lucas "Luke" Garfield (1865 - 1945). His mother was a real estate agent and his father was a travel agent. He had a little sister, Taylor (1916 - 1996), who worked as a paralegal for Balls & Associates. The Garfields also has a family cat named Koko (1907 - 1921).
