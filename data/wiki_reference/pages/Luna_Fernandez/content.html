Luna Fernandez (2241-2328) was a British hairdresser, lawyer, and judge.

Early life[]

Fernandez was born in Sheffield, England. Her father was Bob Fernandez. She went to high school and graduated in 2259.

Hairdresser career[]

In 2259, Fernandez became a hairdresser for Greenhouse Salon. She resigned in 2278 and enrolled at the University of Sheffield. She graduated with a BS in Political Science in 2282. She then enrolled at University of Sheffield School of Law.

Legal career[]

Fernandez passed the bar exam in 2285. She became a junior associate for Cockfoster and Associates. The same year, she mastered the violin.

In 2289, Fernandez was promoted to associate.

In 2293, Fernandez was promoted to junior partner.

In 2297, Fernandez made partner.

In 2302, Fernandez became a magistrate for the City of Sheffield.

In 2307, Fernandez resigned.

Death[]

In 2328, Fernandez died of natural causes. Her ashes were scattered at a church.

Relationship and children[]

From 2278 to 2281, Fernandez dated a nail technician named Ryan Winwood (2246-2299). They had two daughters, Penelope (2280-after 2332) and Sara Winwood (2281-after 2332).

Fernandez has five children, Alexander Fernandez, Spencer (2284-after 2332), Audrey (2287-after 2332), Joan (2289-after 2332), and Rita Fernandez (2290-after 2332), who were conceived via artificial insemination.
