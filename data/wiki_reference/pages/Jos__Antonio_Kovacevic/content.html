José Antonio Kovacevic (3580-3567) was a Mexican businessman.

Early life (3580-3598)[]

Kovacevic was born in Nogales, Sonora. His parents were Tin Kovacevic and Virginia Marquez.

In 3581, Tin Kovacevic married Rosa Cabrera.

Kovacevic went to Family Secondary School and graduated in 3598.

Business career (3598-3651)[]

In 3598, Kovacevic acquired a pet supply store called Platinum Pet Land. He sold the store in 3651.

Death[]

In 3567, Kovacevic died of old age. His daughter attended his funeral.

Relationship and child[]

In 3594, Kovacevic started dating a student named Petra Espinoza (3580-3566). They had a daughter, Gloria Kovacevic II.
