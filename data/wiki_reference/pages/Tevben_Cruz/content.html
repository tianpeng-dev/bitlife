Tevben Cruz (March 5, 1961-November 9, 2023) was an American killer, IT support manager, and computer programmer.


Tevben graduated from high school in 1981, and applied to college, graduating in 1985, becoming an IT support manager from 1985-1998, later becoming a jr. computer programmer, being promoted to Computer Programmer in 2001. In 2001 he assaulted his half brother, and was fired and put behind bars until 2004. In 2005 his wife asked for a divorce, and was finalized and assaulted his wife by biting her lip off, she died from injuries, he was then put in the slammer for 14 years, leaving the prison in 2019. He died on November 9, 2023 after suffering a stroke.
