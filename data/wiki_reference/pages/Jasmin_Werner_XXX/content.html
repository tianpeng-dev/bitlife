Early life (5031-5053)[]

Werner was born in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. She was the youngest of three children born to Eberhard Werner XXIX and his ex-girlfriend Ellen Singh.

In 5037, Werner enrolled at Little Poirot Primary School.

In 5043, Werner enrolled at St. Chichi’s Middle School.

In 5046, Werner enrolled at Henri Dube Secondary School.

In 5049, Werner enrolled at the University of Saskatchewan.

Career (5053-5071)[]

In 5053, Werner became a crime scene technician for the Saskatoon Police Service.

In 5059, Werner was promoted to forensic investigator.

In 5071, Werner resigned. She emigrated to Guayaquil, Ecuador.

Life in Ecuador (5071-5075)[]

In 5072, Werner installed X.

Prison sentence (5075-5097)[]

In 5075, Werner emigrated to Blackpool, England. She was convicted of murdering her second husband, Jesse Statham, and was sentenced to 47 years in prison. She appealed the sentence to the Court of Appeal, Criminal Division, and her sentence was reduced to 27 years. She served her sentence at Blackpool Max Security Prison.

In 5097, Werner was released on licence.

Death[]

In 5115, Werner died of a stroke. Her stepdaughter, Kate, attended her funeral.
