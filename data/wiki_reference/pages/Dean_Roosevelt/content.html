Dean Roosevelt (2904-after 2947) was an American-Bolivian pornographer.

Early life (2904-2922)[]

Roosevelt was born in San Francisco, California. He was the youngest of five brothers born to Carlton Roosevelt XIX and the only one born to his second girlfriend Shauna Houle.

In 2909, Roosevelt enrolled at Woody Cockburn Elementary School.

In 2915, Roosevelt enrolled at Seamen Middle School.

In 2918. Roosevelt enrolled at St. Raven’s High School.

In 2922, Roosevelt graduated from high school.

Porn career (2922-2947)[]

In 2922, Roosevelt emigrated to Sucre, Bolivia. He became a porn actor for Cum Laude Adult Theater.

Roosevelt was alive in 2947, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 2914 to 2923, Roosevelt dated a student named Lisa Miller (2904-after 2974), who became a restaurateur in 2923. They had two sons, Carlton XXII (2921-after 2974) and Winston Roosevelt.
