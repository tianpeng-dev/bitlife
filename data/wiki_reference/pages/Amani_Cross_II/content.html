Amani Cross II (2490-after 2554) was an American-Peruvian football player and restaurateur.

Early life (2490-2512)[]

Cross was born in Jacksonville, Florida. He was the only child born to Quincy Cross and his girlfriend Desiree Anderson. He was named after his 5x great-granduncle.

In 2504, Cross burgled a thug’s house.

Cross went to Rosebud Preparatory School and was on the football team.

Cross went to the University of North Florida, where he was a member of Alpha Phi Alpha and played football for the Ospreys. He graduated in 2512 with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing.

In 2511, Cross went clubbing at Marathon.

Sports career (2512-2519)[]

In 2512, Cross joined the Los Angeles Stars as a quarterback.

In 2518, Cross bought a haunted chateau.

In 2519, Cross was released by the Stars. He emigrated to Lima, Peru.

Restaurant career (2542-2554)[]

In 2542, Cross became a dishwasher for Rebeca’s Restaurant.

In 2544, Cross was promoted to busser.

In 2546, Cross was promoted to host.

In 2548, Cross was promoted to food runner.

In 2550, Cross was promoted to server.

In 2552, Cross was promoted to head waiter.

In 2554, Cross was promoted to expeditor. His death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

In 2500, Cross started dating his friend, Ally Parker (2490-after 2554), who became a restaurateur in 2509. They had four children:

  1. Bart Cross II (2507-after 2554), lobbyist
  2. Abigail Cross II (2521-after 2554), teacher
  3. Mikayla Cross II (2522-after 2554), hairdresser
  4. Julian Cross II
