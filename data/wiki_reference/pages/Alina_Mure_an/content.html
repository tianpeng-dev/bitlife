Alina Mureșan (1505-1576) was the daughter of Barbu Mureșan and Carmen Barbu and a wallachian boyaress.

Art Handler[]

Just like her grandma, Ioana, Alina had an unusual occupation for somebody from that time and was an art handler from 1533 to 1566.

Personal Life[]

Alina was together with Andreas Sandu (1506-1580) and in 1524 they got an illegitimate daughter, Voica Sandu-Mureșan (1524-?), which forced them to marry in 1525. They got five other children:

Barbu Sandu-Mureșan (1528-1587)

Alexandru Sandu-Mureșan (1532-?)

Dimitrie Sandu-Mureșan (1534-1540), who died of cancer of the lungs

Ruxandra Sandu-Mureșan (1536-?)

Dumitrana Sandu-Mureșan (1544-?), who was the grandmother of Alexandru Stan (1644-?), adopted in 1646 by Maria Sandu-Mureșan.

Alina and Andreas divorced in 1550, after he cheated on her. In 1561 she remarried to retired knight (retired marine), Augustin Rusu (1490-?), but divorced in 1565, after he also cheated on her.

Alina died of old age in 1576.
