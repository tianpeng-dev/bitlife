Audrey II, Queen of England
[
]
Audrey Thatcher, born Princess Audrey of Marlborough, reigned as the Queen of England until her death of (CAUSE).
She was preceded by her father, King Louis II, and she was succeeded by her son Prince James of Clarendon.
She was married to her husband, Prince Louis Curry of England. The couple had two children, Prince James of Clarendon and Princess Elizabeth of Stansgate.
She had two brothers, Prince Adam of Marlborough (Duke Hugo of Exeter, Duke Leo of Nottingham),  and Prince Russell of Marlborough.
