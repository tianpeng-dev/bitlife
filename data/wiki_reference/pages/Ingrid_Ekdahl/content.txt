Ingrid Ekdahl
(4075-after 4113) was a Swedish politician and mail carrier.

Early life (4075-4093)
[
]
Ekdahl was born in Malmö, Sweden. She was the younger of two children born to
Isabella Petterson II
and her second boyfriend Noah Ekdahl.
In 4082, Ekdahl enrolled at Nutti Primary School.
In 4091, Ekdahl enrolled at Illumina Preparatory School. She graduated in 4093.
Political career (4093-4097)
[
]
In 4093, Ekdahl ran for election to the Malmö School Board. She beat Hugo Nutti and was inaugurated.
In 4097, Ekdahl did not run for reelection.
Postal career (4098-4113)
[
]
In 4098, Ekdahl became a junior mail carrier for PostNord Sweden.
In 4101, Ekdahl was promoted to mail carrier.
In 4104, Ekdahl was promoted to senior mail carrier.
Ekdahl was alive in 4113, but her death date is unknown.
Relationships and children
[
]
From 4085 to 4091, Ekdahl dated a student named Alvin Bjorklund (4075-after 4094).
In 4091, Ekdahl dated a student named Casper Jansson (4075-after 4113). They have two children, Viktor (4092-after 4113) and
Olivia Jansson
.
