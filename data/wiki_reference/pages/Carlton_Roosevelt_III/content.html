Carlton Roosevelt III (2130-2214) was an Israeli-American retired receptionist.

Early life (2130-2148)[]

Roosevelt was born in Tel Aviv, Israel. He was the youngest of three children born to Kevin Roosevelt and his ex-girlfriend Leah Avraham.

In 2148, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school. He emigrated to Los Angeles, California.

Career (2160-2196)[]

In 2160, Roosevelt became a receptionist for Gravity Mills.

In 2196, Roosevelt retired.

Death[]

In 2214, Roosevelt died of old age. His son planned his funeral and had him cremated.

Relationship and child[]

From 2148 to 2149, Roosevelt dated an Army enlistee named Jessica Pyle (2130-2186). They had a son, Carlton Roosevelt IV.
