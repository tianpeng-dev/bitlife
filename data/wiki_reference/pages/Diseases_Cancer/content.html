Cancer

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)

A Cancer is the worst type of disease. Like any other disease, you can randomly get it. It is extremely hard to cure and will lead to a quick death if not cured. However, characters can still live as long as the health bar is still high and the player attempts to treat the disease. It is the most common deadly illness in the game. It is likely that your character could contract a cancer when they are mid-aged or while in prison. If a character dies from any cancer while very young, they will get the Unlucky ribbon. If the character is still in their childhood or an adult in prison when they contracted the cancer, there is a higher chance they will die. Having a gender-specific cancer will not go away with gender reassignment surgery, they will still continue to suffer from it and the player will still need their character cured as with all other cancers. Relationships could die from cancer. Pets may also get cancer and could die from it as well. The tanning bed can cause cancer of the skin, especially when you're older.
