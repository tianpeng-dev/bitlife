Paul Fernandez (2418-after 2464) was a German politician and hairdresser.

Early life[]

Fernandez was born in Berlin, Germany. His father is Leon Fernandez. He went to San Antonio High School.

Political career[]

In 2436, Fernandez ran for election to the Berlin GEB. He defeated Leo Schafer and was inaugurated the same year.

In 2440, Fernandez ran for reelection against Kaiser Schneider and won.

In 2444, Fernandez ran for a third term against Romy Schmitz and won.

In 2448, Fernandez ran for a fourth term against Ronja Decker and won.

In 2452, Fernandez ran for a fifth term against Carlo Krause and won.

In 2456, Fernandez announced that he would not be running for a sixth term.

Hairdresser career[]

In 2457, Fernandez became a hairdresser for Chateau Salon Berlin.

Fernandez outlived his second son Faber, but his date of death is unknown.

Relationship[]

From 2434 to 2437, Fernandez dated a student named Helena Pfeiffer (2418-after 2464), who became a camera operator in 2436. They had two sons, Luis (2436-after 2464) and Faber.
