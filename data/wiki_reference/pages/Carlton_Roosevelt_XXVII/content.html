Carlton Roosevelt XXVII (3124-May 28, 3172) was a Canadian farmer.

Early life (3124-3142)[]

Roosevelt was born in Halifax, Nova Scotia. He is the youngest of 29 children born to Venus Roosevelt XIII and the younger of two born to her second boyfriend Clement Gagnon.

In 3130, Roosevelt enrolled at Rebecca Landry Primary School.

In 3136, Roosevelt enrolled at Hole Middle School.

In 3139, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Alice’s Secondary School.

In 3142, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school.

Career (3142-3172)[]

In 3142, Roosevelt became a farmer for Crafty Meadow. He went on a Hot Cheetos diet.

Death[]

On May 28, 3172, Roosevelt tried to impale a magician on a wooden stake. The magician took the stake and stabbed Roosevelt in self-defense.

Relationship and children[]

From 3134 to 3141, Roosevelt dated a student named Alicia Herbert-Page (3124-3212). They had three children:

  1. Carlton Roosevelt XXVIII, twin
  2. Eli Roosevelt (3139-3227), twin, restaurateur
  3. Maryjane Zimmerman (3141-3230), trucker, with whom he had a perfect relationship
