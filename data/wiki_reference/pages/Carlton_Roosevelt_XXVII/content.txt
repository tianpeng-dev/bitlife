Early life (3124-3142)
[
]
Roosevelt was born in Halifax, Nova Scotia. He is the youngest of 29 children born to
Venus Roosevelt XIII
and the younger of two born to her second boyfriend Clement Gagnon.
In 3130, Roosevelt enrolled at Rebecca Landry Primary School.
In 3136, Roosevelt enrolled at Hole Middle School.
In 3139, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Alice’s Secondary School.
In 3142, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school.
Career (3142-3172)
[
]
In 3142, Roosevelt became a farmer for Crafty Meadow. He went on a Hot Cheetos diet.
Death
[
]
On May 28, 3172, Roosevelt tried to impale a magician on a wooden stake. The magician took the stake and stabbed Roosevelt in self-defense.
