In DogLife and CatLife, Human encounters are a type of scenario that is applied when you are in a habitat elsewhere than a household, you will commonly play through this storyline. A human encounter means you may get adopted.

Description[]

Shelter/Pet Store[]

When you live in a shelter or pet shop. A human encounter cames and you are many possibilities.

If the human encounter refuse to adopt you, you will stay on the pet store/shelter, if you get rejected too many times and died to the shelter, you will get the Reject ribbon.

If the human encounter accepts, he/her takes you in his/her household, possibly changes your name and your start a new life.

  • Misbehaving : There is a horrible choice, misbehaving to a human encounter (like attack, bark, flash your butt, mess or jump) lead to refuse to adopt you, employees will argue at you for doing this.
  • Give him/her puppy/kitty eyes : This option lead the dog/cat give puppy/kitty eyes when a human encounter arrived on the shelter/pet store, depending of your cuteness, he/her can refused to adopt you.
  • Nuzzle up : This option lead the dog/cat give puppy/kitty eyes when a human encounter arrived on the shelter/pet store, depending of your cuteness, he/her can refused to adopt you.
  • Be playful : This option lead the dog/cat to play when a human encounter arrived on the shelter/pet store, depending of your cuteness, he/her can refused to adopt you.

Street[]

When you are a stray dog/cat, sometimes, a human encounter can arrived and discovered you inside a street, you have many possibilities.

  • Lick him/her : If you choose to lick the human encounter, the human encounter can choose to tries to take you or left you out.
  • Attack him/her : If you choose to attack the human encounter, the human encounter can choose to tries to take you or left you out. You can also attack him and depending of the intensity of attack, kill him/her.
  • Misbehaving : If you choose to misbehaving at the human encounter, the human encounter can choose to tries to take you or left you out.
  • Run away for him/her : If you choose to flee the human encounter, a minigame starts and you need to avoid and jump all the obstacles. If you failed, the human encounter catch you and take you in the animal shelter or adopt you.

If you lick, attack or misbbehaving at a human encounter. The human encounter can choose to left you out or tries to take you, there are two possibilities.

  • Escaping : If you choose to escaping, a minigame starts and you need to avoid and jump all the obstacles. If you failed, the human encounter catch you and take you in the animal shelter or adopt you.
  • Let him/her take me : If you choose to let the human encounter take you, this option is considered like a surrending, the human encounter can take you in the animal shelter or adopt you.

Household[]

Sometimes, if you spend time with your owner(s), you can meet a human encounter, you can choose to attack him/her, pay him/her no attention, misbehaving, smell him/her or nuzzle/lick at him/her. If you choose to misbehaving (like : bark, attack, flash you butt, jump) at a human encounter while spending time, your owner(s) can argue at you for doing this, behaving at him (like : lick, nuzzle) will lead the human encounter can insult you or compliment you, pay him not attention is like a ignoring but your relationships with your owner(s) will not be impacted.

Sometimes, a burglar can to break into a house. You have four possibilities, hump his leg, attack him, run and hide or sit and watch. If you choose to hump his leg to reminder that you here, the burglar can attack you with a weapon (and sometimes to death) and escaping with precious treasures and cash. If you choose to attack him, he can attack you back, escaping with nothing or ignoring you and escaping with precious treasures. Sit and watch & Run and hide lead the burglar to escaping with cash and precious treasures. Depending of the event, failing to protect the house can lead your owner(s) to argue at you for not enough protecting the house (if you are lower or empty relationships), ignoring you, or reward you if you have kill the burglar.

The human encounter also is a mailman, you can misbehaving, behaving or pay the mailman not attention. Depending of his kindness and relationship, the mailman can insult you or ignore you.
