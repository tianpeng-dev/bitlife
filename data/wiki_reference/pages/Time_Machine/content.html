Time Machine

Launched on iOS:

July 2019

Launched on Android:

October 2019
Time Machine costs $0.99 and has to be purchased per usage. It is a new activity added to BitLife in July 2019 following its update. It was previously released only to Canadian players of BitLife prior. Time Machine can get you back up to 8 years. In version 3.5.1, character may also purchase an unlimited uses version for $14.99-$19.99. Time machines can help characters leave prison and avoid being diagnosed with a disease, but if a family member or lover dies, it will not prevent their death and will only keep them alive until the time that they died again, where they will die at the same age and cause of death that they were in their original death. If you murdered a family member or lover or were unable to save their life in unofficial SOS scenarios, it will revert their death as these deaths were caused by the player's actions, not by nature. In Prison, the time machine will remind the player how many years their character has been in prison as a guide of when to undo. As of October 2019, the time machine can revert your own character's death, but loved ones are still dead. When using the time machine, the character will age back to whatever they were in the years they were sent back to and their visit in the time machine will appear in their journal.

For some reason, if you reset the app after you use the Time Machine, the game will undo your time machine’s action and your character will continue to be deceased, therefore meaning you wasted your money.

Unlimited Time Machine[]

Since an app update, an Unlimited Time Machine purchase is available, where you do not need to purchase it per usage.
