Early life (2659-2677)[]

Roosevelt was born in San Francisco, California. She was the eldest of three children born to Sean Roosevelt and Nakita Peña-Reyes.

In 2677, Roosevelt graduated from high school.

Modeling career (2677-2678)[]

In 2677, Roosevelt became a foot model for San Francisco Models.

In 2678, Roosevelt prayed to the BitLife developers.

In 2679, Roosevelt resigned.

Porn career (2679-2691)[]

In 2686, Roosevelt became famous.

Relationships and children[]

From 2717 to 2719, Roosevelt and Brown dated a third time. They had a son, August Roosevelt (2719-after 2784), from their third relationship.

From 2748 to 2751, Roosevelt dated her talent agent, Angel Hole (2668-2758). They had two children, Penelope Hole (2750-after 2784) and Boomer Roosevelt.
