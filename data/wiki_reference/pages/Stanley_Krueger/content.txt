Early life (2628-2650)
[
]
Krueger was born in Jacksonville, Florida. His parents are
Jesse Krueger
and his first girlfriend Brittany Diggler. He graduated from the University of North Florida in 2650 with a BS in Engineering.
Military career (2650-2667)
[
]
In 2650, Krueger became an officer for the United States Navy. He reached the rank of first lieutenant before being honorably discharged in 2667.
Music career (2669-2697)
[
]
In 2669, Krueger signed with Tidal Beats as a rhythm and blues singer. He released his first alternative rap album,
Lectures of the Boulevards
.
In 2670, Krueger released his second album,
Taste of Brimstone
.
In 2671, Krueger released his third album,
Wading to Deflate
.
In 2672, Krueger released his fourth album,
Good Energy
.
In 2673, Krueger released his fifth album,
The Vectors We Stroke
. It went gold in 2680.
In 2676, Krueger released his sixth album,
The Vectors We Sketch
. It went gold less than a year later.
In 2679, Krueger released his seventh album,
Sincerely, Me
. It went gold in 2680.
In 2682, Krueger released his eighth album,
Perilous Moves
. It went gold in 2683.
In 2685, Krueger released his ninth album,
Afterlife of My Songs
. It went gold in 2686.
In 2688, Krueger released his 10th album,
Nip of Fire
. It went gold within a year and went platinum in 2695.
In 2696, Krueger released his 11th album,
When I Get on the Throne
. It went gold within a year.
In 2697, Krueger walked out on his contract and retired.
Death
[
]
In 2713, Krueger died of natural causes. His ashes were scattered in a garden.
Relationship and child
[
]
In 2667, Krueger started dating Wendy Chen (2649-after 2718), the CEO of Avalon Car Wash. They have a daughter,
Miley Krueger
.
