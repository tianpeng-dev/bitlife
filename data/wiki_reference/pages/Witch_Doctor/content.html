Witch Doctor

Launched on iOS:

September 2018 (default)

Launched on Android:

February 2019 (default)
The Witch Doctor is a type of doctor that can be used to cure a disease. It is most commonly used when the Medical Doctor and/or the Alternative Doctor doesn't work. If you attempt to get cured by the witch doctor, there is a chance you will be cured of your disease. Otherwise, it may kill you. If a character dies from a witch doctor, it may say their "life was cut short by a witch doctor" or vaguely, "died in mysterious circumstances" but in some cases, the gravestone will say "murdered by a witch doctor", meaning it could be intentional that the witch doctor is killing. On average, the witch doctor's treatments will not cure or kill you, but could lower your bars, especially your health bar. There is an achievement you can obtain if your ailment is cured by the witch doctor. It is believed that any disease or ailment can be cured by the witch doctor but it is rare that it will actually happen.
