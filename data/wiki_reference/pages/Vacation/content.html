Cruise[]

Cruises are the more expensive option. The location list is less than the vacation list. Unlike Vacations, parents do not decide to take their children or not in childhood. Cruises also bring happiness up higher than vacations. There are four cabin choices, listed from cheapest to priciest: Interior; Ocean View; Balcony; Suite.

Vacation[]

These are more like plane trips, and cost less than cruises. There are much more vacation locations than cruise locations. The four types of plane categories that can be paid for are Budget, Economy, Business and First. In childhood, parents may decide to take their child(s) with them, but the player can decide how their controlled NPC will react. They can choose to gratefully go, complain but go, or refuse. Visiting Winnipeg, Canada will unlock the "Winnipeg, Eh?"achievement. Custom cities can also be added by the player and can show up as vacation spots. Families can now go on vacation together and parents may ask you whether you'd like to go with them. You may go and be appreciative, go begrudgingly, or refuse to go. Vacation options reset every year.
