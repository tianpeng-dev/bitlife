Jasmin Werner X (4666-after 4722) was a Japanese restaurant worker.

Early life (4666-4704)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. She was the only child of Eberhard Werner XXI and his ex-girlfriend Rin Taniguchi.

In 4684, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4704-4722)[]

In 4704, Werner became a dishwasher for Yuko’s Cafe.

In 4706, Werner was promoted to busser.

In 4708, Werner was promoted to host.

In 4710, Werner was promoted to food runner.

In 4712, Werner was promoted to server.

In 4714, Werner was promoted to head waiter.

In 4716, Werner was promoted to expeditor.

In 4718, Werner was promoted to assistant manager.

In 4720, Werner was promoted to general manager.

Werner was alive in 4722, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4684 to 4685, Werner dated a Portuguese-born unemployed man named Cristiano Carvalho (4663-after 4722). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XXII.
