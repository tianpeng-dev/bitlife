Isabella Petterson II
(4056-after 4094) was a Swedish politician and exotic dancer.

Early life (4056-4074)
[
]
Petterson was born in Malmö, Sweden. She is the younger of two children born to
Carl Petterson
and his girlfriend Ines Berggren.
In 4063, Petterson enrolled at Avalon Primary School.
In 4072, Petterson enrolled at Malmö Secondary School. She graduated in 4074.
Political career (4074-4078)
[
]
In 4074, Petterson ran for election to the Malmö School Board. She beat Casper Qvist and was inaugurated.
In 4078, Petterson did not run for reelection. She left office.
Dance career (4079-4094)
[
]
In 4079, Petterson became an exotic dancer for Dag’s Showgirls.
Petterson was alive in 4094, but her death date is unknown.
Relationships and children
[
]
From 4066 to 4072, Petterson dated a student named Ludvig Olofsson (4056-after 4075).
In 4072, Petterson started dating a student named Noah Ekdahl (4056-after 4094). They have two daughters, Alice (4074-after 4094) and
Ingrid Ekdahl
.
