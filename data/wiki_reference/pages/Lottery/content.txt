Lottery
is an
activity
where the player may buy a ticket or 10 tickets to win a lottery jackpot prize. The prize is shown before buying the tickets and is random, but at least 5 figures. An
achievement
can be unlocked if the player wins the lottery jackpot. Winning the lottery is very rare and does not vary by
nation
, however all nations allow it unlike nations that ban gambling.
A popup in
Notifications
will sometimes say that "this is a good time" to play the lottery and may automatically win your character the jackpot if they buy a ticket but rarely, it will not.
