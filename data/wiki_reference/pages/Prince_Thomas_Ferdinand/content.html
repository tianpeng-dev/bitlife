Early Life (1967-1985)[]

Ferdinand was born in Hull, UK to a royalty of £21,620,094, many say he was regal from birth. According to his classmates, he exceled at everything school related. At age 4, he was suddenly diagnosed with whooping cough. Luckily, it was cured the very same year. At age 6, he was diagnosed with chicken pox, this stuck with him for many years. At age 10, he reportedly started crashing his classmates birthday parties. At age 12, he rejected a love interest by the name of Annabelle Dulwich. At age 14, Thomas' cat named Gypsy died of old age, this was the first of many events that sent Thomas over the edge. At age 16, he was diagnosed with acne that stayed until death.

Later Life (1986-1994)[]

By 18, he was an absolute star. One of the most famous people on Earth. At age 19, he started dating his future wife, Samantha Kingswood. He got payed over 2 million pounds to be in a commerical for Lillypad Securites. This was the first thing he did that infuriated the public. At age 20, he did lots. While his brother Ethan Ferdinand graduted university, he went on a shopping spree, buying thousands of dollars worth of instruments. He also won 36 million pounds gambling on keno across two sessions at a casino, starting his gambling addiction. At age 21, he proposed to Samantha Kingswood with a 16.77 karat emerald ring and was accepted. At age 22, he was payed 550 thousand to appear centerfold on the March issue of the Member magazine. This was another publicity stunt hated by the public and his fiancee, he denounced the criticism. Also at this age, he got married to Samantha in a cathedral to an attendance of 3,712 guests. Samantha quit her job as a magician to became Samantha Ferdinand, Princess of Robson-Burton. At age 23, he was diagnosed with depression, lost £9,070,000 on a game of keno, bought a 9 million pound airplane, went on a talk show, got shamed, denounced his critics, and earned £1.3M starring in a commerical. At 24, he bought a £2.5M mansion and moved in there with his wife. At 25, he was diagnosed with bunions and his wife became pregnant with his baby. At 26, his life turned completely around. The good news was that Samantha had given birth to his child, the baby girl was named Sierra Ferdinand, Duchess of Northampton. The bad news is that between 5 casino sessions, he lost £97M. This caused drama between him and his wife. To cope, he posed centerfold for £600K for the May issue of the Hoser magazine. At 27, he went over the edge. First, his big brother Ethan died in a terrorist attack.

Last Minutes (July 18-19th 1994)[]

On July 18th 1994, Las Vegas, 11:30pm, while drunk, in the span of 15 minutes, he lost 80 million dollars on blackjack. He then cried, left the casino, drove to his hotel room, and then supposedly had a panic attack for 20 minutes and killed himself on July 19th 1994, 12:15am. This was heard by a hotel janitor who then called the police, the police confirmed the body to Thomas Ferdinand. This event made worldwide coverage just the morning after.

After Death[]

This caused his wife to disband from the British royal family, come back to being a magician, and life a regular life. She now has 3 children, aged 31, 28, and 27. Her wife kept the last name Ferdinand, and her daughter Sierra lived the rest of her life as the Duchess of Northampton.
