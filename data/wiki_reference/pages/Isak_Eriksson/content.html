Isak Eriksson was the head of the Eriksson family, an old Swedish family that raised to prominence due to their real state business, due to disagreements with the Swedish Government Isak's grandfather Valter abandoned Sweeden settling with his wealth on neighbouring Russia.

The Eriksson family's patrimony has grown so big that the major part of its leaders choose to let others administrate it and dedicate themselves to their passions, among them was Isak's father that worked for more than fifty years as a District Judge while on the side lead his own cult the mysterious Henriks collective.

As the favourite son of a respected yet shady man, Isak inherited all the family's possessions, when he was on his late thirties being known as a trusted mechanical engineer.

Isak married Avdeyeva on his early twenties staying with her nearly until retirement, one of the two women of his life, Avdeyeva brought Isak into the cause of the Russian Green Party and it is through her reputation that Isak was able to climb the ranks.

As a politician Isak has long being criticised for his defense of the environment or the importance he places in the quality of public services, many calling him "The European" due to his close his agenda allingns with the European Union in contrast with Russian authoritarianism.

Being his background so wealthy, Isak contributed greatly to the finances of the Green Party, his charisma and dynamism raising the popularity of the party whenever he was a candidate, being the Mayor of Saint.Petersburg for two consecutive terms(18 years) before jumping to the Presidential elections.

Few Presidents expanded so much Russian's wellfare system, Isak is known for openly recognising the rights of ethnic minorities and granting independence referendums to the Republic of Sakha,Buryatia and the Caucasus, many painting him as a traitor that weakened Russia like no other but to others a true liberator, Green Isak modernised Russian industry ending their reliability on Fossil fuels and actively pushed for trye democracy in Russia.

After two terms(12 years), an old Isak retired to his family home, where along his second wife Polina indulged in orgies and fathered children until the end of his days, due to his disregard of moral conduct during this year he became known as "Rotten Isak", though to many Isak only did openly what other olygarchs had been doing in the shadows since the times of the old Soviet Union.

With his passing Ubba Eriksson, one of his sons by his second wife was recognised as the next head of the family, Isak's body was cremated with his ashes being brought to Halmstad the true starting point of the family and where they center their activity in Europe, thousands gathered to see the wind scatter the last remains of Isak the Good.
