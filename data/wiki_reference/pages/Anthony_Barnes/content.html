Anthony Barnes (September 14, 2011 - May 13, 2021) was an American child victim of measles.

Early Life[]

The son of police officer Tina and wedding planner Tyson, Barnes was a happy toddler who enjoyed playing with his big sister, Sandra (born 2006) and the family bluetick coonhound, Bruno. On some occasions, however, he and the dog didn't get along; Bruno attacked Anthony once when he was one, and another time when he was six.

Measle Diagnosis and Death[]

In 2020, at age 9, Barnes was diagnosed with measles. He reportedly didn't pay much attention to it and continued to enjoy life as if everything was okay. Barnes died on May 13, 2021, four months and one day short of his 10th birthday. He is survived by his parents and his sister.

On the day after Barnes' death, his parents started an online measle treatment donation organization in memory of Anthony.
