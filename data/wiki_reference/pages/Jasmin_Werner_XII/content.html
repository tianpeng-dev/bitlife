Jasmin Werner XII (4742-after 4798) was a Japanese jeweler.

Early life (4742-4780)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. She was the only child of Jasmin Werner XI and her ex-boyfriend Sora Imai.

In 4760, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4780-4798)[]

In 4780, Werner became a sales associate for Heirloom Jewelry.

In 4786, Werner was promoted to assistant manager.

In 4792, Werner was promoted to store manager.

Werner was alive in 4798, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4760 to 4761, Werner dated a student named Riku Matsumoto (4742-after 4798). They had a daughter, Jasmin Werner XIII.
