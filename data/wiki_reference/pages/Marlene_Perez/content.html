Marlene Perez (April 4, 1987 - April 2, 2023) was a Nicaraguan hustler and scam artist.

Early life[]

Marlene was born in Leon, Nicaragua to insurance agent Ada Mendez (born 1959) and retired mailman Santiago Perez (1923 - 1992). She had a pet cat named Raven (1986 - 2001). Santiago died when Marlene was five years old, leaving her to be raised by her mother and her stepfather, policy analyst Edward Nygma (born 1965). Her stepbrother and Edward's son is Alejandro (born 1989), a salesman for Sanchez Jewelers.

Personal life[]

Perez met future salesman Omar Cruz (born 1987) at her first middle school dance. They had their first daughter, Olga (born 2004) when they were seventeen. Two years later, they had a second daughter, Xiomara (born 2006), and they eloped shortly after. Marlene gave birth to their third daughter, Concepcion, in 2007 while in prison. Following Marlene's history of legal issues, she and Omar divorced in 2013.

Death[]

Perez died on April 2, 2023 from a prolonged illness, two days shy of her 36th birthday. She is survived by her mother, her daughters, and her stepbrother.
