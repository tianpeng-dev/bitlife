Not to be confused with Eberhard Werner or his other namesakes.

Eberhard Werner XVI (4552-after 4608) was a Japanese mailman.

Early life (4552-4590)[]

Werner was born in Tokyo. He is the only child of Jasmin Werner IX and his ex-boyfriend Hiroo Tanenaka.

In 4570, Werner graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (4590-4608)[]

In 4590, Werner became a junior mail carrier for Japan Post Holdings.

In 4593, Werner was promoted to mail carrier.

In 4596, Werner was promoted to senior mail carrier.

Werner was alive in 4608, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 4570 to 4571, Werner dated an unemployed woman named Yasuko Fujita (4552-after 4608). They had a son, Eberhard Werner XVII.
