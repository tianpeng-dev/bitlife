Leo Statham XLIII
(6950-7024) was an Austrian-Kazakh former actor, politician, and exorcist.

Early life (6950-6975)
[
]
Statham was born in Salzburg, Austria. He was the youngest of five children born to
Wolfgang Statham
and the youngest of four born to his third partner Marie Steiner.
In 6956, Statham enrolled at St. Vanessa’s Primary School.
In 6958, Statham started taking acting lessons.
In 6960, Statham enrolled at Schwarz Middle School.
In 6964, Statham enrolled at Vitality Preparatory School.
In 6968, Statham graduated from secondary school. He emigrated to Honolulu, Hawaii. He enrolled at the University of Hawaii.
From 6969 to 7008, Statham went to the gym annually.
In 6973, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science. He enrolled at William S. Richardson School of Law.
In 6976, Statham graduated with a Doctorate of Jurisprudence.
Acting career (6976-6977)
[
]
In 6976, Statham played a gas station attendant in
No Nation for Young Women
. He became famous.
In 6977, Statham played Cameron Bell in
Whitewashers
.
First political career (6977-6997)
[
]
In 6977, Statham ran for election to the Honolulu City Council and beat Jimmy Kent.
In 6981, Statham ran for reelection and beat Luther Lawrence.
In 6985, Statham ran for Mayor of Honolulu as a Democrat and beat Republican candidate Apollo Joyner.
In 6989, Statham ran for reelection and beat Republican candidate Goober Campbell.
In 6993, Statham ran for Governor of Hawaii and beat Republican candidate Imani Dickey-Nelson.
In 6997, Statham did not run for reelection.
Second political career (7008-7012)
[
]
In 7008, Statham ran for a second term as Governor of Hawaii and beat Libertarian candidate Amber Mendez. He bought a pastoral haunted rambler, a charming haunted cabin, and a haunted craftsman home.
In 7009, Statham bought a charming haunted cottage and a charming haunted farm.
In 7010, Statham bought a majestic haunted mansion and a majestic haunted lodge.
In 7011, Statham bought a lovely haunted duplex and a charming haunted equestrian property.
Exorcism career (7012-7013)
[
]
In 7012, Statham became an exorcist for Honolulu Community Church.
In 7013, Statham exorcised 10 ghosts from his haunted houses, including the ghosts of
Faber Fernandez
,
Lauren Gulch
, and
Virgil Kovacevic
. He resigned shortly after.
Later life
[
]
In 7014, Statham emigrated to Oranjestad, Aruba.
In 7017, Statham emigrated to Astana, Kazakhstan.
Death
[
]
In 7024, Statham died of a stroke. His funeral was held in Astana.
