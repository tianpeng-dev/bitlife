Liling Agessy (née Zedong; July 10, 1887 - July 29, 1974) was a Canadian and American professional volleyball player who spent most of her career playing for the Vancouver Vans from 1905 to 1919. She also played for the American teams Boulder Brownies from 1919 to 1924 and the Detroit Material Girls from 1924 to 1928. She won many awards throughout her career in both her home country and the United States.

After retiring from volleyball, Agessy spent twenty years working as a marriage counselor from the early 1930s to the early 1950s. She then embarked on a political career spanning the last two decades of her life, whereas she served as a school board director and the mayor of her city.
