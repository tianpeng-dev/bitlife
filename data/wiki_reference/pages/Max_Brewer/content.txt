Maxwell Thomas Brewer
(September 7, 1992 - August 12, 2021), also known by the stage name
Brewster Max
, was an American disk jockey. Originally wanting to be a singer, he became a DJ after being mercilessly rejected by labels. In 2018, he decided to take a break from his career to join the Marines; he wanted to become a war hero to lift his chances of becoming a singer. He died in combat in Iraq in August of 2021, not long before the end of the War in Afghanistan.
