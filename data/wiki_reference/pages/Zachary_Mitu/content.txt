Early life (3154-3176)
[
]
Mitu was born in the United Kingdom. In 3156, he was adopted by
Jennifer Mitu
. He was raised in Cardiff, Wales.
In 3172, Mitu enrolled at Cardiff University. He was on the soccer team for a year before graduating in 3176 with a BS in Psychology.
Sports career (3176-3191)
[
]
In 3176, Mitu joined Arson Hall FC, the same team as
his grandmother
, as a goalkeeper. He won five championships, two Golden Gloves, and a Ballon d’Or while on the team.
In 3191, Mitu retired.
Counseling career (3192-3210)
[
]
In 3192, Mitu became a marriage counselor for Corona Health and Addiction.
Mitu was alive in 3210, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and child
[
]
From 3172 to 3173, Mitu dated a student named Eva Lawley (b. 3154). They had a daughter,
Mildred Mitu
.
