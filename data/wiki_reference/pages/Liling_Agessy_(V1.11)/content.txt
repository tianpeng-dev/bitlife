Liling Agessy
(née
Zedong
; July 10, 1897 - July 29, 1984) was a Canadian and American professional volleyball player who spent most of her career playing for the Vancouver Vans from 1915 to 1929. She also played for the American teams Boulder Brownies from 1929 to 1934 and the Detroit Material Girls from 1934 to 1938. She won many awards throughout her career in both her home country and the United States.
After retiring from volleyball, Agessy spent twenty years working as a marriage counselor from the early 1940s to the early 1960s. She then embarked on a political career spanning the last two decades of her life, whereas she served as a school board director and the mayor of her city.
