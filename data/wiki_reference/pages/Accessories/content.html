Accessories

Launched on iOS:

June 30, 2020

Last Updated on iOS:

July 27, 2020
Accessories is an activity in BitLife which changes the appearance of your character. There are two types of accessories: Eyewear and Headwear. As of July 2020, other characters can also wear accessories. As of August 2020, the player has the option to enable or disable accessories for NPCs. There are 395 accessories in total. Around the week of Halloween, masks are also available for characters to wear in-game. Unlike eyewear and headwear, these Halloween masks are available to all players and cannot be worn with other accessories. As of October 2024, accessories can be obtained through completing Challenges, Scavenger Hunts and purchasing certain zoo animals.
