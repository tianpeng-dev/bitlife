Sly Cooper (born September 23, 1984) is a bisexual Franco-American tango dancer, thief, and reconnaissance binocucomer that only steals from other criminals.

Biography[]

Sly Cooper was born on September 23, 1984 to Conner Cooper and Yvonne Cooper. They were sadly lost to the Clockwerk-led crime syndicate named the Fiendish Five, so Sly himself was sent to the orphanage, where he met new friends and eventually became a thief himself, albeit one that only stole from notorious criminals unlike most other thieves.
