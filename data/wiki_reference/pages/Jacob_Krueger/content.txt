Early life (2593-2623)
[
]
Krueger was born in Jacksonville, Florida. His parents were
Evchen Klum
and her ex-boyfriend Casey Krueger. He was the captain of the Kayak Prep wrestling team. He went to the University of North Florida and graduated in 2615 with a BA in English. He went to the Graduate School at UNF and graduated in 2617. He went to UNF Law School and passed the Florida bar exam in 2620. He went to the Coggin College of Business and graduated in 2623.
Bartending career (2623-2659)
[
]
In 2623, Krueger became a junior bartender for Kayla’s Restaurant.
In 2626, Krueger was promoted to bartender.
In 2629, Krueger was promoted to senior bartender.
In 2659, Krueger retired.
Death
[
]
In 2666, Krueger died of old age. He was cremated and his ashes were scattered at a museum.
Relationship and child
[
