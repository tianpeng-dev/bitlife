When was it added to iOS?

December 19, 2019
Vietnam is a country that was on BitLife in December 2019. Like China, Vietnam is also one-party country but unlike China, your character that live in Vietnam still can working as School Board Director and Mayor of each cities. Its default cities are the capital, Hanoi, along with Biên Hòa, Cần Thơ, Đà Nẵng, Hai Phong, and Ho Chi Minh City.


In December 2019 on iOS; new countries were added. Along with Vietnam; there was also Austria, Bahamas, Costa Rica, DR Congo, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Kuwait, Lebanon, Mongolia, Nepal, and Ukraine. This was added in the Friends Update.

Name List[]

Vietnam is one of the two countries where people's names are backwards (last name goes first) for example, "Hoàng Tiên", the others being South Korea and Hungary.
