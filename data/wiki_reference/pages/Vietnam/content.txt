country

Like China, Vietnam is also one-party country but unlike China, your character that live in Vietnam still can working as School Board Director and Mayor of each cities. Its default cities are the capital, Hanoi, along with Biên Hòa, Cần Thơ, Đà Nẵng, Hai Phong, and Ho Chi Minh City.
