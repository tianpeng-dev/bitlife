Melissa Cameron
(Russian: Мелисса Кэмерон), born in Toronto, is an associate of the Stanislavovich Family.
As a kid she was a wholiganka, interested in Russian wholiganism early in life, writing haikus on street signs and then stealing them often. She had fallen in love with her classmate, Sadie Davis, whom she would often run off and vandalize random apartment buildings with.
As a teenager, she fled the police in a Toronto mall after she reportedly locked an unnamed “socialite” into a bathroom, and then immediately after the locking, shoplifted a Chrysler Pacifica.
