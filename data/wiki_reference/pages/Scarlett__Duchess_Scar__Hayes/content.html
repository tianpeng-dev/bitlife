Scarlett Hayes (January 29, 1980 - July 5, 2021) was an American pop star and murderer who preformed with The Sacremento Strippers from 2001 to 2014.

Early Life[]

She was born to Payton Hayes and a brilliant doctor who donated his sperm. She started learning the bass guitar when she was 6 and mastered it at 14. She was a good sister to their half siblings, Jenna and Reggie. She came out as gay in 1993, which came with loads of unconditional support.

Music Career[]

Scarlett signed a 4-year contract with Cash Money Beats that included $41,000 in advance in 1999 under the name "Duchess Scar". She then Produced her first album, "I Hope He Knows", to moderate reviews. The next year she produced "Pure Cocaine", which flopped horribly. In 2001, She joined the band, The Sacremento Strippers, as a bassist. The band went on an 18-week tour in 2003. She retired from the group in 2014.

Personal Life[]

Scarlett started dating Sandra Licker in 2001. Scarlett proposed in 2004 with a $690,400 ring. She said yes. The had a church wedding and went to Bangkok, Thailand for the Honeymoon in 2006.

Scarlett entered the lottery in 2005 and won $70,763,456.

Scarlett and Sandra bought a house on Sacramento Hights Avenue and a Lincoln Continental. They then adopted Gen Fu, a 2 year old from Hong Kong whose parents had addiction issues.

Scarlett was sentenced to 1 year in prison after beating one of her stalkers. As soon as she got out, she pushed her wife off a cliff. She attempted to run from the police but was caught and convicted of Sandra's murder. She was given the death penalty and was executed using the electric chair on July 5, 2021. No one went to her funeral.

Gravestone[]

"A glamorous and astute young woman, Scarlett's years of dedication paid off when she joined The Sacremento Strippers as a bassist. Friends say she was an abject and detestable person."
