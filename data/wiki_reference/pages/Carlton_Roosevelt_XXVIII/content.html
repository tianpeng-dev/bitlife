Carlton Roosevelt XXVIII (3139-3253) was a Canadian-Romanian hairdresser, actor, and convicted murderer.

Early life (3139-3157)[]

Roosevelt was born in Halifax, Nova Scotia. He was the eldest of three children born to Carlton Roosevelt XXVII and his ex-girlfriend Alicia Herbert-Page.

In 3157, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school.

Hairdressing career (3157-3173)[]

In 3157, Roosevelt became a hairdresser for Unity Salon and Spa.

In 3173, Roosevelt bit both of his siblings. He bought a haunted palace which was built in 3084.

Prison sentence (3173-3208)[]

In 3173, Roosevelt impaled a club promoter on a wooden stake. He was convicted of murder and sentenced to 40 years in prison. He served his sentence at Flynn Penitentiary. He bit a prisoner named Vito.

In 3208, Roosevelt was released on parole. He impaled a drunk and a furry on wooden stakes. He emigrated to Transylvania, Romania.

Acting career (3210-3242)[]

From 3210 to 3211, Roosevelt played Cameron Budd in the TV show Mitt’s Creek.

From 3211 to 3217, Roosevelt played Beavis in Glass Slippers.

In 3218, Roosevelt played Clifford Glasscock in the film Trust Me.

In 3219, Roosevelt played Preston Stoner in Headstone.

From 3219 to 3220, Roosevelt played Daevon Kim in the TV show In for a Penny.

From 3220 to 3225, Roosevelt played Thigpen in Rubbish.

From 3225 to 3226, Roosevelt played Chevy Harrison in Annie McBeal.

In 3227, Roosevelt played Harry Hughes in the film Paranormal Smack-tivity.

In 3228, Roosevelt played Andrew Moorehead in Spotless Sunshine on the Eternal Mind.

From 3228 to 3242, Roosevelt played Samantha Sylvester in Glee Club.

Death[]

In 3253, Roosevelt died of old age.
