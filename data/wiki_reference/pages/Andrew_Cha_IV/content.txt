Early life (3235-3253)
[
]
Cha was born in Seville, Spain. He was the youngest of four children born to
Andrew Cha III
and his first girlfriend Laura Campagna.
In 3238, Cha emigrated to London, England.
In 3241, Cha enrolled at Little Andrews Primary School.
In 3251, Cha enrolled at Seamen Preparatory School. He joined the soccer team.
In 3253, Cha graduated from secondary school.
Sports career (3253-3256)
[
]
In 3253, Cha joined Tottenham as a defender.
In 3256, Cha was released by Tottenham.
Trucking career (3257-3301)
[
]
In 3257, Cha became an apprentice trucker for Overture Trucking.
In 3259, Cha was promoted to trucker.
In 3301, Cha retired.
Retirement
[
]
Cha was alive in 3315, but his death date is unknown.
Relationship and children
[
]
