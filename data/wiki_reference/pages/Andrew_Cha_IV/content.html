Andrew Cha IV (3235-after 3315) was a Spanish-British soccer player and trucker.

Early life (3235-3253)[]

Cha was born in Seville, Spain. He was the youngest of four children born to Andrew Cha III and his first girlfriend Laura Campagna.

In 3238, Cha emigrated to London, England.

In 3241, Cha enrolled at Little Andrews Primary School.

In 3251, Cha enrolled at Seamen Preparatory School. He joined the soccer team.

In 3253, Cha graduated from secondary school.

Sports career (3253-3256)[]

In 3253, Cha joined Tottenham as a defender.

In 3256, Cha was released by Tottenham.

Trucking career (3257-3301)[]

In 3257, Cha became an apprentice trucker for Overture Trucking.

In 3259, Cha was promoted to trucker.

In 3301, Cha retired.

Retirement[]

Cha was alive in 3315, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

In 3245, Cha started dating a student named Elizabeth Nottingham (3235-after 3315), who became an insurance agent in 3254. They had five children:

  1. Ha-Eun Cha VIII (3252-after 3315), school psychologist
  2. Scarlett Cha VII (3253-after 3315), architect
  3. Emma Cha II (3254-after 3315), financial analyst
  4. Lily Cha (3255-after 3315), Royal Navy officer
  5. Chin Cha XIII
