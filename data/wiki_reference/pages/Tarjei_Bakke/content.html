Tarjei Bakke, Sr. (2007-April 12, 2121) was the King of Norway, who later became a drug dealer.

Prince of Ålesund and King of Norway (2007-2025)[]

Bakke was born the Prince of Ålesund in Trondheim. He was the younger of two children born to Monica, Queen of Norway and her husband Prince Eirik.

On April 12, 2025, Bakke briefly became Tarjei, King of Norway, before abdicating the throne.

Bakke dropped out of upper secondary school after he abdicated.

Drug dealing career (2025-2122)[]

In 2025, Bakke joined the Bone Breakers as a dealer. His nickname is Back Alley Lars.

Between 2025 and 2032, Bakke sold over €44,000 ($50,000) worth of ascetic.

In 2028, Bakke took the fall for a fellow dealer. He was sentenced to 3 years in prison, but escaped.

In 2030, Bakke was promoted to runner.

In 2036, Bakke took the fall for a lieutenant. He was sentenced to 11 years in prison, but appealed his sentence and had it reduced to 3 years.

In 2039, Bakke was released from prison. He was promoted to manufacturer.

In 2044, Bakke was promoted to lieutenant.

In 2050, Bakke took the fall for a dealer. He was sentenced to 15 years in prison.

In 2060, Bakke was released on parole.

In 2121, Bakke died of a stroke.

Relationships and children[]

From 2025 to 2026, Bakke dated a student named Linda Moen (2007-2090). They had a son, Tarjei Bakke, Jr. (2026-2108).

From 2108 to 2109, Bakke dated a runner for the Bone Breakers, Ellen “Bong Bong” Hauge (2070-2152). They had a son, Tarjei Bakke III.
