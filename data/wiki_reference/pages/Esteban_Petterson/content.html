Esteban Petterson (3993-after 4056) was a Swedish microbiologist and politician.

Early life (3993-4017)[]

Petterson was born in Malmö, Sweden. He is the youngest of three children born to Lucas Petterson and his ex-girlfriend Eva Camacho.

In 4011, Petterson graduated high school. He enrolled at Malmö University Faculty of Culture and Society, and graduated in 4015 with a BA in English.

In 4015, Petterson enrolled at Skåne School of Business. He graduated in 4017 with an MBA.

First microbiology career (4017-4036)[]

In 4017, Petterson became a junior microbiologist for Akerman Engineering. He was promoted to senior microbiologist before resigning in 4036.

Political career (4036-4040)[]

In 4036, Petterson ran for election to the Malmö School Board after his father announced his retirement. He beat Max Simonson and was inaugurated.

In 4040, Petterson did not run for reelection.

Second microbiology career (4041-4056)[]

In 4041, Petterson became a junior microbiologist for Akerman Engineering.

In 4047, Petterson was promoted to microbiologist.

In 4053, Petterson was promoted to senior microbiologist.

Petterson was alive in 4056, but his death date is unknown.

Marriage and child[]

In 4022, Petterson married a student named Olivia Persson (4004-4037). They had a son, Carl Petterson.
