Nellie Larsson (4132-after 4193) was a Swedish-Canadian politician and paralegal.

Early life (4132-4154)[]

Larsson was born in Malmö, Sweden. She is the younger of two children born to Henrik Larsson and his girlfriend Lilly Vikander.

In 4139, Larsson enrolled at Nutti Primary School.

In 4148, Larsson enrolled at Matt Qvist Secondary School.

In 4150, Larsson graduated from high school. She ran for election to the Malmö School Board, but lost to Ines Larsson. She enrolled at Malmö University and graduated in 4154 with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science.

Political career (4154-4158)[]

In 4154, Larsson ran for election to the Malmö School Board and beat Freja Dahlberg.

In 4158, Larsson did not run for reelection. She emigrated to Toronto, Ontario.

Paralegal career (4160-4193)[]

In 4160, Larsson became a legal secretary for Wright-Cook and Parsons.

In 4166, Larsson was promoted to legal records manager.

Larsson was alive in 4193, but her death date is unknown.

Relationships and children[]

From 4142 to 4158, Larsson dated a student named Carl Andersson (4132-after 4159), who became a reporter in 4154. They had a daughter, Tuva Andersson (4149-after 4193).

In 4158, Larsson started dating a policeman named Ryker Cook (4136-after 4193). They have a son, Francois Cook.
