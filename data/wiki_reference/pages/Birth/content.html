Birth happens when you click a button saying “New Life”. You will be assigned a randomly generated name with randomly generated stats unless you made a Custom character where their name and location is decided by the player. They may have siblings who are older, younger, or twin; such as siblings, half-siblings, adopted siblings (only in new Generations), or twin siblings. There are 3 stages of childhood: Birth, Infancy, and School. This can be cut short by diseases like Cancer if untreated, but usually it happens in adulthood. It is impossible for a baby to die at 0 unless they surrender, which can happen at any age. The youngest age characters can die is 2, due to a fatal disease (such as Cancer, Whooping cough) caught the previous year, or 1 in Iraq, Afghanistan, Venezuela, Pakistan and Syria, where a terrorist attack can kill a 1-year old, however both scenarios are extremely rare.

If starting a new life via the menu rather than the Age Up button, the player will be able to select their first and last names and their birthplace. Characters can have their own babies via Relationships or Hook-ups if they decide to keep the baby and the pregnancy does not end in miscarriage or stillbirth. Lower heath increases the chances of miscarriage or stillbirth.
