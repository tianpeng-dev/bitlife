Andi Roosevelt (3313-3347) was an American choreographer.

Early life (3313-3335)[]

Roosevelt was born in New York City. He was the youngest of three children born to Carlton Roosevelt XXXI and his ex-girlfriend Sara Rusu.

In 3318, Roosevelt enrolled at Fountainhead Elementary School.

In 3321, Roosevelt took acting classes.

In 3323, Roosevelt mastered the bass guitar.

In 3324, Roosevelt enrolled at Magnitude Middle School.

In 3327, Roosevelt enrolled at St. Addison’s High School.

In 3330, Roosevelt became the president of the St. Addison’s High School Drama Club.

In 3331, Roosevelt enrolled at Columbia University. He majors in dance and is a member of Alpha Epsilon Pi.

In 3335, Roosevelt graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Dance.

Career (3335-3347)[]

In 3335, Roosevelt became a junior choreographer for Aria Dance Troupe.

In 3341, Roosevelt was promoted to choreographer.

In 3347, Roosevelt was promoted to senior choreographer. He and his girlfriend were murdered by their daughter.
