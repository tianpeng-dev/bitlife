Becky Snatch (3347-after 3383) was an American receptionist.

Early life (3347-3370)[]

Snatch was born in New York City. She was the only child of Genesis Roosevelt and her boyfriend Darius Snatch.

In 3352, Snatch enrolled at Payton Scrooge Elementary School.

In 3358, Snatch enrolled at St. Wade’s Middle School.

In 3361, Snatch enrolled at Devin Rogers High School.

In 3364, Snatch was sent to an orphanage after murdering her parents.

In 3365, Snatch graduated from high school.

In 3366, Snatch enrolled at Empire State University. She majored in history.

In 3370, Snatch graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Historical Studies.

Career (3370-3383)[]

In 3370, Snatch became a receptionist for Paramount Communications.

Snatch was alive in 3383, but her death date is unknown.
