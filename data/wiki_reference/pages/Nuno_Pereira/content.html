Early life (2007-2025)[]

Nuno Pereira was born on December 5, 2007 in Lisbon in the family of a 69-year-old policeman and a 24-year-old wedding planner. His family was quite wealthy. His father Gonzalo Pereira (1938 - 2025) and mother Carlota Jesus (1983 - 2025). In 2012, when Nuno was 5 years old, his parents divorced. At the age of 6, he went to elementary school, where he made many friends. When he was 7 years old (in 2014), his brother Francisco Jesus (2014 - 2018) was born. He also had a turtle named Goober. In 2015, his mother married Diogo Carvalho. At the age of 10, he began dating Sabine Dias (his future wife). In 2017, Pereira's sister Catarina Carvalho (born in 2017) was born. In 2018, when Nuno Pereira was 11 years old, his younger brother Francisco Jesus died at the age of 4 due to whooping cough. It was a huge loss for Nuno. Then everything was fine in the boy's life (until 2025): his stepfather was friends with him, Nuno spent time with friends, everything was fine. But in 2025, first his 87-year-old father Gonzalo Pereira died of old age, and then his 42-year-old mother died under a landslide. Nuno then fell into a terrible depression, there were thoughts of suicide. But he was able to overcome depression and entered the university.

Personal life[]

In 2030, at the age of 23, Nuno Pereira married Sabine Dias. Then, 2 years later, his son Rui Pereira (born in 2032) was born. Everything was fine in his personal life: he had a great relationship with his stepfather, sister, son, wife, visited his friends when he had time. And in general, Nuno Pereira was lucky in his personal life.

Soccer career[]

At the age of 18, Nuno Pereira moved to Spain, hoping to join a football club. But he was not taken anywhere, and he moved to Germany. There, too, no team wanted to see him at home. He went to university and played for the local football team, and focused on his career. He performed well, and at the age of 23 he became a Wolfsburg player. He performed well there, scored in important moments, and was very good, although he did not win the championship in Germany.

Death[]

Nuno Pereira was flying on his private jet, but he sensed something was wrong, the plane began to fall, and Nuno Pereira simply accepted his fate. At the age of 30, Nuno Pereira died in a plane crash, his family was not on board, everything is fine with them. Rest in peace, Nuno Pereira.
