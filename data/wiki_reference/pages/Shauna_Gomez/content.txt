Early life

Shauna is a female who came into the world through artificial insemination in New York City in March 30, 1919. Her single mother was impregnated by a sperm load that was anonymously donated to a fertility clinic.
In January 1921, her mother married Andre Burger.
At the age of 5, she enrolls Atticus Ho Elementary School in September 1, 1924.
In August 30, 1929 before The Great Depression impacts New York, she went to her first school dance for her birthday and tried to take Steven Ramos, she was rejected by him.
In October 25, 1930, she enrolled middle school, and a day later, her dog Ace died peacefully.
In January 8, 1934, Shauna enrolled high school, and she learned how to use guns during weapon training.
In August 31, 1937, Shauna graduated from high school and her mother bought a Ford Model B, colored in White.

Career
[
]
In February 10, 1938, Shauna enlisted the military after she graduated from high school. In February 20, 1938, one of the military officers gave Shauna a M1 Garand Sniper. In April 13, 1938, Shauna was sent into an unstable guerilla-controlled region in Uruguay, and stepped on a mine and her kneecap was seared, her chest was mutilated, and her clitoris was fried. In April 16, 1938, she decided to work out on a gym and she decided to get some med kits to recover her from burnt skin.
In May 27, 1941, Shauna brings her military car and her sniper rifle to a treacherous region of Haiti, where the Japanese attempt to fully control the nation. Shauna managed to take the Japanese down with her sniper rifle, having a bunch of them killed. She was later rewarded a medal for her heroism and wore it with pride.
In May 18, 1942, Shauna and her military officers go on aeroplanes to Mount Fuji and attempt to stop the country from invading land, but Shauna suddenly unable to sleep due to the time change so she almost counted sheep and suddenly an explosion appears, one of the planes crashed and Shauna could not even confront the Japanese military forces. So Shauna decided to return home from the Japanese Empire.
In November 3, 1943, Shauna was sent to Angola during the Battle of Angola campaign, most of the military soldiers did well without any injuries, but some such as Shauna stepped on a mine and she was exploded. She later got into the med bay for 2 weeks before she can fight the war again.
In August 9, 1950, Shauna started a conflict between the United States and Kuwait, and she stepped on a mine again although she completely hunted a large amount of hostile people in a single shot, she managed to do it by herself and she was rewarded with a military knife but she was only rewarded with it because she didn't flag the mines by herself.
In January 1, 1955, Shauna was sent to Lebanon by an accident but cleared several mines and shot down the Russians attempting to eradicate Lebanese people. She was rewarded with a medal in a day later.
