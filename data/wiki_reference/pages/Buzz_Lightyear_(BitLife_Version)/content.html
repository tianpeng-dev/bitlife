Buzz Lightyear (March 16, 1923 - November 28, 2023) was an American astronaut, space ranger, United States Air Force Captain, and commercial pilot.

Early life[]

Lightyear was born in San Francisco, California to Miranda and Harry Lightyear (both 1882 - 1957). His mother was a receptionist and his father was an insurance agent. He had an older brother, Grayson (1920 - 2006), a trucker with a degree in archeology.
