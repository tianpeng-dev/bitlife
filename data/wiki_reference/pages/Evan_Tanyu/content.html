Evan Tanyu (May 5th, 1913 - June 9th, 1975) was an Japanese-American Republican Politican who was the President of United States of America from 1965 to 1975. He got assassinated in June 9th, 1975 when russian assassin shot him to dead via sniper rifle.

Childhood (0-17)[]

Evan Tanyu was born to Eliza Tanyu in San Francisco on May 5th, 1913 after his father disappeared.

Evan started Elementery School on September, 1918 at 5.

Evan studied about historical US Presidents at 6.

He had a first kiss with a girl named Alexis Lawson at age of 12.

He got driver's license after passing the driving test at 16.

University (18-22)[]

He enrolled into University with Political Science as his main course 4 months after graduation from High School.

On May 12th, 1933, Evan was the one of the victims of the great 1933 California University San Francisco Campus fire. He got almost burned during escape from the burning building. He later transfer to Michigan University the following day.

He graduated on 1935 and enrolled Law School.

Law School (22-26)[]

He saw African-American female student being harassed by racist people, He intervene to stop them but he got beaten up by them. Thankfully, Campus Police came and arrest them for Assault and Racial Harrassment.

He graduated on 1939 and started focus on Governorship of California
