Matteo Spinelli (April 28, 1925 - August 14, 2021) was an Italian chef from Florence. He was also a one-time, non-convicted killer of an enemy, who was suspected to be a killer himself.

Early life[]

Matteo was born to Analia Nitti (1897 - 1975), a massage therapist, and policy analyst Ercole Spinelli (1899 - 1984). His older brother was Gabriele (1921 - 2011), a librarian from Florence Public Library.

Personal Life[]

Killing of enemy[]

In 1961, Pasquale Ferro, a friend of Spinelli's since age 19, became his enemy for undisclosed reasons. It seemed to anger Matteo. He performed a drive-by shooting on Ferro, and he died. He was never arrested for the crime, as the police believed he had helped them take down a serial killler, whose profile appeared to match Ferro's description.

Death[]

Spinelli died on August 14, 2021. He is survived by his children and grandchildren.
