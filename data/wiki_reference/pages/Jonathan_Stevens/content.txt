Early Life
[
]
Jon Stevens was born to Peyton Stevens, an internal auditor (Aged 44), and Erica Stevens, a police officer (aged 22) on February 15th, 1998 in Las Vegas, United States. His first murder was in 2004, where he was only 6 years old when he killed a boy by biting him on the jaw after he called his mother a prostitute. Stevens graduated from Karen Seaman-Kemper Highschool in 2016, the same year he joined the Mafia.
Joining the Mafia
[
]
At the age of 18, Stevens joined the Mafia as an associate after hanging out at their club for 32 days straight. At the age of 21, Stevens became a “made man” after killing a male surfer dude. Less than a year after his first promotion, Stevens got promoted to Caporegime/Captain at the age of 22 after his first son, Edward, was born. In the year 2030, five years after several of his syndicate members went missing, Stevens got promoted to the Underboss of the Garibaldi Family.
The Four Missing Garibaldi’s Incident
[
]
In 2025, four people, who were later found to be members of the Garibaldi Family (Childish Casiraghi, Annabelle Fannuci, One-Tit Alessia , and Sonny Green) went missing after Stevens’ mother got killed by his own syndicate. According to reports, they dropped his mother in a vat filled with Battery Acid. It is believed that Stevens murdered the syndicate members as retaliation to them murdering his mother.
Later Life
[
]
Stevens would go on to father 5 kids with Aliveni and had four grandchildren. His syndicate was notorious to the point Hollywood Producers wanted to make a limited series on them called “The Angry Housewives of the Garibaldi Family”, to which he agreed, calling it a brilliant idea. His crimes seemed to decline in his 70s and would eventually cease committing crimes all together due to his age. He outlived his wife, who passed away at the age of 110, as well as outliving two of his children. Stevens was described as a “caring” and “loving” father, husband, and grandfather, despite his crimes. Stevens passed away peacefully in his sleep on April 12th, 2106, at the age of 108. It has been revealed that he killed over 50 people.
