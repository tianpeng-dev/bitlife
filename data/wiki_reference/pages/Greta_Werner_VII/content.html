Greta Werner VII (4921-5010) was a Canadian-Mexican forensic scientist.

Early life (4921-4943)[]

Werner was born in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan. She was the youngest of three children born to Eberhard Werner XXVI and the younger of two born to his third girlfriend, Isabella Poulin.

In 4927, Werner enrolled at Lifeblood Primary School.

In 4933, Werner enrolled at St. Chichi’s Middle School.

In 4936, Werner enrolled at Nana Simard Secondary School.

In 4939, Werner enrolled at the University of Saskatchewan. She majored in criminal justice and was a member of Alpha Chi Omega.

Career (4943-4966)[]

In 4943, Werner graduated with a BS in Criminal Justice. She became a crime scene technician for the Saskatoon Police Service.

In 4949, Werner was promoted to forensic investigator.

In 4966, Werner retired and emigrated to Guadalajara, Mexico.

Death[]

In 5010, Werner died of old age.
