Tarjei Bakke III (2109-2201) was a Norwegian alternative metal singer.

Early life (2109-2128)[]

Bakke was born in Trondheim, Norway. His parents were Tarjei Bakke and his second girlfriend Ellen “Bong Bong” Hauge.

In 2122, Bakke enrolled at St. Marie’s Lower Secondary School.

In 2125, Bakke enrolled at Thomas Berge Upper Secondary School.

In 2128, Bakke graduated from upper secondary school. He emigrated to Detroit, Michigan. He created his Twitch channel.

Career (2132-2162)[]

In 2132, Bakke signed with Delta Studios as an alternative singer. He released his debut alternative metal album, Songs for the Young People.

Between 2133 and 2140, Bakke earned over $100,000 from promoting products.

In 2135, Bakke released his second album, Discombobulated and Isolated.

In 2137, Bakke streamed Fortnite on Twitch, and the stream went viral.

In 2139, Bakke released his third album, Blood Agave Sex Magik.

In 2144, Bakke released his fourth album, Final Second, which went gold in 2159.

In 2157, Bakke’s Twitch channel reached 10,000,000 followers.

In 2162, Bakke retired. He emigrated back to Norway.

Death[]

In 2201, Bakke died of a stroke.
