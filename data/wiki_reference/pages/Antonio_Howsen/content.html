Antonio Howsen (born 1960), also known as Tony Howzer, is an American makeup artist. He is the younger brother of restaurateur Donald Howsen and football player-turned-Marine Derek Howsen.

Early life[]

Antonio was born in Arlington County, Virginia in 1960 to author Dennis Howsen (1921 - 1994) and magician Camille Howsen (1924 - 2012).

Career[]

Between 1978 and 1979, Howsen got a job at Borchlet Film Institute as a makeup artist.

Personal life[]

In the late 1970s, Howsen met Brooklyn "Brook" Collins (born 1960), a now-retired enlisted sailor in the US Navy. They would often go out on boat trips as dates. Antonion also used his job experience to help Brook with her makeup. They married by the sea in 1980 and had three children together, Cody (born 1983), Maddie (born 1985), and Sierra (born 1987). After 25 years of marriage, they divorced in 2005.
