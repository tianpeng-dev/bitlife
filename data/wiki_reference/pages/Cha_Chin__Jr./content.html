Cha Chin, Jr. (2930-after 2976) was a South Korean-Australian pornographer.

Early life (2930-2948)[]

Cha was born in Gangnam, Seoul. He was the younger of two children born to Yu Kok Sun and her boyfriend Cha Chin, Sr.

In 2945, Cha enrolled at Maestro Upper Secondary School.

In 2948, Cha graduated from upper secondary school.

Career (2948-2976)[]

In 2948, Cha briefly worked as a porn writer for Destiny Erotica. He emigrated to Townsville, Queensland. He became a porn actor for Terrestrial Erotica.

Cha was alive in 2976, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

In 2947, Cha started dating a student named Han Oh Ae (2930-2976), who became a wedding planner in 2949. They had three children:

  1. Cha Chin III (2948-after 2976), hairdresser
  2. Ha-Eun Cha I (2950-after 2976), photographer
  3. Sung Cha
