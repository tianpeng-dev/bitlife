John Paul Yousef Al-Assad, more formally known as King John I in the Assadian Kingdom of the Realm cult and more informally known as John Paul during his life, was a British actor, politician, and the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 2088 to his eventual defeat in the 2108 Prime Ministerial election.

Origins[]

John Paul was born as the eldest son of Aland Al-Assad Jr. and his wife, Maisie Ali. Growing up, his father was very liberal compared to his more conservative father (and John Paul's grandfather), Aland al-Assad. This caused John Paul to be more lenient on Christian ideals than his grandfather was. At an early age, John Paul was inspired by his grandfather and father's positions as Mayor of Hull and his grandfather's position in the East Yorkshire Council Chair. John Paul had four elder sisters, which his eldest sister, Isla, held the title of "The Custodian of the Crown" until John Paul was born.

When John Paul started primary school, he met Frederick "Freddie" Ashford, who would become his best friend one year later. During his middle school years, John Paul had a keen interest with performing as the lead role during his school's numerous plays, which led mahy, including his parents, believing that John Paul could achieve a career in acting. He also loved to play the drums and sing, prompting his father to say that his son is, "...like a gift from heaven. My goodness, that boy can act and sing."

Political Career[]

Immediately after he finished graduate school, John Paul worked as an Investigator and then quit that job to unexpectedly win the spot as Mayor of Hull. During his reign, John Paul fought for the rights of disabled people, provided more accessible structures and devices for disabled people, made people thank others at least once per day, and forced prisoners to listen to Taylor Swift. His approval rating was exceedingly high, and he soon ran for a spot in the East Yorkshire Council Chair, which he won. After one term in the Council Chair, John Paul decided to become the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom. It was always a childhood dream of his - even when his time as mayor was up, he unsuccessfully lost against the Conservative candidate in the election.

Suddenly, news broke out - John Paul was elected as the new Prime Minister with 57% of the vote. Affectionately called "Prime JP" by younger people in his family and by the media, John Paul pushed for more liberal reforms in contrast to his Conservative predecessor. He was the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom for 20 years, until his mental health started deteriorating and he lost the Election of 2108.

Acting Career[]

John Paul started out as a supporting role in the movie, "Buckshot". Unfortunately, this was his first and last role in a movie - and his last appearance to the media.

Death[]

While filming for Buckshot on December 7, 2113, John Paul had to perform a scene involving a crocodile. Unfortunately, the crocodile attacked John Paul, killing him instantly as his body had become weakened past his prime. Leaders and civilians all over the world mourned him, and the government of the United Kingdom paid tribute to him by lowering their flag at half mass. The British monarch also gave out a speech praising John Paul for his commitment to the United Kingdom, and his sudden, tragic death.
