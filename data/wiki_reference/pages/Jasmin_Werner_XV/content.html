Jasmin Werner XV (4800-4835) was an American-German nurse.

Early life (4800-4824)[]

Werner was born in Buffalo, New York. She was the only child of Jasmin Werner XIV and her ex-boyfriend Lucas Hayes.

In 4811, Werner enrolled at St. Virginia’s Middle School.

In 4814, Werner enrolled at Galore High School.

In 4818, Werner enrolled at the State University of New York at Buffalo. She majors in nursing and is a member of Alpha Chi Omega.

In 4822, Werner graduated from university with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing. She emigrated to Turin, Italy. She enrolled at the University of Turin.

Career (4824-4827, 4834-4835)[]

In 4824, Werner graduated with a Doctor of Nursing Practice. She became an associate nurse at Turin General Hospital.

In 4827, Werner emigrated to Buenos Aires, Argentina.

In 4833, Werner emigrated to Toulouse, France; Pamplona, Spain; Recife, Brazil; and Dortmund, Germany.

In 4834, Werner became an associate nurse at Dortmund General Hospital.

Murder[]

In 4835, Otello Werner IV murdered Jasmin XV with a set of plastic vampire teeth.

Relationship and children[]

From 4810 to 4822, Werner dated a Czech-American student named Eda Cermak (4800-after 4867). They had five children, Eberhard Werner XXIV (4817-4858), Otello Werner IV, Jasmin Werner XVI (4819-after 4867), Jessica Cermak (4820-after 4867), and Benjamin Cermak (4821-after 4867).

From 4822 to 4833, Werner dated a student named Carmine LaSpina (4792-after 4833), who became a translator in 4824. They had 10 children, Roberto (4823-4831), Sammy (4824-4837), Stefano (4825-after 4867), Ricky (4826-after 4867), Kendrick (4827-after 4867), Claire (4829-4836), Luna (4830-after 4867), Saverio (4831-after 4867), Teresa (4832-after 4867), and Sergio LaSpina (4833-after 4867).
