Ambre Marie (2901-after 2958) was a French-British soccer player and pilot.

Early life (2901-2923)[]

Marie was born in Lyon, France. Her parents were Ali Marie and his ex-girlfriend Laura Marie.

In 2919, Marie enrolled at the École Centrale de Lyon. She was on the rugby team. She graduated in 2923 with a Diplôme d’Ingénieur.

Sports career (2923-2939)[]

In 2923, Marie emigrated to Manchester, England and joined City FC as a defender.

In 2928, Marie was traded to Birmingham.

In 2939, Marie retired.

Pilot career (2940-2958)[]

In 2940, Marie became a pilot trainee for Richardson Air.

In 2946, Marie was promoted to copilot.

In 2952, Marie was promoted to pilot.

Marie was alive in 2958, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2919 to 2921, Marie dated a student named Adam Henry (2898-after 2958). They had a daughter, Lilou Henry.
