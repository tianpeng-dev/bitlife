Francois Cook (4159-after 4199) was a Canadian grocer and convicted murderer.

Early life (4159-4176)[]

Cook was born in Toronto, Ontario. He is the younger of two children born to Nellie Larsson and the only child born to her second boyfriend Ryker Cook.

In 4165, Cook enrolled at Evelyn Anderson Primary School.

In 4169, Cook gave his enemy, Mathis Williams (b. 4158), a Rat King figurine, a Sugarplum Fairy figurine, alcohol-free eggnog, roasted chestnuts, and a DogLife sticker wreath for Christmas.

In 4171, Cook enrolled at Ambrosia Middle School.

In 4174, Cook enrolled at St. Bentley’s Secondary School.

Prison sentences (4176-4193)[]

In 4176, Cook attempted to murder his girlfriend by putting an elephant laxative in her smoothie. He was convicted of attempted murder and sentenced to one year of juvenile detention and 14 years in prison, but escaped from Jones Juvenile Detention Facility. He was sent back to juvenile detention after a second failed attempt to murder his girlfriend.

In 4177, Cook appealed his conviction and was released. He attempted to murder his girlfriend a third time, but failed and was convicted of attempted murder. As he was now an adult, he was sentenced to life in prison. He spent 14 years at Toronto Supermax.

In 4191, Cook appealed his sentence and was released. He successfully murdered his girlfriend with an elephant laxative. He was convicted of murder and sentenced to life in prison. He is held at Toronto Supermax.

In 4193, Cook escaped from prison in a riot.

Career (4194-4199)[]

In 4194, Cook became an apprentice grocer for Adams Grocery.

In 4196, Cook was promoted to grocer.

Cook was alive in 4199, but his death date is unknown.

Relationships and child[]

From 4169 to 4175, Cook dated a student named Avery Simard (4159-after 4193).

In 4175, Cook started dating a Puerto Rican-born student named Ariana Santiago (4159-4191). They had a daughter, Sydney Cook.

In 4193, Cook had two one-night stands with a jeweler named Romy Pickler (4150-after 4193).
