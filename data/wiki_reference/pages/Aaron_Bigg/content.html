Aaron Bigg (6 August 1917-27 May 2026) was an Army Enliste and WWII Veteran.

History[]

He was born 6 August 1917. He was Bastard. Year later his parents ended their relationship.

In 1936 he enlisted in Army. He participated in WWII, including the Normandy landings.

In 1940 he become going with Mary Hooper,

In 1942 he was father for first time for Wendy Bigg.

In 1947 he and Mary married in church and went to Mount Kilimanjaro. She deciced to change The last name to Mary Hooper-Bigg.

In 1951 he become the father of Paisley Bigg. In 1953 he become the father of Jasmin Bigg. In 1955 he become the father of Evelyn Bigg.

In 1959 his daughter Evelyn died from complications associated with whopping coughs. In this same year he become father for his first son named Nick Bigg.

In 1973 he become for the first time Grandpa for Isaac Oliver-Daniels (Wendy's son)

In 1979 he retired from the US Army after reaching the mandatory retirement age. He began earning retirement pay of $77,384 per year.

In 1981 he become suffering from Diabetes.

In 1983 he become a grandpa for Brock Powell (Paisley's son). In 1985 he become a grandpa for Brett Bigg (Nick's son). In 1987 he become a grandpa for Becky Bigg (Nick's daughter). In 1991 he become a grandpa for Dick Bigg (Nick's son)

In 2018 his daughter Wendy died while sleeping peacefully.

In 27 May 2026 he died while sleeping peacefully.
