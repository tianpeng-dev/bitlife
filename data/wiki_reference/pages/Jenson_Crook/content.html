Jenson Crook (3070-after October 6, 3115) was a British disk jockey.

Early life (3070-3088)[]

Crook was born in Cardiff, Wales. His parents were Annabelle Longstaff and her ex-boyfriend Billy Crook.

Crook graduated high school in 3088.

Career (3088-3115)[]

In 3088, Crook became a disk jockey for Acoustic Entertainment.

In 3095, Crook became famous.

Crook outlived his daughter, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 3088 to 3090, Crook dated an unemployed woman named Amelia Banbury-King (3065-3114). They had a daughter, Isabelle Crook.
