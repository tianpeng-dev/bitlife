Vlad Șerban (1359-1439) was the son of Alexandru Șerban and Georgiana Popescu.

Career[]

Vlad was a burgrave (VP in Engineering) from 1423 to 1424.

Personal Life[]

Vlad had a love affair with Maria Rădulescu, which resulted in a daughter, Ramona Șerban (1386-1472). Vlad married a year later (1387) with Oana Constantin (1354-1446). They never got to have children, until they a adopted a friend's daughter in 1429: Ioana Popa (1427-1474), which changed her name to Șerban. Vlad divorced Oana in 1435.

Vlad died of old age at 80 years old.
