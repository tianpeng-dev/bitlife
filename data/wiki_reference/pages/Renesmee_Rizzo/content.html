Renesmee Rizzo (2025-after 2115) was an American-Dominican millionaire.

Early life (2025-2043)[]

Rizzo was born in Phoenix, Arizona. She was the only child of Amber Hood II and her boyfriend Elvis Rizzo.

Rizzo went to Catamaran Middle School and was a goth. She then went to Royalty High School and graduated in 2043.

In 2043, Rizzo emigrated to Puerto Plata, Dominican Republic.

Later life[]

Rizzo was alive in 2115, but her death date is unknown.

Marriage and children[]

In 2039, Rizzo started dating a student named Darius Cross, Sr. (2025-2112). They were married in 2043, and Cross became a hairdresser in 2044 and retired in 2091. They had two children:

  1. Renesmee Cross I (2042-after 2115), hairdresser
  2. Darius Cross, Jr.
