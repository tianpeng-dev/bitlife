The Mafia is a job that your character can do, If they want a career in organised crime! There are 6 different types of Mafia. Sicillian Mafia, Irish Mob, Russian Mafia, Yakuza (Japanese Mafia), Latin Mafia and Triad.

Since you only get 10% of your money back when you generate income for the family, you would think independent crime would be easier. But it's not. While you just get a bit of money back, you get lots more protection. Plus, you get lots of respect when you catch a rat, kill someone successfully, think of a good idea for something, or generally give the family income.


Moving up in the ranks[]

There are 6 roles in every Mafia. Associate, Soldier, Caporegime, Underboss, and Godfather/Godmother. All members of the Mafia start off as associates. A decent worker usually gets their first promotion after around 8 years, or 6 years if they try really hard and have good standing. To become a soldier, you have to kill a random person for the Mafia. Then you take an oath, and then you get promoted. The other roles don't have this, and you get an instant promotion. There are no further roles above godfather/godmother, because they are the leader and on the top!

Things that happen in the Mafia[]

If you decide to leave the Mafia while you are just an associate, nothing will really happen. However, if you leave when you are a soldier or higher, you have already taken the oath to not leave. That means that you have a really high chance to find their calling card in your house and get killed by the Mafia for breaking your promise. The only way to fully escape this is to emigrate to another country.
