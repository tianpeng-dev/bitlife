Camille Howsen (1924 - 2012) was an American magician and the mother of football player Derek Howsen.

Early life[]

Camille was born in Virginia to a family of popular entertainers, with both of her parents being multimedia actors, performing in film and on stage.

Magic career[]

By the time she graduated high school in 1942, Camille had completed her apprenticeship. After a few more final training lessons over the summer, she became a full-time magician.

Camille retired in 1990.

Personal life[]

In her teens, Camille met her boyfriend, Dennis Howsen, a rookie writer and World War II veteran. After the war, they married and had three sons, including Donald (born 1952), Derek (April 1, 1953 - May 15, 2024), and Antonio (born 1960). Dennis died in 1994, and Camille followed in 2012.
