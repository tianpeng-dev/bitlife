The Model Job Pack is a special career that became available for purchase following the December 2023 update, players can now pursue a modeling career with this job pack. Players can now be born with a modeling talent, join a modeling agency, audition for modeling gigs, walk in Fashion weeks and more.

Haute Couture Outfits[]

After the promotion to elite model in a modeling agency, you will be able to receive invitations to walk at a Fashion Week when you age up. Some of your sponsors may gift you haute couture outfits that you may choose to accept and keep. There are a total of 30 unique haute couture outfits that will be viewable under your Main Menu under Designer Clothes.
