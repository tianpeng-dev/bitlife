Early life
[
]
Lil was born in Boston to a proud military family. His father, "Big" Mountjoy, was a World War II veteran, while his mother worked in military medicine. Lil has hardly spoke negatively about his childhood, though it has been proven that he endured at least some abuse from his father, who had been scarred by war and seemed to treat Lil more like his personal cadet trainee than as his son.
Military career
[
]
After finishing high school in 1970, Mountjoy was drafted into the Army.
Personal life
[
]
During the early 1970s, Mountjoy met rookie trucker
Veronica Ryder
. After a short time of dating, they married and had a son named Elijah (born 1974).
