Greece
is an original
country
that was on BitLife in September 2018. Its default cities are the capital, Athens, along with Santorini.
On iOS, Greece provides free healthcare, making characters able to consult doctors and treat
diseases and ailments
free of charge. In the
420 challenge
, Greece is one of many nations where cannabis is legal.
