Early life (5642-5660)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He is the youngest of three children born to Leo Statham XIV and his ex-girlfriend Faye Griffiths.

Career (5660-5685)[]

In 5660, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Maureen Johnston.

In 5665, Statham ran for reelection and beat Andrew Dunne.

In 5670, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Alexander Dewey.

In 5675, Statham ran for reelection and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Danielle Connolly.

In 5680, Statham ran for a third term and beat Worker’s Party candidate Eoghan Morgan.

In 5685, Statham did not run for a fourth term.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5701, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 5660 to 5664, Statham dated a jeweler named Peggy O’Hara (5642-after 5701). They had four children, Kate XVI (5661-after 5701), Hannah (5662-after 5701), Rosie (5663-after 5701), and Leo Statham XVI.
