Evchen Altmann (2532-after 2588) was a German politician and receptionist.

Early life (2532-2550)[]

Altmann was born in Berlin, Germany. Her parents were Amy Schumacher and her ex-boyfriend Anton Altmann. She graduated from high school in 2550.

Political career (2550-2570)[]

In 2550, Altmann ran for election to the Berlin GEB. She beat Marlene Kant and was inaugurated.

In 2554, Altmann ran for reelection and beat Marlene Meier.

In 2558, Altmann ran for a third term and beat Zoe Konig.

In 2562, Altmann ran for a fourth term and beat Amy Schulte.

In 2566, Altmann ran for a fifth term and beat Isabella Lang.

In 2570, Altmann did not run for a sixth term.

Front desk career (2571-2588)[]

In 2571, Altmann became a receptionist for Seamen Development.

Altmann was alive in 2588, but her death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2550 to 2551, Altmann dated a retired grocer named Fritz Schmidt (2467-2552). They had a daughter, Helena Schmidt.
