Molnar Istvan (January 4, 1910 - March 30, 2024) was a Hungarian dental hygienist and supercentenarian.

Early life and education[]

Molnar was born on a camping trip in Debrecen to hairdresser Dobos Fanni (1873 - 1949) and programmer Molnar Balazas (1863 - 1947). He had an older brother named Frigyes (1906 - 1977), a mailman, and a younger sister named Adel (1912 - 1995), an agent for Celestial Insurance Group. The family owned a miniature pinscher named DonQuixote (d. 1919).

Personal life[]

Istvan maintained a bachelor lifestyle up to his early thirties. He met Orosz Adrienn in 1942. They were married in 1945 at a banquet hall and went to Copenhagen for their honeymoon. Their daughter, Karola, was born in 1950. Karola is a wedding planner for Kerekes Ideas.

Death[]

Istvan died on March 30, 2024 at the age of 114 years, 2 months, and 26 days. He is survived by his daughter, any potential grandchildren and great-grandchildren, and many nieces and nephews.
