Queen Lily
(Lily Cahill; 1951 – ) was
Queen of the United Kingdom
and other Commonwealth realms from April 1975 until her death in TBD. She has reigned for 44 years as of 2019.
Lily is the mother of her only child,
Lola Cahill
, who famously defied royal traditions and abdicated from her title in 2005, aged eighteen opting for an acting career in the United States. Lily has been praised for her non judgemental attitude towards her daughter, especially during her infamously rebellious teenage years in the 2000s.
