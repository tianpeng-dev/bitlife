Leo Statham XXIII (5803-after 5861) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5803-5828)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the younger of two children born to Leo Statham XXII and his ex-girlfriend Megan Wilde-O’Hara.

In 5821, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5825, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Political Science and enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5828-5843)[]

In 5828, Statham graduated with a Doctorate in Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Emmett Bennett.

In 5833, Statham ran for reelection and beat Clodagh O’Malley.

In 5838, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Evan Edwards.

In 5843, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5861, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 5821 to 5824, Statham dated a student named Carrie Bulkley (5803-after 5861). They have three children, Kate XX (5822-after 5861), Chelsea (5823-after 5861), and Leo Statham XXIV.
