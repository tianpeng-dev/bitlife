Dene Givesoft
(August 7, 1951 - June 17, 2025) was an American racecar driver, basketball player, outdoorsman, and police detective. He began racing during a brief period in the National Basketball Association in the mid-to-late 1970s. Following the end of his basketball career, Givesoft became a police detective in the 1980s while still being a racing driver. He was also an avid adventurer, exploring the outdoors every chance he got, whether alone or with friends and family.

Dene was born in Speedway, Indiana to model Kiana Tyson (1920 - 2005) and World War II veteran-turned-trucker
Mason Givesoft
(1918 - 2001). Although he was an only child, he had many friends growing up, even his pet cat, Larry (1949 - 1966), and was very athletic. He got his first bike at the age of six as a gift from his father, and he rode it for many years until his late high school years at which point he gave it to his best friend.
Givesoft attended Indiana University Bloomington, Class of 1973, majoring in criminal justice.
