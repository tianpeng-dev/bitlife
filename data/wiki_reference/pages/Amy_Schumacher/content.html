Amy Schumacher (2513-after 2569) was a German politician and magician.

Early life (2513-2531)[]

Schumacher was born in Berlin, Germany. Her parents were Lisa Achen and her ex-boyfriend Dieter Schumacher. He graduated high school in 2531.

Political career (2531-2550)[]

In 2532, Schumacher ran for election to the Berlin GEB. She beat Juna Kraus and was inaugurated the same year.

In 2535, Schumacher ran for reelection, but lost to Theodor Berger. She became a magician for Muller & Fasdbender World Circus.

In 2538, Schumacher was appointed as the Lord Mayor of Berlin, beating Danek Winkler. She joined the Social Democratic Party of Germany.

In 2542, Schumacher resigned as Lord Mayor of Berlin. She ran for a second term in the Berlin GEB and beat Julius Dell.

In 2546, Schumacher ran for a third term and beat Fabian Hesse.

In 2550, Schumacher did not run for a fourth term.

Magic career (2551-2569)[]

In 2551, Schumacher became a magician again, this time for Liquid World Circus.

Schumacher was alive in 2569, but the date of her death is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 2531 to 2532, Schumacher dated a retired real estate agent named Anton Altmann (2449-2536). They had a daughter, Evchen Altmann.
