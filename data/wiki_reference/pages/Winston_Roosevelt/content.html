Winston Roosevelt (2923-after 2984) was a Bolivian-American receptionist.

Early life (2923-2941)[]

Roosevelt was born in Sucre, Bolivia. He was the younger of two brothers born to Dean Roosevelt and his ex-girlfriend Lisa Miller.

In 2929, Roosevelt enrolled at Little Alvarado Primary School.

In 2934, Roosevelt enrolled at Chaves Middle School.

In 2937, Roosevelt enrolled at Sucre Secondary School.

In 2941, Roosevelt graduated from secondary school. He emigrated to Los Angeles, California.

Career (2948-2984)[]

In 2948, Roosevelt became a receptionist for Delta Corp.

Roosevelt was alive in 2984, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and children[]

From 2933 to 2947, Roosevelt dated a Venezuelan-born student named Mia Bacca (2923-after 2984), who became a jeweler in 2942. They had six children, Carlton XXIII (2940-after 2984), Yuliana (2942-2969), Sonia (2943-after 2984), Deyla (2944-after 2984), Luciana (2946-after 2984), and Esteban Roosevelt.
