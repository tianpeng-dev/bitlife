His Imperial Majesty King Frederick I, formerly commonly known as Prince Frederick, or "Fred Windsor", is the only child of the Queen of the United Kingdom, Jane Windsor I, and Prince Consort Arlo Windsor. He is the current King of the United Kingdom.

Early Life[]

Born on November 18, 2024, Prince Frederick was the only child and son of the Queen of the United Kingdom, Jane I, and her consort, Prince Arlo. Being born into the noble house of Windsor, Frederick was expected to uphold the roots of the English monarchy. His birth was celebrated across the country for 86 consecutive days.

Prince Frederick started attending Little Warwick Primary School when he was 5 years of age. At the time, the whole nation of the UK respected the little Prince dearly. During his time in primary school, the Prince of Anderson was not very social with anyone, only making one friend: Grover Underwood, in the school. Underwood would soon become his lifelong friend. When he was 8, Frederick's father, Prince Consort Arlo, died while snowboarding, getting trapped in avalanche in his final moments. Young Frederick's happiness slipped away in an instant, causing the young boy to fall into a depression. However, thanks to his mother, Frederick soon found happiness once again.

When he was 13, he met Chelsea Davies, a popular jock girl at his middle school. Being a member of the soccer team, Frederick had a date with Chelsea. This worked out, and the two officially started dating unbeknownst to Frederick's mother.

When he's not serving his country as Crown Prince, Frederick plays piano, takes acting and voice lessons, and practices martial arts, specifically kung fu.

Adult Years[]

When he turned 17, Fred's mother gave him a Ferrari 812 Superfast in honor of Fred getting his drivers license. When he turned 18, Fred got the guts to propose to his longtime girlfriend, Chelsea. She accepted immediately and the two got married in a cathedral with over 1000 guests when they turned 20.

One year later, Chelsea, now Princess Chelsea, Princess Frederick of Anderson, gave birth to the couple's first child, Jane Windsor, Duchess of Beaufort (born 2045). She was born with beautiful green eyes, something that the couple never had. A year later, the couple gave birth to their first baby boy - William Windsor, Duke of Carlisle (born 2046). Another baby soon followed, whom the couple christened as George Windsor, Duke of Morley (born 2047). Their last child was named Gregory Windsor, Duke of Croughton (born 2048).
