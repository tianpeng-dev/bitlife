Leo Statham XXVIII (5901-after 5958) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5901-5926)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the only child of Leo Statham XXVII and his ex-girlfriend Agnes Smyth.

In 5919, Statham enrolled at the University of Dublin.

In 5923, Statham graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in English. He enrolled at Trinity College Dublin School of Law.

Career (5926-5941)[]

In 5926, Statham graduated with a Doctorate of Jurisprudence. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Dorothy Graham.

In 5931, Statham ran for reelection and beat Dillon O’Malley.

In 5936, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Joshua O’Toole.

In 5941, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5958, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5919 to 5920, Statham dated a student named Clara Campbell (5901-after 5958). They had a son, Leo Statham XXIX.
