Benjamin Cha II (3407-after 3444) was an Aruban-American grocer.

Early life (3407-3426)[]

In 3407, Cha was born Benjamin Yepes in Oranjestad, Aruba.

In 3408, Cha was adopted by Brooklyn Cha, becoming the youngest of her four children.

In 3424, Cha graduated from secondary school.

In 3426, Cha emigrated to Phoenix, Arizona.

Career (3439-3444)[]

In 3439, Cha became an apprentice grocer for Medley Grocery.

Cha outlived his daughter, but his death date is unknown.
