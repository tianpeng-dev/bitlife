Leo Statham XVIII (5702-after 5758) was an Irish politician.

Early life (5702-5720)[]

Statham was born in Dublin, Ireland. He was the only child of Leo Statham XVII and his ex-girlfriend Katie Graham.

Career (5720-5740)[]

In 5720, Statham graduated from secondary school. He ran for election to the Dublin City Council and beat Charles Joyce.

In 5725, Statham ran for reelection and beat Emmett Farrell-Murray.

In 5730, Statham ran for a third term and beat Karen Driscoll.

In 5735, Statham ran for Mayor of Dublin as a member of Sinn Fein and beat Fianna Fáil candidate Emmett Beckett.

In 5740, Statham did not run for reelection.

Later life[]

Statham was alive in 5758, but his death date is unknown.

Relationship and child[]

From 5720 to 5721, Statham dated an insurance agent named Freya Jenkins-Walsh (5702-after 5758). They had a son, Leo Statham XIX.
