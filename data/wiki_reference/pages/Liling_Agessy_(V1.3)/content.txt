Liling Agessy
(née
Zedong
; born July 10, 1987) is a Canadian and American professional volleyball player. She played for the Vancouver Vans from 2005 to 2019 and the Boulder Brownies from 2019 to 2024. She currently plays for the Detroit Material Girls. Agessy competed in the 2024 Paris Olympics, representing her team and both her countries.
